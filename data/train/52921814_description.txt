The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is octacosanoic acid having six double bonds located at positions 10, 13, 16, 19, 22 and 25 (the 10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and an octacosahexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z)-octacosahexaenoate.
