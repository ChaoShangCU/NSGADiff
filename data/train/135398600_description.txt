The molecule is dianion of 2,5-diamino-4-hydroxy-6-(5-phosphoribosylamino)pyrimidine. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a 2,5-diamino-4-hydroxy-6-(5-phosphoribosylamino)pyrimidine. It is a tautomer of a 2,5-diamino-6-(1-D-ribosylamino)pyrimidin-4(3H)-one 5'-phosphate(2-).
