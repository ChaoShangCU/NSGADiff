The molecule is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide that is 3',5'-cyclic GMP in which the hydrogen at position 2 on the purine fragment is replaced by a 6-aminohexylthio group. It is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide, a ribonucleotide, an aryl sulfide and a primary amino compound. It derives from a 3',5'-cyclic GMP.
