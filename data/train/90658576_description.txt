The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3E)-tetradecenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a trans-3-enoyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3E)-tetradecenoyl-CoA.
