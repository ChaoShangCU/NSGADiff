The molecule is an aryl beta-D-glucoside that is 3-hydroxyphloretin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 2' is replaced by a 6-O-beta-D-xylosyl-beta-D-glucosyl group. It is an aryl beta-D-glucoside, a member of dihydrochalcones, a disaccharide derivative and a polyphenol. It derives from a phloretin.
