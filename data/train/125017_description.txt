The molecule is a tertiary amino compound that is N,N-dimethylethanamine substituted at position 1 by a 1-hydroxycyclohexyl and 4-hydroxyphenyl group. It is a metabolite of the drug venlafaxine. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite, a drug metabolite and an antidepressant. It is a member of cyclohexanols, a member of phenols and a tertiary amino compound.
