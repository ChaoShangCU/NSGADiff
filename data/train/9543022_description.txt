The molecule is a 3-hydroxyacyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 3-hydroxypimelic acid. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA and an omega-carboxyacyl-CoA. It derives from a pimeloyl-CoA. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxypimeloyl-CoA(5-).
