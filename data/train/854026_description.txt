The molecule is a sesquiterpene alkaloid isolated from a club moss Huperzia serrata that has been shown to exhibit neuroprotective activity. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, a neuroprotective agent and a plant metabolite. It is a sesquiterpene alkaloid, a pyridone, a primary amino compound and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
