The molecule is an oxylipin that is [(1S)-3-oxocyclopentyl]acetic acid substituted by a (2Z)-pent-2-en-1-yl group at position 2 on the cyclopentanone ring. It has a role as a member of jasmonates and a plant metabolite. It is an oxo carboxylic acid and an oxylipin. It is a conjugate acid of a (+)-7-isojasmonate.
