The molecule is a para-terphenyl that is 1,1':4',1''-terphenyl substituted by methoxy groups at positions 3' and 6', hydroxy groups at positions 2', 3 and 4'' and a prenyloxy group at position 4. It has been isolated from Aspergillus taichungensis. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a para-terphenyl, a dimethoxybenzene and a member of phenols.
