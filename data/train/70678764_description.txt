The molecule is an 18-membered cyclodepsipeptide having a 3-amino-2-hydroxybenzoyl group attached to the amino terminus. It is isolated from an Alaskan Kitasatospora sp. and exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a cyclodepsipeptide, a primary arylamine, a member of phenols and a member of benzamides.
