The molecule is a benzenedicarboxamide compound having N-substituted carbamoyl groups at the 1- and 3-positions, iodo substituents at the 2-, 4- and 6-positions and an acetyl(methyl)amino group at the 5-position. It has a role as a radioopaque medium. It is an organoiodine compound, a benzenedicarboxamide and a member of benzoic acids.
