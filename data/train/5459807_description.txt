The molecule is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion resulting from the removal of the proton from the carboxy group of (R)-10-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion, a hydroxy saturated fatty acid anion and a 10-hydroxyoctadecanoate. It derives from an octadecanoate. It is a conjugate base of a (R)-10-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid.
