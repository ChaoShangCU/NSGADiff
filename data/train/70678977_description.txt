The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the triphosphate and phosphonate OH groups of alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-triphosphate. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and an organophosphonate oxoanion. It is a conjugate base of an alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-triphosphate(4-).
