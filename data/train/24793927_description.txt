The molecule is an N-acylpiperidine obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 5-[(3-methoxyphenoxy)methyl]-1,2-oxazole-3-carboxylic acid with the secondary amino group of 4-(pyridin-4-yl)piperidine. It has a role as a P450 inhibitor. It is a N-acylpiperidine, an aromatic amide, an aromatic ether, a member of isoxazoles, a member of pyridines and a ring assembly.
