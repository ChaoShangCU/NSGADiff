The molecule is an alkaloid isolated from the club moss Lycopodium japonicum and has been shown to exhibit acetylcholinesterase inhibitory and anti-HIV-1 activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor and an anti-HIV-1 agent. It is an alkaloid, a lactone, a cyclic ketone, a bridged compound and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
