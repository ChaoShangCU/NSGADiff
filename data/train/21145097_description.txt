The molecule is a dioxo monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of 2,3-diketogulonic acid, and the major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a dioxo monocarboxylic acid anion, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion and a carbohydrate acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2,3-diketogulonic acid.
