The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid based on the caryophyllene ring system. Isolated from the fungal fermentation broth of Chrysosporium pilosum, it exhibits antifungal activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a sesquiterpenoid, a carbobicyclic compound, a primary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, an enone and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
