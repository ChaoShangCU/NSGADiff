The molecule is a 3-(3-sulfooxyphenyl)propanoic acid anion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy and sulfo groups of 3-(3-sulfooxyphenyl)propanoic acid. It is a 3-(3-sulfooxyphenyl)propanoic acid anion, a monocarboxylic acid anion and a phenyl sulfate oxoanion. It is a conjugate base of a 3-(3-sulfooxyphenyl)propanoic acid.
