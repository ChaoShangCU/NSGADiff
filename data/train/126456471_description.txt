The molecule is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the two hydroxy substituents are located at the 14R- and 22-positions. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid, a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14R,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoate. It is an enantiomer of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid.
