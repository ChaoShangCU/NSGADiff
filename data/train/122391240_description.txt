The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is 3',5'-di-O-methyltricetin (tricin) having a 6-O-malonyl-beta-D-glucosyl residue attached at the 7-hydroxy position It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a monosaccharide derivative, a dimethoxyflavone, a dihydroxyflavone, a beta-D-glucoside and a polyphenol. It derives from a 3',5'-di-O-methyltricetin.
