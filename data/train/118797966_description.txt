The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is cyanidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(Z-4 coumaryl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an anthocyanin cation, a cinnamate ester, a polyphenol and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a cyanidin cation and a cis-4-coumaric acid.
