The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate OH groups of (9S,10R)-10-hydroxy-9-(phosphonatooxy)octadecanoic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (9S,10R)-10-hydroxy-9-(phosphonooxy)octadecanoic acid.
