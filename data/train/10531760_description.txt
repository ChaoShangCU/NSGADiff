The molecule is a CMP-sugar having 3-deoxy-D-glycero-beta-D-galacto-nonulosonic acid as the sugar component. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It derives from a 3-deoxy-D-glycero-beta-D-galacto-nonulosonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a CMP-3-deoxy-D-glycero-beta-D-galacto-nonulosonate(2-).
