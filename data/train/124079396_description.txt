The molecule is a monoterpene glycoside that is albiflorin carrying a galloyl substituent at position 6. Isolated from the roots of Paeonia lactiflora. It has a role as a plant metabolite, an androgen antagonist and an antineoplastic agent. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a gamma-lactone, a gallate ester, a bridged compound, a monoterpene glycoside and a secondary alcohol. It derives from an albiflorin.
