The molecule is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion that is the conjugate base of N-arachidonoyl-L-leucine, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion, a N-(fatty acyl)-L-alpha-amino acid anion and a N-(fatty acyl)-L-leucine(1-). It is a conjugate base of a N-arachidonoyl-L-leucine.
