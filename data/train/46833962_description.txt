The molecule is a benzochromenone that is 6H-benzo[c]chromen-6-one which is substituted by a methyl group at position 1, chloro group at position 2, hydroxy groups at positions positions 3 and 7, and by a methoxy group at position 9. It has a role as an antifungal agent, a fungal metabolite and an antimicrobial agent. It is a benzochromenone, an aromatic ether, an organic hydroxy compound, a delta-lactone and an organochlorine compound. It derives from a 6H-dibenzo[b,d]pyran-6-one.
