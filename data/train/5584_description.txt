The molecule is a dibenzoazepine that is 10,11-dihydro-5H-dibenzo[b,f]azepine substituted by a 3-(dimethylamino)-2-methylpropyl group at the nitrogen atom. It is used as an antidepressant. It has a role as an antidepressant, an environmental contaminant and a xenobiotic. It is a dibenzoazepine and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from an imipramine.
