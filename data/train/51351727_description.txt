The molecule is a member of the class of phenylacetic acids that is phenylacetic acid having a (2,6-dichlorophenyl)amino group at the 2-position and a 2-(2-oxoethoxy)ethoxy group at the 5-position. It has a role as an allergen. It is a dichlorobenzene and a member of phenylacetic acids. It derives from a diphenylamine and a phenylacetic acid.
