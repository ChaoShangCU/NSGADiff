The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is quercetin attached to a 6-deoxy-4-O-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-alpha-L-mannopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a tetrahydroxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a quercetin.
