The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is lanosta-7,9(11)-dien-21-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 16 and 25 and a methylidene group at position 24. It has been isolated from Poria cocos. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol and a tetracyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
