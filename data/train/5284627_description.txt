The molecule is a hexose derivative that is 2,3:4,5-di-O-isopropylidene-beta-D-fructopyranose in which the hydroxy group has been converted to the corresponding sulfamate ester. It blocks voltage-dependent sodium channels and is used as an antiepileptic and for the prevention of migraine. It has a role as an anticonvulsant and a sodium channel blocker. It is a cyclic ketal, a sulfamate ester and a ketohexose derivative.
