The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid monoanion that is the conjugate base of 2-aminomuconic acid. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a 2-aminomuconic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-aminomuconate(2-).
