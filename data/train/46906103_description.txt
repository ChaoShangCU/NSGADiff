The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of a 3-O-sulfo-beta-D-glucuronic acid residue, a D-galactosyl residue and an N-acetyl-D-glucosamine residue (at the reducing end) in a linear sequence. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and an oligosaccharide sulfate. It is a conjugate acid of a beta-D-GlcA3S-(1->3)-beta-D-Gal-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc(2-).
