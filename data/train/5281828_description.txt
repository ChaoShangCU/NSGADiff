The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound and 19-membered macrocyclic lactam antibiotic originally isolated from the Ethiopian shrub Maytenus serrata but also found in other Maytenus species. It exhibits cytotoxicity against many tumour cell lines. It has a role as a plant metabolite, an antimicrobial agent, an antineoplastic agent, a tubulin modulator and an antimitotic. It is an epoxide, a carbamate ester, an organochlorine compound, an alpha-amino acid ester, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and a maytansinoid.
