The molecule is an organic heteroheptacyclic compound isolated from the bark of Indonesian Garcinia gaudichaudii and exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a bridged compound, a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, an organic heteroheptacyclic compound, an oxo monocarboxylic acid and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid.
