The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is hept-6-en-3-one substituted by a 4-hydroxyphenyl group at position 1 and a phenyl group at position 7 respectively. It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma kwangsiensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a ketone and a member of phenols.
