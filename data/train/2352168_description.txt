The molecule is a member of the class of benzamides obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-phenoxybenzoic acid with the amino group of (pyridin-2-yl)methylamine. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzamides, an aromatic ether and a member of pyridines.
