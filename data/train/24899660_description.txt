The molecule is a indolocarbazole alkaloid that is staurosporine substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 8 and 9 and an oxo group at position 7. Isolated from Cystodytes solitus, it exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an indolocarbazole, an indolocarbazole alkaloid, an organic heterooctacyclic compound and a polyphenol. It derives from a staurosporine.
