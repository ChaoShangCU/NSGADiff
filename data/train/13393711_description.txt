The molecule is a member of the class of naphthols that is naproxen in which the 6-methoxy group has been demethylated. It has a role as a drug metabolite, an environmental contaminant, a human blood serum metabolite and a human urinary metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid and a member of naphthols. It is a conjugate acid of a desmethylnaproxen(1-).
