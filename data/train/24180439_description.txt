The molecule is a 21-membered macrocycle isolated from the fermentation broth of Streptomyces sp.AC654. It exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimicrobial agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a secondary alcohol, a carboxylic ester, an ether, a lactam, a macrocycle, an aryl sulfide and a member of hydroquinones.
