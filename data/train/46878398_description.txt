The molecule is tetraanion of N(6)-(1,2-dicarboxyethyl)-AMP arising from deprotonation of carboxylic acid and phosphate functions. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a N(6)-(1,2-dicarboxyethyl)-AMP.
