The molecule is a member of the class of thiazolidines that is (4S)-4-methyl-4,5-dihydro-1,3-thiazole-4-carboxylic acid which is substituted at position 2 by a (1S)-1-hydroxy-1-{(4R)-2-[(4R)-2-(2-hydroxyphenyl)-4,5-dihydro-1,3-thiazol-4-yl]-1,3-thiazolidin-4-yl}-2-methylpropan-2-yl group. A siderophore found in the gram-negative bacterium species, Yersinia enterocolitica and Yersinia pestis. It has a role as a siderophore and a bacterial metabolite. It is a member of phenols, a member of thiazolidines, a monocarboxylic acid and a secondary alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of a yersiniabactin(1-).
