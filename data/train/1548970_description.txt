The molecule is an N-methyl-3-phenyl-3-[4-(trifluoromethyl)phenoxy]propan-1-amine that has R configuration. [The antidepressant drug fluoxetine is a racemate comprising equimolar amounts of (R)- and (S)-fluoxetine]. It has a role as an antidepressant and a serotonin uptake inhibitor. It is a conjugate base of a (R)-fluoxetine(1+). It is an enantiomer of a (S)-fluoxetine.
