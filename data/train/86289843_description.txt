The molecule is a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid that is tricosanoic acid in which the pro-R hydrogens at positions 3 and 22 are replaced by hydroxy groups. It is a very long-chain fatty acid, an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid, a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid and a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a tricosanoic acid.
