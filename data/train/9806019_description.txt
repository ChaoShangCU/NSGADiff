The molecule is an organic nitrate salt obtained by reaction of equimolar amounts of arasertaconazole and nitric acid. The active R-enantiomer of sertaconazole nitrate that is used for treatment of vulvovaginal candidiasis. The racemate itself is also used as a broad-spectrum antifungal drug. It is an organic nitrate salt, a conazole antifungal drug and an imidazole antifungal drug. It contains an arasertaconazole(1+). It is an enantiomer of a (S)-sertaconazole nitrate.
