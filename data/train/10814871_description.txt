The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-12-en-28-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 23 and 29. It has been isolated from the stem bark of Kalopanax pictus. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
