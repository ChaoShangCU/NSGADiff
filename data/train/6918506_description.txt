The molecule is a macrolide that is a novel secondary metabolite isolated from a New Zealand marine sponge, Mycale hentscheli. It has a role as a marine metabolite, an antimitotic, a microtubule-stabilising agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a macrolide, a cyclic hemiketal, an ether, an organic heterobicyclic compound, a secondary alcohol and a primary alcohol.
