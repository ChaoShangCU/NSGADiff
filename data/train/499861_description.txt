The molecule is a tetracyclic diterpenoid consisting of sordaricin in which the primary hydroxy hydrogen has been replaced by a 6-deoxy-beta-D-altropyranosyl group. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a tetracyclic diterpenoid, a monosaccharide derivative, a glycoside, an aldehyde, a bridged compound and a 3-oxo monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 4'-O-demethylsordarin(1-).
