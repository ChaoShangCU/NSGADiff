The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isovitexin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by a beta-D-glucosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a glycosyloxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone, a C-glycosyl compound and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from an isoscoparin.
