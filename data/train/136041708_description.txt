The molecule is an Mo-molybdopterin cofactor comprising two molecules of molybdopterin guanine dinucleotide attached via their sulfur atoms to a central Mo(=O) moiety. It is a molybdopterin dinucleotide and a Mo-molybdopterin cofactor. It is a conjugate acid of a Mo(=O)-bis(molybdopterin guanine dinucleotide)(4-).
