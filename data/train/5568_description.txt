The molecule is a member of the class of phenothiazines that is 10H-phenothiazine having a trifluoromethyl subsitituent at the 2-position and a 3-(dimethylamino)propyl group at the N-10 position. It has a role as a dopaminergic antagonist, an antiemetic, a first generation antipsychotic and an anticoronaviral agent. It is a tertiary amine, a member of phenothiazines and an organofluorine compound. It derives from a hydride of a 10H-phenothiazine.
