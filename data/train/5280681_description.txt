The molecule is a tetrahydroxyflavone having the 4-hydroxy groups located at the 3'- 4'- 5- and 7-positions as well as a methoxy group at the 2-position. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimicrobial agent. It is a tetrahydroxyflavone and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a quercetin. It is a conjugate acid of a 3',4',5-trihydroxy-3-methoxyflavon-7-olate.
