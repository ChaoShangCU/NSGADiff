The molecule is a hydroxy fatty-acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 2-hydroxypalmitic acid. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA, a hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 2-hydroxyhexadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxypalmitoyl-CoA(4-).
