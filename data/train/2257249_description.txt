The molecule is a pyrazolopyrimidine that is 4H-pyrazolo[1,5-a]pyrimidin-7-one which is substituted at positions 2, 3, and 5 by benzyl, p-chlorophenyl, and p-nitrophenyl groups, respectively. It is an inhibitor of ADP-ribosylation factor 6 (ARF6), a member of the ADP ribosylation factor family of GTP-binding proteins. It has a role as an inhibitor. It is a pyrazolopyrimidine, a C-nitro compound and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
