The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazolopyrimidines that is the amide obtained from formal condensation of the carboxy group of 5-phenyl-7-(trifluoromethyl)pyrazolo[1,5-a]pyrimidine-2-carboxylic acid with the endocyclic amino group of 3-methyl-5-(trifluoromethyl)-4,5-dihydropyrazol-5-ol. It is an organofluorine compound, an aromatic amide, a tertiary alcohol, a pyrazolopyrimidine and a member of pyrazoles.
