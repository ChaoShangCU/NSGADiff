The molecule is a 1-monoglyceride resulting from the condensation of the carboxy group of prostaglandin F2alpha with the 1-hydroxy group of glycerol. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 1-monoglyceride, an olefinic compound, a secondary alcohol, a prostaglandins Falpha and a pentol. It derives from a prostaglandin F2alpha.
