The molecule is a polyanionic polymer obtained by global deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate groups of reduced coenzyme F420-(gamma-Glu)n. Major microspecies at pH 7.3 It is a carboxylic acid anion, a dialkyl phosphate anion and a polyanionic polymer. It derives from a 7,8-didemethyl-8-hydroxy-5-deazariboflavin.
