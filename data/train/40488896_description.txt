The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 8(S)-HETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a HETE anion. It is a conjugate base of an 8(S)-HETE.
