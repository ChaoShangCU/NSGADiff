The molecule is a member of the class of 1,2-benzoxazoles carrying 2,4-dihydroxy-4-chlorophenyl and (2-morpholin-4-ylethyl)amino substituents at positions 3 and 5 respectively. It has a role as a Hsp90 inhibitor. It is a member of 1,2-benzoxazoles, a member of morpholines, a member of resorcinols, a member of monochlorobenzenes, a tertiary amino compound, a secondary amino compound and an aromatic amine.
