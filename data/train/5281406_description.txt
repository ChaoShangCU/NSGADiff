The molecule is a member of the class of coumarins that is coumarin substituted by a hydroxy group at position 7, a methoxy group at position 6 and a (2-oxo-2H-chromen-7-yl)oxy group at position 3. It has a role as a metabolite, an antiviral agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a hydroxycoumarin and an aromatic ether. It derives from a coumarin.
