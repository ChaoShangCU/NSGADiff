The molecule is a 26-oxo steroid resulting from the oxidation of (25R)-3beta,26-dihydroxycholest-5-en-7-one to the corresponding aldehyde. It is a 7-oxo steroid, a cholestanoid, a 26-oxo steroid, an oxysterol, a 3beta-sterol, a steroid aldehyde and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid. It derives from a cholesterol.
