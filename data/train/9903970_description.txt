The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is 2,3,3a,12b-tetrahydrodibenzo[2,3:6,7]oxepino[4,5-c]pyrrole bearing methyl and chloro substituents at positions 2 and 5 respectively. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, an organochlorine compound, a cyclic ether and a tertiary amino compound.
