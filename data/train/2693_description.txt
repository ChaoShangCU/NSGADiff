The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound of the class of epipolythiodioxopiperazine. Isolated from Chaetomium globosum and Farrowia seminuda, it exhibits immunosuppressive activity. It has a role as an immunosuppressive agent and a Chaetomium metabolite. It is a member of indoles, an organic disulfide and an organic heteropentacyclic compound.
