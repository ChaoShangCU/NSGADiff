The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of erucic acid. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA, an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from an erucic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an erucoyl-CoA(4-).
