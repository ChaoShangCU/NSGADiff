The molecule is a para-terphenyl that is 1,1':4',1''-terphenyl substituted by methoxy groups at positions 3' and 6', a prenyl group at position 5 and hydroxy groups at positions 2', 3, 4 and 4''. Isolated from the fungus, Aspergillus taichungensis, it exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a para-terphenyl, a member of catechols and a dimethoxybenzene.
