The molecule is a tirucallane triterpenoid that is (13alpha,14beta,17alpha,20S)-lanost-5-ene substituted by an oxo group at position 3 and a beta-hydroxy group at position 1. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a secondary alcohol, a tirucallane triterpenoid and a 3-oxo-Delta(5)-steroid.
