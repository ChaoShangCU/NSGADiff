The molecule is a member of the class of menaquinones that is menaquinone-9 in which the double bond that is second nearest to the naphthoquinone moiety has been dihydrogenated and one of the terminal methyl hydrogens has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a member of menaquinones and a primary allylic alcohol. It derives from a menaquinone-9.
