The molecule is a trihydroxyflavone that is 5,6,3'-trihydroxyflavone further substituted by methoxy groups at positions 7, 8 and 4'. Isolated from Crinum latifolium, it exhibits inhibitory effect on the tube-like formation of human umbilical vein endothelial cells (HUVECs). It has a role as a metabolite. It is a trimethoxyflavone and a trihydroxyflavone.
