The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-12-en-28-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 19 and 24 (the 3beta,19alpha stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the roots of Rubia yunnanensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a sapogenin, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from an oleanolic acid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
