The molecule is a member of the class of thromboxanes that is thromboxane A2 in which the bridging oxygen has been replaced by a methylene group. It has a role as a vasoconstrictor agent and a thromboxane A2 agonist. It is a thromboxane, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a bridged compound, a cyclic ether and a secondary allylic alcohol. It derives from a thromboxane A2. It is a conjugate acid of a carbocyclic thromboxane A2(1-).
