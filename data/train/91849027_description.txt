The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of alpha-L-threo-hex-4-enopyranuronic acid and 2-deoxy-6-O-sulfo-2-(sulfoamino)-D-glucopyranose joined in sequence by a (1->4) glycosidic bond. It is a disaccharide derivative, an oligosaccharide sulfate, a member of sulfamic acids, a monocarboxylic acid and an enol.
