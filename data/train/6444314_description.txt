The molecule is a fatty acid derivative isolated from the fermentation broth of Alcaligenes sp. YL-02632S. It is an antibiotic with antibacterial activity. It has a role as an antibacterial agent, an antimicrobial agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is a carbamate ester, a fatty acid derivative, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and a secondary carboxamide.
