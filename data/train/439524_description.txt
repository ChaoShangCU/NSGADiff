The molecule is the D-enantiomer of leucine. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a leucine and a D-alpha-amino acid. It is a conjugate base of a D-leucinium. It is a conjugate acid of a D-leucinate. It is an enantiomer of a L-leucine. It is a tautomer of a D-leucine zwitterion.
