The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion arising from deprotonation of the phospho and carboxy groups of 2,3-bisphospho-D-glyceric acid; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2,3-bisphospho-D-glyceric acid.
