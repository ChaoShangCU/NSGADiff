The molecule is a member of the class of chromenes that is 2H-1-benzopyran-8-methanol diacetate substituted by methyl groups at positions 2 and 2, an ethyl group at position 4 and a 4-methoxyphenyl group at position 3 respectively. It is a member of chromenes, an acetate ester, a monomethoxybenzene and a diester.
