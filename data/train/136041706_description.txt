The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion arising from deprotonation of the four diphosphate OH groups of molybdate-bis(molybdopterin guanine dinucleotide); major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a Mo-molybdopterin cofactor. It is a conjugate base of a Mo(=O)-bis(molybdopterin guanine dinucleotide).
