The molecule is a lipopeptide consisting of a 2-methyloct-7-ynoyl moiety attached to a linear tetrapeptide comprising of N-methyl-L-phenylalanyl and N-methyl-L-valine units. It is isolated from a Panamanian strain of the marine cyanobacterium Lyngbya majuscula and displays antimalarial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is a lipopeptide and a terminal acetylenic compound.
