The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is friedelin substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 16 and 28. It is isolated from the stems of Celastrus hindsii and exhibits anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a diol, a primary alcohol and a secondary alcohol.
