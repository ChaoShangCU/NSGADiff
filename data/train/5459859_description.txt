The molecule is a C21-steroid that is pregnane which contains a double bond between positions 4 and 5 and is substituted by an oxo group at position 3 and by hydroxy groups at the 17alpha and 20 positions. It is a C21-steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid and a 20-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
