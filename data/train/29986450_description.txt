The molecule is a dialkyl phosphate anion formed by deprotonation of both the phosphoric and carboxylic acid groups of 6-(O-phosphocholine)hydroxyhexanoic acid. It is a carboxylic acid anion and a dialkyl phosphate anion. It is a conjugate base of a 6-(O-phosphocholine)oxyhexanoic acid betaine and a 6-(O-phosphocholine)oxyhexanoate.
