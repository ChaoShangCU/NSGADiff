The molecule is a sesquiterpene alkaloid that is a macrolide incorporating a substituted pyridine and dihydroagarofuran moieties. Isolated from root barks of Tripterygium hypoglaucum, it exhibits anti-HIV activity. It has a role as an anti-HIV agent and a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid, an acetate ester, a 2-furoate ester, a macrolide, a sesquiterpene alkaloid and a pyridine alkaloid.
