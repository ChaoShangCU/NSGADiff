The molecule is a steroid glucuronide anion that is the conjugate base of 17beta-estradiol 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion and a beta-D-glucosiduronate. It is a conjugate base of a 17beta-estradiol 3-glucosiduronic acid.
