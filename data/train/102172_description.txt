The molecule is a steroid sulfate obtained by the formal condensation of hydroxy group at position 21 of cortisol with sulfuric acid. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a steroid sulfate, an 11beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 20-oxo steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone and a cortisol ester. It is a conjugate acid of a cortisol 21-sulfate(1-).
