The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid and potent cardiotoxin that is found in the leaves and berries of the European yew (Taxus baccata). It has a role as a cardiotoxic agent. It is an enone, a carbotricyclic compound, an acetate ester, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a homoallylic alcohol, a diterpene alkaloid and a tertiary amino compound.
