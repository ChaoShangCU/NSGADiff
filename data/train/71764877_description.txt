The molecule is a member of the class of 2-pyranones that is 5,6-dihydro-2H-pyran-2-one substituted by a 10-(acetyloxy)-2,4,6,8-tetrahydroxypentacosyl group at position 6.It has been isolated from Cryptocarya species. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a member of 2-pyranones and a tetrol.
