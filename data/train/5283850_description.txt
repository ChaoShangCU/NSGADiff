The molecule is a trihydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid in which the three hydroxy groups are located at positions 1beta, 3alpha, and 12alpha. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite and a bile acid metabolite. It is a trihydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid, a 12alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid and a 1-hydroxy steroid.
