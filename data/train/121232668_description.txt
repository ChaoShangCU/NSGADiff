The molecule is a fatty acid-taurine conjugate derived from N-[12(S)-hydroperoxy-(5Z,8Z,10E,14Z)]-icosatetraenoic acid. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,10E,14Z)-12-hydroxyicosatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-[12(S)-hydroperoxy-(5Z,8Z,10E,14Z)-icosatetraenoyl]taurine(1-).
