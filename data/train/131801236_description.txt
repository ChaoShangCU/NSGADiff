The molecule is a member of the class of glycerophosphoglycerols obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of oleic acid with one of the primary hydroxy groups of (S,S)-glycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (S,S)-3-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol(1-).
