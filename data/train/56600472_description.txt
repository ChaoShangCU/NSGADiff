The molecule is a fatty acid ester that is egonol-9(Z),12(Z)linoleate in which the methoxy group at position 7 is replaced by a hydrogen. Isolated from the fruits of Styrax agrestis, it exhibits inhibitory activity against acetylcholinesterase. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, a member of benzodioxoles and a polyunsaturated fatty ester. It derives from a linoleic acid and an egonol-9(Z),12(Z)linoleate. It derives from a hydride of a 1-benzofuran.
