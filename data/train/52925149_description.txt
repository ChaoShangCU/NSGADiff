The molecule is a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl group is specified as heptadecanoyl. It is a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a lysophosphatidylethanolamine 17:0. It derives from a heptadecanoic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1-heptadecanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
