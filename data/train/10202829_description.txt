The molecule is a member of the class of aminopyrimidines that is pyrimidine-2,4-diamine substituted at positions N-2 and N-4 by 3-methoxy-4-(morpholin-4-yl)phenyl and quinolin-3-yl groups respectively. It has a role as an insulin-like growth factor receptor 1 antagonist. It is a member of morpholines, a monomethoxybenzene, an aminoquinoline, an aminopyrimidine, a tertiary amino compound and a secondary amino compound.
