The molecule is an organic sulfamate oxoanion that is the conjugate base of (8-methylnonyl)sulfamic acid. It has been isolated from Daphnia pulex and has been shown to cause morphological changes in the green alga Scenedesmus gutwinskii. It has a role as a kairomone and a Daphnia pulex metabolite. It is a conjugate base of an (8-methylnonyl)sulfamic acid.
