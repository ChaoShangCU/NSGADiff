The molecule is a beta-D-glucoside with 2-(3,4-dihydroxyphenyl)ethoxy residue at the anomeric position and a [(3,4-dihydroxyphenyl)acetyl]oxy residue at position 2. Isolated from Ternstroemia japonica, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a phenylethanoid, a member of catechols, a monosaccharide derivative, a beta-D-glucoside and a carboxylic ester.
