The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is cyanidin(1+) carrying a beta-D-xylosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-galactosyl substituent at position 3. It is an anthocyanin cation, an anthocyanidin glycoside and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a cyanidin cation. It is a conjugate acid of a cyanidin 3-O-[beta-D-xylosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-galactoside](1-).
