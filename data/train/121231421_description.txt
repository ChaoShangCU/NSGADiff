The molecule is an aminoglycoside that is 4alpha,6alpha-diaminocyclohexane-1beta,2alpha,3beta-triol in which the pro-R hydroxy group has been converted into its 2-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-erythro-hexopyranosid-4-ulose derivative. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is an aminoglycoside, a primary amino compound and a triamine. It derives from a 2-deoxystreptamine.
