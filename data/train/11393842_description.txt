The molecule is a neolignan that consists of a 2,3-dihydro-1,4-benzodioxine ring substituted by a hydroxymethyl group at position 3, a 3-hydroxypropyl group at position 7 and a 4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl group at position 2. It has been isolated from Taxus yunnanensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a neolignan, a triol, an oxacycle and a member of guaiacols.
