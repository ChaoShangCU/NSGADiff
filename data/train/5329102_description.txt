The molecule is a member of pyrroles and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It has a role as an angiogenesis inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent, an EC 2.7.10.1 (receptor protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor, a vascular endothelial growth factor receptor antagonist, an immunomodulator and a neuroprotective agent. It derives from a 3-methyleneoxindole.
