The molecule is a member of the class of 7-hydroxyisoflavones that consists of 2,3,3',4'-tetrahydro-2'H,4H-3,8'-bichromen-4-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 5' and 7 and methyl groups at positions 2' and 2'. It has been isolated from Glycyrrhiza uralensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite.
