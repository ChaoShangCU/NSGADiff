The molecule is a sulfonamide consisting of 7-sulfamoyl-3,4-dihydro-2H-1,2,4-benzothiadiazine 1,1-dioxide in which the hydrogen at position 6 is substituted by a trifluoromethyl group and that at position 3 is substituted by a benzyl group. It has a role as a diuretic and an antihypertensive agent. It is a benzothiadiazine and a sulfonamide.
