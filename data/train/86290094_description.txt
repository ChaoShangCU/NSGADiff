The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the hydroperoxy group is located at position 14. It is a hydroperoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a docosanoid. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 14-HPDHE(1-).
