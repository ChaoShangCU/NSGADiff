The molecule is a member of the class of 7-hydroxyisoflavones that is 7-hydroxyisoflavone with additional hydroxy groups at positions 5, 3' and 4' and a prenyl group at position 2'. Isolated from the roots of Psorothamnus arborescens, it exhibits antileishmanial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antileishmanial agent.
