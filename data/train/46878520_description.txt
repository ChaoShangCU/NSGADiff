The molecule is the anion resulting from the removal of a proton from the carboxylic acid group of 3-[(5S,6R)-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dienyl]propanoic acid. It is a conjugate base of a 3-[(5S,6R)-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dienyl]propanoic acid. It is an enantiomer of a 3-[(5R,6S)-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dienyl]propanoate.
