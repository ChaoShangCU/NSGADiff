The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is a rearranged pentanortriterpenoid derived from cycloartane. Isolated from the leaves and stem of Schisandra lancifolia, it exhibits anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is an oxaspiro compound, a tetracyclic triterpenoid, a gamma-lactone, a diol and a monocarboxylic acid.
