The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is hept-6-en-3-ol substituted by two 4-hydroxyphenyl groups at the terminal carbon atoms respectively (the R-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma kwangsiensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a member of phenols and a secondary alcohol.
