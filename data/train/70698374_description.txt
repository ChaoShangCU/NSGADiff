The molecule is an amino pentasaccharide consisting of a linear sequence of N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminyl, beta-D-galactosyl, N-acetyl-beta-DD-glucosaminyl, beta-D-galactosyl and N-acetyl-beta-DD-glucosamine residues linked respectively (2->3), (1->3), (1->3) and (1->3). It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino pentasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
