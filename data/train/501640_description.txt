The molecule is a hydroxypiperidine that is deoxynojirimycin (duvoglustat) in which the amino hydrogen is replaced by a nonyl group. It has a role as an EC 3.2.1.45 (glucosylceramidase) inhibitor, an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor and an antiviral agent. It is a hydroxypiperidine and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a duvoglustat.
