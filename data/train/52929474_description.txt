The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate in which the acyl substituents at positions 1 and 2 are specified as oleoyl and tetradecanoyl respectively. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate and a tetradecanoate ester. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-oleoyl-2-myristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-).
