The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of N-arachidonoyl-gamma-aminobutyric acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion and a N-acyl-gamma-aminobutyrate. It is a conjugate base of a N-arachidonoyl-gamma-aminobutyric acid.
