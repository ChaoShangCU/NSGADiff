The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of alpha-D-galactose, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and N-acetyl-beta-D-galactosamine residues connected in sequence by (1->3) linkages. It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
