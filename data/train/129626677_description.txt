The molecule is a bis(hydroperoxy)icosatetraenoate that is the conjugate base of (5S,15S)-dihydroperoxy-(6E,8Z,11Z,13E)-icosatetraenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3.
