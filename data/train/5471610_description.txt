The molecule is an enone that is a tetraprenyl benzophenone derivative isolated from Rheedia gardneriana and Garcinia brasiliensis. It exhibits anti-allergic, antibacterial, trypanocidal and vasodilating activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an anti-allergic agent, an antibacterial agent, a trypanocidal drug, a vasodilator agent and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is an enol, an enone, a bridged compound and an aromatic ketone.
