The molecule is a 18-membered cyclodepsipeptide isolated from the culture broth of Flexibacter. It exhibits potent inhibitory activity against human leukocyte elastase (EC 6.5.1.1.). It has a role as a metabolite, an antimicrobial agent and an EC 3.4.21.37 (leukocyte elastase) inhibitor. It is a cyclodepsipeptide and a macrocycle.
