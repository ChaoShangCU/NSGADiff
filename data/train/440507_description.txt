The molecule is a steroid acid consisting of 3beta-hydroxy-4beta-methyl-5alpha-cholest-7-ene having a carboxy group at the 4-position. It is a steroid acid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3beta-hydroxy-4beta-methyl-5alpha-cholest-7-ene-4alpha-carboxylate. It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-cholestane.
