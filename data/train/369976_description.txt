The molecule is a carbamate ester that is fumagillol in which the hydroxy group has been converted to the corresponding N-(chloroacetyl)carbamate derivative. It has a role as a methionine aminopeptidase 2 inhibitor, a retinoic acid receptor alpha antagonist, an angiogenesis inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an EC 1.5.1.3 (dihydrofolate reductase) inhibitor. It is a carbamate ester, a sesquiterpenoid, an organochlorine compound, a semisynthetic derivative and a spiro-epoxide. It derives from a fumagillol.
