The molecule is a sulfonamide that is benzenesulfonamide which is substituted at positions 2, 4, and 5 by chlorine, (2-thienylmethyl)amino and 1H-tetrazol-5-yl groups, respectively. It is a diuretic that has been used in the management of oedema and hypertension. It has a role as a loop diuretic. It is a member of tetrazoles, a member of monochlorobenzenes, a sulfonamide and a member of thiophenes.
