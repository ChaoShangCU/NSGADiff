The molecule is the carbohydrate acid derivative anion formed by loss of a proton from the carboxy group of N-acetyl-D-muramoyl-L-alanine; principal microspecies at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion and a carbohydrate acid derivative anion. It is a conjugate base of a N-acetyl-D-muramoyl-L-alanine.
