The molecule is an oxonium betaine that is the conjugate base of cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-glucoside, arising from selective deprotonation of the 5-hydroxy group on the chromene ring; major species at pH 7.3. It is a beta-D-glucoside and an oxonium betaine. It is a conjugate base of a cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-glucoside. It is a conjugate acid of a cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-glucoside(1-).
