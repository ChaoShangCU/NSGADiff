The molecule is a steroid acid anion that is the conjugate base of (2Z,10Z)-3-hydroxy-5,17-dioxo-4,5-secoestra-2,10-diene-3-oic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. A meta-cleavage metabolite in the estrogen degradation pathway. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a (2Z,10Z)-3-hydroxy-5,17-dioxo-4,5-secoestra-2,10-diene-3-oic acid.
