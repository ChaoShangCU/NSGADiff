The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is tetratriacontapentaenoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 16, 19, 22, 25 and 28 (the 16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z,28Z-isomer). It is a tetratriacontapentaenoic acid and an omega-6 fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z,28Z)-tetratriacontapentaenoate.
