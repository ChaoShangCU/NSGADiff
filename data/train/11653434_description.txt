The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone that is a 3alpha-hydroxy epimer of heliangin. Isolated from Eupatorium kiirunense, it exhibits cytotoxicity against human oral epidermoid (KB), cervical epitheloid (Hela) and liver (hepa59T/VGH) carcinoma cells. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an enoate ester, an epoxide and a sesquiterpene lactone. It derives from a tiglic acid.
