The molecule is a withanolide that is withaferin A substituted by a hydroxy group at position 11. Isolated from Physalis longifolia, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a 27-hydroxy steroid, a 4-hydroxy steroid, an 11beta-hydroxy steroid, an enone, an ergostanoid, a primary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a withanolide and an epoxy steroid. It derives from a withaferin A.
