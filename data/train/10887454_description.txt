The molecule is an organic sodium salt which is the disodium salt of clathsterol disulfonic acid. It is obtained from the extract of a sponge Clathria sp. and acts as an inhibitor of HIV-1 reverse transcriptase (RT). It has a role as a metabolite and a HIV-1 reverse transcriptase inhibitor. It is an organic sodium salt and a butyrate ester. It contains a clathsterol(2-).
