The molecule is a linear amino trisaccharide comprising alpha-neuraminyl, N-acetyl-beta-D-galactosaminyl and N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine residues linked sequentially (2->6) and (1->4). It has a role as an epitope. It is a glucosamine oligosaccharide, a galactosamine oligosaccharide and an amino trisaccharide.
