The molecule is a doubly-charged organophosphate oxoanion arising from deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate OH groups of the cationic species 5,10-(methanylylidene)tetrahydromethanopterin; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a 5,10-(methanylylidene)tetrahydromethanopterin.
