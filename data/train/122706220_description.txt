The molecule is a steroidal acyl-CoA(4-) oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 7alpha-hydroxy-3-oxochol-4-en-24-oyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a steroidal acyl-CoA(4-) and a 3-oxo bile acid CoA thioester(4-). It is an enantiomer of a 7beta-hydroxy-3-oxochol-4-en-24-oyl-CoA(4-).
