The molecule is a member of the class of phenothiazines that is 4aH-phenothiazine substituted by dimethylamino groups at positions 3 and 7. It is a member of phenothiazines and a tertiary amino compound. It is a conjugate base of a 3,7-bis(dimethylamino)phenothiazin-5-ium. It derives from a hydride of a 4aH-phenothiazine.
