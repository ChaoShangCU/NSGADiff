The molecule is a cyclic peptide antibiotic that is A47934 lacking the O-sulfo group; it is produced by a strain of Streptomyces toyocaensis. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a cyclic ether, a heterodetic cyclic peptide, an organochlorine compound, a peptide antibiotic and a polyphenol. It is a conjugate acid of an A41030A(1-).
