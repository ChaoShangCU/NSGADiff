The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid that is (4Z,9E,11E,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid having an epoxy group across positions 7 and 8 as well as a hydroxy substituent at the 17S-position. It is an epoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7E,9E,11E,13Z,15E,17S,19Z)-7,8-epoxy-17-hydroxydocosahexaenoate.
