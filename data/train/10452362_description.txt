The molecule is a member of the class of chalcones that is chalcone substituted by methoxy groups at positions 2', 6' and 7 and a dimethylpyrano ring substituted across positions 3' and 4'. Isolated from Pongamia pinnata, it has been found to induce quinone reductase. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a member of chalcones and an enol ether.
