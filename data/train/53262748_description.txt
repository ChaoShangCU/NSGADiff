The molecule is a para-terphenyl that is the 6'-O-desmethyl deriavtive of terphenyllin. Isolated from Penicillium chermesinum, it exhibits inhibitory activity against alpha-glucosidase. It has a role as an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor and a Penicillium metabolite. It is a para-terphenyl, a member of resorcinols and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a terphenyllin.
