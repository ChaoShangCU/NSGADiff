The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-18-ene substituted by an acetyloxy group at position 3 and a carboxy group at position 28. It has been isolated from Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a monocarboxylic acid and an acetate ester. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
