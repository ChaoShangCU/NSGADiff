The molecule is a hydroxydocosapentaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,15E)-docosapentaenoic acid in which the hydroxy group is located at the 17S-position. The 17S-oxygenated product of omega-6-DPA which possesses anti-inflammatory activity. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a hydroxydocosapentaenoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol.
