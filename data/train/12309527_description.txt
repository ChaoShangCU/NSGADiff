The molecule is a hydroxy steroid that consists of 5beta-cardanolide having a double bond at the 20(22)-position as well as hydroxy groups at the 3alpha,- 12beta- and 14beta-positions. It is a 12beta-hydroxy steroid, a 14beta-hydroxy steroid and a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a hydride of a 5beta-cardanolide.
