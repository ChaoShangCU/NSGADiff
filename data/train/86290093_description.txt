The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 14-HPDHE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a HPDHE(1-). It derives from a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 14-HPDHE.
