The molecule is an methoxyisoflavone having methoxy substituents at the 6- and 2'-positions, a hydroxy group at position 7 and a methylenedioxy moiety at the 4'- and 5'-positions. It is a methoxyisoflavone, a member of 7-hydroxyisoflavones and a member of benzodioxoles. It derives from an isoflavone. It is a conjugate acid of a dalpatein(1-).
