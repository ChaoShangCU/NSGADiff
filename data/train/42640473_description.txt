The molecule is an organic heterobicyclic compound that is a lactol isolated from the entomopathogenic fungus Isaria tenuipes and exhibits antimalarial and antineoplastic activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimalarial and an antineoplastic agent. It is a lactol, an organochlorine compound, an organic heterobicyclic compound, an enamide, an enone, a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from a trans-2-dodecenoic acid.
