The molecule is a (5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid in which the two stereocentres at positions 8 and 9 both have S-configuration. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (5Z,8S,9S,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoate. It is an enantiomer of a (5Z,8R,9R,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid.
