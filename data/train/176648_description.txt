The molecule is a methyl N-(2,6-dimethylphenyl)-N-(phenylacetyl)alaninate that is the more active R-enantiomer of benalaxyl. It is a methyl N-(2,6-dimethylphenyl)-N-(phenylacetyl)alaninate, a D-alanine derivative, an acylamino acid fungicide and an anilide fungicide. It is an enantiomer of a (S)-benalaxyl.
