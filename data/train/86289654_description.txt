The molecule is a phosphatidylethanolamine 32:1 obtained by transfer of a proton from the amino to the phosphate group of 1-oleoyl-2-myristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine; major species at pH 7.3. It is a phosphatidylethanolamine 32:1 zwitterion and a tetradecanoate ester. It is a tautomer of a 1-oleoyl-2-myristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine.
