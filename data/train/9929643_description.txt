The molecule is a macrolide that is isolated from the cultured broth of Hypomyces subiculosus and shows antifungal activity and inhibits the growth of some human cancer cells. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent, a fungal metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a macrolide, an enone, an aromatic ether, an epoxide, a diol, a polyketide, a member of phenols and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
