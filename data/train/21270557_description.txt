The molecule is an aldehydic acid anion that is the conjugate base of 9-oxononanoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an EC 6.4.1.2 (acetyl-CoA carboxylase) inhibitor. It is an aldehydic acid anion, an omega-oxo fatty acid anion and a medium-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 9-oxononanoic acid.
