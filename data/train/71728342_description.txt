The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that is a tetrasaccharide derivative of 3,12,17,24-tetrahydroxy-18,20-epoxylanost-9(11)-en-18-one. It is a heterocyclyl sulfate, a tetrasaccharide derivative and a triterpenoid saponin. It is a conjugate acid of a holothurin A4(1-). It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
