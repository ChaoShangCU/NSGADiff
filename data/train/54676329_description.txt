The molecule is a member of the class of quinolones that is the amide obtained from formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-hydroxy-1-isopentyl-2-oxo-1,2-dihydroquinoline-3-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 2-amino-3-methyl-1,3-thiazole. It is a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a monocarboxylic acid amide, a monohydroxyquinoline and a quinolone.
