The molecule is a leukotriene that is leukotriene E4 in which the terminal methyl grop has been oxidised to the corresponding carboxylic acid. It is a L-cysteine thioether, a leukotriene, a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid, a secondary alcohol and a tricarboxylic acid. It derives from a leukotriene E4. It is a conjugate acid of a 20-carboxyleukotriene E4(2-).
