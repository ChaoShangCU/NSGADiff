The molecule is a dihydroxyquinoline that is quinoline substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 8. Isolated from the centipede Scolopendra subspinipes, it exhibits cytotoxic activity against human tumour cell lines. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a quinoline alkaloid, a member of quinolines and a dihydroxyquinoline.
