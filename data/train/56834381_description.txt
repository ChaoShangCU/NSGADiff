The molecule is a cembrane diterpenoid that is cembra-2E,7E,11Z-trien-20,10-olide substituted by a hydroxy group at position 1 and a methoxy group at position 4. It has been isolated from the leaves of natural product found in Croton gratissimus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a cembrane diterpenoid, a diterpene lactone, an ether, a macrocycle and a tertiary alcohol.
