The molecule is a branched amino trisaccharide comprising N-acetyl-D-galactose having a beta-D-galactosyl residue at the 3-position and a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl residue at the 6-position. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
