The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion obtained by transfer of a proton from the phosphate to the amino group of 2-octadecanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion and a lysophosphatidylethanolamine zwitterion 18:0. It is a tautomer of a 2-octadecanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine.
