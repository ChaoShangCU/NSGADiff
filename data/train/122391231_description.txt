The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is alpha-bergamotene in which one of the methyl groups attached to the exocyclic C=C double bond has been oxidised to form the corresponding carboxylic acid. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an insecticide. It is a bridged compound, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and a sesquiterpenoid. It derives from an alpha-bergamotene.
