The molecule is a oxysterol that is cholesterol in which the hydrogen at the 4beta position has been replaced by a hydroxy group. A metabolite of cholesterol formed by the drug-metabolizing enzyme cytochrome P450 3A4, it is one of the major oxysterols in the human circulation. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an oxysterol, a 3beta-sterol, a diol and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid. It derives from a cholesterol.
