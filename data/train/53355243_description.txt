The molecule is a withanolide that is 18,22:23,26-diepoxyergosta-1,4-diene substituted by oxo groups at positions 3, 18 and 26. It has been isolated from a Formosan soft coral Paraminabea acronocephala. It has a role as a coral metabolite. It is a gamma-lactone, an enone, an ergostanoid and a withanolide.
