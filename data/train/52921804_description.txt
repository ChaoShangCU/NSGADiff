The molecule is an omega-6 fatty acid that is tetratriacontanoic acid having four double bonds located at positions 19, 22, 25 and 28 (the 15Z,18Z,21Z,24Z-isomer). It is an omega-6 fatty acid and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (19Z,22Z,25Z,28Z)-tetratriacontatetraenoate.
