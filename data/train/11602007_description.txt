The molecule is an alkaloid of the class of acridone derivatives that is 1,11-dihydrofuro[2,3-c]acridin-6(2H)-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 10 and a prop-1-en-2-yl group at position 2. Isolated from Oriciopsis glaberrima, it exhibits radical scavenging and alpha-glucosidase inhibitory activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor and a radical scavenger. It is a member of acridone derivatives, an alkaloid, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a polyphenol and a cyclic ether.
