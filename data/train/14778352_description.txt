The molecule is a nucleobase analogue that is adenine in which one of the exocyclic amino hydrogens is replaced by a carbamoylmethyl group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a member of 6-aminopurines, an amino acid amide, a glycine derivative, a nucleobase analogue and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from an adenine.
