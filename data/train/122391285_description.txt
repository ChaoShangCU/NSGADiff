The molecule is a leukotriene anion that is the conjugate base of Delta(6)-trans-12-epi-leukotriene B4, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is a leukotriene anion, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a Delta(6)-trans-12-epi-leukotriene B4.
