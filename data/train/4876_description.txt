The molecule is an aromatic amino-acid anion that is the conjugate base of 4-aminobenzoic acid. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite, a plant metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an aromatic amino-acid anion and an aminobenzoate. It derives from a benzoate. It is a conjugate base of a 4-aminobenzoic acid.
