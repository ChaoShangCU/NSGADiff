The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is abieta-8(14),9(11),12-triene substituted at positions 3 and 14 respectively by oxo and hydroxy groups. It is found in Tripterygium wilfordii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, a member of phenols, a carbotricyclic compound, a cyclic terpene ketone and a tricyclic diterpenoid.
