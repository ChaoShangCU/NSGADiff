The molecule is a phorbol ester that consists of 12-deoxyphorbol esterified at position 13 by deca-3,5-dienoic acid (the 3E,5E stereoisomer). It is obtained from the leaves and stems of Excoecaria agallocha and exhibits anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is a phorbol ester, a primary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
