The molecule is a nonclassic icosanoid that is (6Z,8E,10E,14Z,16E)-icosa-6,8,10,14,16-pentaenoic acid carrying two hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 12 as well as an oxo group at position 18. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a nonclassic icosanoid, a long-chain fatty acid, an oxo fatty acid, an enone and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 18-oxoresolvin E1(1-).
