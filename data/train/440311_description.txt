The molecule is a beta-amino acid comprising propionic acid having amino and 4-hydroxyphenyl groups attached at the 3-position. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It derives from a propionic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-amino-3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)propanoate. It is a tautomer of a 3-amino-3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)propanoic acid zwitterion.
