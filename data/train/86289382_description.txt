The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is a dianionic form of (2S)-2-amino-3-oxo-4-(phosphonooxy)butanoic acid. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is an alpha-amino-acid anion and an organophosphate oxoanion. It is a conjugate acid of a (2S)-2-amino-3-oxo-4-(phosphonatooxy)butanoate(3-).
