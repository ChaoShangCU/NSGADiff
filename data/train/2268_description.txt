The molecule is a member of the class of benzotriazines that is 1,2,3-benzotriazine substituted by an oxo group at position 4 and a [(dimethoxyphosphorothioyl)sulfanyl]methyl group at position 3. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, an EC 3.1.1.8 (cholinesterase) inhibitor and an agrochemical. It is an organic thiophosphate, an organothiophosphate insecticide and a member of benzotriazines. It derives from a hydride of a 1,2,3-benzotriazine.
