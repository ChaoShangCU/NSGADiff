The molecule is a branched-chain saturated fatty acid comprising tetradecanoic acid carrying a methyl substituent at positions 10 and 13. It is a marine metabolite isolated from the Caribbean Sponges Calyx podatypa and Agelas dispar. It has a role as an animal metabolite and a marine metabolite. It is a branched-chain saturated fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a methyl-branched fatty acid.
