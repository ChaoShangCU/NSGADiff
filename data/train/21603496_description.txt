The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenod that is 9beta,19-cyclolanost-24-ene which is substituted by hydroxy groups at the 3beta and 28 positions. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a member of phytosterols and a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-hydroxymethyl-4beta-methylsteroid. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
