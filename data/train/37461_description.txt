The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is 2,3,4,4a,8,9,13b,14-octahydro-1H-benzo[6,7]cyclohepta[1,2,3-de]pyrido[2,1-a]isoquinoline substituted at position 3 by both hydroxy and tert-butyl groups. It has a role as a dopaminergic antagonist. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound and an amino alcohol.
