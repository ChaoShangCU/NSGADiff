The molecule is a germacranolide that is (3aR,5E,9E,11aS)-2,3,3a,4,7,8,11,11a-octahydrocyclodeca[b]furan substituted by an oxo group, methylidene group, methyl group and methyl group at positions 2,3,6 and 10, respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It derives from an 8alpha-hydroxygermacra-1(10),4,11(13)-trien-12-oate.
