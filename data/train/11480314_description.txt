The molecule is an organosulfur heterocyclic compound isolated from the ascidian Lissoclinum badium. It has been shown to exhibit cytotoxicity against human cancer cell lines. It has a role as an animal metabolite, a marine metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an aromatic ether, an aryl sulfide, a tertiary amino compound, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and an organosulfur heterocyclic compound.
