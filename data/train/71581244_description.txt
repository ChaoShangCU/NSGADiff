The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of (9Z)-heptadecenoic acid. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA, an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA. It is a conjugate acid of a (9Z)-heptadecenoyl-CoA(4-).
