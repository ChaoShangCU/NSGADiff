The molecule is an N(2)-acyl-L-glutamine that has 3-hydroxy-3-methylhexanoyl as the N(2)-acyl group. It is a N(2)-acyl-L-glutamine, a primary carboxamide, a tertiary alcohol and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from a 3-hydroxy-3-methylhexanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N(2)-(3-hydroxy-3-methylhexanoyl)-L-glutaminate.
