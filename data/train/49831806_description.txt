The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is heptan-3-ol substituted by a 3,4-dihydroxyphenyl group at position 1 and a 4-hydroxyphenyl group at position 7 (the 3R-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma kwangsiensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a member of catechols and a secondary alcohol.
