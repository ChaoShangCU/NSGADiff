The molecule is a defensive steroid lactone that is produced by males of the species Photinus pyralis. It is a 5beta-hydroxy steroid, a steroid ester, a 14beta-hydroxy steroid, an 11alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 12-oxo steroid, a member of 2-pyranones, a steroid lactone and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a hydride of a 5beta-bufanolide.
