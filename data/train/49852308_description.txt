The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside triphosphate oxoanion arising from deprotonation of three of the four triphosphate OH groups of 2'-deoxyuridine 5'-triphosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a dUTP. It is a conjugate acid of a dUTP(4-).
