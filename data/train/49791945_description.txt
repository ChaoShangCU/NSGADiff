The molecule is trianion of [1-(5-phosphoribosyl)imidazol-4-yl]acetic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate groups; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a [1-(5-phosphoribosyl)imidazol-4-yl]acetic acid.
