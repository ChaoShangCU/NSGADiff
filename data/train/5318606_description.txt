The molecule is a myricetin O-glucoside that is myricetin with a beta-D-glucosyl residue attached at position 3. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a myricetin O-glucoside, a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a pentahydroxyflavone. It derives from a beta-D-glucose. It is a conjugate acid of a myricetin 3-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside(1-).
