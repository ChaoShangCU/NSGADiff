The molecule is a secondary ammonium ion resulting from the protonation of the amino group of solasodine. The major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an anticonvulsant, an antifungal agent, an antiinfective agent, an antioxidant, an antipyretic, an antispermatogenic agent and an apoptosis inducer. It is a conjugate acid of a solasodine.
