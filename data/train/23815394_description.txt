The molecule is a lignan that is tetrahydrofuran substituted at positions 2, 3 and 4 by 4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl, hydroxymethyl and 4-hydroxy-3-methoxybenzyl groups respectively (the 2R,3S,4S-diastereomer). It is a lignan, a member of oxolanes and a member of phenols. It is an enantiomer of a (+)-lariciresinol.
