The molecule is an olefinic compound that is 2,3-dimethylbuta-1,3-diene substituted by a 4-hydroxyphenyl and a 1,3-benzodioxol-5-yl group. It has been isolated from Terminalia sericea. It has a role as an antibacterial agent and a plant metabolite. It is a member of benzodioxoles, a member of phenols and an olefinic compound.
