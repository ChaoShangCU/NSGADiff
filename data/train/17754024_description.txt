The molecule is a 2,3-dihydro-3-hydroxyanthranilic acid in which the stereocentre attached to the amino group has R-configuration, whilst that attached to the hydroxy group has S-configuration. It is an enantiomer of a (2S,3R)-2,3-dihydro-3-hydroxyanthranilic acid. It is a tautomer of a (2R,3S)-2,3-dihydro-3-hydroxyanthranilic acid zwitterion.
