The molecule is a triterpene glycoside obtained from the New Zealand and South Australian sea cucumber Australostichopus mollis. It has a role as a marine metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a lactone, an olefinic compound, an oligosaccharide sulfate, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a tetrasaccharide derivative and a triterpenoid saponin. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
