The molecule is an amino disaccharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 3 has been converted into the corresponding beta-D-glucopyranoside. It is an amino disaccharide, a member of acetamides and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine.
