The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of (2E,4E,8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosapentaenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a 6-saturated-trans,trans-2,4-dienoyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (2E,4E,8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosapentaenoyl-CoA.
