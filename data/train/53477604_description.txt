The molecule is a three-membered deoxy oligosaccharide which has a 6-deoxy-N-acetyl-D-glucosamine unit at the reducing end with an alpha-D-galactosyl-(1->3)-beta-D-galactosyl group attached at the 4-position. It is an amino trisaccharide, a deoxy oligosaccharide derivative and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
