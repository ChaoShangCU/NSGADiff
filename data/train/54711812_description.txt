The molecule is a member of the class of pyrrolidin-2-ones isolated from the fungal fermentation broth of Chaetomium globosum and shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against chemokine receptor CCR-5. It has a role as a Chaetomium metabolite and a chemokine receptor 5 antagonist. It is a carbobicyclic compound, an enol, a monocarboxylic acid, a member of pyrrolidin-2-ones and a member of octahydronaphthalenes.
