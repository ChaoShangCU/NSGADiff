The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is chenodeoxycholic acid having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 3. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a dicarboxylic acid. It derives from a chenodeoxycholic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a chenodeoxycholate 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(2-).
