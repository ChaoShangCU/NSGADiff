The molecule is a 1,3-diazepanone ring with two 4-(hydroxymethyl)benzyl groups as substituents at positions N-1 and N-4, two benzyl groups at C-4 and C-7, and two hydroxy groups at C-5 and C-6 respectively. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is a diazepanone and a member of ureas.
