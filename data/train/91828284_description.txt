The molecule is an (S)-3-hydroxyacyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3S)-3-hydroxyoleoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain (3S)-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3S)-3-hydroxyoleoyl-CoA.
