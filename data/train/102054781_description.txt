The molecule is a methyl glycoside that consists of methyl 3-(2-glycerylphospho)-beta-D-galactoside having an alpha-L-rhamnosyl residue at the 2-position. The core disaccharide from the Streptococcus pneumoniae type 23F capsular polysaccharide antigen functionalized with a phosphoglycerol side chain. It is a beta-D-galactoside, a disaccharide derivative and a methyl glycoside.
