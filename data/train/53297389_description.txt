The molecule is a methyl ester derived from (1R,2S)-2,5,7-trihydroxy-2-methyl-4,6,11-trioxo-1,2,3,4,6,11-hexahydrotetracene-1-carboxylic acid. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a member of tetracenes, a methyl ester, a tertiary alcohol, a polyketide, a member of tetracenequinones and a member of p-quinones. It is a conjugate acid of a nogalaviketone(1-).
