The molecule is a dihydroxyflavone that is vitexin having a beta-L-rhamnosyl residue attached at the 2''-position of the glucitol moiety and a methyl group attached at the O-7 position of the chromene. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a dihydroxyflavone, a disaccharide derivative and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a vitexin. It is a conjugate acid of a 7-O-methylvitexin 2''-O-beta-L-rhamnoside(1-).
