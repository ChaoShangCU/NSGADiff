The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid that is oxoacetic acid substituted by a (2,6-dimethylphenyl)(1H-pyrazol-1-ylmethyl)amino group at position 2. It is metabolite of the herbicide metazachlor. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of pyrazoles, a monocarboxylic acid and an aromatic amide.
