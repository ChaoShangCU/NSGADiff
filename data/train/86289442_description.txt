The molecule is a zwitterion obtained by transfer of a proton from the 5-hydroxy to the tertiary amino group of aclacinomycin Y. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as an antimicrobial agent. It is a conjugate base of an aclacinomycin Y(1+). It is a tautomer of an aclacinomycin Y.
