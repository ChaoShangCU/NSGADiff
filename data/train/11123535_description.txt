The molecule is a 14-membered macrolide isolated from the Serratia liquefaciens and exhibits anti-hyperlipidemic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antilipemic drug. It is a macrolide, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a cyclic ether, an acetate ester, a secondary alcohol, an organochlorine compound, an organic heterobicyclic compound and a bridged compound.
