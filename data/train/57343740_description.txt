The molecule is a secondary carboxamide resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-(difluoromethyl)-1-methylpyrazole-4-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 7-fluoro-1,1,3-trimethylindan-4-amine. It is a member of pyrazoles, a secondary carboxamide, a member of indanes and an organofluorine compound.
