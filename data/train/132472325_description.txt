The molecule is a dihydroxydocosapentaenoic acid (DiHDPE) that is (8Z,15E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,13,15,19-pentaenoic acid carrying two hydroxy substituents at positions 7 and 17. An intermediate of specialized proresolving mediators It has a role as a specialised pro-resolving mediator and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a 7,17-dihydroxy-(8Z,15E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,13,15,19-pentaenoate.
