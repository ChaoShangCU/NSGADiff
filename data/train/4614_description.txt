The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid that is a propionic acid derivative having a 4,5-diphenyl-1,3-oxazol-2-yl substituent at position 3. It is non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug commonly used to relieve the pain and inflammatory responses associated with osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis. It has a role as a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug and an analgesic. It is a member of 1,3-oxazoles and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a propionic acid.
