The molecule is an aromatic ether in which the oxygen atom is connected to a guaiacylglyceryl and a vanillyl group. It has been isolated from the stems of Sinocalamus affinis. It is a triol, an aromatic ether, a member of benzoic acids and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a (+)-(7S,8S)-guaiacylglycerol and a vanillic acid.
