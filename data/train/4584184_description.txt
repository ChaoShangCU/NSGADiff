The molecule is the 2-oxo monocarboxylic acid anion derived from 4-methylthio-2-oxobutanoic acid. The major microspecies at pH 7.3, it is formed from L-methionine via the action of methionine transaminase. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It derives from a butyrate. It is a conjugate base of a 4-methylthio-2-oxobutanoic acid.
