The molecule is a member of the class of rifamycins that is rifamycin itself in which the hydroxy hydrogen at position 21 has been replaced by a phosphono group. It is a member of rifamycins, a N-iminopiperazine, a N-methylpiperazine, a cyclic ketal, a hydrazone, a semisynthetic derivative and a phosphate monoester. It derives from a rifampicin. It is a conjugate acid of a 21-phosphorifampicin(2-).
