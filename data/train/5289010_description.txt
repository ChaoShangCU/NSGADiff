The molecule is a branched amino tetrasaccharide consisting of beta-D-glucose at the reducing end having an N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosaminyl-(1->3)-[alpha-L-fucosyl-(1->2)]-beta-D-galactosyl moiety attached at the 4-position. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
