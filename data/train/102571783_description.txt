The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 5,20-DiHETE, arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a dihydroxyicosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 5,20-DiHETE.
