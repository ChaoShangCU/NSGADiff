The molecule is a ceramide phosphoethanolamine (38:2) in which the acyl and the sphingoid base specified is (15Z)-tetracosenoyl and tetradecasphing-4-enine respectively. It is a ceramide phosphoethanolamine (38:2) and a N-acyltetradecasphingosine-1-phosphoethanolamine. It derives from a (15Z)-tetracosenoic acid.
