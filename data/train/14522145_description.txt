The molecule is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid consisting of (5Z,9E,14Z)-icosa-5,9,14-trienoic acid having additional (8R)-hydroxy- and (11S,12S)-epoxy groups. It derives from a (5Z,9E,14Z)-icosa-5,9,14-trienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an (8R)-hydroxy-(11S,12S)-epoxyicosa-(5Z,9E,14Z)-trienoate.
