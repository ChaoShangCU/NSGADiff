The molecule is beta-D-Galp3S-(1->4)-[alpha-L-Fucp-(1->3)]-D-Glcp with beta configuration at the glucose anomeric centre. A trisaccharide derivative that consists of beta-D-glucose having an alpha-L-fucosyl residue attached at position 3 and a 3-sulfated beta-D-galactosyl residue attached at position 4.
