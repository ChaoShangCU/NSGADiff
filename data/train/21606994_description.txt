The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose and beta-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->2) glycosidic linkage. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a beta-D-glucose and a N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine.
