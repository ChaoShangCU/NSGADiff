The molecule is a polycyclic cage that is the methyl ester derivative of platensimycin A1. It is isolated from Streptomyces platensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, a polycyclic cage, a secondary alcohol, a member of resorcinols, an aromatic amide, a methyl ester and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from a platensimycin A1.
