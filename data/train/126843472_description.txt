The molecule is a steroid glucuronide anion that is the conjugate base of chenodeoxycholic acid 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid functions; major species at pH 7.3. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronate, a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a chenodeoxycholic acid 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide).
