The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate OH groups of pyridinium-3,5-biscarboxylic acid mononucleotide; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a dicarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a pyridinium-3,5-biscarboxylic acid mononucleotide.
