The molecule is a monohydroxyflavone that is flavone substituted by a hydroxy group at position 2' and methoxy groups at positions 7, 4' and 5'. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a trimethoxyflavone and a monohydroxyflavone. It derives from a flavone.
