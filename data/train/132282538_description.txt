The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (4Z,8E,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14-hydroperoxydocosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (4Z,8E,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14-hydroperoxydocosahexaenoic acid.
