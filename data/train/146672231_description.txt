The molecule is a polyketide macrolactam containing a tetramic acid (pyrrolidine-2,4-dione) ring system. It is the 3-hydroxyl derivative of ikarugamycin and isolated from Micromonospora sp. K310. It has a role as a marine metabolite, a bacterial metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is an azamacrocycle, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an enone, a lactam and a polyketide. It derives from an ikarugamycin.
