The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-sn-glycerol)(1-) in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as hexadecanoyl (palmitoyl) and 9Z-octadecenoyl (oleoyl) respectively; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 1-hexadecanoyl-2-[(9Z)-octadec-9-enoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-sn-glycerol).
