The molecule is an amino trisaccharide composed of 2-acetamido-4-O-sulfo-beta-D-galactopyranosyl, 2-acetamido-beta-D-glucopyranosyl, and alpha-D-mannopyranose residues coupled in sequence by (1->4) and (1->2) glycosidic bonds. It is an oligosaccharide sulfate, an amino trisaccharide and a member of acetamides.
