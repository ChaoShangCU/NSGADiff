The molecule is a member of the class of oxindoles that is 5-chlorooxindole in which the two hydrogens at position 3 are replaced by a (3,5-dimethylpyrrol-2-yl)methylidene group. It has a role as a vascular endothelial growth factor receptor antagonist. It is a member of pyrroles, a member of oxindoles and an organochlorine compound.
