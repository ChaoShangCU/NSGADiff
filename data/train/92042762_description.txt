The molecule is a beta-lactam that is 7-oxo-1-azabicyclo[3.2.0]heptane-2-carboxylic acid carring additional (2-aminoethyl)sulfanyl and ethyl substituents at positions 3 and 6 respectively. An intermediate in the biosynthesis of carbapenem. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a beta-lactam, an aliphatic sulfide, a monocarboxylic acid, an organic heterobicyclic compound and a primary amino compound.
