The molecule is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the two hydroxy substituents are located at the 14R- and 21R-positions. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14R,16Z,19Z,21R)-dihydroxydocosahexaenoate. It is an enantiomer of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z,21S)-dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid.
