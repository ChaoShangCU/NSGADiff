The molecule is a steroid glucuronide anion that is the conjugate base of 16-epiestriol 16-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronate, a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 16-epiestriol 16-O-(beta-D-glucuronide).
