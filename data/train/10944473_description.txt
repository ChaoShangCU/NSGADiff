The molecule is a furofuran that is (3aR,6aR)-hexahydrofuro[3,4-b]furan substituted by an oxo group at positions 2 and 4, methylene group at position 3 and by an hexyl group at position 6. It is a metabolite isolated from several species of fungi. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a fungal metabolite. It is a gamma-lactone and a furofuran.
