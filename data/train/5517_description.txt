The molecule is a 1,8-naphthyridine derivative that is 4-oxo-1,4-dihydro-1,8-naphthyridine-3-carboxylic acid bearing additional 2,4-difluorophenyl, fluoro and 3-aminopyrrolidin-1-yl substituents at positions 1, 6 and 7 respectively. It is a 1,8-naphthyridine derivative, an amino acid, a monocarboxylic acid, an organofluorine compound, an aminopyrrolidine, a tertiary amino compound, a primary amino compound and a quinolone antibiotic. It is a conjugate base of a 1-[6-carboxy-8-(2,4-difluorophenyl)-3-fluoro-5-oxo-5,8-dihydro-1,8-naphthyridin-2-yl]pyrrolidin-3-aminium.
