The molecule is a ribose monophosphate that is D-ribofuranose 5-phosphate in which the anomeric OH group is replaced by a 4-hydroxy phenyl group (the beta-D-anomer). It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound and a ribose monophosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-(5-O-phosphonato-beta-D-ribofuranosyl)phenol(2-).
