The molecule is an azaphilone that is 1H-isochromene-6,8(7H,8aH)-dione substituted by a prop-1-en-1-yl group at position 3, hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 8a, a methyl group at position 7 and a (3,4,6-trihydroxy-2-methylbenzoyl)oxy group at position 7. It has been isolated from Penicillium purpurogenum. It has a role as an antiviral agent and a Penicillium metabolite. It is an azaphilone, a benzoate ester, an enone, a member of isochromenes, a polyketide and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
