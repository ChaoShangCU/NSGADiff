The molecule is an N-glycosyl compound with formula C12H15N2O11P that is a common intermediate in the biosynthesis of nikkomycins and polyoxins by bacteria. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, a furopyran, a 5-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a secondary alcohol, a pyrimidone and a monoalkyl phosphate. It derives from a uridine 5'-monophosphate.
