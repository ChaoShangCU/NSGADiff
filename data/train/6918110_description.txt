The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C35H49NO11 that is isolated from several Aconitum species. It has a role as a plant metabolite, a phytotoxin, an antifeedant, a human urinary metabolite and a xenobiotic. It is an acetate ester, an aromatic ether, a benzoate ester, a bridged compound, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a tertiary amino compound, a secondary alcohol and a tertiary alcohol. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
