The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 18-hydroxyoleic acid. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA, a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA, an unsaturated fatty acyl-CoA and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 18-hydroxyoleoyl-CoA(4-).
