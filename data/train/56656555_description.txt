The molecule is a cembrane diterpenoid that is cembra-2E,8-(19),11Z-trien-20,10-olide substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 4 and 7. It has been isolated from the leaves of Croton gratissimus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a cembrane diterpenoid, a diterpene lactone, a macrocycle and a triol.
