The molecule is a dolichyl diphosphooligosaccharide in which the oligosaccharide moiety is the Man3GlcNAc2 branched pentasaccharide alpha-D-Man-(1->3)-[alpha-D-Man-(1->6)]-beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc. It is a conjugate acid of an alpha-D-Man-(1->3)-[alpha-D-Man-(1->6)]-beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc(PP-Dol)(2-).
