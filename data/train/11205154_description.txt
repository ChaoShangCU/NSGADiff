The molecule is a beta-triketone that is 2-benzoylcyclohexane-1,3-dione in which the benzoyl group is substituted at positions 2, 3, and 4 by chlorine, (tetrahydrofuran-2-ylmethoxy)methyl, and methylsulfonyl groups, respectively. It is a sulfone, a member of monochlorobenzenes, an ether, an aromatic ketone, a member of cyclohexanones, a member of oxolanes and a beta-triketone.
