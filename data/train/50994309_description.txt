The molecule is a tirucallane triterpenoid that is (13alpha,14beta,17alpha,20S)-lanosta-7,24-diene substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 3 and a methoxy group at position 23. It has been isolated from the stem and stem barks of Cornus walteri. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an ether, a tirucallane triterpenoid and a secondary alcohol.
