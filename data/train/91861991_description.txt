The molecule is a glycosylglucose derivative consisting of beta-D-galactopyranose and 2-amino-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->3) glycosidic bond. It is a glycosylglucose derivative and a primary amino compound. It derives from a 2-amino-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose and a beta-D-galactose.
