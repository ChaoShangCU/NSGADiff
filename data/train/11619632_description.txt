The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3, 6 and 7, a prenyl group at position 2 and a geranyl group at position 5. Isolated from Cratoxylum cochinchinense, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a polyphenol and a member of xanthones.
