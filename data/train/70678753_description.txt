The molecule is a benzothiazine that is 2H-1,4-benzothiazin-3(4H)-one substituted by a hydroxy and a hydroxymethyl group at positions 7 and 5 respectively. It is an antibacterial agent from Ampelomyces. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is a member of phenols, a member of benzyl alcohols, a lactam and a benzothiazine.
