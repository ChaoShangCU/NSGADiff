The molecule is a HDoHE obtained by hydroxylation at position 2 of all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and a homoallylic alcohol. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-22-hydroxydocosahexaenoate.
