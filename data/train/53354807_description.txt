The molecule is a member of the class of dibenzofurans that is 3,6,9-trimethoxydibenzo[b,d]furan-2-ol substituted by a 4-hydroxy-3-(3-methylbut-2-en-1-yl)phenyl substituent at position 3. It has been isolated from Aspergillus taichungensis. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an aromatic ether, a member of dibenzofurans and a member of phenols.
