The molecule is a member of the class of thioureas that is 3,4-dihydropyrimidine-2(1H)-thione substituted by a 3-methoxyphenyl group at position 4, a methyl group at position 6 and a ethoxycarbonyl group at position 5. It is a pyrimidinecarboxylate ester, an aromatic ether, a member of thioureas and an ethyl ester.
