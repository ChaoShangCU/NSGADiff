The molecule is a macrolide that is a 14-memebered macrocycle fused to a 1,3-dihydroxybenzene. Isolated from Fungi, it exhibits inhibitory activity against NF-kappaB. It has a role as a NF-kappaB inhibitor and a fungal metabolite. It is a macrolide, a member of resorcinols, a secondary alcohol and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
