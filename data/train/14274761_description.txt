The molecule is a member of the class of propane-1,3-diols that is propane-1,3-diol substituted at position 1 by a 4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl and at position 2 by a 4-[(1E)-3-hydroxyprop-1-en-1-yl]-2-methoxyphenoxy group (the 1R,2R stereoisomer). It is isolated from the whole plant of Lepisorus contortus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a member of propane-1,3-diols, a phenylpropanoid, a guaiacyl lignin and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a glycerol.
