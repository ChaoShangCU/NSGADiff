The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3Z,6Z)-dodecadienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3Z,6Z)-dodecadienoyl-CoA.
