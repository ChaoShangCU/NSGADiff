The molecule is an L-lysyl ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of L-lysine with the 3'-hydroxy group of AMP. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is an adenosine 5'-phosphate, a L-lysyl ester and a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate.
