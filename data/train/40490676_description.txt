The molecule is a (5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoate obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group of (5Z,8R,9R,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a (5Z,8R,9R,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid. It is an enantiomer of a (5Z,8S,9S,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoate.
