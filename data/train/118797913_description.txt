The molecule is an L-lysine derivative that is the phosphoramidate obtained by formal condensation of the phosphate group of AMP with the side-chain amino group of L-lysine. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a L-lysine derivative, a nucleotide-amino acid and an organic phosphoramidate. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate.
