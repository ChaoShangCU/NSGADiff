The molecule is a steroid acid consisting of 6-{6-[3-(thiopropionyl)aminohexanoyl]amino}hexanoic acid having a progesterone-4-yl group attached to the sulfur. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 20-oxo steroid, a steroid acid, a steroid sulfide and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a progesterone.
