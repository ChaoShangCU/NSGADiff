The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid with formula C30H40O6, originally isolated from Tripterygium hypoglaucum. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a carbopolycyclic compound, an enone, an oxo monocarboxylic acid, an aromatic primary alcohol, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a cyclic terpene ketone and a member of guaiacols.
