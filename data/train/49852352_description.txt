The molecule is a 4-oxo monocarboxylic acid anion that is 2,3-dihydro-3-oxo tautomer of 3-hydroxyanthranilate and results from the removal of a proton from the carboxylic acid group of 2,3-dihydro-3-oxoanthranilic acid. It is a 4-oxo monocarboxylic acid anion and an oxo monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2,3-dihydro-3-oxoanthranilic acid. It is a tautomer of a 3-hydroxyanthranilate.
