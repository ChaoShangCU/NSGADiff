The molecule is a linear amino pentasaccharide comprising N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine, N-acetyl-beta-D-galactosamine, alpha-D-galactose, beta-D-galactose and beta-D-glucose residues linked sequentially (1->3), (1->3), (1->4) and (1->4). It is an amino pentasaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
