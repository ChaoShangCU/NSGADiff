The molecule is an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound that is ajmaline which is lacking the methyl substituent attached to the nitrogen of the dihydroindole moiety. It is a tertiary amino compound, a secondary amino compound, a secondary alcohol, an indole alkaloid, a bridged compound, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound and a hemiaminal. It derives from an ajmaline. It is a conjugate base of a norajmaline(1+).
