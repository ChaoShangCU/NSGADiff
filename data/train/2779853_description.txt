The molecule is a member of the class of 1-benzothiophenes that is 1-benzothiophene-1,1-dioxide substituted at position 6 by a nitro group. Used as a radiosensitising agent for esophageal squamous cell carcinoma. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, a STAT3 inhibitor and a radiosensitizing agent. It is a member of 1-benzothiophenes, a C-nitro compound and a sulfone.
