The molecule is a sesterterpenoid isolated from Red Sea sponge Diacarnus erythraeanus and has been shown to exhibit anti-HSV-1 activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HSV-1 agent. It is a sesterterpenoid, a terpene ketone, an organic peroxide, a methyl ketone and a dioxo monocarboxylic acid.
