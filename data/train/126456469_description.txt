The molecule is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the two hydroxy substituents are located at the 14S- and 22-positions. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a secondary allylic alcohol, a dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoate. It is an enantiomer of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14R,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid.
