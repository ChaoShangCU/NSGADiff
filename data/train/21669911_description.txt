The molecule is a macrolide antibiotic that is tylactone having a beta-D-mycaminosyl residue attached at position 5. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a macrolide antibiotic, a monosaccharide derivative and an enone. It derives from a tylactone. It is a conjugate base of a 5-O-beta-D-mycaminosyltylactone(1+).
