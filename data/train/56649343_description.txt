The molecule is a withanolide that is withaferin A substituted by a hydroxy group at position 19. Isolated from Physalis longifolia, it exhibits antineoplastic activitiy. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a 27-hydroxy steroid, a 4-hydroxy steroid, an enone, an ergostanoid, a primary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a withanolide, a 19-hydroxy steroid and an epoxy steroid. It derives from a withaferin A.
