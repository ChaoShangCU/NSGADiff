The molecule is a fatty acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of octadecanoic acid with the hydroxy group of 9-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a fatty acid ester and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an octadecanoic acid and a 9-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 9-(octadecanoyloxy)octadecanoate.
