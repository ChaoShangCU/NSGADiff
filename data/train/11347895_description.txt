The molecule is an aminobenzoic acid that is anthranilic acid substituted at position 5 by a (1-methoxy-2-methylindolizin-3-yl)carbonyl group. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a fibroblast growth factor receptor antagonist. It is a member of indolizines, an aminobenzoic acid, an aromatic ether and an aromatic ketone. It derives from an anthranilic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-amino-5-[(1-methoxy-2-methylindolizin-3-yl)carbonyl]benzoate.
