The molecule is an anthracycline that is aklavinone having an alpha-L-rhodosaminyl residue attached at position 4 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, an antineoplastic agent and a metabolite. It is an anthracycline, an aminoglycoside, a deoxy hexoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a member of tetracenequinones, a methyl ester, a polyketide, a member of phenols and a tertiary alcohol. It derives from an aklavinone. It is a conjugate base of an aclacinomycin T(1+). It is a tautomer of an aclacinomycin T zwitterion.
