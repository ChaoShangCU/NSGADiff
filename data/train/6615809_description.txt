The molecule is an organic disulfide composed of two molecules of 2-{[2-(1H-indol-3-yl)ethyl]amino}ethane-1-thiol linked together via a disulfide bond. It is a glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) activator which reduces oxidative stress in cells and zebrafish. It is an organic disulfide, a member of indoles and a secondary amino compound.
