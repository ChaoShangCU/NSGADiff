The molecule is the (3-chloro-2-hydroxypropyl) (2,3-dihydroxypropyl) diether of bisphenol A; a small molecule that inhibits transactivation of the AR amino-terminal domain (NTD). It has a role as an androgen antagonist. It is an organochlorine compound and a diether. It derives from a bisphenol A and a glycerol.
