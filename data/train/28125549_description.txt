The molecule is conjugate base of (3R)-3-hydroxy-L-aspartic acid arising from deprotonation of the two carboxy groups and protonation of the amino group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a L-alpha-amino acid anion and a dicarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (3R)-3-hydroxy-L-aspartic acid. It is an enantiomer of a (3S)-3-hydroxy-D-aspartate(1-).
