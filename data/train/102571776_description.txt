The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 5-hydroperoxy-15-HETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a hydroperoxy(hydroxy)icosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 5-hydroperoxy-15-HETE.
