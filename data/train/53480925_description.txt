The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl group is specified as oleoyl. It is a lysophosphatidylethanolamine 18:1 and a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a tautomer of a 2-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
