The molecule is an anthraquinone that is knipholone in which the O-methyl group is replaced by a beta-D-glucopyranosyl group. It is isolated from the roots of Bulbine frutescens and exhibits trypanocidal and antiplasmodial activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an antiplasmodial drug and a trypanocidal drug. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a polyphenol, a methyl ketone, an aromatic ketone and a dihydroxyanthraquinone. It derives from a knipholone.
