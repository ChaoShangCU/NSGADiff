The molecule is a mannooligosaccharide derivative consisting of a D-mannosyl residue beta-linked to a 5-aminopentyl group and which carries an alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->2)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->2)-alpha-D-mannosyl unit linked (1->3) and an alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->2)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->3)-alpha-D-mannosyl unit linked (1->6).
