The molecule is a member of the class of pterocarpan that is a cis-pterocarpan substituted by a hydroxy group at position 9, a 3-hydroxy-3-methylbutyl group at position 8, a methoxy group at position 1 and a dimethylpyran ring fused across positions 2 and 3. It has been isolated from Glycyrrhiza uralensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of pterocarpans, a member of phenols and an aromatic ether.
