The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-13,14-epoxydocosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-13,14-epoxydocosapentaenoic acid.
