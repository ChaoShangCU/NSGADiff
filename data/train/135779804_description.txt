The molecule is an N-nitro compound consisting of 2-nitroguanidine having a (2-chloro-1,3-thiazol-5-yl)methyl group at position 1 and a methyl group at position 3. It has a role as a nicotinic acetylcholine receptor agonist, a neonicotinoid insectide, an environmental contaminant and a xenobiotic. It is a 2-nitroguanidine derivative, a member of 1,3-thiazoles and an organochlorine compound. It derives from a 2-nitroguanidine and a 2-chlorothiazole.
