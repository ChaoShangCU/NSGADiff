The molecule is an oxysterol that is cholesterol which is substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 24S and 27 and has S-configuration at position 25. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an oxysterol, a cholestanoid, a 3beta-sterol, a 24-hydroxy steroid, a 26-hydroxy steroid and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid.
