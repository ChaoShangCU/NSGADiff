The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 21-HDoHE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxydocosahexaenoate, an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 21-HDoHE.
