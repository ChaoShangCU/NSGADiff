The molecule is an amino disaccharide that is alpha-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 3 has been converted into the corresponding 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranosyl derivative. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and an alpha-D-glucose.
