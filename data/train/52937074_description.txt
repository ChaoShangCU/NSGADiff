The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is podocarpa-8,11,13-triene substituted by a propan-2-yl group at position 14 at an epoxy group across positions 11 and 17. It is isolated from Podocarpus latifolius. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, a cyclic ether and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
