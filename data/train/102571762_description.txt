The molecule is a prostaglandin H that consists of prostaglandin H1 bearing an additional hydroxy substituent at position 19. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a bridged compound, an olefinic compound, an organic peroxide, an oxylipin, a prostaglandins H and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a prostaglandin H1. It is a conjugate acid of a 19-hydroxyprostaglandin H1(1-).
