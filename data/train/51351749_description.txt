The molecule is a dolichyl diphosphooligosaccharide in which the oligosaccharide moiety is the Man1GlcNAc2 linear trisaccharide beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc. It is a dolichyl diphosphooligosaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide. It is a conjugate acid of a beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc(PP-Dol)(2-).
