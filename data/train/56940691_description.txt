The molecule is a cholestanoid that is 7-dehydrocholesterol carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at position 24 (24S-stereoisomer). It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a cholestanoid, a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid, a 24-hydroxy steroid and a C27-steroid. It derives from a cholesta-5,7-dien-3beta-ol.
