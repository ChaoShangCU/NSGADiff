The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-(difluoromethyl)-1-methylpyrazole-4-carboxylic acid with the amino group of N-methoxy-1-(2,4,6-trichlorophenyl)propan-2-amine. It is a trichlorobenzene, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an aromatic amide, a member of pyrazoles and an organofluorine compound.
