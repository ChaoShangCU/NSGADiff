The molecule is a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid that is (6Z,8E,14Z)-icosatrienoic acid carrying hydroxy and oxo substituents at positions 5 and 12 respectively. It is a nonclassic icosanoid, a long-chain fatty acid, an oxo fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from a leukotriene B4. It is a conjugate acid of a 10,11-dihydro-12-oxoleukotriene B4(1-).
