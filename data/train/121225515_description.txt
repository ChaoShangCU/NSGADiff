The molecule is a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate having 5,6-dihydroxy-5,6-dihydrocytosine as the nucleobase. It is a metabolite produced by the bacterium Mycoplasma genitalium. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate and a nucleoside monophosphate analogue. It derives from a 2'-deoxycytosine 5'-monophosphate.
