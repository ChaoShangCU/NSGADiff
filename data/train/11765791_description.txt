The molecule is a dibenzodioxonine isolated from the culture broth of Penicillium asperosporum that acts as an acyl-CoA:cholesterol acyltransferase inhibitor. It has a role as an EC 2.3.1.26 (sterol O-acyltransferase) inhibitor and a Penicillium metabolite. It is an acetate ester, an aromatic ether, a member of benzophenones, a dibenzodioxonine, a lactone, a polyphenol and a secondary alcohol.
