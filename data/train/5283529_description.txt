The molecule is a 1-acyl-2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate in which the 1-acyl group is specified as stearoyl (octadecanoyl). It is a 1-acyl-2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate and a phosphatidic acid 38:4. It derives from an octadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-stearoyl-2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-).
