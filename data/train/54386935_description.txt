The molecule is a nitramine that is N,N-bis(2-hydroxypropyl)nitrosamine in which the nitroso group has undergone formal oxidation to the corresponding nitro group. A potent pancreatic carcinogen. It has a role as a carcinogenic agent. It is a diol, a secondary alcohol and a nitramine. It derives from a N,N-bis(2-hydroxypropyl)nitrosamine.
