The molecule is a phosphatidylethanolamine 32:2 obtained by transfer of a proton from the amino to the phosphate group of 1,2-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine. It is an organic molecular entity and a phosphatidylethanolamine 32:2. It is a tautomer of a 1,2-di-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine.
