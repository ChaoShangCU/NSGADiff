The molecule is an indole alkaloid cation that is the conjugate acid of (3R)-1,2-didehydro-3-hydroxy-16-methoxy-2,3-dihydrotabersonine, obtained by protonation of the tertiary amino group. Major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate acid of a (3R)-1,2-didehydro-3-hydroxy-16-methoxy-2,3-dihydrotabersonine.
