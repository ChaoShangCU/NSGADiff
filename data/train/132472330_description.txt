The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-17-oxodocosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, an oxo fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-17-oxodocosapentaenoic acid.
