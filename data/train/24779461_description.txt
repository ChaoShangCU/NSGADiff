The molecule is a 1-O-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine in which the 1-acyl group is (9Z)-hexadecenoyl (palmitoleoyl). It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a human metabolite. It is a lysophosphatidylcholine 16:1, a 1-O-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine and a [(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine. It derives from a palmitoleic acid.
