The molecule is a 9,10-epoxyoctadecanoate that is the conjugate base of (9S,10R)-epoxyoctadecanoic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a (9S,10R)-epoxyoctadecanoic acid. It is an enantiomer of a (9R,10S)-9,10-epoxyoctadecanoate.
