The molecule is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 2-hydroxyoctacosanoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion 28:0 and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-hydroxyoctacosanoic acid.
