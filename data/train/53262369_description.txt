The molecule is a branched amino tetrasaccharide consisting of N-acetyl-D-glucosamine at the reducing end having an alpha-L-fucosyl residue attached at the 3-position and an alpha-L-fucosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-galactosyl moiety attached at the 4-position. It has a role as an antigen. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
