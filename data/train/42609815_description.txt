The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside triphosphate oxoanion that is a trianion of 2'-deoxycytidine 5'-triphosphate, arising from deprotonation of three of the four triphosphate OH groups. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a dCTP. It is a conjugate acid of a dCTP(4-).
