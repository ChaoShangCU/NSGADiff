The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosa-4,8,10,13,15,19-hexaenoic acid carrying a hydroperoxy group at the 7S-position and a hydroxy group at the 17R-position. It is an intermediate involved in the resolvin D1 biosynthesis pathway. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and a hydroperoxy fatty acid.
