The molecule is a triterpene glycoside obtained from the New Zealand and South Australian sea cucumber Australostichopus mollis. It has a role as a marine metabolite. It is a triterpenoid saponin, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a tetrasaccharide derivative, an oligosaccharide sulfate, a lactone, a ketone and an olefinic compound. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
