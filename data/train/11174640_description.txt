The molecule is a member of the class of aromatic amides obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-ethyl-2-(ethylamino)-1,3-thiazole-5-carboxylic acid with the amino group of amino(2-thienyl)acetonitrile. It is an aromatic amide, an aromatic amine, a secondary amino compound, a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a member of thiophenes and a nitrile.
