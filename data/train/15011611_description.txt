The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is abieta-8(14),9(11),12-triene substituted at positions 3, 11 and 14 respectively by oxo, hydroxy and methoxy groups. It is found in Tripterygium wilfordii and Tripterygium hypoglaucum. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, a member of phenols, an aromatic ether, a cyclic ketone and a carbotricyclic compound.
