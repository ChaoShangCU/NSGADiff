The molecule is a glycosyloxyisoflavone that is genistein substituted by a 6-O-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside group at position 7. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a disaccharide derivative, a gentiobioside, a glycosyloxyisoflavone and a hydroxyisoflavone. It derives from a genistein.
