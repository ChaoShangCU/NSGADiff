The molecule is a complex cob(III)alamin in which cobalt is positioned in the centre of a planar corrin tetrapyrrole ring and bound axially to a 5,6-dimethylbenzimidazole moiety and a cyano group. It is one of several forms of the vitamin known as vitamin B12. It has a role as a vitamin. It is an alkylcob(III)alamin and a member of cob(III)alamins.
