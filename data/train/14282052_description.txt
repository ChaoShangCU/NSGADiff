The molecule is a pyranobenzodioxin obtained by formal cyclocodensation between syringylglycerol and esculetin. It is found in Arabidopsis thaliana. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of coumarins, a dimethoxybenzene, a member of phenols, a primary alcohol, a lignan and a pyranobenzodioxin. It derives from an esculetin and a 2,6-dimethoxyphenol.
