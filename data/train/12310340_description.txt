The molecule is a tetracyclic diterpenoid with formula C20H28O2 which exhibits anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, antileishmanial and wound-healing properties. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent, an antibacterial agent, an antileishmanial agent, a plant metabolite and a vulnerary. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a tetracyclic diterpenoid and an ent-kaurane diterpenoid.
