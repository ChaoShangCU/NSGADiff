The molecule is a UDP-amino sugar compound having 2-acetamido-2,6-dideoxy-beta-D-xylo-hexopyranosyl-4-ulose as the sugar residue. It is an UDP-amino sugar and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from an UDP-D-glucosamine. It is a conjugate acid of an UDP-2-acetamido-4-dehydro-2,6-dideoxy-beta-D-glucose(2-).
