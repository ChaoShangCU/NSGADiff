The molecule is a monosaccharide derivative that is trans-chalcone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 4, 4' and 6 and a beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy group at position 2' respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a member of chalcones, a member of resorcinols, a beta-D-glucoside and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a trans-chalcone.
