The molecule is an epoxide isolated from Streptomyces sp. E-1625. An inhibitor of interleukin-1beta converting enzyme (ICE), it acts as an anti-inflammatory agent and antibiotic. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent, an antimicrobial agent, an EC 3.4.22.36 (caspase-1) inhibitor and a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ketone, an enol, an epoxide, a tertiary alcohol and a monocarboxylic acid amide.
