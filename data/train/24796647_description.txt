The molecule is an organosulfate oxoanion that is the conjugate base of (3Z)-9-methyldec-3-en-1-yl hydrogen sulfate. It has been isolated from Daphnia pulex and has been shown to cause morphological changes in the green alga Scenedesmus gutwinskii. It has a role as a kairomone and a Daphnia pulex metabolite.
