The molecule is an azaphilone that is 9,9a-dihydro-2H-furo[3,2-g]isochromen-2-one substituted by an acetyl group at position 3, a hydroxy group at position 9 a 5-hydroxy-3,5-dimethylhepta-1,3-dien-1-yl group at position 6 and a methyl group at position 9a. It has been isolated from Chaetomium cupreum. It has a role as a Chaetomium metabolite. It is a gamma-lactone, an azaphilone, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a methyl ketone and a tertiary alcohol.
