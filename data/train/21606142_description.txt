The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is oleanolic acid substituted by a 2-O-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl moiety at position O-3. A natural product found in Luffa acutangula and Viola hondoensis. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a disaccharide derivative, a glycoside, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an oleanolic acid.
