The molecule is an acyl-CoA having trans-dodec-2-enoyl as the S-acyl group. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a trans-2-enoyl-CoA and a dodecenoyl-CoA. It derives from a coenzyme A and a trans-2-dodecenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a trans-dodec-2-enoyl-CoA(4-).
