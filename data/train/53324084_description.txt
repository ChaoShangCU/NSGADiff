The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is 4',5,7-trihydroxy-3-methoxyflavone attached to a rutionsyl residue at position 7. It is isolated from the whole plant of Lepisorus contortus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a disaccharide derivative, a rutinoside, a dihydroxyflavone and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a kaempferol.
