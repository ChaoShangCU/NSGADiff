The molecule is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide that is 3',5'-cyclic AMP in which the exocylic amino hydrogens on the purine fragment are replaced by methyl groups. It is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide, a nucleoside 3',5'-cyclic phosphate and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a hydride of a 3',5'-cyclic AMP.
