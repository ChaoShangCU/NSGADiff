The molecule is a sesquiterpene alkaloid that is isolated from Tripterygium forrestii, Platanus chiapensis and Maytenus chiapensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a benzoate ester, a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid, a macrolide, a pyridine alkaloid and a sesquiterpene alkaloid.
