The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is lup-20(29)-ene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2 and 3 (the 2alpha,3beta stereoisomer). It has been isolated from Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a diol. It derives from a hydride of a lupane.
