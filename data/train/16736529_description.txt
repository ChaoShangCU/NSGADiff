The molecule is a member of the class of 2-aminopurines that is 2-aminopurine which is substituted by a chlorine at position 6 and by a (4-methoxy-3,5-dimethylpyridin-2-yl)methyl group at position 9. It has a role as a Hsp90 inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of pyridines, a member of 2-aminopurines, an organochlorine compound and an aromatic ether.
