The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside that is oscr#20 in which the pro-R hydrogen beta to the carboxy group is replaced by a hydroxy group. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside, a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an oscr#20 and a (3R)-3,12-dihydroxylauric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a bhos#20(1-).
