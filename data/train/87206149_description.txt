The molecule is a hydroxy monounsaturated fatty acid that is dodec-2-enoic acid in which the hydrogen at position 2 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. A minor tautomer of 2-oxododecanoic acid. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid, a hydroxy monounsaturated fatty acid and a medium-chain fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxydodec-2-enoate. It is a tautomer of a 2-oxododecanoic acid.
