The molecule is an acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 3-dehydrocarnitine. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an acyl-CoA and a quaternary ammonium ion. It derives from a 3-dehydrocarnitine. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-dehydrocarnityl CoA(3-).
