The molecule is an alpha,omega-dicarboxylic acid that is decanedioic (sebacic) acid carrying a hydroxy substituent at position 3. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is an alpha,omega-dicarboxylic acid and a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid. It derives from a sebacic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxysebacate and a 3-hydroxysebacate(2-).
