The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 9-decenoic acid. It is a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a dec-9-enoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 9-decenoyl-CoA(4-).
