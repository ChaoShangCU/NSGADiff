The molecule is a member of the class of benzyl alcohols in which the hydrogen at position 4 on the phenyl ring of benzyl alcohol has been replaced by an isopropyl group. It has a role as a fragrance, an insect repellent, a volatile oil component, a plant metabolite and a xenobiotic metabolite. It is a p-menthane monoterpenoid and a member of benzyl alcohols. It derives from a p-cymene.
