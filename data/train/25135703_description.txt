The molecule is a member of the class of 1-benzofurans isolated from Sorocea muriculata that has been shown to exhibit antibacterial activity against methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus. It has a role as a metabolite, an antibacterial agent and an antifungal agent. It is a member of resorcinols, a member of 1-benzofurans and an aromatic ketone.
