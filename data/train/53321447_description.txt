The molecule is an extended flavonoid that is flavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 2', 4' and 5', a 3-(hydroxymethyl)but-2-en-1-yl group at position 3 and a 6,6-dimethyl-3,6-dihydro-2H-pyran group across positions 7 and 8. It has been isolated from Artocarpus odoratissimus. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a tetrahydroxyflavone and an extended flavonoid.
