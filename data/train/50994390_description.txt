The molecule is a tirucallane triterpenoid that is (13alpha,14beta,17alpha,20S,23S)-21,23-epoxylanosta-7,24-diene substituted by an oxo group at position 3. It has been isolated from the stem and stem barks of Cornus walteri. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a tirucallane triterpenoid and a member of oxolanes.
