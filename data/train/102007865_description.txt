The molecule is a lysobisphosphatidic acid with (R,R)-configuration in which both acyl groups are specified as oleoyl and are located at the 2 and 2'-positions. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (R,R)-bis(2-oleoylglycero)-3-phosphate(1-). It is an enantiomer of a (S,S)-bis(2-oleoylglycero)-1-phosphate.
