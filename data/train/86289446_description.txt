The molecule is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy and 8-hydroxy groups of tetracenomycin D3. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion and a phenolate anion. It is a conjugate base of a tetracenomycin D3(1-).
