The molecule is a 3-hydroxydocosanoyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3S)-hydroxydocoscanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3-hydroxydocosanoyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain (3S)-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3S)-3-hydroxydocosanoyl-CoA.
