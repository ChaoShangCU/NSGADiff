The molecule is an unusual iridoid monoterpenoid isolated from the leaves of Morinda citrifolia. It exhibits significant inhibition of UVB-induced Activator Protein-1 (AP-1) activity in cell cultures. It has a role as a metabolite and an AP-1 antagonist. It is an iridoid monoterpenoid, a beta-D-glucoside, a member of phenols, a gamma-lactone, an enone, an oxaspiro compound, an aromatic ether, an epoxide, an aromatic ketone and a methyl ester.
