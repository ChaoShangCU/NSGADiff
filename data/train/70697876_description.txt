The molecule is a steroid saponin isolated from Ornithogalum thyrsoides and has been shown to exhibit antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a 17-hydroxy steroid, an acetate ester, a benzoate ester, a cholestanoid and a steroid saponin. It derives from a 3,4-dimethoxybenzoic acid.
