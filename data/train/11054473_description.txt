The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone obtained by formal dehydrogenation of the 4-methyl position of pentalenolactone D. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a sesquiterpene lactone, an organic heterotricyclic compound and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a pentalenolactone E(1-).
