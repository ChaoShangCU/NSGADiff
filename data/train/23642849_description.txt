The molecule is an aromatic ester that is methyl 2-hydroxy-4-methylbenzoate attached to a (6-methoxy-1,4-benzoquinon-2-yl)methyl group at position 6. Isolated from Aspergillus variecolor, it exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is an aromatic ester, a member of phenols, a methyl ester and a member of 1,4-benzoquinones.
