The molecule is a member of the class of purines that is carrying 9-cyclopentyl-9H-purine-2,8-diamine in which the amino groups at positions 2 and 8 are carrying 4-methoxyphenyl and 2-fluorophenyl substituents respectively. It has a role as a c-Jun N-terminal kinase inhibitor. It is a member of purines, a member of cyclopentanes, a monomethoxybenzene, a member of monofluorobenzenes, a substituted aniline and a secondary amino compound.
