The molecule is a dioxo monocarboxylic acid derived from morellin by oxidation of the aldehyde group to the corresponding carboxy group. Isolated from Garcinia morella and Garcinia hanburyi, it exhibits antineoplastic, antibacterial and anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antibacterial agent, an antineoplastic agent and an anti-HIV-1 agent. It is a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, an organic heterohexacyclic compound, a member of phenols, a polycyclic cage and a dioxo monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a morellin.
