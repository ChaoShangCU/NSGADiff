The molecule is a hydroxycalciol that is 25-hydroxyvitamin D3 carrying an additional hydroxy group at position 23 (with 23S-configuration) and an oxo group at position 24. An intermediate in the degradation pathway of 25-OH-vitamin D3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an oxocalciol, a hydroxycalciol, a member of D3 vitamins, a triol, a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
