The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (9Z,12R)-12-hydroxyoctadec-9-enoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (9Z,12R)-12-hydroxyoctadec-9-enoyl-CoA.
