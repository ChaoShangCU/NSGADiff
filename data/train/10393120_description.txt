The molecule is an alkaloid that is manzamine A with a hydroxy substituent at position 6. Isolated from Haliclona and Acanthostrongylophora, it exhibits inhibitory activity against Glycogen Synthase Kinase-3 (EC 2.7.11.26). It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 2.7.11.26 (tau-protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of beta-carbolines, an alkaloid and a member of isoquinolines. It derives from a manzamine A.
