The molecule is a BODIPY dye in which the 4,4-difluoro-4-bora-3a,4a-diaza-s-indacene skeleton is substituted at position 5 by a p-methoxyphenyl group and at position 3 by an 11-[(3beta)-cholest-5-en-3-yloxy]-11-oxoundecyl group. An orange-red BODIPY dye. It is an organic molecular entity, a cholesteryl ester and a BODIPY dye.
