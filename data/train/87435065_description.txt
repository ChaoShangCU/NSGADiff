The molecule is a trienoic fatty acid that is (6E,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid carrying an additional oxo substituent at position 5. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an oxo fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a trienoic fatty acid. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-oxo-ETrE(1-).
