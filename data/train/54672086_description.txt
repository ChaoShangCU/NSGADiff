The molecule is a hydroxyhomoflavonoid that is isochromeno[4,3-b]chromen-7(5H)-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 4 and methoxy groups at positions 3, 10 and 11. It has been isolated from Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy homoflavonoid, an aromatic ether and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
