The molecule is a dihydroxybenzoic acid that is olivetolic acid in which the hydrogen at position 3 is substituted by a neryl group. A biosynthetic precursor to Delta(9)-tetrahydrocannabinol, the principal psychoactive constituent of the cannabis plant. It is a dihydroxybenzoic acid, a member of resorcinols, a polyketide, a diterpenoid and a phytocannabinoid. It derives from an olivetolic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a cannabinerolate.
