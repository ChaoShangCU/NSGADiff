The molecule is an octadecanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 9,10-epoxy-18-hydroxy-(12Z)-octadecenoic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion and an octadecanoid anion. It derives from a 9(10)-EpOME(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 9,10-epoxy-18-hydroxy-(12Z)-octadecenoic acid.
