The molecule is a glycosylglucose derivative that is alpha-L-rhamnopyranosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-glucopyranose in which the anomeric hydroxy hydrogen has been replaced by a 2-(4-hydroxyphenyl)ethyl group. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a glycoside, a member of phenols and a glycosylglucose derivative. It derives from a 2-(4-hydroxyphenyl)ethanol.
