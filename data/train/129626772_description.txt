The molecule is 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl groups at positions 1 and 2 are specified as heptanoyl and hexanoyl respectively. It derives from a hexanoic acid and a heptanoic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1-heptanoyl-2-hexanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
