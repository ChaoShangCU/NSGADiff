The molecule is a beta-D-glucoside that is beta-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 1R is substituted by a (2-hydroxybenzyl)oxy group. It is isolated from the flowers of Filipendula ulmaria. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a member of phenols.
