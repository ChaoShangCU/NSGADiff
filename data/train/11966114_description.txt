The molecule is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA that is the S-(4,8,12-trimethyltridecanoyl) derivative of coenzyme A. It is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA, a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a coenzyme A. It is a conjugate acid of a 4,8,12-trimethyltridecanoyl-CoA(4-).
