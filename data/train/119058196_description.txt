The molecule is an epoxy fatty acid that is (9Z)-11-hydroxyoctadecenoic acid in which the epoxy group is located across positions 12 and 13. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an epoxy fatty acid, an octadecanoid, a hydroxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a monounsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of an 11-hydroxy-(12S,13S)-epoxy-(9Z)-octadecenoate(1-).
