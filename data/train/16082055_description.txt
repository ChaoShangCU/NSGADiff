The molecule is a neolignan isolated from the whole plant of Bidens parviflora that has been found to inhibit histamine release from the peritoneal exudate mast cells induced by antigen-antibody reaction. It has a role as a metabolite and a histamine antagonist. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an aromatic ether, a neolignan, a polyphenol, a diol and a secondary alcohol.
