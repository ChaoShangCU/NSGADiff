The molecule is a lignane that is the demethoxy derivative of (-)-pinoresinol. Isolated from Balanophora abbreviata, it exhibits inhibitory activity against nitric oxide synthase. It has a role as an EC 1.14.13.39 (nitric oxide synthase) inhibitor and a plant metabolite. It is a furofuran, a polyphenol, an aromatic ether and a lignan. It derives from a (-)-pinoresinol.
