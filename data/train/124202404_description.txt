The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of 11,12-epoxy-(5Z,8Z,14Z)-icosatrienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of an 11,12-epoxy-(5Z,8Z,14Z)-icosatrienoyl-CoA.
