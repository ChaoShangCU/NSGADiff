The molecule is an omega-hydroxy-long-chain fatty acid that is palmitoleic acid [(9Z)-16-hexadec-9-enoic acid) which is substituted by a hydroxy group at the terminal carbon. It is an omega-hydroxy-long-chain fatty acid and a monounsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (9Z)-16-hydroxyhexadec-9-enoate.
