The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate(2-) in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as hexadecanoyl (palmitoyl) and 9Z,12Z-octadecadienoyl (linoleoyl) respectively; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 1-hexadecanoyl-2-(9Z,12Z-octadecadienoyl)-sn-glycero-3-phosphate.
