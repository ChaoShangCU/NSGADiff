The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by a hydroxy group at positions 1, chloro groups at positions 2 and 4, methoxy groups at positions 3 and 6 and a methyl group at position 8. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a member of xanthones, a polyphenol, an aromatic ether and an organochlorine compound.
