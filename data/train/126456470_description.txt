The molecule is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoate that is the conjugate base of (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14R,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a dihydroxydocosahexaenoate and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14R,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoic acid. It is an enantiomer of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14,22-dihydroxydocosahexaenoate.
