The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-galactopyranosyl, alpha-D-galactopyranosyl, beta-D-galactopyranosyl, and beta-D-glucopyranose units joined together in sequence by (1->3), (1->4), and (1->4) glycosidic linkages, respectively. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
