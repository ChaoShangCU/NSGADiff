The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is a trianion resulting from the deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate OH groups of 2-carboxy-D-arabinitol 1-phosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion, a carbohydrate acid derivative anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-carboxy-D-arabinitol 1-phosphate.
