The molecule is a linear amino trisaccharide comprising N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminic acid, beta-D-galactose and N-acetyl-6-O-sulfo-D-glucosamine residues linked sequentially (2->3) and (1->3). It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and an oligosaccharide sulfate.
