The molecule is an octadecanoid that is 12,13-epoxy-(9Z)-octadecenoic acid carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at position 18. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid, an epoxy fatty acid and an octadecanoid. It derives from a linoleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 12,13-epoxy-18-hydroxy-(9Z)-octadecenoate.
