The molecule is a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl group is specified as stearoyl. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a lysophosphatidylethanolamine 18:0. It derives from an octadecanoic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1-stearoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
