The molecule is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate having guanine as the nucleobase. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate, a guanyl deoxyribonucleotide and a deoxyguanosine phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a 2'-deoxyguanosine 5'-monophosphate(2-). It is an enantiomer of a 9-(2-deoxy-5-O-phosphono-beta-L-ribofuranosyl)guanine. It is a tautomer of a 2-amino-9-(2-deoxy-5-O-phosphono-beta-D-erythro-pentofuranosyl)-9H-purin-6-ol.
