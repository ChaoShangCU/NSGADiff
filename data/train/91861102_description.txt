The molecule is a linear amino hexasaccharide comprising a (1->3), (1->4), (1->3), (1->4), (1->3) sequence of N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and (at the reducing end) N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine. It is an amino hexasaccharide, a galactosamine oligosaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
