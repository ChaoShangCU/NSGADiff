The molecule is a steroid acid anion resulting from the deprotonation of the carboxy group of 3beta-hydroxy-9beta-9,19-cyclolanost-24-en-28-oic acid. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3beta-sterol, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a member of phytosterols and a steroid acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 3beta-hydroxy-9beta-9,19-cyclolanost-24-en-28-oic acid. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
