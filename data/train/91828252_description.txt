The molecule is an iminium salt composed of 4-{(4-anilinonaphthalen-1-yl)[4-(diethylamino)phenyl]methylidene}-N,N-diethylcyclohexa-2,5-dien-1-iminium and chloride ions in a 1:1 ratio. It has a role as a histological dye and a fluorochrome. It is an organic chloride salt and an iminium salt. It contains a night blue(1+).
