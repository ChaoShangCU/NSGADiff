The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is pyrido[3',2':4,5]furo[3,2-d]pyrimidine substituted at positions 2 and 4 by 3-hydroxyphenyl and morpholin-4-yl groups respectively. A dual-kinase inhibitor with anti-cancer properties. It has a role as an EC 2.7.1.137 (phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase) inhibitor, a mTOR inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of morpholines, a member of phenols, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a tertiary amino compound and an aromatic amine.
