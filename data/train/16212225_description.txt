The molecule is an organosulfonic acid that is 2-oxoethanesulfonic acid substituted by a (4-fluorophenyl)(propan-2-yl)amino group at position 2. It is metabolite of the herbicide flufenacet. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of monofluorobenzenes, an organosulfonic acid and an aromatic amide.
