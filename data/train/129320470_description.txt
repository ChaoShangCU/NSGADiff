The molecule is a (4-alkanoyl-5-oxo-2,5-dihydrofuran-3-yl)methyl phosphate(2-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of [4-(6-methylheptanoyl)-5-oxo-2,5-dihydrofuran-3-yl]methyl phosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a [4-(6-methylheptanoyl)-5-oxo-2,5-dihydrofuran-3-yl]methyl phosphate.
