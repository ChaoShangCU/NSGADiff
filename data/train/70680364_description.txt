The molecule is an N-acyl-15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine in which the acyl group has 21 carbons and 0 double bonds. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a N-acyl-15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine and a Cer(d38:1). It derives from a 15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine and a henicosanoic acid.
