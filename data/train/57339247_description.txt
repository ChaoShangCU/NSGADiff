The molecule is a branched amino tetrasaccharide that consists of the linear trisaccharide alpha-D-galactosyl-(1->3)-beta-D-galactosyl-(1->3)-N-acetyl-D-glucosamine having an alpha-L-fucosyl residue attached at the 2-position of the central galactose. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
