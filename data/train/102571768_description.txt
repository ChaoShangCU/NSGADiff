The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 11(R)-HEPE arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an algal metabolite, a human xenobiotic metabolite and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a HEPE(1-) and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an 11(R)-HEPE.
