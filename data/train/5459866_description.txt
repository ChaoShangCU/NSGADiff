The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazines in which the pyrazine ring is substituted at positions 2, 3, and 5 by [4-(sulfooxy)phenyl]acetamido, benzyl, and 4-(sulfooxy)phenyl groups, respectively. It has a role as a member of oxidized luciferins. It is an aryl sulfate and a member of pyrazines. It is a conjugate acid of an oxidized Watasenia luciferin(2-).
