The molecule is a 7-hydroxyflavonol substituted by additional hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 3' and prenyl groups at positions 6, 8 and 4'. Isolated from the roots of Dorstenia psilurus, it exhibits alpha-glucosidase inhibitory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor.
