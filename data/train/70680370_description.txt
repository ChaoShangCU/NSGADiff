The molecule is an N-acyl-15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine in which the acyl group has 24 carbons and 0 double bonds. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a N-acyl-15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine, a Cer(d41:1) and a N-(very-long-chain fatty acyl)-sphingoid base. It derives from a 15-methylhexadecasphing-4-enine and a tetracosanoic acid.
