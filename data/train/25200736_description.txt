The molecule is anion of delphinidin 3-O-(6''-O-malonyl)-beta-D-glucoside arising from deprotonation of the malonyl carboxy and 5-hydroxy groups. It is a conjugate base of a delphinidin 3-O-(6''-O-malonyl)-beta-D-glucoside. It is a conjugate acid of a delphinidin 3-O-(6''-O-malonyl)-beta-D-glucoside(2-).
