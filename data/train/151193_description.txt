The molecule is a succinate salt obtained by combining vatalanib with one molar equivalent of succinic acid. It is a multi-targeted tyrosine kinase inhibitor for all isoforms of VEGFR, PDGFR and c-Kit. It has a role as an angiogenesis inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent, an EC 2.7.10.1 (receptor protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor and a vascular endothelial growth factor receptor antagonist. It contains a vatalanib.
