The molecule is a ketone that is heptan-3-one substituted by a dimethylamino group at position 6 and two phenyl groups at position 4. It has a role as an opioid analgesic, a mu-opioid receptor agonist, a xenobiotic and an environmental contaminant. It is a tertiary amine, a ketone and a member of benzenes. It derives from a heptan-3-one.
