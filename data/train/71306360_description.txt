The molecule is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion that is the conjugate base of 17alpha-(N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosaminyl)estradiol 3-glucuronoside, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 17alpha-(N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosaminyl)estradiol 3-glucuronoside.
