The molecule is a thiourea resulting from the formal condensation of the secondary amino group of 1-methylpiperazine and the primary amino group of N-(4-nitrophenyl)benzene-1,4-diamine with carbonothioic O,O-acid. It has a role as an antinematodal drug. It is a C-nitro compound, a N-methylpiperazine, a member of thioureas and a secondary amino compound. It derives from a diphenylamine.
