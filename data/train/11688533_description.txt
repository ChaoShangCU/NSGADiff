The molecule is an aromatic amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-(difluoromethyl)-1-methylpyrazole-4-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 2-[1,1'-bi(cyclopropyl)-2-yl]aniline. It is an aromatic amide, an organofluorine compound, a member of pyrazoles, a ring assembly and a member of cyclopropanes.
