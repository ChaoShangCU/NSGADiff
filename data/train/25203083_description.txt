The molecule is a 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of 3-oxodocosanoyl-CoA; principal microspecies at pH 7.3. It is an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 3-oxodocosanoyl-CoA.
