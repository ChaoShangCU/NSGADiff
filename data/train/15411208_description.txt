The molecule is a withanolide that is the 2,3-dihydro derivative of withaferin A. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Physalis longifolia. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a 27-hydroxy steroid, a 4-hydroxy steroid, an ergostanoid, a primary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a withanolide and an epoxy steroid. It derives from a withaferin A.
