The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate functions of 2-methylhexenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a 2,3-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 2-methylhexenoyl-CoA.
