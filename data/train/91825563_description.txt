The molecule is a limonoid with a phragmalin skeleton isolated from the leaves of Trichilia connaroides. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a delta-lactone, a bridged compound, a member of furans, a limonoid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an enoate ester and a methyl ester. It derives from a tiglic acid.
