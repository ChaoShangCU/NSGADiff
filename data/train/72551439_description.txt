The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is kaempferol attached to a beta-D-galactopyranosyl moiety at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-D-galactoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a trihydroxyflavone, a glycosyloxyflavone and a member of flavonols. It derives from a kaempferol.
