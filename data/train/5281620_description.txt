The molecule is a monosaccharide derivative that consists of gossypetin substituted by a 6-deoxy-alpha-L-mannopyranosyl moiety at position 8 via a glycosidic linkage. It is a monosaccharide derivative, a 7-hydroxyflavonol, a pentahydroxyflavone, an alpha-L-rhamnoside and a glycosyloxyflavone. It derives from a gossypetin.
