The molecule is an L-aspartic acid derivative that is the ester obtained by formal condensation of the alpha-carboxy group of L-aspartic acid with the 3'-hydroxy group of AMP. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is an adenosine 5'-phosphate, a L-aspartic acid derivative, an alpha-amino acid ester and a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate.
