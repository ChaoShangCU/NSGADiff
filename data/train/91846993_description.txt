The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of beta-L-fucopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->6) glycosidic bond. It is an amino disaccharide, a member of acetamides and a glycosylglucose derivative. It derives from a beta-L-fucose and a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine.
