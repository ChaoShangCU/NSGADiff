The molecule is an S-substituted N-acetyl-L-cysteine that is the conjugate base of S-(5-acetamido-2-hydroxyphenyl)-N-acetyl-L-cysteine, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a drug metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a S-(5-acetamido-2-hydroxyphenyl)-N-acetyl-L-cysteine.
