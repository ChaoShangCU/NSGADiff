The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is 3a,4,5,9b-tetrahydro-3H-cyclopenta[c]quinoline which is substituted by a pyridin-3-yl at position 4 and by fluorines at positions 6 and 8. It is an organic heterotricyclic compound, a member of pyridines, an organofluorine compound and a secondary amino compound.
