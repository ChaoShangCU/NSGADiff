The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid that is estr-4-ene substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at positions 3 and 17. It is a synthetic anabolic steroid that is used as a dietary supplement by athletes to enhance performance. It has a role as a nutraceutical and a prohormone. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, an anabolic androgenic steroid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid and a diol. It derives from a hydride of an estrane.
