The molecule is a leukotriene that is the 6-trans,12S-isomer of leukotriene B4. It is a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a leukotriene, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from an icosa-6,8,10,14-tetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a Delta(6)-trans-12-epi-leukotriene B4(1-).
