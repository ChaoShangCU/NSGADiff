The molecule is a polyunsaturated oxo fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 11-oxo-ETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, an oxo fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an oxo-ETE anion. It is a conjugate base of an 11-oxo-ETE.
