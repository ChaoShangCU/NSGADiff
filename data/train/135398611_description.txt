The molecule is trianion of (2-amino-4-hydroxy-7,8-dihydropteridin-6-yl)methyl diphosphate arising from deprotonation of the three diphosphate OH groups. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a (2-amino-4-hydroxy-7,8-dihydropteridin-6-yl)methyl trihydrogen diphosphate. It is a tautomer of a (7,8-dihydropterin-6-yl)methyl diphosphate(3-).
