The molecule is a trihydroxyflavanone that is (S)-naringenin having a prenyl group at position 8. It has a role as a platelet aggregation inhibitor and a plant metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a (2S)-flavan-4-one. It derives from a (S)-naringenin. It is a conjugate acid of a sophoraflavanone B(1-).
