The molecule is a diterpenoid natural product obtained from the cyanobacterium Tolypothrix nodosa. It shows strong anti-inflammatory activity in the mouse ear edema assay. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a diterpenoid, a diol, a secondary alcohol, a polycyclic ether, an oxacycle, an organic heteropentacyclic compound and a methyl ester.
