The molecule is a carbobicyclic compound that is 2,3,4,4a,5,6,7,8-octahydronaphthalen-2-ol which carries a methyl, methyl and prop-1-en-2-yl groups at positions 4, 4a, and 6, respectively. It is an eremophilane sesquiterpenoid, an olefinic compound, a secondary allylic alcohol and a member of octahydronaphthalenes.
