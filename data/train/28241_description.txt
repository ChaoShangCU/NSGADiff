The molecule is a member of the class of aminoacridines that is acridine which is substituted by a methoxy group at position 2, chlorine at position 6, and a {3-[(2-chloroethyl)amino]propyl}amino group at position 9. It has a role as a mutagen. It is a member of aminoacridines, a secondary amino compound, an organochlorine compound and an aromatic ether. It is a conjugate base of an acridine half-mustard(2+).
