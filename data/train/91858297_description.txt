The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of alpha-D-mannopyranose, 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by (1->4) glycosidic bonds. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a member of acetamides.
