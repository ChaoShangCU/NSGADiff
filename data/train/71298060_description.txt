The molecule is a disaccharide consisting of a 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-D-galactose residue and a 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-galactose residue joined by a (1->6) glycosidic bond. It is an amino disaccharide, a glycosylgalactose derivative and a member of acetamides. It derives from a N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine and a N-acetyl-D-galactosamine.
