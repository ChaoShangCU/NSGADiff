The molecule is a N-glycosyl-1,2,4-triazine that is the 6-aza analogue of thymidine 5'-monophosphate. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl-1,2,4-triazine, a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate and a nucleoside monophosphate analogue. It derives from a 6-azathymine.
