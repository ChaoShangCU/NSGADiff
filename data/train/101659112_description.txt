The molecule is a triazolopyridazine that is tetrahydro-1H-[1,2,4]triazolo[1,2-a]pyridazine which is substituted at positions 1, 3, and 4 by oxo, sulfanyl and 4-chloro-2-fluoro-5-[(2-methoxy-2-oxoethyl)sulfanyl]phenyl groups, respectively. The active metabolite of the proherbicide fluthiacet-methyl. It has a role as an EC 1.3.3.4 (protoporphyrinogen oxidase) inhibitor and a herbicide. It is an aryl sulfide, a member of monochlorobenzenes, a member of monofluorobenzenes, a triazolopyridazine, a methyl ester and a thiocarbonyl compound.
