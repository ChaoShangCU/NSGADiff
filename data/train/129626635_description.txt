The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of Ni(II)-pyridinium-3,5-bisthiocarboxylic acid mononucleotide; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a cofactor. It is a conjugate base of a Ni(II)-pyridinium-3,5-bisthiocarboxylic acid mononucleotide.
