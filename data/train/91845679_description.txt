The molecule is an oligosaccharide sulfate that is Delta(4)-beta-D-GlcpA2S-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcpNS in which the hydroxy group at position 6 of the 2-sulfoamino-beta-D-glucopyranose moiety has been converted into the corresponding sulfamic acid derivative. It is a disaccharide derivative, an oligosaccharide sulfate, a member of sulfamic acids and a monocarboxylic acid.
