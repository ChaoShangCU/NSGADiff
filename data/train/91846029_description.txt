The molecule is an amino disaccharide that is D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 3 has been converted into the corresponding 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranosyl derivative. It is an amino disaccharide, a glycosylglucose derivative and a member of acetamides. It derives from a D-glucopyranose and a N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine.
