The molecule is a diterpene that is cyclotetradeca-1,3,7,11-tetraene which is substituted by methyl groups at positions 1, 7, and 11, and by an isopropyl group at position 4 (the 1Z,3E,7E,11E isomer). It is a constituent of the essential oils from Pinus koraiensis and Pinus sibirica. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a monocyclic hydrocarbon, a diterpene and a macrocycle.
