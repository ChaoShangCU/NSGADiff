The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 14(R),15(S)-DiHPETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a bis(hydroperoxy)icosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 14(R),15(S)-DiHPETE.
