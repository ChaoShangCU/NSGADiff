The molecule is a 2-oxo monocarboxylic acid that is pyruvic acid in which one of the methyl hydrogens has been replaced by a methylenecyclopropyl group. It has a role as a rat metabolite and a xenobiotic metabolite. It is a 2-oxo monocarboxylic acid, a member of cyclopropanes and an olefinic compound. It derives from a pyruvic acid.
