The molecule is an aromatic amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of pyrazine-2-carboxylic acid with the amino function of 3',4'-difluoro[1,1'-biphenyl]-2-amine. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a monocarboxylic acid amide, an aromatic amide, a member of pyrazines, a member of biphenyls, a difluorobenzene and an organofluorine pesticide.
