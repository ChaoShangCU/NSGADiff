The molecule is a myricetin O-glucoside that is myricetin with a beta-D-glucosyl residue attached at position 7. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a myricetin O-glucoside, a pentahydroxyflavone and a member of flavonols. It derives from a beta-D-glucose.
