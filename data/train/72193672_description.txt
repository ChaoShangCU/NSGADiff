The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isoorientin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by a 6-feruloylglucosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavone, a C-glycosyl compound, a cinnamate ester and a glycosyloxyflavone. It derives from an isoorientin and a ferulic acid.
