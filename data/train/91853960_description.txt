The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of beta-D-galactopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose residuces linked in sequence by a (1->2) glycosidic bond. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and a beta-D-galactose.
