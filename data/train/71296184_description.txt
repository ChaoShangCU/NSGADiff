The molecule is a ribose monophosphate that is 4-(beta-D-ribofuranosyl)aminobenzene carrying a single monophospate substituent at position 5'. It is a ribose monophosphate, a C-glycosyl compound and a substituted aniline. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-(beta-D-ribofuranosyl)aminobenzene 5'-phosphate(2-).
