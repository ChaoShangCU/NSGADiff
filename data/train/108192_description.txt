The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic that is testosterone carrying a glucosiduronic acid residue at position 17. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 3-oxo steroid, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, an enone and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a testosterone. It is a conjugate acid of a testosterone 17-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
