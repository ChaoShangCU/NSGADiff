The molecule is a flavonoid oxoanion that is the conjugate base of apigenin 7-O-beta-D-glucoside, obtained by deprotonation of the 7-hydroxy group. Major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as an antibacterial agent, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug and a metabolite. It is a conjugate base of an apigenin 7-O-beta-D-glucoside.
