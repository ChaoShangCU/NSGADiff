The molecule is a branched amino trisaccharide that consists of N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine having a beta-D-galactosyl residue attached at the 3-position and an alpha-N-acetylneuraminosyl residue attached at the 6-position. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino trisaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
