The molecule is trianion of (9S,10S)-10-hydroxy-9-(phosphonooxy)octadecanoic acid arising from deprotonation of carboxylic acid and phosphate functions. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (9S,10S)-10-hydroxy-9-(phosphonooxy)octadecanoic acid.
