The molecule is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid that is 17beta-estradiol in which the hydrogen at position 2 has been replaced by a hydroxy group and the phenolic hydrogen at position 3 has bee replaced by a methyl group. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a 2-hydroxy steroid, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a C19-steroid and an aromatic ether. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol.
