The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a alpha-D-rhamnopyranosyl moiety at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an alpha-D-rhamnoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a tetrahydroxyflavone, a quercetin O-glycoside and a member of flavonols. It derives from an alpha-D-rhamnose.
