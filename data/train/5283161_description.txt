The molecule is a leukotriene compound having double bonds in the 7-, 9-, 11- and 14-positions and 5(S)- and 6(S)-hydroxy substituents. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a leukotriene and a dihydroxyicosatetraenoic acid. It derives from an icosa-7,9,11,14-tetraenoic acid.
