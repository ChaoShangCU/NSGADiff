The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (9Z,12Z,15Z,18Z)-tetracosatetraenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a very long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a tetracosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a (9Z,12Z,15Z,18Z)-tetracosatetraenoic acid.
