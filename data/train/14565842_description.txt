The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-hydroxyphenylacetic acid with the 8-hydroxy group of 11beta,13-dihydrolactucin. Found in chicory. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a primary alcohol, an azulenofuran, a cyclic terpene ketone, an enone, a member of phenols and a sesquiterpene lactone. It derives from a 4-hydroxyphenylacetic acid and a lactucin.
