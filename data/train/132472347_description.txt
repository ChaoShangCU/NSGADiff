The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (7R,14S)-dihydroxy-(8E,10E,12Z,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7R,14S)-dihydroxy-(8E,10E,12Z,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid.
