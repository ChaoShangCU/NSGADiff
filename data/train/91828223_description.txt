The molecule is a linear amino pentasaccharide consisting of beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-galactosamine, alpha-D-galactose, betaD-galactose and N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine units connected via sequential (1->3), (1->3), (1->4) and (1->4) linkages. It is an amino pentasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
