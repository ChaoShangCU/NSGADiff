The molecule is a steroid saponin that is 6beta-hydroxytestosterone attached to a alpha-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 6 via glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a Daphnia magna metabolite. It is a steroid saponin, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, an alpha-D-glucoside and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a 6beta-hydroxytestosterone.
