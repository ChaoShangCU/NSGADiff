The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is the 11alpha-hydroxy derivative of asiatic acid. Isolated from the methanolic extract of the leaves of Symplocos lancifolia, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from an asiatic acid. It derives from a hydride of an ursane.
