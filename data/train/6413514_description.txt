The molecule is a macrocyclic lactam with oximo amide groups having a -E,E configuration. Isolated from the marine sponge Ianthella, it has been shown to exhibit calcium channel modulatory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and a calcium channel modulator. It is an organobromine compound, a polyphenol, a lactam, a macrocycle, a ketoxime and a cyclic ether.
