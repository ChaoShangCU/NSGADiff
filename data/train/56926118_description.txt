The molecule is a withanolide that is 2,3-dihydrowithaferin A substituted by a hydroxy group at position 19 and a sulfoxy group at position 3. Isolated from Physalis longifolia, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a 27-hydroxy steroid, a 4-hydroxy steroid, a 19-hydroxy steroid, an ergostanoid, a primary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a withanolide, a steroid sulfate and an epoxy steroid. It derives from a withaferin A.
