The molecule is a docosahexaenoic acid having six cis-double bonds at positions 4, 7, 10, 13, 16 and 19. It has a role as a nutraceutical, an antineoplastic agent, a human metabolite, a Daphnia tenebrosa metabolite, a mouse metabolite and an algal metabolite. It is a docosahexaenoic acid and an omega-3 fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoate.
