The molecule is an indole alkaloid that is tryprostatin A substituted by an oxo substituent at position 18. Isolated from Aspergillus sydowii, it exhibits cytotoxicity. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is an indole alkaloid, a member of indoles, a pyrrolopyrazine, a dipeptide, an aromatic ether, an enone and an aromatic ketone. It derives from a brevianamide F.
