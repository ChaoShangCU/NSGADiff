The molecule is an amino pentasaccharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxyglucopyranose in which the hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 6 have been glycosylated by 2-O-(alpha-L-fucopyranosyl)-beta-D-galactopyranosyl and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-4-O-beta-D-galactopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl groups, respectively. It is an amino pentasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
