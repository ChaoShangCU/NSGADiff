The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol in which the acyl groups positions 1 and 2 are specified as alpha-linolenoyl and icosanoyl (arachidoyl) respectively. It has a role as a human blood serum metabolite. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol and a diacylglycerol 38:3. It derives from an alpha-linolenic acid and an icosanoic acid.
