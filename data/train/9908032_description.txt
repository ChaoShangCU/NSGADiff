The molecule is an amino disaccharide that is beta-D-galactopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 4 has been converted to the corresponding 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-galactopyranoside. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a beta-D-galactose and a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine.
