The molecule is a member of the class of quinazolines that is quinazoline which is substituted at position 4 by a 2-chloro-5-methoxyanilino group, at position 6 by a methoxy group and at position 7 by a 3-(morpholin-4-yl)propoxy group. It has a role as an EC 2.7.10.2 (non-specific protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor. It is an aromatic ether, a polyether, a member of quinazolines, a secondary amino compound, a member of morpholines, a tertiary amino compound and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
