The molecule is an androstanoid that is dehydroepiandrosterone 3-sulfate substituted by a hydroxy group at the 16alpha-position. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a steroid sulfate, a 17-oxo steroid, an androstanoid, a 16alpha-hydroxy steroid and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a dehydroepiandrosterone. It is a conjugate acid of a 16alpha-hydroxydehydroepiandrosterone 3-sulfate(1-).
