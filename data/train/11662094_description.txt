The molecule is a member of the class of 7-hydroxyisoflavones that is isoflavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7, 3' and 4', a prenyl group at position 8 and a 2-hydroxy-3-methylbut-3-enyl moiety at position 6. Isolated from the leaves of Millettia pachycarpa, it exhibits antiestrogenic activity. It has a role as an anti-estrogen and a plant metabolite. It is a secondary alcohol and a member of 7-hydroxyisoflavones.
