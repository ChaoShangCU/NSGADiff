The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a beta-D-xylopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has been isolated from Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a quercetin O-glycoside, a tetrahydroxyflavone, a xylose derivative and a monosaccharide derivative.
