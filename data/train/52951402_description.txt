The molecule is a scalarane sesterterpenoid that is 12-epi-deoxoscalarin with an additional acetoxy group at position 23. It has been isolated from the sponge, Hyattella species. It has a role as an animal metabolite. It is an acetate ester, an organic heteropentacyclic compound and a scalarane sesterterpenoid. It derives from a 12-epi-deoxoscalarin.
