The molecule is a member of the class of acetamides obtained by formal condensation of acetic acid with the amino group of (S)-(1-aminoethyl)phosphonic acid. It is a member of acetamides and a member of phosphonic acids. It derives from a (S)-(1-aminoethyl)phosphonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a [(S)-1-acetamidoethyl]phosphonate(1-).
