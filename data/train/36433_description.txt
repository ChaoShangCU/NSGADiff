The molecule is a organic heterotetracyclic compound that is 10H-benzo[b]xanthene-7,10,12-trione substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 6 and 11, methoxy groups at positions 3 and 8 and a methyl group at position 1. It has a role as a fungal metabolite, an antifungal agent and an antibacterial agent. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a polyphenol, an aromatic ether, a cyclic ether and a cyclic ketone.
