The molecule is a phenethylamine alkaloid that is tyramine in which one of the amino hydrogens has been replaced by a 3,4-dihydroxybenzyl group. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a phenethylamine alkaloid, a polyphenol, a secondary amino compound and a member of catechols. It derives from a tyramine. It is a conjugate base of a norbelladine(1+).
