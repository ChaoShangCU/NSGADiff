The molecule is a benzoate ester resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3,5-dimethoxybenzoic acid with the hydroxy group of (-)-borneol. It is a dimethoxybenzene, a benzoate ester, a monoterpenoid and a carbobicyclic compound. It derives from a (-)-borneol and a 3,5-dimethoxybenzoic acid.
