The molecule is an epoxy fatty acid that is (7E,9E,11Z,14Z,16E)-icosa-7,9,11,14,16-pentaenoic acid carrying an epoxy group at position 5S and a hydroxy group at position 18S. It is an intermediate in resolvin E1 biosynthesis. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an epoxy fatty acid, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid and a long-chain fatty acid.
