The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of (2E,4Z)-deca-2,4-dienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a (2E,4Z)-dienoyl-CoA(4-), a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (2E,4Z)-deca-2,4-dienoyl-CoA.
