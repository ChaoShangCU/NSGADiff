The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin thatr is the beta-D-glucopyranosyl ester of 3,23-O-hydroxyethylidene-3beta,23-dihydroxyurs-12,19(20)-dien-28-oic acid. Isolated from Ilex oblonga, it exhibits activity against TMV. It has a role as an antiviral agent and a plant metabolite. It is a triterpenoid saponin, a carboxylic ester, a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a hexacyclic triterpenoid and an oxacycle.
