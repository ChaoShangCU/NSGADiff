The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that has hederagenin as the sapogenin. It has been isolated from the stem barks of Kalopanax pictus. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a primary alcohol and a triterpenoid saponin. It derives from a hederagenin. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
