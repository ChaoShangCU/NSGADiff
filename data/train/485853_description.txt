The molecule is a tetracyclic diterpenoid that is produced by several fungi including Sordaria araneosa. It has a role as a fungal metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a tetracyclic diterpenoid, a bridged compound, a 3-oxo monocarboxylic acid, an aldehyde and a primary alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of a sordaricin(1-).
