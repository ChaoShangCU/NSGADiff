The molecule is a hepoxilin having (5Z,9E,14Z) double bond stereochemistry, an (11S)-hydroxy substituent and a 14S,15S-epoxy group. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a hepoxilin, an epoxy fatty acid, a hydroxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a trienoic fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of an 11(S)-hydroxy-14(S),15(S)-hepoxilin A3(1-).
