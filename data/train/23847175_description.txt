The molecule is a member of the class of xanthenes that is the acetic anhydride of dihydrofluorescein diacetate carrying additional chloro substituents at positions 2 and 7 as well as a chloromethyl substituent meta to the anhydride function. It is a member of xanthenes, an acetate ester, an organochlorine compound and an acyclic carboxylic anhydride. It derives from a fluorescein.
