The molecule is a HETrE that is (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid substituted at position 19 by a hydroxy group. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a HETrE and an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 19-HETrE(1-).
