The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is apigenin substituted by a 6-O-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside group at position 7. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a disaccharide derivative, a glycosyloxyflavone and a gentiobioside. It derives from an apigenin.
