The molecule is a carbobicyclic compound that is decahydronaphthalene substituted at positions 2, 4a, 8 and 8a by 2-hydroxypropan-2-yl, methyl, methyl and hydroxy groups, respectively. The (3R,4aS,5S,8aS) stereoisomer. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a carbobicyclic compound, a eudesmane sesquiterpenoid, a tertiary alcohol and a diol.
