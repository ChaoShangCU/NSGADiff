The molecule is a dolichyl diphosphooligosaccharide in which the oligosaccharide moiety is the Man2GlcNAc2 linear tetrasaccharide alpha-D-Man-(1->3)-beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc. It is a dolichyl diphosphooligosaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide. It is a conjugate acid of an alpha-D-Man-(1->3)-beta-D-Man-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcNAc-(1->4)-D-GlcNAc(PP-Dol)(2-).
