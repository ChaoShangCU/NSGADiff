The molecule is a 1-(6-chloro-5-fluoroindol-1-yl)-propan-2-amine that has S configuration. A selective agonist for both the 5-hydroxytryptamine 2B (5-HT2B) and 5-hydroxytryptamine 2C (5-HT2C)serotonin receptor subtypes, commonly used as fumarate salt. It has a role as a 5-hydroxytryptamine 2B receptor agonist and a 5-hydroxytryptamine 2C receptor agonist.
