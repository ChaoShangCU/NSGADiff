The molecule is an organic nitrate salt obtained by reaction of equimolar amounts of butaconazole and nitric acid. An antifungal agent, it is used in gynaecology for treatment of vulvovaginal infections caused by Candida species, particularly Candida albicans. It is an organic nitrate salt, an aryl sulfide, a member of imidazoles, an imidazole antifungal drug and a conazole antifungal drug. It contains a butoconazole(1+).
