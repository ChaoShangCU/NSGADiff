The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (4Z,8E,10Z,12E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the hydroperoxy group is located at position 14S. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a docosanoid, a hydroperoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a lipid hydroperoxide. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,8E,10Z,12E,14S,16Z,19Z)-14-hydroperoxydocosahexaenoate.
