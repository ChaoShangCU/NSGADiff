The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is lithocholic acid having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 3. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a dicarboxylic acid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a lithocholic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a lithocholate 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(2-).
