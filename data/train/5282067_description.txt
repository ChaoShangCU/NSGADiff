The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is cyanidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(trans-4 coumaryl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue It is an anthocyanin cation, a beta-D-glucoside, a cinnamate ester, a polyphenol and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a cyanidin cation and a trans-4-coumaric acid.
