The molecule is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid consisting of (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosa-5,9,14-trienoic acid having additional (13R)-hydroxy- and (14S,15S)-epoxy groups. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (13R)-hydroxy-(14S,15S)-epoxyicosa-(5Z,8Z,11Z)-trienoate.
