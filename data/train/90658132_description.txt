The molecule is a cis-3-enoyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of cis-3-octenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a cis-3-enoyl-CoA(4-), a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a cis-3-octenoyl-CoA.
