The molecule is an aromatic amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-methylthiophene-2-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 2-amino-1-(4-isopropoxy-2-methylphenyl)-2-methylpropan-1-one. It has a role as an EC 1.3.5.1 [succinate dehydrogenase (quinone)] inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical. It is an aromatic ether, an aromatic amide, a member of thiophenes, an aromatic ketone and an amide fungicide.
