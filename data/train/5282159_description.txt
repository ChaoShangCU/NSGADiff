The molecule is a quercetin O-glucoside that is quercetin attached to a 6-O-malonyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a metabolite. It is a quercetin O-glucoside, a malonate ester, a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a tetrahydroxyflavone.
