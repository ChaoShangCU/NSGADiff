The molecule is a 20-oxo steroid that is pregnenolone carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at the 7alpha-position. It has a role as a rat metabolite, a marine metabolite, a nootropic agent and a dopaminergic agent. It is a 20-oxo steroid, a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid, a C21-steroid and a 7alpha-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a pregnenolone.
