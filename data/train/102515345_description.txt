The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is (24Z)-stigmasta-7,24(28)-diene which is substituted at the 3beta and 4alpha positions by hydroxy and formyl groups, respectively. It is a member of phytosterols, a 3beta-sterol, a Delta(7)-sterol and a 4alpha-formyl steroid. It derives from a 4alpha-formyl-ergosta-7,24(28)-dien-3beta-ol and a 4alpha-hydroxymethyl-stigmasta-7,24(24(1))-dien-3beta-ol.
