The molecule is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid that is (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid having the epoxide group across positions 14-15 and the hydroxy substituent located at position 13. It is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol. It is a conjugate acid of a 13-hydroxy-14,15-epoxy-(5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoate.
