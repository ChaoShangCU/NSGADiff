The molecule is an icosanoid anion resulting from the removal of a proton from the carboxy group of 18-oxoresolvin E1; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an icosanoid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an oxo fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 18-oxoresolvin E1.
