The molecule is a brassinosteroid that is castasterone carrying an alpha-D-glucosyl residue at position O-23. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a 2alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 22-hydroxy steroid, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 6-oxo steroid, a brassinosteroid, a steroid saponin and an alpha-D-glucoside. It derives from a castasterone.
