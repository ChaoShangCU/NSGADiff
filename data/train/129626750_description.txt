The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy, thiocarboxy and phosphate OH groups of pyridinium-3-carboxy-5-thiocarboxylic acid mononucleotide; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organic sulfur anion, an organophosphate oxoanion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a pyridinium-3-carboxy-5-thiocarboxylic acid mononucleotide.
