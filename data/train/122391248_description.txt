The molecule is a zwitterion obtained by transfer of a proton from the 2-hydroxy group to the 1-amino group of 11a-hydroxytetracycline. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a zwitterion and an an 11a-hydroxytetracyline zwitterion. It is a tautomer of an 11a-hydroxytetracycline.
