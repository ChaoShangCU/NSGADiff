The molecule is a terpenoid indole alkaloid that is 1H-indole substituted by cyclohexylmethyl ring at position 3 which in turn is substituted by additional methyl groups at positions 2 and 3, a propan-2-ylidene group at position 6 and a 2-carboxyethyl group at position 1. Isolated from Greenwayodendron suaveolens, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is a terpenoid indole alkaloid, a sesquiterpenoid and a monocarboxylic acid.
