The molecule is an organic cation obtained by protonation of the 5-amino group of 5-amino-5-(4-hydroxybenzyl)-6-((2,3,4,5-tetrahydroxypentyl)amino)dihydropyrimidine-2,4-dione. It is an ammonium ion derivative and an organic cation. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-amino-5-(4-hydroxybenzyl)-6-(D-ribitylimino)-5,6-dihydrouracil.
