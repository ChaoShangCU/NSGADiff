The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone that is decahydroazuleno[6,5-b]furan-2(3H)-one substituted by acetyloxy groups at positions 5 and 7, a hydroxy group at position 4, methyl groups at positions 4a and 8 and a methylidene group at position 3. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Inula hupehensis. It has a role as a metabolite, an anti-inflammatory agent and a plant metabolite. It is a gamma-lactone, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a sesquiterpene lactone, an acetate ester and a secondary alcohol.
