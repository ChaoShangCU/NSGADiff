The molecule is an N-acyl-L-phenylalanine resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of arachidonic acid with the amino group of L-phenylalanine. It is a N-acyl-L-phenylalanine and a fatty amide. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-arachidonoyl-L-phenylalaninate.
