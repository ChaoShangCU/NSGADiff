The molecule is a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid that is L-phenylalanine in which the phenyl ring is methylated at positions 2, 4 and 6. It is a 2,4,6-trimethylphenylalanine, a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid and a L-phenylalanine derivative. It is an enantiomer of a D-2,4,6-trimethylphenylalanine.
