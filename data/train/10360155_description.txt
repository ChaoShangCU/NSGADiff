The molecule is a class I yanuthone that is 5,6-epoxy-4-hydroxy-3-methylcyclohex-2-en-1-one which is substituted at position 6 by an (E,E)-3,7,11-trimethyldodeca-2,6,10-trien-1-yl group (the R,R,R stereoisomer). It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a class I yanuthone and a secondary alcohol.
