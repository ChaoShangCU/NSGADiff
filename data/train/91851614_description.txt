The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of alpha-D-mannopyranosyl, beta-D-mannopyranosyl, 2-acetamido-alpha-D-glucopyranosyl and alpha-D-mannopyranosyl residues joined in sequence by (1->2), (1->3) and (1->6) glycosidic linkages. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
