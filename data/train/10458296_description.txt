The molecule is a member of the class of chalcones that is trans-chalcone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 4, 2' and 4', a prenyl group at position 3' and a 6-methyl-6-(4-methylpent-3-en-1-yl)-3,6-dihydro-2H-pyran ring fused across positions 5' and 6' It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of chalcones, a chromenol and a polyphenol.
