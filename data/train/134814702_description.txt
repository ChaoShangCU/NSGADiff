The molecule is an eremorphilane sesquiterpenoid that is (+)-5-epi-aristolochene in which the pro-R hydrogen at position 2 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is a carbobicyclic compound, an eremophilane sesquiterpenoid, a member of octahydronaphthalenes, a secondary alcohol and a homoallylic alcohol. It derives from a (+)-5-epi-aristolochene.
