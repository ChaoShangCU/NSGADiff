The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of beta-D-galactopyranosyl, 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-2-O-sulfo-beta-D-glucopyranosyl and 2-acetamido-2-deoxygalactitol residues linked together in sequence by (1->3) glycosidic bonds. It is a N-acyl-hexosamine, an amino trisaccharide, a member of acetamides and an oligosaccharide sulfate.
