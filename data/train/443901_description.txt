The molecule is a derivative of vitexin having a alpha-L-rhamnosyl residue attached at the 2''-position of the glucitol moiety and a methyl group attached at the O-7 position of the chromene. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone, a disaccharide derivative and a C-glycosyl compound. It derives from a vitexin. It is a conjugate acid of a 7-O-methylvitexin 2''-O-alpha-L-rhamnoside(1-).
