The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C24H39NO9 that is isolated from the roots of Aconitum carmichaelii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound and a pentol. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
