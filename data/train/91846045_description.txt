The molecule is an amino disaccharide composed of D-galactose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranosyl residues in (alpha-1->3) linkage. It is an amino disaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a glycosylglucose derivative. It derives from a N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine and an alpha-D-galactose.
