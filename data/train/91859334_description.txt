The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of 2-amino-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose and 2-amino-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->6) glycosidic bond. It is an aminoglycoside, an amino disaccharide and a primary amino compound. It derives from a 2-amino-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose and an octadeca-9,11-dienoate.
