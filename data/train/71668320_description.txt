The molecule is an octadecenoyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of (6Z)-octadecenoic acid. It is an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA and an octadecenoyl-CoA. It derives from a petroselinic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (6Z)-octadecenoyl-CoA(4-).
