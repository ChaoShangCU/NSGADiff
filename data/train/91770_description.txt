The molecule is a pyridinemonocarboxylic acid that is 5-methylpyridine-3-carboxylic acid which is substituted at position 2 by a 4,5-dihydro-imidazol-2-yl group, which in turn is substituted at positions 4, 4, and 5 by isopropyl, methyl, and oxo groups, respectively. It is a pyridinemonocarboxylic acid, an imidazolone, a member of imidazolines and a member of methylpyridines.
