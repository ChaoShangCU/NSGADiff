The molecule is a (14)C-modified compound that is erythromycin A in which one of the methyl groups of the dimethylnitrilo group has a (14)C label. It is used in a breath test to detect liver cytochrome P450 enzyme (CYP3A4) activity in humans. It has a role as a probe. It is an erythromycin, a cyclic ketone and a (14)C-modified compound.
