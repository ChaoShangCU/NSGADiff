The molecule is an indolyl carboxylate ester obtained by formal condensation between the carboxy group of 1-methylindole-3-carboxylic acid with the hydroxy group of N-{2-[4-(hydroxymethyl)piperidin-1-yl]ethyl}methanesulfonamide. It has a role as a serotonergic antagonist. It is an indolyl carboxylate ester, a member of piperidines and a sulfonamide.
