The molecule is a quinoline having a chloro group at the 7-position and an aryl amino group at the 4-position. It has a role as an antimalarial, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, a drug allergen, a prodrug, an EC 2.1.1.8 (histamine N-methyltransferase) inhibitor and an anticoronaviral agent. It is a member of phenols, an aminoquinoline, a secondary amino compound, a tertiary amino compound and an organochlorine compound. It is a conjugate base of an amodiaquine(1+).
