The molecule is a neolignan that is 5-[(1E)-prop-1-en-1-yl]-2,3-dihydro-1-benzofuran carrying additional 3,4-dimethoxyphenyl, methyl and methoxy substituents at positions 2, 3 and 7 respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a fungal metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, a dimethoxybenzene, a ring assembly, an olefinic compound and a neolignan.
