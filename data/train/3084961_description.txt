The molecule is the glycosyloxyflavone which is the 7-O-glucuronide of wogonin. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone, a monohydroxyflavone, a monosaccharide derivative and a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a wogonin. It is a conjugate acid of a wogonin 7-O-beta-D-glucuronate.
