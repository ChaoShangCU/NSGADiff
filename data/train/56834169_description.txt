The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone that is flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 7, a 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-2-en-1-yl group at position 8 and 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-2-en-3-yl groups at position 6. It has been isolated as a racemate from the bark of Cryptocarya chartacea and exhibits inhibitory activity against dengue virus NS5 polymerase. It has a role as an antiviral agent and a plant metabolite. It is a dicarboxylic acid and a dihydroxyflavanone.
