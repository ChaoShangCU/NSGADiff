The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is the C-4 epimer of hederagenin. It has been isolated from the roots of Rubia yunnanensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a sapogenin. It derives from an oleanolic acid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
