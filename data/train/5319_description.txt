The molecule is a sulfonamide containing a benzamido substituent on nitrogen. An antibacterial/antimicrobial, it is often used in conjunction with sulfathiazole and sulfacetamide as a topical, intravaginal antibacterial preparation. It has a role as an antibacterial drug and an antimicrobial drug. It is a sulfonamide, a member of benzenes and a sulfonamide antibiotic.
