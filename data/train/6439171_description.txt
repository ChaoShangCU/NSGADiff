The molecule is a C-type aurachin that is quinolin-4-one which is substituted by a hydroxy group at positions 1, a methyl group at position 2, and a triprenyl group at position 3. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite, an EC 1.8.5.4 (sulfide:quinone reductase) inhibitor and an antibacterial agent. It is a C-type aurachin, a member of hydroxylamines, a quinolone and an organic heterobicyclic compound.
