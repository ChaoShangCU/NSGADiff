The molecule is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate having adenine as the nucleobase. It has a role as a fundamental metabolite. It is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate and a 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-monophosphate(2-).
