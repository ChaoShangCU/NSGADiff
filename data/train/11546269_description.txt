The molecule is a member of the class of coumestans that is 3,4-didehydroterocarpan substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 7 and 4', a methoxy group at position 5 and prenyl groups at positions 6 and 5'. Isolated from the roots of Glycyrrhiza uralensis, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as an antibacterial agent and a plant metabolite. It is a polyphenol, a member of coumestans and an aromatic ether.
