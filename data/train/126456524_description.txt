The molecule is a fatty acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of (9Z)-hexadecenoic acid with the hydroxy group of 5-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a fatty acid ester and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a palmitoleic acid and a 5-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyloxy]octadecanoate.
