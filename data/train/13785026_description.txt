The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is abieta-8,11,13-trien-20-al substituted by a hydroxy group at position 12. It has been isolated from the stem bark of Fraxinus sieboldiana. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, an aldehyde, a member of phenols and a tricyclic diterpenoid.
