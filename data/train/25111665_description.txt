The molecule is a polycyclic cage that is platensimycin with a 1,3-oxazinane ring fused onto the aromatic ring. It is isolated from Streptomyces platensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, a polycyclic cage, a benzoxazine, an aromatic amide and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from a platensimycin.
