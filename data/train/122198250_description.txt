The molecule is a docosanoid that is (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid carrying a hydroperoxy substituent at position 17. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators. It is a docosanoid, a hydroperoxy fatty acid and a long-chain fatty acid. It derives from a (7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-17-hydroperoxydocosapentaenoate.
