The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-isobutoxyphenoxyacetic acid with the amino group of 4-methoxyaniline. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a glycerophosphoinositol synthesis inhibitor. It is a monocarboxylic acid amide, an aromatic ether and an aromatic amide. It derives from a phenylacetic acid.
