The molecule is a (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-17-hydroperoxydocosapentaenoate in which the chiral centre at position 17 has S-configuration. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators. It is a docosanoid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,17S,19Z)-17-hydroperoxydocosapentaenoic acid.
