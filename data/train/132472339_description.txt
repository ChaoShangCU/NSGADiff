The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-7,17-bis(hydroperoxy)docosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an (8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-7,17-bis(hydroperoxy)docosapentaenoic acid.
