The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is urs-12-ene substituted by an oxo group at position 3, a carboxy group at position 28 and a hydroxy group at position 23. It has been isolated from Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a cyclic terpene ketone and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a hydride of an ursane.
