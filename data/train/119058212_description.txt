The molecule is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion that is the conjugate base of N-[(15S)-hydroperoxy-(5Z,8Z,11Z,13E)-icosatetraenoyl]alanine, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3.
