The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound obtained by intramolecular formation of a C-C bond between positions 8 and 5' of 2'-deoxyguanosine. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, an aromatic amine, a bridged compound, a diol and an organic heterotetracyclic compound. It derives from a 2'-deoxyguanosine.
