The molecule is a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate having beta-D-glucopyranosyloxymethyluracil (base J) as the nucleobase It has a role as a eukaryotic metabolite. It is a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a 5-hydroxymethyluracil.
