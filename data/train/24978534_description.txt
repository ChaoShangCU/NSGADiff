The molecule is a maleate salt obtained by reaction of 2-{[(2E)-3-iodoprop-2-en-1-yl](propyl)amino}tetralin-7-ol with one equivalent of maleic acid. This ligand has uniquely high affinity and selectivity for the D3 receptor. It has a role as a dopamine agonist. It contains a 2-{[(2E)-3-iodoprop-2-en-1-yl](propyl)ammonio}tetralin-7-ol(1+).
