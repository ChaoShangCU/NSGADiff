The molecule is a chloro-1,3,5-triazine that is 6-chloro-1,3,5-triazine-2,4-diamine in which one of the hydrogens of the amino group is replaced by a propan-2-yl group. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a chloro-1,3,5-triazine and a diamino-1,3,5-triazine. It derives from a 6-chloro-1,3,5-triazine-2,4-diamine.
