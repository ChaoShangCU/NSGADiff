The molecule is a chloro-1,3,5-triazine that is 2-chloro-1,3,5-triazine substituted by an ethyl amino and a (2-cyanopropan-2-yl)amino group at positions 6 and 4 respectively. It has a role as a herbicide, an environmental contaminant and a xenobiotic. It is a 1,3,5-triazinylamino nitrile and a chloro-1,3,5-triazine.
