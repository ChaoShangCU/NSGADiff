The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-18-ene substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 3 and oxo group at position 1. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a secondary alcohol and a cyclic terpene ketone. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
