The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3, 6 and 7 and a 1,5-anhydro-D-glucitol moiety at position 1. It has a role as an anti-HSV-1 agent and a plant metabolite. It is a member of xanthones, a C-glycosyl compound and a polyphenol.
