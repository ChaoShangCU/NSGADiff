The molecule is an isoquinoline alkaloid that is pyrano[2,3,4-ij]isoquinoline substituted by a hydroxy group at position 8 and methyl groups at positions 2 and 5. It is isolated from the leaves of Cassia siamea and exhibits antiplasmodial activity against Plasmodium falciparum. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is an isoquinoline alkaloid, a member of isoquinolines, a member of phenols, a cyclic ether and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
