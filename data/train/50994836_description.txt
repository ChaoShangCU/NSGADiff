The molecule is a beta-glucoside having 4-hydroxyphenoxy residue as the anomeric substituent and a [(2E)-3-(1,4-dihydroxy-6-oxocyclohex-2-en-1-yl)prop-2-enoyl]oxy residue at position 6. Isolated from Grevillea, it exhibits antimalarial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is an enoate ester, a member of phenols, a monosaccharide derivative, a beta-D-glucoside and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
