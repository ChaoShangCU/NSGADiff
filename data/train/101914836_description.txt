The molecule is a carboxylic ester resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of indole-3-acetic acid with the hydroxy group of 2-phenylethanol. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a fungal metabolite. It is a member of indoles and a carboxylic ester. It derives from a 2-phenylethanol and an indole-3-acetic acid.
