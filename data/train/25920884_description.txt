The molecule is a member of the class of phenylureas obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of (3-{5-[4-(aminomethyl)phenyl]pyrrolo[2,3-b]pyridin-3-yl}phenyl)carbamic acid with the amino group of 2-phenoxyaniline. An inhibitor of tyrosine kinase. It has a role as an EC 2.7.10.1 (receptor protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor. It is a pyrrolopyridine, an aromatic ether, a primary amino compound and a member of phenylureas.
