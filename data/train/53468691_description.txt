The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is 1-benzofuran substituted by a methyl group at position 3, a 3-methylbutyl group at position 2 and a carboxy group at position 6. An Aspergillus metabolite isolated from the sea fan derived fungus Aspergillus sydowii. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, a sesquiterpenoid and a monocarboxylic acid.
