The molecule is a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 2,6-dimethylheptanoic acid. It is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA. It is a conjugate acid of a 2,6-dimethylheptanoyl-CoA(4-).
