The molecule is a vinca alkaloid that is vindoline in which the acetate ester group at position 17 has been hydrolysed to give the corresponding secondary alcohol. It is a vinca alkaloid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a methyl ester, a tertiary alcohol and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a vindoline. It is a conjugate base of a 17-O-deacetylvindolinium.
