The molecule is an indoleacetic acid amide conjugate obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of indole-3-acetic acid with the amino group of glutamic acid. It is a N-acylglutamic acid and an indoleacetic acid amide conjugate. It derives from an indole-3-acetic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-(indole-3-acetyl)glutamate(2-).
