The molecule is a diester compound consisting of pentane-1,5-diol with both hydroxyls bearing 3-[1-(3,4-dimethoxybenzyl)-6,7-dimethoxy-2-methyl-3,4-dihydroisoquinolinium-2(1H)-yl]propanoyl groups. It has a role as a muscle relaxant and a nicotinic antagonist. It is a quaternary ammonium ion and a diester.
