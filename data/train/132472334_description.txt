The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (7S,17S)-bis(hydroperoxy)-(4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7S,17S)-bis(hydroperoxy)-(4Z,8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid.
