The molecule is a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 17(R)-HDoHE arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a hydroxydocosahexaenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 17(R)-HDoHE.
