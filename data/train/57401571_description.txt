The molecule is a meroterpenoid found in Penicillium rubrum and has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against caspase-1. It has a role as a cysteine protease inhibitor and a Penicillium metabolite. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a meroterpenoid, a carbotricyclic compound, a tertiary alcohol, a methyl ester, a beta-diketone, a dicarboxylic acid monoester and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
