The molecule is an L-cysteine derivative that is the amide obtained by formal condensation of one of the carboxy groups of L-cystine with the amino group of 2-naphthylamine. It is a N-(2-naphthyl)carboxamide, a L-cysteine derivative, an amino acid amide, a dicarboxylic acid monoamide and an organic disulfide. It derives from a L-cystine.
