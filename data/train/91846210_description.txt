The molecule is an amino trisacharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 2 have been converted into the corresponding 2-O-methyl-beta-D-galactopyranosyl and alpha-L-fucopyranosyl derivatives, respectively. It is a member of acetamides and an amino trisaccharide.
