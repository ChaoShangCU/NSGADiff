The molecule is a limonoid that is salannin in which the furanyl group at position 17 is replaced by an oxo group. Isolated from Azadirachta indica, it exhibits anti-inflammatory activity. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a limonoid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, an enone and a methyl ester. It derives from a tiglic acid and a salannin.
