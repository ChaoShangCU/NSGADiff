The molecule is a tetracenomycin that is 1-methyl-6,11-dioxo-6,11-dihydrotetracene-2-carboxylic acid bearing four hydroxy substituents at positions 3, 8, 10 and 12. It is a tetracenomycin, a member of tetracenequinones, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a member of phenols. It is a conjugate acid of a tetracenomycin D3(1-).
