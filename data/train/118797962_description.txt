The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside obtained by obtained by formal condensation of the alcoholic hydroxy group of (2E,24R)-2-methylpentacos-2-enoic acid with ascarylopyranose (the alpha anomer). It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid.
