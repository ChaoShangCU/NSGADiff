The molecule is a glycosyl alditol derivative consisting of N-acetyllactosamine and N-acetyl-D-galactosaminitol joined in sequence by a (1->6) glycosidic bond. It is a glycosyl alditol derivative, a glycoside and a member of acetamides. It derives from a N-acetyl-D-galactosaminitol and a N-acetyllactosamine.
