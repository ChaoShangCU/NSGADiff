The molecule is a 3-hydroxy steroid that is estrone substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 6. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 6beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17-oxo steroid, a 3-hydroxy steroid and a member of phenols. It derives from an estrone. It derives from a hydride of an estrane.
