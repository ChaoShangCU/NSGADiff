The molecule is a dipeptide that is N-[(2S)-2-aminobutanoyl]glycine in which the terminal amino group has been acylated by formal condensation of the 5-carboxy group of 2-oxopentanedioic acid. It is a dipeptide, a secondary carboxamide and an oxo dicarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-(4-carboxy-4-oxobutanoyl)-L-ethylglycylglycine(2-).
