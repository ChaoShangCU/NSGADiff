The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide with formula C27H40ClNO7, that is produced by Chondromyces crocatus and exhibits antibiotic properties. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is an enone, a diol, a secondary alcohol, an ether, a monocarboxylic acid amide, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of phenols.
