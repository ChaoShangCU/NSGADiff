The molecule is a hydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone that is flaviolin in which the hydrogen at position 3 is replaced by a linalyl group. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of phenols and a hydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone. It derives from a flaviolin. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-linalylflaviolin-2-olate.
