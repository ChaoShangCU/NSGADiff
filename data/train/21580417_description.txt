The molecule is a meroterpenoid found in Penicillium rubrum. It has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against caspase-1. It has a role as a cysteine protease inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and a Penicillium metabolite. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a meroterpenoid, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a terpene lactone, a tertiary alcohol, a methyl ester, a beta-diketone and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
