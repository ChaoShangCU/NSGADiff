The molecule is an ultra-long-chain fatty acid that is (13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z)-octacosatetraenoic acid substituted at position 2 by a hydroxy group. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid. It derives from a (13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z)-octacosatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z)-2-hydroxyoctacosatetraenoate.
