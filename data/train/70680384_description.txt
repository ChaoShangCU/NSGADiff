The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of 3-hydroxyicosanoyl-CoA. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-), an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 3-hydroxyicosanoyl-CoA.
