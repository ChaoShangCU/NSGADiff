The molecule is a flavanone glycoside that is hesperidin in which the hydroxy group at position 3' has been replaced by a methoxy group. It is a monohydroxyflavanone, a dimethoxyflavanone, a disaccharide derivative, a flavanone glycoside, a rutinoside, a member of 4'-methoxyflavanones and a member of 3'-methoxyflavanones. It derives from a hesperidin.
