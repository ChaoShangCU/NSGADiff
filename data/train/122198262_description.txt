The molecule is a class I yanuthone that is 22-deacetoxyyanuthone A in which one of the hydrogens of the trans-terminal methyl groups of the sesquiterpenoid side-chain has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a class I yanuthone and a triol. It derives from a 22-deacetylyanuthone A.
