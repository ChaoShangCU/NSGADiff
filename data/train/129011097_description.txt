The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 4-hydroxyestrone having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 4. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a 17-oxo steroid, a 3-hydroxy steroid and a steroid glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a 4-hydroxyestrone. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-hydroxyestrone 4-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
