The molecule is a carbohydrate acid anion arising from deprotonation of both carboxy groups of 4-(alpha-D-galactopyranuronosyl)-D-galactopyranuronic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is a carbohydrate acid anion, a dicarboxylic acid dianion and a digalacturonate. It is a conjugate base of an alpha-D-GalpA-(1->4)-D-GalpA.
