The molecule is a macrolide that is a 14-memebered macrocycle fused to a 3-methoxyphenol ring. Isolated from Fungi and Cochliobolus lunatus, it exhibits antibacterial and inhibitory activity against NF-kappaB. It has a role as a metabolite, an antibacterial agent and a NF-kappaB inhibitor. It is a macrolide, a member of phenols, a secondary alcohol and an aromatic ether.
