The molecule is a trihydroxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 5 and 7 and a prenyl group at position 8 respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavanone, a member of dihydroflavonols and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a (2S)-flavanone.
