The molecule is a monosaccharide derivative that is (-)-11-hydroxy-9,10-dihydrojasmonic acid attached to a beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 11 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a dihydrojasmonic acid. It derives from a (-)-11-hydroxy-9,10-dihydrojasmonic acid.
