The molecule is a quassinoid isolated from Simaba guianensis and has been shown to exhibit antimalarial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is a delta-lactone, a cyclic ether, an enone, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a quassinoid, a triol, an enoate ester and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a 3-methylbut-2-enoic acid.
