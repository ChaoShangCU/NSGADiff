The molecule is a member of the class of imidazoquinolines that is 3-methyl-2-oxo-2,3-dihydro-1H-imidazo[4,5-c]quinoline substituted at position 1 by a 4-(1-cyanoisopropyl)phenyl group and at position 8 by a pyrimidin-5-yl group. It is a member of pyrimidines, a nitrile, an imidazoquinoline and a member of ureas.
