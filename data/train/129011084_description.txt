The molecule is a steroid glucuronide anion that is the conjugate base of 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone 17-O-[beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->2)-glucuronide] arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid functions; major species at pH 7.3. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone 17-O-[beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->2)-glucuronide].
