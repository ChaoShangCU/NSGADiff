The molecule is a limonoid that is azadiradione substituted by a hydroxy group at position 15. It has been isolated from Azadirachta indica. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a member of furans, a limonoid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and an enol. It derives from an azadiradione.
