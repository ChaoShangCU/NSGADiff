The molecule is an organic calcium salt having [3-(4-chlorophenyl)-1-phenylpyrazol-4-yl]acetate as the counterion. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an EC 1.14.99.1 (prostaglandin-endoperoxide synthase) inhibitor, a non-narcotic analgesic and a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug. It contains a lonazolac(1-).
