The molecule is an amino cyclitol glycoside that is (1R,2S,3S,4S,5S,6R)-2-amino-1,3,4,6-tetrahydroxy-5-(methylamino)cyclohexane in which the hydroxy group at position 1 is glycosylated by a heptapyranosyl residue. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an amino cyclitol glycoside, an aminoglycoside antibiotic and an aldoheptose derivative.
