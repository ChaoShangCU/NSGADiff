The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid resulting from the replacement of the 3'-methyl group of the phenyl ring of torasemide by a carboxy group. It is a metabolite of torasemide. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite, a drug metabolite and a human urinary metabolite. It is a secondary amino compound, an aminopyridine, a N-sulfonylurea, a member of benzoic acids and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a torasemide.
