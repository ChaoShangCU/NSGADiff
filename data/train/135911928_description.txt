The molecule is a 1-deoxy-D-ribitol-5-phosphate having a (5,6-diamino-4-oxopyrimidin-2-yl)amino group at the 1-position. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, an aminopyrimidine, a pyrimidone and a ribitol phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a 2,5-diamino-6-(1-D-ribitylamino)pyrimidin-4(3H)-one 5'-phosphate(2-).
