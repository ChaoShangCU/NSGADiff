The molecule is a spiruchostatin with molecular formula C21H33N3O6S2 originally isolated from a Pseudomonas culture broth. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite, an EC 3.5.1.98 (histone deacetylase) inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is a spiruchostatin, an organic heterobicyclic compound, an organic disulfide and a macrocyclic lactone.
