The molecule is a member of the class of 1-benzofurans that is egonol in which the methoxy group at position 7 is replaced by a hydrogen. It has been isolated from the fruits of Styrax agrestis. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, a member of benzodioxoles and a primary alcohol. It derives from an egonol. It derives from a hydride of a 1-benzofuran.
