The molecule is an ergostanoid that is (5alpha)-ergosta-8,24(28)-diene substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 3 and an oxo group at position 7. It has been isolated from Aspergillus ochraceus. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 7-oxo steroid and an ergostanoid. It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-ergostane.
