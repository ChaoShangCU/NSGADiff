The molecule is a trihydroxyflavone that is isoscoparin glycosylated at position 2'' on the glucosyl ring by an alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monomethoxyflavone, a trihydroxyflavone, a C-glycosyl compound and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from an isoscoparin.
