The molecule is a glycinyl ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of glycinne with the 3'-hydroxy group of AMP. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is an adenosine 5'-phosphate, a glycinyl ester and a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate.
