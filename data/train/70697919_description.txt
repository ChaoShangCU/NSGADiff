The molecule is a furanocoumarin that is 7H-furo[3,2-g]chromen-7-one substituted by a (2,2,5,5-tetramethyl-1,3-dioxolan-4-yl)methoxy moiety at position 4. Isolated from Peucedanum turcomanicum and Angelica dahurica, it exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a furanocoumarin, an aromatic ether and a dioxolane.
