The molecule is a eudesmane sesquiterpenoid that is octahydro-2H-3,9a-methano-1-benzoxepine substituted by methyl groups at positions 2, 2, 5a and 9 (the 3R,5aS,9R,9aS stereoisomer). It has a role as a metabolite. It is an organic heterotricyclic compound, a bridged compound, a eudesmane sesquiterpenoid and a cyclic ether.
