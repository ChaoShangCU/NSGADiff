The molecule is a 17-oxo steroid that is andrastin D in which the keto group at position 3 has undergone formal reduction to give the corresponding 3beta-hydroxy compound. It is a 15-hydroxy steroid, a 17-oxo steroid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 5beta steroid, an enol, a meroterpenoid, a methyl ester and a 3beta-hydroxy-4,4-dimethylsteroid. It derives from an andrastin D. It is a conjugate acid of an andrastin F(1-).
