The molecule is a lysophosphatidylinositol 18:0(1-) that is a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-D-myo-inositol which has octadecanoyl as the acyl group and a free hydroxy group at position 2 of the glycerol moiety. It is a lysophosphatidylinositol 18:0(1-) and a 1-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-1D-myo-inositol(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 1-stearoyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-1D-myo-inositol.
