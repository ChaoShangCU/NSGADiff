The molecule is a member of the class of piperazines that is piperazine in which the pro-S hydrogens at positions 2 and 5 have each been replaced by a p-hydroxybenzyl group. It is a secondary amino compound, a polyphenol and a member of piperazines. It is a conjugate base of a (S,S)-2,5-di-(p-hydroxybenzyl)piperazine(1+).
