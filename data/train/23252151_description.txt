The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by a (3,3-dimethyoxiran-2-yl)methyl group at position 5, a hydroxy group at position 8, a hydroxymethyl group at position 1, a methyl group at position 3 and a prenyloxy group at position 2. It has been isolated from Aspergillus. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a member of xanthones, a member of phenols, an epoxide and an aromatic primary alcohol.
