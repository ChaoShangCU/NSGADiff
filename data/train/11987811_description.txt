The molecule is a 3-oxo Delta(4)-steroid that is pregn-4-en-3-one carrying two hydroxy substituents at positions 20 and 21. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and an EC 1.3.1.22 [3-oxo-5alpha-steroid 4-dehydrogenase (NADP(+))] inhibitor. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 20-hydroxy steroid and a 21-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
