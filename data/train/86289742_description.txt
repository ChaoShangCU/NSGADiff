The molecule is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside derived from (5R)-5-hydroxyhexanoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside, a monocarboxylic acid and an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside. It derives from an ascr#12 and a (5R)-5-hydroxyhexanoic acid.
