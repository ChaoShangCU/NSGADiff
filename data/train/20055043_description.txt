The molecule is a member of the class of cardenolides that is 7,8-epoxycard-20(22)-enolide substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 14 (the 3beta,5beta,7beta stereoisomer). It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a metabolite. It is a member of cardenolides, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, an epoxy steroid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid and a 14beta-hydroxy steroid.
