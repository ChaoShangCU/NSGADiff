The molecule is a tertiary amino compound that is p-methoxybenzylamine in which the hydrogens attached to the amino group have been replaced by methyl and 3-methyloxetan-3-yl groups. It is a member of oxetanes, a tertiary amino compound and an aromatic ether. It is a conjugate base of a 1-(4-methoxyphenyl)-N-methyl-N-[(3-methyloxetan-3-yl)methyl]methanamine(1+).
