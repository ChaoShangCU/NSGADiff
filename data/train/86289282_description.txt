The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of 2-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-) and an oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-). It is a conjugate base of a 2-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate.
