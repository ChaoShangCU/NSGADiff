The molecule is a member of the class of cinnamamides obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of ferulic acid with the amino group of agmatine. It has a role as an antifungal agent, an EC 5.99.1.2 (DNA topoisomerase) inhibitor and a plant metabolite. It is an alkaloid and a 4-hydroxy-3-methoxycinnamoylagmatine. It derives from an agmatine and a ferulic acid.
