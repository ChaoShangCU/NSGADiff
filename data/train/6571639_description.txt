The molecule is a member of the class of 2,5-diketopiperazines that is piperazine-2,5-dione substituted by 1H-indol-3-ylmethyl groups at positions 3 and 6 respectively. Isolated from Penicillium species, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as an antibacterial agent and a Penicillium metabolite. It is a member of 2,5-diketopiperazines and an indole alkaloid.
