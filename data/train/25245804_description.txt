The molecule is a long-chain unsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (3E,5Z)-tetradecadienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a straight-chain fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (3E,5Z)-tetradecadienoic acid.
