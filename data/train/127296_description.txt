The molecule is a myo-inositol pentakisphosphate that consists of myo-inositol having the five phospho groups located at positions 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 as well as a diphospho group at position 1. It is a myo-inositol pentakisphosphate and a diphosphoinositol polyphosphate. It derives from a myo-inositol. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-diphospho-1D-myo-inositol 2,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate(13-).
