The molecule is a benzoate ester obtained by the formal condensation of the hydroxy group of 5-(8-hydroxyundecyl)benzene-1,3-diol with 4,6-dihydroxy benzoic acid which is also substituted by a 8-hydroxyundecyl group at position 2. It is isolated from Cytonaema sp. and has anti-HIV-1 activity. It has a role as a metabolite and a HIV-1 integrase inhibitor. It is a member of resorcinols, a benzoate ester and a secondary alcohol.
