The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (11Z)-18-hydroxyoctadecenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of an (11Z)-18-hydroxyoctadecenoyl-CoA.
