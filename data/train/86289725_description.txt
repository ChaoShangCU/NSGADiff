The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid that is trans-2-icosenoic acid in which the pro-R hydrogen at position 19 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and a hydroxy monounsaturated fatty acid. It derives from a trans-2-icosenoic acid.
