The molecule is a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 3'-monophosphate that is the mono-(5-bromo-4-chloro-indol-3-yl) ester of thymidine 3'-phosphate. It has a role as a chromogenic compound. It is a bromoindole, a chloroindole and a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 3'-monophosphate. It derives from a thymidine 3'-monophosphate.
