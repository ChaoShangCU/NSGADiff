The molecule is an eremophilane sesquiterpenoid with anti-HIV-1 activity. It is isolated from Xylaria sp. MF6254. It has a role as a metabolite and a HIV-1 integrase inhibitor. It is a carboxylic ester, an enone, a cyclic ketone, an eremophilane sesquiterpenoid, an enal and a dioxo monocarboxylic acid.
