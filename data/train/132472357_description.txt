The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 7,16,17-trihydroxy-(14E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,12,14,19-pentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 7,16,17-trihydroxy-(14E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,12,14,19-pentaenoic acid.
