The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin with an arborinane-type terpenoid as the aglycone. It has been isolated from the roots of Rubia yunnanensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a diol, an aldehyde, a disaccharide derivative, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a triterpenoid saponin.
