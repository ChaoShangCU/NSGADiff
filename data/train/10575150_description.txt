The molecule is a organic heterotetracyclic compound isolated from the fermentation broth of Trichoderma harzianum and exhibits anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a beta-diketone, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound, an enone, a cyclic ether, a carboxylic ester and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a 3-hydroxybutyric acid.
