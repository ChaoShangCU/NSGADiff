The molecule is a resolvin that is docosa-4Z,9E,11E,13Z,15E,19Z-hexaenoic acid which is substituted by hydroxy groups at the 7, 8, and 17 positions (the 7S,8R,17S-stereoisomer). It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a triol, a resolvin and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a resolvin D1(1-).
