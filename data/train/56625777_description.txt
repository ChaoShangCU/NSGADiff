The molecule is a disaccharide that is beta-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 6 has been converted to its alpha-D-xylopyranoside. The disaccharide unit of xyloglucan in plant cell-walls. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a glycoside and a glycosylglucose. It derives from an alpha-D-xylose and a beta-D-glucose.
