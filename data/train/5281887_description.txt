The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that consists of 17beta-estradiol having a beta-glucuronyl residue attached at position 17 via a glycosidic linkage. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a 3-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 17beta-estradiol. It is a conjugate acid of a 17beta-estradiol 17-glucosiduronate.
