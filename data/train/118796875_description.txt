The molecule is a member of the class of quinoline N-oxides that is 2-methyl-1-oxo-4-3,4-dihydroquinoline-3,4-diol carrying an additional (2E,6E)-farnesyl group at position 4. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a quinoline N-oxide, a tertiary alcohol, a secondary alcohol and an olefinic compound.
