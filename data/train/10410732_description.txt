The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid with a quinone and taurine functionality. It is isolated from the sponge Dysidea avara, and exhibits inhibitory activity against tyrosine kinase. It has a role as a metabolite and a tyrosine kinase inhibitor. It is an amino sulfonic acid, a sesquiterpenoid, a member of 1,4-benzoquinones and a member of octahydronaphthalenes. It derives from a taurine.
