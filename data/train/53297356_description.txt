The molecule is a cyclopentapyran that is 7-deoxyloganin with a methyl and hydrogen replaced by hydroxy and hydroxymethyl groups at position 7. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a cyclopentapyran, an enoate ester, a monosaccharide derivative, a methyl ester and a tertiary alcohol. It derives from a 7-deoxyloganin.
