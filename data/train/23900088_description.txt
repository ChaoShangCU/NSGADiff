The molecule is a xylose derivative that is beta-D-xylose in which the hydrogen of the anomeric hydroxy group is replaced by myricetin. it has been isolated from Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a xylose derivative, a glycosyloxyflavone, a monosaccharide derivative and a pentahydroxyflavone. It derives from a myricetin.
