The molecule is an organic calcium salt that is the hemicalcium salt of zofenopril. A prodrug for zofenoprilat. It has a role as an anticonvulsant, an apoptosis inhibitor, a cardioprotective agent, an EC 3.4.15.1 (peptidyl-dipeptidase A) inhibitor, a prodrug and a vasodilator agent. It contains a zofenopril(1-).
