The molecule is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside derived from (2E)-9-hydroxynon-2-enoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid, a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside. It derives from a (2E)-9-hydroxynon-2-enoic acid and an oscr#3.
