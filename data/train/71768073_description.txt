The molecule is a member of the class of isocoumarins that is asperentin substituted by an additional hydroxy group at position 5'. It has been isolated from Chaetomium globosum and Aspergillus flavus. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite and a Chaetomium metabolite. It is a member of isocoumarins, a member of pyrans and a member of resorcinols. It derives from an asperentin.
