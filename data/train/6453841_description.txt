The molecule is an oxysterol that is cholesterol substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 20 and 22 (the 20R,22R-stereoisomer). It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 20-hydroxy steroid, a 22-hydroxy steroid, a 3beta-sterol, a cholestanoid, an oxysterol and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid.
