The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-sn-glycerol) in which the phosphatidyl acyl groups at positions 1 and 2 are specified as oleoyl and lauroyl respectively. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-sn-glycerol) and a dodecanoate ester. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-oleoyl-2-lauroyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-sn-glycerol)(1-).
