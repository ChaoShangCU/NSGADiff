The molecule is a monoterpenoid that is (2E)-oct-2-enoic acid substituted by hydroxy group at position 8 and methyl groups at positions 2 and 6 respectively (the 6R stereoisomer). Isolated from the Chinese herb Cistanche salsa, It exhibits anti-osteoporotic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and a bone density conservation agent. It is a monoterpenoid and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid.
