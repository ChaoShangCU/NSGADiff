The molecule is an ergot alkaloid produced by the fungus Aspergillus fumigatus that is ergoline which is substituted by methyl groups at the 6 and 8beta positions, and by an acetoxy group at the 9beta position. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an acetate ester and an ergot alkaloid. It is a conjugate base of a fumigaclavine A(1+). It derives from a hydride of an ergoline.
