The molecule is a tetrahydroxyflavone that is flavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7, 2' and 4', a prenyl group at position 3 and a 3-methylbut-1-en-3-yl group at position 8. It has been isolated from the twigs of Morus nigra and has been found to promote adipogenesis. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite.
