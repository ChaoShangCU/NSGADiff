The molecule is a trans-3-enoyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (2E,5Z)-octadienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2,3-trans-enoyl CoA(4-), a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a 4-saturated trans-2-enoyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (2E,5Z)-octadienoyl-CoA.
