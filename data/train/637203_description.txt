The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is ergosta-7,22-dien-3-ol substituted by additional hydroxy groups at positions 5, 6 and 9 (3beta,5alpha,6beta,22E stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the endophytic fungus, Chaetomium globosum. It has a role as a Chaetomium metabolite. It is a 3beta-sterol, a 5alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 6beta-hydroxy steroid and a 9-hydroxy steroid.
