The molecule is a dimeric branched amino tetrasaccharide consisting of beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->3)-N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine having a further beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->3)-N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl moiety attached at the 6-position of the glucosamine. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
