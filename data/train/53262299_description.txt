The molecule is a nucleotide-sugar oxoanion obtained via deprotonation of the diphosphate and carboxy OH groups and protonation of the primary amino group of UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is a nucleotide-sugar oxoanion and a carbohydrate acid derivative anion. It is a conjugate base of an UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronic acid.
