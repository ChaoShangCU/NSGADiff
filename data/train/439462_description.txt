The molecule is an N-glycosyl compound that consists of 5,6-diaminouracil in which one of the hydrogens on the 6-amino function is substituted by a 5-phospho-beta-D-ribosyl residue. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, a ribose monophosphate and an aminouracil. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-amino-6-(5-phospho-D-ribosylamino)uracil(2-).
