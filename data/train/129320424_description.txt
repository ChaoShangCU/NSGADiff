The molecule is a butan-4-olide that is (5-oxooxolan-3-yl)methyl phosphate carrying an additional 6-methylheptanoyl substituent at position 4. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a monoalkyl phosphate, a butan-4-olide and a beta-ketoester. It is a conjugate acid of a [(3S)-4-(6-methylheptanoyl)-5-oxooxolan-3-yl]methyl phosphate(2-).
