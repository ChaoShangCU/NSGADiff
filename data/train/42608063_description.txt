The molecule is an extended flavonoid that is 2,3-dihydro-4H,8H-pyrano[2,3-f]chromen-4-one which is substituted by a p-methoxyphenyl group at the 2-pro-S position, a methoxy group at position 5, and two methyl groups at position 8. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an extended flavonoid, an aromatic ether, an organic heterotricyclic compound, an aromatic ketone, a polyketide and a member of flavanones.
