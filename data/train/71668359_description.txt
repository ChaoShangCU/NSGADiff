The molecule is a phosphatidylinositol 28:0 in which the phosphatidyl acyl groups at positions 1 and 2 are specified as palmitoyl (hexadecanoyl) and lauroyl (dodecanoyl) respectively. It is a phosphatidylinositol 28:0(1-) and a 1-hexadecanoyl-2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-1D-myo-inositol(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 1-palmitoyl-2-lauroyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-1D-myo-inositol.
