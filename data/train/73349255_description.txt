The molecule is a chondramide that is chondramide A in which the hydrogen at position 2 of the indole moiety has been replaced by a chlorine. It is produced by strains of the myxobacterium, Chondromyces crocatus. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a chondramide, an organochlorine compound, a member of indoles and a member of phenols. It derives from a chondramide A.
