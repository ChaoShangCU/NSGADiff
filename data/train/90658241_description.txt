The molecule is a hexonic acid derivative that is (4S,5R)-4,5,6-trihydroxyhexanoic acid substituted at position 2 by an imino group. It is a dehydroamino acid, a ketimine and a hexonic acid derivative. It is a tautomer of a (4S,5R)-4,5,6-trihydroxy-2-iminohexanoic acid zwitterion and a (2Z,4S,5R)-2-amino-4,5,6-trihydroxyhex-2-enoic acid.
