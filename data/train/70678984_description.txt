The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and phosphonate OH groups of alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-phosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and an organophosphonate oxoanion. It is a conjugate base of an alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-phosphate.
