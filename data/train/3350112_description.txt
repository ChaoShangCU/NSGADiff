The molecule is a benzocycloheptapyridine that consists of 8-chloro-3-methyl-6,11-dihydro-5H-benzo[5,6]cyclohepta[1,2-b]pyridine bearing a 1-(pyridin-4-ylacetyl)piperidin-4-ylidene group at position 11. It is an organochlorine compound, a benzocycloheptapyridine, a N-acylpiperidine and a member of pyridines.
