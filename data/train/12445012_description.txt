The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone that is decahydroazuleno[4,5-b]furan-2(3H)-one substituted by methylidene groups at positions 3, 6 and 9 and an acetlyoxy group at position 8. Isolated from Zaluzania triloba and Laurus nobilis, it exhibits trypanocidal activity. It has a role as a metabolite and a trypanocidal drug. It is a gamma-lactone, a sesquiterpene lactone, an organic heterotricyclic compound, an acetate ester and a guaiane sesquiterpenoid.
