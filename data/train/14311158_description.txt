The molecule is a beta-D-glucoside having a malonyl group at the 6-position and a 3-O-delphinidin moiety at the anomeric position. It is a beta-D-glucoside and an anthocyanin cation. It derives from a delphinidin. It is a conjugate acid of a delphinidin-5-olate 3-O-(6''-O-carboxylatoacetyl)-beta-D-glucoside(1-).
