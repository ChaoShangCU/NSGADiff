The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid dianion resulting from the deprotonation of both of the carboxy groups of mono(glucosyluronic acid)bilirubin; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a mono(glucosyluronic acid)bilirubin.
