The molecule is a carboxyalkyl phosphate consisting of D-glyceric acid having an alpha-D-glucosyl residue at the 2-position and a phospho group at the 3-position It is an alpha-D-glucoside and a carboxyalkyl phosphate. It derives from a D-glyceric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-O-(alpha-D-glucopyranosyl)-3-O-phosphonato-D-glycerate(3-).
