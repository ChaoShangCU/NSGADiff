The molecule is a steroid glucuronide anion that is the conjugate base of 4-hydroxy-17beta-estradiol 4-O-(beta-D-glucuronide) arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion, a beta-D-glucosiduronate and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 4-hydroxy-17beta-estradiol 4-O-(beta-D-glucuronide).
