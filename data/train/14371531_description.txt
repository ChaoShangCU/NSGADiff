The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid that is 3,5-dioxocyclohexanecarboxylic acid substituted by a cyclopropyl(hydroxy)methylidene group at position 4. It is a metabolite of the plant growth regulator trinexapac-ethyl. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite, an agrochemical, a plant growth regulator and a gibberellin biosynthesis inhibitor. It is a member of cyclohexanones, a monocarboxylic acid, a beta-hydroxy ketone, an enol and a member of cyclopropanes.
