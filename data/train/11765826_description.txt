The molecule is a diterpene glycoside that is 20-hydroxygeranyllinalool substituted carrying beta-D-glucosyl and beta-D-glucosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-glucosyl residues at position O-3 and O-20 respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diterpene glycoside, a beta-D-glucoside and a sophoroside. It derives from a geranyllinalool.
