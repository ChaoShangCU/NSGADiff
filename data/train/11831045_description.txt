The molecule is a carbobicyclic compound that is cis-decalin substituted by a propan-2-yl group at position 1, methyl groups at position 4 and 7, and a hydroxy group at position 4a, and containing a double bond between positions 7 and 8 (the 1R,4S,4aR,8aS diastereoisomer). It is a carbobicyclic compound, a sesquiterpenoid, a tertiary alcohol and a member of octahydronaphthalenes.
