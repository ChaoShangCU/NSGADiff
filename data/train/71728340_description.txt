The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that is a tetrasaccharide derivative of 3,12,17,25-tetrahydroxy-18,20-epoxylanost-9(11)-ene-18,22-dione. It is a triterpenoid saponin, a tetrasaccharide derivative and a heterocyclyl sulfate. It is a conjugate acid of a holothurin A3(1-). It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
