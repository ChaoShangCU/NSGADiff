The molecule is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 3,4,4-trimethylhepta-2,5-dienoic acid. It is an unsaturated fatty acyl-CoA and a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a coenzyme A.
