The molecule is a double-stranded DNA polynucleotide consisting of in one strand a repeating unit of thymidine and deoxycytidine residues, with in a complementary strand a repeating unit of deoxyguanosine and deoxyadenosine residues, all residues in each strand beiing connected by 3'->5' phosphodiester linkages. It contains a poly[d(GA)] and a poly[d(TC)].
