The molecule is a member of the class of 1-benzofurans that is a lignan obtained by cyclodimerisation of ferulic acid. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid, a lignan, an aldehyde and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a ferulic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (-)-DCA-CL(1-). It is an enantiomer of a (+)-DCA-CL.
