The molecule is a limonoid that is azadiradione with an epoxy group across positions 14 and 15. Isolated from Azadirachta indica it exhibits insecticidal activitry against mosquitoes. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent, an insecticide and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a member of furans, a limonoid, an epoxide and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from an azadiradione.
