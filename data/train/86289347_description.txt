The molecule is a member of the class of syringomycins that has 3-hydroxydodecanoyl as the fatty acyl tail group. It is isolated from Pseudomonas syringae pv. syringae and exhibits phytotoxic and antifungal properties. It has a role as a phytotoxin and an antifungal agent. It is a syringomycin and a lipopeptide antibiotic.
