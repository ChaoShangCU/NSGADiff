The molecule is a dihydroxypyridine that is pseudooxynicotine in which the hydrogens at positions 2 and 6 on the pyridine ring are substituted by hydroxy groups. It is a dihydroxypyridine, a secondary amino compound and an aromatic ketone. It derives from a pseudooxynicotine. It is a conjugate base of a 2,6-dihydroxypseudooxynicotinium(1+).
