The molecule is a limonoid that is azadiradione in which the furanyl group at position 17 is replaced by a hydrogen. It has been isolated from Azadirachta indica. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a limonoid and a tetracyclic triterpenoid. It derives from an azadiradione.
