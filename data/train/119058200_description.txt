The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (14R,15S)-epoxy-(6E,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid in which the hydroperoxy group is located at position 5S. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a (5S)-hydroperoxy-(14R,15S)-epoxy-(6E,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoate.
