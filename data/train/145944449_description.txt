The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is (1R,1'R)-[1,1'-bi(cyclohex-2-en-1-yl)]-6,6'-dione in which position 3 of each ring is joined to a methyl substituent of (2S,5S)-dimethylpiperazine. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a bridged compound, a cyclic ketone, a member of piperazines and an azamacrocycle. It is a conjugate base of a (1S,7R,8R,14S)-6,9-dioxo-15,17-diazatetracyclo[12.2.2.1(3,7).1(8,12)]icosa-3(20),12(19)-dien-15-ium.
