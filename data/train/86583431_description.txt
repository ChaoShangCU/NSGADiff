The molecule is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 3-methylundecanoic acid. It is a methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 3-methylundecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-methylundecanoyl-CoA(4-).
