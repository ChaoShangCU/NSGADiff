The molecule is an N(2)-acyl-L-glutamine that has 4-(1H-indol-3-yl)butanoyl as the acyl group. It is a member of indoles, a N(2)-acyl-L-glutamine, a primary carboxamide and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from an indole-3-butyric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N(2)-[4-(indol-3-yl)butanoyl]-L-glutaminate.
