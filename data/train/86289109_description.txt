The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (8E,10S)-10-hydroperoxy-8-octadecenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, an unsaturated fatty acid anion and a hydroperoxyoctadecenoate. It is a conjugate base of an (8E,10S)-10-hydroperoxy-8-octadecenoic acid.
