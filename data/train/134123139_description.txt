The molecule is an organic potassium salt obtained by formal reaction of equimolar amounts of azulam and potassium. A dihydropteroate synthase inhibitor, it is used as a herbicide (mainly for killing bracken). It has a role as an agrochemical, an EC 2.5.1.15 (dihydropteroate synthase) inhibitor and a herbicide. It contains an asulam(1-).
