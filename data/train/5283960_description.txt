The molecule is an oxo-5beta-cholanic acid in which two oxo substituents are located at positions 3 and 12 together with a hydroxy substituent at position 7beta. It is an oxo-5beta-cholanic acid, a 12-oxo steroid, a 3-oxo-5beta-steroid and a 7beta-hydroxy steroid. It is a conjugate acid of a 7beta-hydroxy-3,12-dioxo-5beta-cholanate.
