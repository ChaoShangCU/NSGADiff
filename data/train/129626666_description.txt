The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 7-oxoresolvin D2, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an oxo fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 7-oxoresolvin D2.
