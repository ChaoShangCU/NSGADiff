The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isovitexin with the hydroxy group at position 7 replaced with a glucopyranosyl entity which in turn is substituted at position 6 by an isoferuloyl moiety. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a cinnamate ester and a C-glycosyl compound. It derives from an isovitexin and an isoferulic acid.
