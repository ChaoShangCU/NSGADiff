The molecule is an O-sulfoamino acid consisting of methyl L-tyrosinate in which the hydrogen of the phenolic hydroxy group has been replaced by a sulfo group. It is an O-sulfoamino acid, a methyl ester and a L-tyrosine derivative. It derives from a methyl L-tyrosinate. It is a conjugate acid of a L-tyrosine methyl ester 4-sulfate(1-).
