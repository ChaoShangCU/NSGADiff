The molecule is a 7-[beta-D-apiofuranosyl-(1->6)-beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy]isoflavone having methoxy substituents at the 6- and 2'-positions and a methylenedioxy moiety at the 4'- and 5'-positions. It is a 7-[beta-D-apiofuranosyl-(1->6)-beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy]isoflavone and a member of benzodioxoles.
