The molecule is a long-chain, divinyl ether fatty acid composed of 8-nonenoic acid having the E- hydrogen at position 9 substituted by a (1E,3Z)-nona-1,3,-dien-1-yloxy group. It is a divinyl ether fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a straight-chain fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a colneleate.
