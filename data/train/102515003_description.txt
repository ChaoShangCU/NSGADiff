The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is 5alpha-stigmasta-7,24(28)-dien-3beta-ol which is substituted at the 4alpha position by a hydroxymethyl group and in which the 24(28) double bond has Z configuration. It is a 3beta-sterol, a member of phytosterols and a Delta(7)-sterol. It derives from a (Z)-24-ethylidenelophenol.
