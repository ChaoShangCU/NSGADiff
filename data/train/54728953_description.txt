The molecule is a member of the class of 2-pyranones that is 2H-pyran-2-one substituted by a methoxy group at position 3, a hydroxy group at position 4, a methyl group at position 5 and a (2E)-3-methyl-9-phenylnon-2-en-1-yl group at position 6. Isolated from the marine sponge of the genus Plakortis, it exhibits cytotoxicity against human promyeloid leukemic HL-60 cells. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of 2-pyranones, an ether and a polyketide.
