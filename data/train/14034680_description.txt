The molecule is a dibenzooxepine that is dibenzo[b,e]oxepin-11(6H)-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 6 and 10, a methyl group at position 8, a prenyl group at position 4 and a prenyloxy group at position 7. Isolated from Aspergillus, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as an antibacterial agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a dibenzooxepine, a cyclic ketone, a polyphenol and a lactol.
