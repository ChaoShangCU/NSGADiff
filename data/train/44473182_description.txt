The molecule is a benzoylpyrazole that is 1H-pyrazole substituted by an ethyl, 3-(2-methoxyethoxy)-2-methyl-4-(methylsulfonyl)benzoyl, and 1-[(methoxycarbonyl)oxy]ethoxy groups at positions 1, 4 and 5, respectively. It is an aromatic ether, a benzoylpyrazole, a carbonate ester, a member of toluenes, an aromatic ketone and a sulfone.
