The molecule is a member of the class of prostaglandins J that is (5Z,9Z,12E,17Z)-prostatetraenoic acid carrying hydroxy and oxo substituents at positions 11 and 15 respectively. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators and potent anti-leukemic agent. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a prostaglandins J and a homoallylic alcohol. It derives from a prostaglandin J3. It is a conjugate acid of a Delta(12)-prostaglandin J3(1-).
