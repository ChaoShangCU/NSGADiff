The molecule is a quercetin O-glucoside that is quercetin 7-O-beta-D-glucoside carrying an additional alpha-L-rhamnosyl residue at position 3. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an alpha-L-rhamnoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol, a beta-D-glucoside, a quercetin O-glucoside and a trihydroxyflavone.
