The molecule is an N-hydroxy-L-polyhomomethioninate that is the conjugate base of N-hydroxy-L-dihomomethionine, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a N-hydroxy-L-polyhomomethioninate and a N-hydroxydihomomethioninate. It is a conjugate base of a N-hydroxy-L-dihomomethionine.
