The molecule is a member of the class of glycerophosphoglycerols obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of oleic acid with one of the secondary hydroxy groups of (R,R)-glycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (R,R)-2-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol(1-). It is an enantiomer of a (S,S)-2-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol.
