The molecule is an oligomycin with formula C44H72O11 that is oligomycin A in which the methyl substituent adjacent to the spirocyclic centre has been replaced by a hydrogen. It has a role as an EC 3.6.3.14 (H(+)-transporting two-sector ATPase) inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and a nematicide. It is an oligomycin, a pentol, a triketone and an antibiotic antifungal agent.
