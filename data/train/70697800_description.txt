The molecule is a steroid glycoside isolated from the roots of Cynanchum auriculatum and has been shown to exhibit cytotoxicity against human tumour cell lines. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a cinnamate ester, a methyl ketone, a deoxy hexoside, a steroid ester, a steroid saponin and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
