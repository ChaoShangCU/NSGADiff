The molecule is a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid with a beta-hydroxy substituent at position 3. The 3beta-hydroxy epimer of lithocholic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite, a rat metabolite and a xenobiotic metabolite. It is a bile acid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid and a C24-steroid. It is a conjugate acid of an isolithocholate.
