The molecule is a 2-monoglyceride obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of prostaglandin G2 with the 2-hydroxy group of glycerol. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 2-monoglyceride, a prostaglandins G, a peroxol, an olefinic compound, an organic peroxide and a bridged compound. It derives from a prostaglandin G2.
