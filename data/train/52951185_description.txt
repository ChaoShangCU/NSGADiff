The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is urs-20-ene substituted by a carboxy group at position 28 and a beta-hydroxy group at position 3. It has been isolated from Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a hydride of an ursane.
