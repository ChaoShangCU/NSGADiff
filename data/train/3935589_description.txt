The molecule is an organic sodium salt having 3-[(4-anilinophenyl)diazenyl]benzene-1-sulfonate as the counterion. Used in one variant of Masson's trichrome to stain collagen yellow in contrast to the red muscle, but is not in common used. It has a role as a histological dye. It contains a 3-[(4-anilinophenyl)diazenyl]benzene-1-sulfonate.
