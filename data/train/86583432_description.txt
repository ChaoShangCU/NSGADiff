The molecule is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) oxanion arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 12-methyltridecanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3 It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA. It is a conjugate base of an isomyristoyl-CoA.
