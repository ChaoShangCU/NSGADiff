The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound whose skeleton is composed of 3,5-diformyl-isopentyl substituted phloroglucinol fused with a cadinane ring system. Isolated from Eucalyptus globulus, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a tertiary alcohol, a cyclic ether, a member of benzaldehydes and a member of resorcinols.
