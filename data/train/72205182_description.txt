The molecule is a hydroxydocosapentaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,16Z)-docosapentaenoic acid in which the hydroxy group is located at the 14S-position. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a hydroxydocosapentaenoic acid and a secondary allylic alcohol. It derives from a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z)-docosa-4,7,10,13,16-pentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14S,16Z)-14-hydroxydocosapentaenoate.
