The molecule is a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid that is a reactive intermediate in the biosynthesis of vindoline by Catharanthus roseus. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a methyl ester, an aromatic ether, a tertiary alcohol and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a 16-hydroxytabersonine. It is a conjugate base of a (3R)-1,2-didehydro-3-hydroxy-16-methoxy-2,3-dihydrotabersoninium.
