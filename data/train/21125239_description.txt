The molecule is a glutathione conjugate in which the mercapto hydrogen of glutathione has been replaced by a 1,4-dihydroxy-3-methylnaphthalen-2-yl group It is an aryl sulfide, a member of naphthalenediols and a glutathione conjugate. It derives from a menadiol. It is a conjugate acid of a S-(1,4-dihydroxy-3-methylnaphthalen-2-yl)glutathione(1-).
