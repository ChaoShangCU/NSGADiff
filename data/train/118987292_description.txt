The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate that is the 8-aza analogue of 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-monophosphate. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate, a member of triazolopyrimidines, a N-glycosyl compound and a nucleoside monophosphate analogue. It derives from an 8-azaadenine.
