The molecule is an aminoalkylindole that is 3-(aminomethyl)indole in which the hydrogen at position 4 has been replaced by a methoxy group. It has a role as an Arabidopsis thaliana metabolite. It is an aminoalkylindole and an aromatic ether. It is a conjugate base of a 4-methoxy-3-indolylmethylamine(1+).
