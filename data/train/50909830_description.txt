The molecule is an anthocyanin cation comprising cyanidin(1+) having a beta-D-glucopyranuronosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue attached at the 3-position. It is an anthocyanin cation and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a cyanidin cation. It is a conjugate acid of a cyanidin 3-O-(2-O-beta-D-glucuronosyl)-beta-D-glucoside betaine.
