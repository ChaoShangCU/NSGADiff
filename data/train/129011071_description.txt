The molecule is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid that is progesterone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 6, 16 and 17 (6beta,16alpha,17alpha-stereoisomer). It is a 16alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 20-oxo steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a C21-steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a progesterone. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
