The molecule is a member of the class of benzamides that is obtained by the formal condensation of 2,4-dichlorobenzoic acid and benzotriazole. It acts as an inhibitor for tubulin acetylation mediated by trichostatin A. It has a role as an inhibitor. It is a member of benzotriazoles, a member of benzamides and a dichlorobenzene. It derives from a 2,4-dichlorobenzoic acid and a benzotriazole.
