The molecule is an oxo monocarboxylic acid that is (3-oxocyclopentyl)acetic acid substituted by a (2Z)-pent-2-en-1-yl group at position 2 of the cyclopentane ring. It has a role as a member of jasmonates. It is an oxo monocarboxylic acid and a member of cyclopentanones. It is a conjugate acid of a (+)-jasmonic acid anion. It is an enantiomer of a jasmonic acid.
