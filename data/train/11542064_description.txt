The molecule is a synthetic macrolide that is an analogue of the marine metabolite leucascandrolide A that exhibits comparable antiproliferative properties. It has a role as an antifungal agent. It is a member of 1,3-oxazoles, a carbamate ester, an enoate ester, a macrolide, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a polycyclic ether and a semisynthetic derivative.
