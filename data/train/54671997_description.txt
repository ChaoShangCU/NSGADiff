The molecule is a pyrrolopyrazine that is hexahydropyrrolo[1,2-a]pyrazine-1,4-dione substituted by a hydroxy group at position 6 and a 2-methylpropyl group at position 3. It has been isolated from the mycelia of Cordyceps sinensis. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a pyrrolopyrazine and a dipeptide.
