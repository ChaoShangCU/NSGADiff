The molecule is a bile acid that is 24-dinor-5beta-cholan-22-oic acid bearing three hydroxy substituents at positions 3alpha, 7alpha and 12alpha. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite and a human blood serum metabolite. It is a bile acid, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 7alpha-hydroxy steroid and a 12alpha-hydroxy steroid.
