The molecule is a prostaglandin A derivative that is prostaglandin A1 in which one of the methyl hydrogens at position 20 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is a primary alcohol, a secondary allylic alcohol, a prostaglandins A and a diol. It derives from a prostaglandin A1. It is a conjugate acid of a 20-hydroxyprostaglandin A1(1-).
