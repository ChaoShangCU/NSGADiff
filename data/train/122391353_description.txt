The molecule is a hydroxydocosapentaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z)-docosapentaenoic acid in which the hydroxy group is located at position 20. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It derives from a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z)-docosa-4,7,10,13,16-pentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z)-20-hydroxydocosapentaenoate.
