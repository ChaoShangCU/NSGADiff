The molecule is a carbotricyclic compound that consists of decahydro-1H-1H-cyclopropa[a]naphthalene bearing four methyl substituents at position 1, 1, 3a and 7 as well as a hydroxy substituent at position 4 (the (1aR,3aS,4S,7R,7aS,7bR)-diastereomer). It is a sesquiterpenoid, a carbotricyclic compound and a secondary alcohol.
