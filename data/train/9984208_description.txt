The molecule is an organic heteroheptacyclic compound isolated from Albophoma yamanashiensis and has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against acyl-CoA:cholesterol acyltransferase. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 2.3.1.26 (sterol O-acyltransferase) inhibitor. It is an organic heteroheptacyclic compound, an epoxide, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound, a secondary amino compound, a secondary alcohol and a tertiary alcohol.
