The molecule is a monoamino-1,3,5-triazine that is 1,3,5-triazin-2-amine substituted by a methoxy group at position 4 and a methyl group at position 6. It is a metabolite of the herbicide thifensulfuron-methyl. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a monoamino-1,3,5-triazine and an aromatic ether.
