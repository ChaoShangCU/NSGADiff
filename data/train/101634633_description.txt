The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is (+)-homalomenol A in which the double bond of the 2-methylpropen-1-yl group has migrated from the 1-2 position to the 2-3 position. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a carbobicyclic compound, a diol, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, an olefinic compound and a sesquiterpenoid.
