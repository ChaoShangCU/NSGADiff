The molecule is a tertiary amino compound that is 1,3,5-triazapenta-1,4-diene substituted by a methyl group at position 3 and 2,4-dimethylphenyl groups at positions 1 and 5. It has a role as an acaricide, a xenobiotic, an environmental contaminant and an insecticide. It is a tertiary amino compound and a member of formamidines.
