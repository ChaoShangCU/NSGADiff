The molecule is a triamino-1,3,5-triazine that is 1,3,5-triazine in which two of the hydrogens have been replaced by morpholin-4-yl groups while the third hydrogen has been replaced by a 2-(difluoromethyl)benzimidazol-1-yl group. It is an inhibitor of phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase. It has a role as an EC 2.7.1.137 (phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase) inhibitor and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of morpholines, a member of benzimidazoles, a triamino-1,3,5-triazine and an organofluorine compound.
