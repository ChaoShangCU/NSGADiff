The molecule is an azaarene that is benzo[c]cinnoline with methoxy substituents at positions 1 and 10. It is isolated from Streptomyces sp. strain 4849 with pharmaceutical potential as an inhibitor of interleukin-4 receptor (IL-4). It has a role as a metabolite and an antimicrobial agent. It is an azaarene, an aromatic ether and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
