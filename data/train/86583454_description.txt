The molecule is a hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-2-hydroxydotriacontapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-2-hydroxydotriacontapentaenoic acid.
