The molecule is an S-substituted glutathione(1-) obtained by deprotonation of the the two carboxy groups and protonation of the glutamyl amino group of S-(2,4-dinitrophenyl)glutathione. Major microspecies at pH 7.3. It derives from a glutathionate(1-). It is a conjugate base of a S-(2,4-dinitrophenyl)glutathione.
