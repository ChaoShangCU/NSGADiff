The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin with rubiarbonol A as the aglycone. It has been isolated from the roots of Rubia yunnanensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a triol, a disaccharide derivative, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a triterpenoid saponin. It derives from a rubiarbonol A.
