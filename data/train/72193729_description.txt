The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (8Z,11Z,14Z)-heptadecatrienoic acid, arising from deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a straight-chain fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an (8Z,11Z,14Z)-heptadecatrienoic acid.
