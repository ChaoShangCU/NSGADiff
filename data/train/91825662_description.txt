The molecule is an organic heterobicyclic compound that is 2,3,4,5-tetrahydro-1H-1,4-benzodiazepine which has been substituted by a carboxy group at position 3 and by oxo groups at positions 2 and 5. It is a 1,4-benzodiazepinone, a monocarboxylic acid, an organic heterobicyclic compound and a lactam.
