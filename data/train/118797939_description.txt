The molecule is a member of the class of benzimidazoles that is the amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-(2-cyclohexylbenzimidazol-1-yl)propanoic acid with the amino group of (1R,2R,4R)-1,7,7-trimethylbicyclo[2.2.1]heptan-2-amine. An inhibitor of teichoic acid biosynthesis. It has a role as a teichoic acid biosynthesis inhibitor. It is a member of benzimidazoles, a monocarboxylic acid amide and a bridged compound.
