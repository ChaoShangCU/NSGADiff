The molecule is an oxysterol that is 7-ketocholesterol which is substituted by a hydroxy group at position 26 and has R-configuration at position 25. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a 26-hydroxy steroid, an oxysterol, a 7-oxo steroid, a 3beta-sterol, a cholestanoid and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid. It derives from a cholesterol.
