The molecule is a substituted aniline that is N,N-dipropylaniline substituted by a nitro groups at positions 2 and 6 and a trifluoromethyl group at position 4. It is an agrochemical used as a pre-emergence herbicide. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic, a herbicide and an agrochemical. It is a C-nitro compound, a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes and a substituted aniline.
