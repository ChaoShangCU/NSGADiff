The molecule is a naphthalenesulfonic acid that is the free acid form of the dye benzo scarlet 4BNS. The trisodium salt is a direct cotton dye that is also capable of hydrogen bonding to amyloid and giving a dark enough red colour to be useful in diagnostic histology. It is a naphthalenesulfonic acid, a member of naphthols, a bis(azo) compound, a member of azobenzenes, a member of ureas and an aromatic ether. It is a conjugate acid of a benzo scarlet 4BNS(3-).
