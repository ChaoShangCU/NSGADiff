The molecule is a phenanthrene substituted by a 2-(methylamino)ethyl group at position 1 and by methoxy groups at positions 2,3,4,6 and 7, respectively. It is a plant metabolite isolated from Annona purpurea and Fagonia olivieri. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of phenanthrenes, an aromatic ether, a polyether, a secondary amino compound and a phenanthrene alkaloid.
