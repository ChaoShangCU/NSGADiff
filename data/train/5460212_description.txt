The molecule is a 1-(phosphoribosyl)imidazolecarboxamide that is the 5-(5-phospho-beta-D-ribosylamino)methylidene derivative of 5-amino-1-(5-phospho-D-ribosyl)imidazole-4-carboxamide. It has a role as a metabolite and an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-(5-phospho-beta-D-ribosyl)-5-[(5-phospho-beta-D-ribosylamino)methylideneamino]imidazole-4-carboxamide(4-).
