The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 7, a methoxy group at position 4' and a prenyl group at position 8. It has been isolated from Macaranga bicolor. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavanone, a monomethoxyflavanone and a member of 4'-methoxyflavanones. It derives from a (2S)-flavanone.
