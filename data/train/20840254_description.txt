The molecule is an indole alkaloid that is vobasan in which the bridgehead methyl group is substituted by a methoxycarbonyl group and an additional oxo substituent is present in the 3-position. It has a role as an antiviral agent. It is an indole alkaloid, a methyl ester, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, an aromatic ketone and a cyclic ketone. It derives from a hydride of a vobasan.
