The molecule is a catechol that is 5-methylbenzene-1,2-diol bearing a 3-hydroxy-5-methylphenoxy substituent at position 3. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimalarial, an anti-HSV-1 agent, an antimycobacterial drug and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of catechols and an aromatic ether. It derives from a diphenyl ether.
