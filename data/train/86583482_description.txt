The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion, an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (5Z,11Z,14Z)-8,9-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid.
