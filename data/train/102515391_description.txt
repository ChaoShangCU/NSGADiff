The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is 24-methylidenelophenol in which the methyl group at position 4 has been oxidised to the corresponding hydroxymethyl group. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3beta-sterol and a Delta(7)-sterol. It derives from a 24-methylidenelophenol. It derives from a hydride of a stigmastane.
