The molecule is the second intermediate in the biosynthesis of vitamin B12 from uroporphyrinogen III, in which methyl groups have been introduced at positions 2 and 7 of the tetrapyrrole framework. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a precorrin and a member of isobacteriochlorins. It is a conjugate acid of a precorrin-2(7-).
