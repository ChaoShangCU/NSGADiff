The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid that is 2-hydroxyhepta-2,4-diene in which the two terminal methyl groups are replaced by carboxy groups (the 2Z,4E-geoisomer). It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is an enol and a polyunsaturated dicarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxyhepta-2,4-dienedioate. It is a tautomer of a 2-oxohept-4-ene-1,7-dioic acid.
