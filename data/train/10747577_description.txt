The molecule is a myo-inositol pentakisphosphate that consists of myo-inositol having the five phospho groups located at positions 1, 2, 4, 5 and 6 as well as a diphospho group at position 3. It derives from a myo-inositol. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-diphospho-1D-myo-inositol 1,2,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate(13-).
