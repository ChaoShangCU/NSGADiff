The molecule is an aralkylglucosinolic acid that consists of 1-thio-beta-D-glucopyranose attached to a 2-phenyl-N-(sulfooxy)ethanimidoyl group at the anomeric sulfur. It is an aralkylglucosinolic acid and a member of benzenes. It derives from a (Z)-desulfoglucotropeolin and a desulfoglucotropeolin. It is a conjugate acid of a glucotropeolin(1-).
