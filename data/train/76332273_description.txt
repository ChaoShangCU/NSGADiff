The molecule is a member of the class of 1,3-thiazoles that is 2-[2-(pentan-3-ylidene)hydrazino]-1,3-thiazole carrying an additional 4-chlorophenyl substituent at position 4. It has a role as an EC 2.3.1.48 (histone acetyltransferase) inhibitor. It is a hydrazone, a member of 1,3-thiazoles and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
