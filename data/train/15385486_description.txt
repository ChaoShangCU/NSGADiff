The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone having the two hydroxy groups located at the C-4' and -7 positions and two additional methoxy substituent at the C-3' and 8-positions. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavanone, a dimethoxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a member of 3'-methoxyflavanones.
