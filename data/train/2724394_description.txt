The molecule is an alpha-amino acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of N(alpha)-acetyl-L-lysine with the hydroxy group of methanol. It is a L-lysine derivative, an alpha-amino acid ester, a member of acetamides, a methyl ester and a primary amino compound. It is a conjugate base of a N(alpha)-acetyl-L-lysine methyl ester(1+).
