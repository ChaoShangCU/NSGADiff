The molecule is a leukotriene anion that is the conjugate base of 20-hydroxy-6-trans-leukotriene B4 arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion and a leukotriene anion. It derives from a 6-trans-leukotriene B4(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 20-hydroxy-6-trans-leukotriene B4.
