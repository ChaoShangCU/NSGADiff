The molecule is a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid in which the two stereocentres at positions 14 and 15 both have S-configuration. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (5Z,8Z,11Z,14S,15S)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoate. It is an enantiomer of a (5Z,8Z,11Z,14R,15R)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid.
