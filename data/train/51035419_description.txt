The molecule is a member of the class of ureas obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of [3-tert-butyl-1-(4-methylphenyl)pyrazol-5-yl]carbamic acid with the amino group of 4-[2-(benzyloxy)ethyl]-1,3-thiazol-2-amine. It is a member of ureas, a N-arylimidazole, a member of 1,3-thiazoles and a benzyl ether.
