The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is tetracosanoic acid having four double bonds located at positions 11, 14, 17 and 20 (the 11Z,14Z,17Z,20Z-isomer). It is an omega-6 fatty acid and a hexacosatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an (11Z,14Z,17Z,20Z)-hexacosatetraenoate.
