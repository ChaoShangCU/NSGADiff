The molecule is a sulfonium compound comprising 4-methylthio-2-oxobutanoic acid having an adenosin-5'-yl group attached to the sulfur. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is a sulfonium compound and an organic cation. It derives from a 4-methylthio-2-oxobutanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a S-adenosyl-4-methylthio-2-oxobutanoate.
