The molecule is a tetrahydroxyflavone that is flavonol substituted by additional hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 3', a methoxy group at position 4' and a geranyl group at position 2'. It has been isolated from the twigs of Morus nigra. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a tetrahydroxyflavone, a member of flavonols and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a flavonol.
