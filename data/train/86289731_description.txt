The molecule is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside derived from (8R)-8-hydroxynonanoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside, a monocarboxylic acid and an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside. It derives from an (8R)-8-hydroxynonanoic acid and an ascr#10.
