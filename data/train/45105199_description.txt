The molecule is a docosanoid that is (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid carrying an oxo substituent at position 17. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an enone, a docosanoid and an oxo fatty acid. It derives from a (7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (7Z,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-17-oxodocosapentaenoate.
