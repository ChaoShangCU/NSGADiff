The molecule is a ring assembly that consist of (+)-catechin and epirobinetinidol units. Isolated from Acacia mearnsii, it exhibits inhibitory activity against alpha-amylase. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.2.1.1 (alpha-amylase) inhibitor. It is a hydroxyflavan and a ring assembly. It derives from a (+)-catechin.
