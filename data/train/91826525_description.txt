The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (10E,12Z)-hexadecadienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (10E,12Z)-hexadecadienoyl-CoA.
