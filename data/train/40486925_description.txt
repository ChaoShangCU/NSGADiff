The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a docosahexaenoate and a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl derivative. It is a conjugate base of an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid.
