The molecule is a member of the class of imidazoles that is 1-ethyl-1H-imidazole in which one of the hydrogens of the methyl group is replaced by a (4-chlorobenzyl)sulfanediyl group while a second is replaced by a 2,4-dichlorophenyl group. It is a member of imidazoles, an organic sulfide, a dichlorobenzene and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
