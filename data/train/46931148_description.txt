The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is the trianion of ditrans,polycis-dodecaprenyl diphosphate arising from deprotonation of the diphosphate OH groups; major species at pH 7.3. It derives from a ditrans,polycis-dodecaprenyl phosphate(2-). It is a conjugate base of a ditrans,polycis-dodecaprenyl diphosphate.
