The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 12(R)-HPETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a hydroperoxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 12(R)-HPETE.
