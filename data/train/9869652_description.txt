The molecule is a (4Z)-7-{5-[([1,1'-biphenyl]-4-yl)methoxy]-2-(morpholin-4-yl)-3-oxocyclopentyl}hept-4-enoic acid in which the stereocentres at positions 1, 2 and 5 have S-, S- and R-configuration respectively. It is a conjugate acid of a (1S,2S,5R)-AH23848(1-). It is an enantiomer of a (1R,2R,5S)-AH23848.
