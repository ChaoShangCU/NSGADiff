The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound and pyridine alkaloid with formula C43H49NO18 originally isolated from the roots of Tripterygium wilfordii. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a phytogenic insecticide. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an acetate ester, a benzoate ester, a macrocyclic lactone, a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid and a pyridine alkaloid.
