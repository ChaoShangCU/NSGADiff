The molecule is pentaanion of 5-O-phosphono-alpha-D-ribofuranosyl diphosphate arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a 5-O-phosphono-alpha-D-ribofuranosyl diphosphate.
