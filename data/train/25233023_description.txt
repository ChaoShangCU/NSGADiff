The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound with formula C23H27ClO6 isolated from Chaetomium globosum. It exhibits antifungal activity against plant pathogenic fungi. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a Chaetomium metabolite. It is an enone, an organochlorine compound, a lactol, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and a delta-lactone.
