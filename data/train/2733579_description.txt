The molecule is an indolyl carbohydrate that is the beta-D-glucuronide of indoxyl in which the indole moiety is substituted at positions 4 and 5 by chlorine and bromine, respectively It has a role as a chromogenic compound. It is an organochlorine compound, an organobromine compound, an indolyl carbohydrate, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from an indoxyl.
