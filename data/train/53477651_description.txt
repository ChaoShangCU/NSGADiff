The molecule is a glycoside that consists of alpha-D-galactosyl-(1->4)-beta-D-galactosyl-(1->4)-beta-D-glucose having a 2-(2-methoxycarbonylethylthio)ethyl (CETE) moiety attached to the reducing end anomeric centre. It is a glycoside, a carbohydrate acid ester, an aliphatic sulfide, a trisaccharide derivative and a methyl ester.
