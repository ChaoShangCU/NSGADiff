The molecule is an epoxide substituted at position 2 by a hydroxymethyl group and at position 3 by a hepta-1,3,5-triyn-1-yl group (the 2S,3S)-diastereomer. It is isolated from Trametes pubescens and exhibits antifungal properties. It has a role as a metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is an epoxide and a primary alcohol.
