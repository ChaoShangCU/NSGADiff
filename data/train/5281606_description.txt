The molecule is a dihydroxyflavone that is flavone substituted by a chloro group at position 3', hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 2' and methoxy groups at positions 3, 7 band 8 respectively. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a trimethoxyflavone, a dihydroxyflavone and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
