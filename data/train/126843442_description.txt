The molecule is an enoate ester that results from the condensation of the carboxylic acid group of 2-trans-abscisic acid with the anomeric hydroxy group of D-glucopyranose. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an enoate ester, an O-acyl carbohydrate and a glucoside. It derives from a 2-trans-abscisic acid and a D-glucopyranose.
