The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is 1,4,6-heptatrien-3-one substituted by 4-hydroxypheny group at positions 1 and 7 respectively. It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma domestica and Etlingera elatior. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a member of phenols and an enone.
