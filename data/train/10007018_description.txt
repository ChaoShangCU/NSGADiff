The molecule is an N-acyl amino acid that is N-(2,6-dichlorobenzoyl)alanine in which one of the hydrogens at the beta position has been replaced by a N 6-(2,6-dimethoxyphenyl)-2-naphthyl group. It is a member of naphthalenes, a dichlorobenzene, a N-acyl-amino acid and a non-proteinogenic amino acid derivative. It contains a 2,6-dichlorobenzoyl group.
