The molecule is a steroid saponin that is (3beta,22E)-stigmasta-5,22-dien-3-ol attached to a beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It is isolated from Symplocos lancifolia. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a member of phytosterols, a steroid saponin, a beta-D-glucoside and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a stigmasterol. It derives from a hydride of a stigmastane.
