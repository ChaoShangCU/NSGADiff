The molecule is a member of the class of aromatic amines that is ammonia with two of the hydrogens replaced by 5-methylpyrazol-3-yl and 2-phenylquinazolin-4-yl groups. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.26 (tau-protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of quinazolines, a member of pyrazoles, a secondary amino compound and an aromatic amine.
