The molecule is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 2-hydroxytetracosanoic acid (also known as 2-hydroxylignoceric or cerebronic acid), obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a very long-chain fatty acid anion and a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion 24:0. It derives from a tetracosanoate. It is a conjugate base of a cerebronic acid.
