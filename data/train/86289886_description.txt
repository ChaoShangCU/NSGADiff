The molecule is a 4-O-(p-hydroxybenzoyl)ascaroside derived from (8R)-8-hydroxynonanoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a 4-O-(p-hydroxybenzoyl)ascaroside, an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an ascr#10 and an (8R)-8-hydroxynonanoic acid.
