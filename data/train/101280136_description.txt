The molecule is an amino cyclitol that is (1S,2S,3R,6S)-6-amino-4-(hydroxymethyl)cyclohex-4-ene-1,2,3-triol in which one of the hydrogens attached to the nitrogen is replaced by a (1R,2S,3R,4R,5S,6S)-2,3,4,6-tetrahydroxy-5-(hydroxymethyl)cyclohexyl group. It is an amino cyclitol and a secondary amino compound. It is a conjugate acid of a validoxylamine B(1+).
