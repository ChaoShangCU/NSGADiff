The molecule is a leukotriene that is 10,11-dihydroleukotriene B4 in which the three methyl hydrogens at position 20 have been replaced by hydroxy groups. It is a leukotriene, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from a leukotriene B4. It is a conjugate acid of a 10,11-dihydro-20,20,20-trihydroxyleukotriene B4(1-).
