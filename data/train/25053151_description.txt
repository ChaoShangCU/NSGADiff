The molecule is 8a,13-dihydrobenzo[2,3]pyrrolo[3',4':5,6]indolizino[8,7-b]indole-6,8(5aH,7H)-dione which is substituted by chlorines at positions 2 and 10, hydroxy groups at positions 5a and 8a, a methoxy group at position 14, and a methyl group at position 7 (the 5aR,8aS diastereoisomer). It has been isolated from the culture broth of Streptomyces uncialis. It is a cladoniamide, an organic heterohexacyclic compound, an organochlorine compound, a dicarboximide, a tertiary alcohol and a diol.
