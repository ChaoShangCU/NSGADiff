The molecule is a precorrin carboxylic acid anion that is the dianionic form of hydrogenobyrinic acid a,c-diamide. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a conjugate base of a hydrogenobyrinic acid a,c-diamide. It is a conjugate acid of a hydrogenobyrinic acid a,c-diamide(4-).
