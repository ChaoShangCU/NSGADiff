The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is 5alpha-ergosta-7,24(28)-diene which is substituted at the 3beta and 4alpha positions by hydroxy and formyl groups, respectively. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a Delta(7)-sterol, a member of phytosterols and a 4alpha-formyl steroid. It derives from a 4alpha-hydroxymethyl-5alpha-ergosta-7,24(28)-dien-3beta-ol. It derives from a hydride of an ergostane.
