The molecule is a chlorobenzoyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 4-chlorobenzoic acid It is a member of monochlorobenzenes and a chlorobenzoyl-CoA. It derives from a benzoyl-CoA and a 4-chlorobenzoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-chlorobenzoyl-CoA(4-).
