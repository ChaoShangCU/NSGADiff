The molecule is an member of the class of phenylureas that is urea having 4-chlorophenyl and 4-methyl-2-(thiophen-2-yl)-1,3-thiazol-5-yl groups attached at positions 1 and 3 respectively. It is a member of thiophenes, a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of phenylureas.
