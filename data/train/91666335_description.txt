The molecule is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide that is 3',5'-cyclic AMP in which the hydrogen at position 2 on the purine fragment is replaced by a 6-aminohexylthio group. It is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide, an adenyl ribonucleotide, a primary amino compound and an aryl sulfide. It derives from a 3',5'-cyclic AMP.
