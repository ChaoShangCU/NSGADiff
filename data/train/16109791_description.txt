The molecule is a diterpenoid of the clerodane group isolated from the leaves and flowers of Casearia nigrescens. It exhibits cytotoxicity against A2780 human ovarian cancer cell line. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a diterpenoid, an acetate ester, a butyrate ester, a cyclic ether, a secondary alcohol and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
