The molecule is a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid with formula C21H26N2O3, isolated from several species of Tabernaemontana. It has a role as a plant metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and an apoptosis inducer. It is a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid, a tertiary amino compound, a methyl ester, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and an alkaloid ester.
