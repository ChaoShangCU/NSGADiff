The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is 1,8-dihydroxy-3-methylanthracen-9(10H)-one substituted by a (1-O-acetyl)-alpha-L-lyxopyranosyl moiety at position 10 via a C-glycosidic linkage (the 10S stereoisomer). It is isolated from the leaves of Alvaradoa haitiensis and exhibits cytotoxicity against human oral epidermoid carcinoma. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an acetate ester, a C-glycosyl compound, a member of anthracenes and a polyphenol.
