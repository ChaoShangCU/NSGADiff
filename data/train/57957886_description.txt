The molecule is a carbamate ester that is the 2,2,2-trifluoroethyl ester of [(2S)-3-methyl-1-(4-methylbenzamido)butan-2-yl]carbamic acid. It is a novel systemic fungicide used for controlling rice blast. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a member of benzamides, a carbamate ester, an organofluorine compound and a carbamate fungicide.
