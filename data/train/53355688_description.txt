The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid that is 4-methylergosta-7,24(28)-dien-3-ol substituted by additional hydroxy groups at positions 2 and 21 (the 2alpha,3beta,4alpha,5alpha stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the roots of Breynia fruticosa and exhibits cytotoxic activity against human cancer cell lines. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 21-hydroxy steroid and a triol.
