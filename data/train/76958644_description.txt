The molecule is an oligomycin with formula C45H74O10 that is oligomycin A in which the hydroxy group that is alpha- to a macrolide keto group has been replaced by a hydrogen. It is an inhibitor of the mitochondrial F1F0 ATP synthase. It has a role as an EC 3.6.3.14 (H(+)-transporting two-sector ATPase) inhibitor. It is an oligomycin, a tetrol and a diketone.
