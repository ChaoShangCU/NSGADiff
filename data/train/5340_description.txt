The molecule is a 1,3-thiazole compound having a 4-aminobenzenesulfonamido group at the 2-position. It has a role as an antiinfective agent, an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic, an EC 2.5.1.15 (dihydropteroate synthase) inhibitor and a drug allergen. It is a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a sulfonamide, a substituted aniline and a sulfonamide antibiotic. It derives from a sulfanilamide.
