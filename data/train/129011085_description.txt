The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone having a beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->2)-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 17. It is a 3-oxo-5alpha-steroid, a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a 17beta-hydroxy-5alpha-androstan-3-one. It is a conjugate acid of a 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone 17-O-[beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->2)-glucuronide](2-).
