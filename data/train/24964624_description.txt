The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is [1,2,4]triazolo[3,4-f][1,6]naphthyridin-3(2H)-one substituted at positions 8 and 9 respectively by 4-(1-aminocyclobutyl)phenyl and phenyl groups. It has a role as an EC 2.7.1.137 (phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase) inhibitor. It derives from a 1,6-naphthyridine.
