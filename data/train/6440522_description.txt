The molecule is an organofluorine compound consisting of cyclopropanecarboxylic acid having a 2-chloro-3,3,3-trifluoroprop-1-en-1-yl group at the 3-position and two methyl groups at the 2-position. It is an organochlorine compound, an organofluorine compound and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a cyclopropanecarboxylic acid.
