The molecule is a trihydroxyflavanone that is (S)-naringenin substituted by a geranyl group at position 8. Isolated from Macaranga bicolor, it exhibits antibacterial and antineoplastic activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and an antibacterial agent. It is a trihydroxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a (2S)-flavan-4-one. It derives from a (S)-naringenin.
