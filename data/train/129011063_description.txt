The molecule is a 1-[(Z)-alk-1-enyl]-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(N-acyl)ethanolamine in which the alk-1-enyl and N-acyl groups are specified as 1Z-octadecenyl and hexadecanoyl respectively. It derives from a hexadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-(1Z-octadecenyl)-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(N-hexadecanoyl)ethanolamine(1-).
