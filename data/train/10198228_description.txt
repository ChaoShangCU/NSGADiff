The molecule is an N-acylpyrrolidine obtained by formal condenstion of the carboxy group of L-proline and the secondary amino group of (R)-pyrrolidine-2-carboxylic acid. It has a role as an EC 3.4.* (hydrolases acting on peptide bond) inhibitor. It is a member of boronic acids, a N-acylpyrrolidine and a L-proline derivative.
