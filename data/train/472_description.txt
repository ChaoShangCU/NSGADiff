The molecule is a dioxo monocarboxylic acid, a beta-diketone, a substituted aniline, a 2-oxo monocarboxylic acid, a 4-oxo monocarboxylic acid and an aromatic ketone. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It derives from a butyric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-(2-aminophenyl)-2,4-dioxobutanoate.
