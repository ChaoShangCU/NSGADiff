The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone that is the 1beta-hydroxy derivative of arbusculin A. Isolated from Saussurea lappa, it exhibits inhibitory activity against melanogenesis. It has a role as a metabolite and a melanin synthesis inhibitor. It is a sesquiterpene lactone, a diol and an organic heterotricyclic compound. It derives from an arbusculin A.
