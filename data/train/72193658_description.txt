The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a beta-L-rhamnopyranosyl moiety at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monosaccharide derivative, a tetrahydroxyflavone, a beta-L-rhamnoside, a quercetin O-glycoside and a member of flavonols. It derives from a beta-L-rhamnopyranose.
