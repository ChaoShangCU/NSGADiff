The molecule is a lignan with a dibenzocyclooctadiene skeleton attached to a fatty acid ester side chain. It is isolated from Kadsura interior and has been shown to exhibit anti-HIV activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV agent. It is an aromatic ether, a lignan, a fatty acid ester, an organic heteropentacyclic compound and an oxacycle. It derives from an angelic acid.
