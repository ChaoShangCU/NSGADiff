The molecule is a thromboxane anion that is the conjugate base of 18-hydroxycarbocyclic thromboxane A2, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a thromboxane anion. It derives from a carbocyclic thromboxane A2(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 18-hydroxycarbocyclic thromboxane A2.
