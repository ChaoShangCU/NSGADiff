The molecule is a member of the class of leukotrienes that is 6-trans-leukotriene B4 bearing an additional hydroxy substituent at position 20. It is a leukotriene, a long-chain fatty acid, an omega-hydroxy fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from a 6-trans-leukotriene B4. It is a conjugate acid of a 20-hydroxy-6-trans-leukotriene B4(1-).
