The molecule is a beta-D-glucosylceramide in which a beta-D-glucosyl residue attached to the primary hydroxyl group of N-tetracosanoyl-14-methylhexadecasphingosine. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It derives from a 14-methylhexadecasphingosine and a tetracosanoic acid.
