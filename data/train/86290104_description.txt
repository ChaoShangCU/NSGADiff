The molecule is an organosulfonic acid that is 2-oxoethanesulfonic acid substituted by a (2,6-dimethylphenyl)(2-methoxyethyl)amino group at position 2. It is a metabolite of the herbicide dimethachlor. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is an aromatic amide, an ether and an organosulfonic acid.
