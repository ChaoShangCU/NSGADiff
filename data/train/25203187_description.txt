The molecule is a steroid sulfate oxoanion and N-acylglycinate that is the dianion of sulfoglycolithocholic acid arising from deprotonation of carboxylic acid and sulfate functions; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a steroid sulfate oxoanion, a N-acylglycinate and a sulfoglycolithocholate anion. It is a conjugate base of a sulfoglycolithocholic acid.
