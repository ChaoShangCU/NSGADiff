The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate in which the phosphatidyl acyl groups at postions 1 and 2 are specified as heptadecanoyl and lauroyl respectively. It is a 1-acyl-2-dodecanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate and a dodecanoate ester. It derives from a heptadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-heptadecanoyl-2-lauroyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-).
