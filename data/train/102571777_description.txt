The molecule is an icosanoid that is (6E,8Z,11Z,13E)-icosa-6,8,11,13-tetraenoic acid substituted at positions 5 and 15 by hydroperoxy and hydroxy groups respectively. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an icosanoid, a hydroperoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-hydroperoxy-15-HETE(1-).
