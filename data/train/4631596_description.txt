The molecule is a dicarboximide of bicyclo[2.2.2]octene, in which the nitrogen is substituted by a 4-acetamidophenyl group and one bridgehead is substituted by a 1-(carboxymethoxy)carboxamido group. It has a role as a hapten. It is a carbamate ester, a dicarboximide, a bridged compound and a member of acetamides.
