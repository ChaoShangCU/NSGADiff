The molecule is a thiopyranopyrimidine in which a 7,8-dihydro-5H-thiopyrano[4,3-d]pyrimidine skeleton is substituted at C-4 by a hydroxy group and at C-2 by a para-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl group. It has a role as a tankyrase inhibitor. It is a thiopyranopyrimidine and a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes.
