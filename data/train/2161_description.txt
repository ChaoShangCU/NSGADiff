The molecule is a pyridochromene-derived monocarboxylic acid having an amino substituent at the 2-position, an oxo substituent at the 5-position and an isopropyl substituent at the 7-position. It has a role as an anti-allergic agent, an anti-ulcer drug and a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug. It is a pyridochromene and a monocarboxylic acid.
