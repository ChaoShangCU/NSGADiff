The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate in which both phosphatidyl acyl groups are specified as (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1,2-di[(4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-).
