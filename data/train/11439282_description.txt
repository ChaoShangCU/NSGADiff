The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is 1,2-dihydro-6H-furo[2,3-c]xanthene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 10, a methoxy group at position 9 and methyl groups at positions 1, 1 and 2 and an oxo group at position 6. Isolated from the stem barks of Garcinia vieillardii, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a member of phenols, an aromatic ether and a cyclic ether.
