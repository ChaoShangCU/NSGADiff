The molecule is a member of the class of dafachronic acids that is (25S)-5alpha-cholestan-26-oic acid which is substituted at position 3 by an oxo group and which contains double bonds at the 1-2 and 7-8 positions. Found in Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a member of dafachronic acids, a 3-oxo-Delta(1) steroid and a 3-oxo Delta(7)-steroid.
