The molecule is a member of the class of 4-pyridones that is nygerone B in which the N-phenyl substituent has been fluorinated at the para position. It has been isolated from Aspergillus niger ATCC 1015. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid amide, a member of 4-pyridones, a member of monofluorobenzenes and a biaryl. It derives from a nygerone B.
