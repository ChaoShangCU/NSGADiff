The molecule is a trimethoxyflavone that is the 7,3',4'-trimethyl ether derivative of quercetin. It has been isolated from Euodia confusa. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone, a member of flavonols, a trimethoxyflavone and a member of 3'-methoxyflavones. It derives from a quercetin.
