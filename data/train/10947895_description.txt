The molecule is a member of the class of benzodioxoles that is paroxetine in which the piperidine hydrogen is substituted by a methyl group. It is a drug impurity of the antidepressant, paroxetine. It has a role as an apoptosis inducer, a serotonin uptake inhibitor and an impurity. It is a member of benzodioxoles, an aromatic ether, a member of piperidines, a tertiary amino compound and a member of monofluorobenzenes. It derives from a paroxetine.
