The molecule is a polyunsaturated hydroxy-fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 14-oxo-DoHE, arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, an oxo fatty acid anion and an oxodocosahexaenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 14-oxo-DoHE.
