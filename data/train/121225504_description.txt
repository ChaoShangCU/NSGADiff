The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone obtained by formal condensation of one of the carboxy groups of oxalic acid with the 15-hydroxy group of 11beta,13-dihydro-8-deoxylactucin. Found in chicory. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an azulenofuran, a cyclic terpene ketone, an enone, an oxo monocarboxylic acid and a sesquiterpene lactone. It derives from an oxalic acid.
