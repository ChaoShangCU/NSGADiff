The molecule is tetracycline in which a hydrogen attached to the amide nitrogen is substituted by a 4-[(2-hydroxyethyl)piperazin-1-yl]methyl group. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent. It is a member of tetracyclines, a N-alkylpiperazine and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It is a conjugate base of a pipacycline(1+).
