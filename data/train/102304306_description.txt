The molecule is a member of the class of isocoumarins that is 1H-isochromen-1-one substituted by a 2-oxopropyl group at position 3 and by hydroxy groups at positions 6 and 8. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a hexaketide, a member of isocoumarins, a methyl ketone and a member of phenols.
