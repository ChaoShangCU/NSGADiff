The molecule is trianion of (R)-5-phosphomevalonic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate OH groups; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (R)-5-phosphomevalonic acid.
