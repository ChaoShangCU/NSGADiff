The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-12-en-28-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2, 3 and 23 respectively (the 2alpha,3alpha-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the leaves of Rosa laevigata. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a triol and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
