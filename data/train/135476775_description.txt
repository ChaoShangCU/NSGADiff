The molecule is a 6-isopentenylaminopurine having a 3-amino-3-carboxypropyl group attached at the 3-position. It is a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid and a 6-isopentenylaminopurine. It derives from a butyric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a discadenine(1-). It is a tautomer of a discadenine zwitterion.
