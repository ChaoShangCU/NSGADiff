The molecule is a hexapeptide consisting of N-{[3-(2-benzamido-4-methyl-1,3-thiazol-5-yl)-1H-pyrazol-5-yl]carbonyl}glycine, D-arginine, glycine, D-alpha-aspartic acid, D-alpha-aspartic acid, and D-alpha-aspartic 1-amide residues coupled in sequence. An angiotensin II type 2 receptor agonist. It has a role as an angiotensin receptor agonist. It is a biaryl, an oligopeptide, a member of benzamides, a member of 1,3-thiazoles and a member of pyrazoles.
