The molecule is an oxoicosatetraenoic acid that is (5Z,8Z,12E,14Z)-icosatetraenoic acid bearing a single oxo substituent located at position 12. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an oxoicosatetraenoic acid and an enone. It derives from an icosa-5,8,12,14-tetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an 11-oxo-ETE(1-).
