The molecule is a bile acid anion that is the conjugate base of 3beta,7alpha-dihydroxy-5beta-cholan-24-oic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. The 3beta-hydroxy epimer of chenodeoxycholate. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 3beta,7alpha-dihydroxy-5beta-cholan-24-oic acid.
