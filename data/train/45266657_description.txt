The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion resulting from the removal of both protons from the phosphate group of 5-amino-1-(5-phospho-D-ribosyl)imidazole-4-carboxamide. It is the major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of an AICA ribonucleotide.
