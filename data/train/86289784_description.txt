The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside obtained by formal condensation of the alcoholic hydroxy group of 14-hydroxytetradecanoic acid (14-hydroxymyristic acid) with ascarylopyranose (the alpha anomer). It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside. It derives from a 14-hydroxymyristic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an oscr#24(1-).
