The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isovitexin substituted at positions 4 and 7 by beta-glucosyl 6-O-sinapoylglucosyl residues respectively It has a role as a metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a monohydroxyflavone, a cinnamate ester, a glycosyloxyflavone and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a trans-sinapic acid and an isovitexin.
