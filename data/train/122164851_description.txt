The molecule is a leukotriene anion obtained by deprotonation of the three carboxy groups and protonation of the cysteinyl alpha-amino group of 20-carboxyleukotriene E4; major species at pH 7.3. It is a leukotriene anion and a tricarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a 20-carboxyleukotriene E4.
