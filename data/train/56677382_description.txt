The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound with formula C23H27ClO7 isolated from Chaetomium globosum. It is a diastereoisomer of chaetomugilin A (which has a significantly different optical rotation). It has a role as a Chaetomium metabolite. It is an enone, a lactol, a delta-lactone, an organochlorine compound and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
