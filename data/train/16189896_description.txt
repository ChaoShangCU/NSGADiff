The molecule is an N-acylpiperidine obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 5-[(2-chloro-4-fluorophenoxy)methyl]-1,2-oxazole-3-carboxylic acid with the secondary amino group of 4-(pyridin-4-yl)piperidine It has a role as a P450 inhibitor. It is a N-acylpiperidine, an aromatic amide, an aromatic ether, a member of isoxazoles, a member of pyridines, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of monofluorobenzenes.
