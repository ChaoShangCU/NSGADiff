The molecule is a carbohydrate acid derivative that consists of D-glucuronic acid having a 6-O-sulfo-N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosaminyl residue attached at position 4. An intermediate glycan in the degradation of heparan sulfate. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is an amino disaccharide, a carbohydrate acid derivative and an oligosaccharide sulfate.
