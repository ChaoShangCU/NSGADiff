The molecule is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid that is the 15-acetoxy derivative of orbiculin G. It is isolated from Microtropis japonica and exhibits antitubercular activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antitubercular agent. It is a benzoate ester, a bridged compound, a cyclic ether, a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid and an organic heterotricyclic compound. It derives from an orbiculin G.
