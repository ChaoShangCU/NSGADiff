The molecule is an oligosaccharide sulfate consisting of 2-(sulfoamino)-alpha-D-glucopyranosyl, 2-O-sulfo-alpha-L-idopyranuronosyl, 6-O-sulfo-2-sulfoamino-alpha-D-glucopyranosyl and 2-O-sulfo-L-idopyranuronic acid residues joined in sequence by (1->4) glycosidic bonds. It is an oligosaccharide sulfate and a member of sulfamic acids.
