The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of beta-D-galactopyranose, 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by (1->4) glycosidic bonds. It is a member of acetamides, an amino trisaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
