The molecule is a cembrane diterpenoid that is cembra-1E,3E,7E,11Z,15-pentaen-20,10-olide substituted by a methoxy group at position 5. It has been isolated from the leaves of Croton gratissimus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a cembrane diterpenoid, a diterpene lactone, a macrocycle and an ether.
