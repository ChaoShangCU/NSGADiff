The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound and sesquiterpenoid that is 11-oxatricyclo[5.3.1.0(2,6)]undecane-1,8-diol which is substituted by methyl groups at positions 5 and 8, and by an isopropyl group at position 5. The absolute stereochemistry is not known; it is either 1R,2S,5R,6R,7R,8R (as shown), or the enantiomer. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a sesquiterpenoid, a lactol, a tertiary alcohol and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
