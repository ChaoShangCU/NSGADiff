The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazoles that is 1H-pyrazole carrying 1,4-benzodioxane-6-yl and 5-ethyl-2,4-dihydroxyphenyl substituents at positions 4 and 5 respectively. It has a role as a Hsp90 inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an apoptosis inducer. It is a benzodioxine, a member of pyrazoles and a member of resorcinols.
