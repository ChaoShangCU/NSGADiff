The molecule is a member of the class of piperazines that is (R)-2-methylpiperazine in which the two amino hydrogens at positions 1 and 4 are replaced by 5-cyanopyridin-2-yl and 4-benzyl-7-chlorophthalazin-1-yl groups respectively. It has a role as an EC 4.1.2.27 (sphinganine-1-phosphate aldolase) inhibitor. It is a N-arylpiperazine, a member of phthalazines, an organochlorine compound and a cyanopyridine.
