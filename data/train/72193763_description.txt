The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3R,11Z)-3-hydroxyicosenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a (R)-3-hydroxyacyl-CoA(4-) and a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3R,11Z)-3-hydroxyicosenoyl-CoA.
