The molecule is a polyketide that is monacolin L bearing an additional hydroxy substituent at position 8. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, a fungal metabolite and an EC 1.1.1.34/EC 1.1.1.88 (hydroxymethylglutaryl-CoA reductase) inhibitor. It is a secondary alcohol, a polyketide, a carbobicyclic compound, a member of 2-pyranones and a member of hexahydronaphthalenes. It derives from a monacolin L.
