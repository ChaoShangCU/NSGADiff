The molecule is a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid that is (5beta)-cholan-24-oic acid substituted by beta-hydroxy and oxo groups at positions 3 and 7 respectively. It is a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid and an oxo-5beta-cholanic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3beta-hydroxy-7-oxo-5beta-cholan-24-oate.
