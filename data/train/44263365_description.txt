The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 17. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a 3-oxo-5alpha-steroid. It derives from a 17beta-hydroxy-5alpha-androstan-3-one. It is a conjugate acid of a 5alpha-dihydrotestosterone 17-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
