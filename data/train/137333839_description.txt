The molecule is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 2-hydroxypalmitoleic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a monounsaturated fatty acid anion. It derives from a palmitoleate. It is a conjugate base of a 2-hydroxypalmitoleic acid.
