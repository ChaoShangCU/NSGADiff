The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA in which the 3-hydroxy fatty acyl group is specified as 3-hydroxyicosanoyl. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 3-hydroxyicosanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxyicosanoyl-CoA(4-).
