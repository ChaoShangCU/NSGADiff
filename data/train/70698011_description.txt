The molecule is a limonoid based on a mexicanolide-type skeleton isolated from Trichilia connaroides. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a bridged compound, a member of furans, a limonoid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a methyl ester and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a 2-methylbutyric acid.
