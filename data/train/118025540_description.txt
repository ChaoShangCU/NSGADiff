The molecule is a member of the class of indoles that is 5-chloro-4-(1H-indol-3-yl)-N-[(3R)-piperidin-3-yl]pyrimidin-2-amine in which the piperidine NH group is substituted by a 4-{[(2E)-4-(dimethylamino)but-2-enoyl]amino}benzoyl group. It is a first-in-class CDK12 and CDK13 covalent kinase inhibitor with IC50 of 158 nM and 69 nM, respectively. It has a role as an apoptosis inducer, an antineoplastic agent and an EC 2.7.11.22 (cyclin-dependent kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of indoles, an organochlorine compound, a N-acylpiperidine, an aminopyrimidine, an enamide, a secondary carboxamide and a secondary amino compound.
