The molecule is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid that is (8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosatrienoic acid having the epoxide group across positions 5-6 and the hydroxy substituent located at position 20. It is an epoxy(hydroxy)icosatrienoic acid and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5,6-epoxy-20-hydroxy-(8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosatrienoate.
