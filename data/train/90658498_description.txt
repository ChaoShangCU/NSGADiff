The molecule is a glutathione conjugate in which the mercapto hydrogen of glutathione has been replaced by an N-hydroxy-2-(indol-3-yl)ethanimidoyl group. It is a member of indoles, a glutathione conjugate and a N-hydroxyimidothioate. It is a conjugate acid of an (E)-1-(glutathion-S-yl)-2-(indol-3-yl)acetohydroximate(1-).
