The molecule is a stilbenoid isolated from the stems of Kobresia nepalensis and has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against topoisomerase II. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 5.99.1.3 [DNA topoisomerase (ATP-hydrolysing)] inhibitor. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a cyclic ether, a polyphenol and a stilbenoid.
