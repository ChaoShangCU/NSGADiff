The molecule is a organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate groups of ADP-5-ethyl-4-methylthiazole-2-carboxylic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It derives from an ADP(3-). It is a conjugate base of an ADP-5-ethyl-4-methylthiazole-2-carboxylic acid.
