The molecule is an organic phosphoramidate that is 5'-O-[amino(3-aminopropoxy)phosphoryl]adenosine in which the nitrogen of the phosphoramidite is acylated by a (2S)-2-amino-3-carboxypropanoyl group. It is an organic phosphoramidate and a nucleotide-amino acid. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate.
