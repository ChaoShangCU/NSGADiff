The molecule is a L-histidine derivative that is N,N-dimethyl-L-histidine substituted at positions N3 and C5 on the imidazole ring by methyl and mercapto groups respectively. It has a role as a marine metabolite, an antioxidant and a radical scavenger. It is an aryl thiol and a L-histidine derivative. It is a tautomer of an ovothiol C zwitterion.
