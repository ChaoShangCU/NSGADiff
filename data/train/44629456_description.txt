The molecule is an erythromycin cation that is the conjugate acid of erythromycin A, arising from protonation of the tertiary amino group on the 3,4,6-trideoxy-3-(dimethylamino)-beta-D-xylo-hexopyranosyl residue; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of an erythromycin A.
