The molecule is a beta-lactam that is 7-oxo-1-azabicyclo[3.2.0]heptane-2-carboxylic acid carring additional (2-aminoethyl)sulfanyl and methyl substituents at positions 3 and 6 respectively. An intermediate in the biosynthesis of carbapenem. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an organic heterobicyclic compound, a beta-lactam, a monocarboxylic acid, an aliphatic sulfide and a primary amino compound.
