The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid that is oxoacetic acid substituted by a (2,4-dimethylthiophen-3-yl)(1-methoxypropan-2-yl)amino group at position 2. It is a metabolite of the herbicide dimethenamid. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a member of thiophenes, a monocarboxylic acid, an ether and an aromatic amide.
