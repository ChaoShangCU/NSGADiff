The molecule is a polyene antibiotic that is TMC-1A in which the 2,4-dimethyloct-2-enoyl group has been replaced by an (E,E)-4,6-dimethyldeca-2,4-dienoyl group. TMC-1C is an antitumour antibiotic isolated from Streptomyces sp. A-230. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is an enone, a cyclic ketone, a polyene antibiotic, an enol, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, an enamide and a secondary carboxamide.
