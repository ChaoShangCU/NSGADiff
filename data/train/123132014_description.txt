The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C24H39NO8, originally isolated from Aconitum carmichaeli. It has a role as a plant metabolite, a human urinary metabolite and a xenobiotic. It is a bridged compound, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tetrol and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
