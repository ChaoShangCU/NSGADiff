The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of (10aS)-10,10a-dihydrophenazine-1-carboxylic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion and an aromatic amino-acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (10aS)-10,10a-dihydrophenazine-1-carboxylic acid.
