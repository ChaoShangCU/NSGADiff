The molecule is a hydrochloride obtained by combining equimolar amounts of tropisetron and hydrogen chloride. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent, an antiemetic, an apoptosis inhibitor, an immunomodulator, a neuroprotective agent, a nicotinic acetylcholine receptor agonist, a serotonergic antagonist and a trypanocidal drug. It contains a tropisetron(1+).
