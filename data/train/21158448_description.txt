The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of all-cis-icosa-8,11,14-trienoic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a straight-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an icosatrienoate. It is a conjugate base of an all-cis-icosa-8,11,14-trienoic acid.
