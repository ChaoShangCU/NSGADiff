The molecule is the hydrochloride salt of cinanserin. It is an inhibitor of 3C-like proteinase of severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus (SARS-Cov) and reduces virus replication in vitro. It has a role as an antiviral agent, an EC 3.4.22.69 (SARS coronavirus main proteinase) inhibitor and an anticoronaviral agent. It contains a cinanserin(1+).
