The molecule is a member of the class of hydroquinones that is benzene-1,4-diol substituted by a methoxy group at position 5 and a prop-en-1-yl group at position 2 which in turn is substituted by a 3-hydroxy-4-methoxyphenyl group at position 1. It has been isolated from Pterocarpus santalinus. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an aromatic ether and a member of hydroquinones.
