The molecule is a trihydroxyflavone that is the 4'''-O-sinapoyl derivative of isoscoparin 2''-O-arabinoside. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monomethoxyflavone, a trihydroxyflavone, a C-glycosyl compound, a disaccharide derivative and a cinnamate ester. It derives from an isoscoparin and a trans-sinapic acid.
