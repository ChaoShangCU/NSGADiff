The molecule is an epoxydocosapentaenoic acid (7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid in which the epoxy group is located at the 13,14-position. An intermediate in the specialised proresolving mediators It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a (7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-13,14-epoxydocosapentaenoate.
