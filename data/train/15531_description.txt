The molecule is a dibromobenzene that is 2,6-dibromophenol substituted by a cyano group at position 4. It has a role as an environmental contaminant, a xenobiotic and a herbicide. It is a member of phenols, a dibromobenzene and a hydroxynitrile. It derives from a 2,6-dibromophenol. It is a conjugate acid of a 3,5-dibromo-4-oxidobenzonitrile(1-).
