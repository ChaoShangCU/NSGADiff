The molecule is a leukotriene that is leukotriene B4 in which two of the methyl hydrogens at position 20 have been replaced by hydroxy groups. It is an aldehyde hydrate, a leukotriene, a long-chain fatty acid, a secondary alcohol and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from a leukotriene B4. It is a conjugate acid of a 20,20-dihydroxyleukotriene B4(1-).
