The molecule is this compound belongs to the class of organic compounds known as quinic acids and derivatives. These are compounds containing a quinic acid moiety (or a derivative thereof), which is a cyclitol made up of a cyclohexane ring that bears four hydroxyl groups at positions 1,3.4, and 5, as well as a carboxylic acid at position 1. It is a quinic acid, a cinnamate ester, a member of styrenes, a member of catechols, a member of cyclohexanols, a tertiary alcohol and a polyol.
