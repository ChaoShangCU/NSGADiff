The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranosyl, beta-D-galactopyranosyl, 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranosyl and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-D-galactopyranoyl residues joined in sequence by (1->3) glycosidic linkages. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
