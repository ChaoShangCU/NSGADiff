The molecule is the prodrug of fluphenazine, an antipsychotic drug used for the symptomatic management of psychosis in patients with schizophrenia. It has a role as a phenothiazine antipsychotic drug, a prodrug and a dopaminergic antagonist. It is a member of phenothiazines, a decanoate ester, an organofluorine compound and a N-alkylpiperazine. It derives from a fluphenazine.
