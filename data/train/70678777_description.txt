The molecule is an antiviral lignan isolated from Larrea tridentata, which consists of a 3,4-dimethyltetrahydrofuran skeleton substituted by a dihydroxybenzyl and hydroxyphenyl groups at position 2 and 5 respectively (the 2R,3S,4R,5R stereoisomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a lignan and a member of oxolanes.
