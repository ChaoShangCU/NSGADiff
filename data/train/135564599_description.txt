The molecule is a ketoxime that is the oxime of indan-1-one in which the hydrogen at position 5 has been replaced by an imidazol-5-yl group which has itself been substituted at positions 2 and 4 by p-[2-(dimethylamino)ethoxy]phenyl and pyridin-4-yl groups, respectively. It is a ketoxime, a member of imidazoles, a tertiary amino compound, an aromatic ether and a member of pyridines.
