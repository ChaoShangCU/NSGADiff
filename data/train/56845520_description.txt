The molecule is a 2-chloro-N-(2,4-dimethylthiophen-3-yl)-N-[(2R)-1-methoxypropan-2-yl]acetamide that has R configuration. It is the inactive enantiomer; its (active) enantiomer is the herbicide known as dimethenamid-P; the racemate is the herbicide dimethenamid. It is an enantiomer of a dimethenamid-P.
