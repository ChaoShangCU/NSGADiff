The molecule is a limonoid that is epoxyazadiradione in which the acetyl group has been replaced by a benzoyl group. It has been isolated from Azadirachta indica. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a benzoate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a member of furans, a limonoid, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a 3-oxo-Delta(1) steroid and an epoxy steroid. It derives from an epoxyazadiradione.
