The molecule is a hydroxyisoflavone with a quinone structure in ring B. Isolated from Dalbergia parviflora, it exhibits antineoplastic and anti-inflammatory activities. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an anti-inflammatory agent and a metabolite. It is a methoxyisoflavone, a hydroxyisoflavone, an oxoisoflavone and a member of 1,4-benzoquinones.
