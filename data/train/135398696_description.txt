The molecule is an aromatic amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of phenylacetic acid with the exocyclic amino group of 4-(5-amino-6-benzylpyrazin-2-yl)phenol. It has a role as a member of oxidized luciferins. It is a member of pyrazines, a member of phenols and an aromatic amide. It derives from a Renilla luciferin. It is a conjugate acid of an oxidized Renilla luciferin(1-).
