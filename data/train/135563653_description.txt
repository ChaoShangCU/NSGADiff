The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is 6H-benzo[c]chromen-6-one substituted by a methyl group at position 1, chloro group at position 2, methoxy group at position 3, and by hydroxy groups at positions 7 and 9. It is a metabolite isolated from Hyalodendriella species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a benzochromenone, an aromatic ether, an organic heterotricyclic compound, an organic hydroxy compound and an organochlorine compound. It derives from a 6H-dibenzo[b,d]pyran-6-one.
