The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as icosanoyl (arachidoyl) and hexadecanoyl (palmitoyl) respectively. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a phosphatidylethanolamine 36:0 and a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine. It derives from an icosanoic acid and a hexadecanoic acid.
