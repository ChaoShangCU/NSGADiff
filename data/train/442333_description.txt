The molecule is a macrocycle that is oxyacanthan that is substituted by methoxy groups at positions 6, 6', and 7, methyl groups at positions 2 and 2', and a hydroxy group at the 12' position. It is a macrocycle, a member of isoquinolines, a tertiary amino compound, a member of phenols and a bisbenzylisoquinoline alkaloid. It derives from a hydride of an oxyacanthan.
