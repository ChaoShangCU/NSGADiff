The molecule is a hydroperoxyoctadecatrienoic acid that is (9Z,11E,14Z)-octadecatrienoic acid in which the hydroperoxy group is located at position 13S. It has a role as a Brassica napus metabolite. It derives from a (9Z,11E,14Z)-octadeca-9,11,14-trienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (9Z,11E,13S,14Z)-13-hydroperoxyoctadeca-9,11,14-trienoate.
