The molecule is an oxo seco-steroid that is 9-oxo-9,10-seco-23,24-bisnorchola-1,3,5(10)-trien-22-oic acid bearing an additional phenolic hydroxy substituent at position 3. It is an oxo seco-steroid, a member of phenols, a 7-oxo monocarboxylic acid and a carbobicyclic compound. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxy-9-oxo-9,10-seco-23,24-bisnorchola-1,3,5(10)-trien-22-oate.
