The molecule is a mixture of three allergens: diphenylguanidine, zinc dibutyldithiocarbamate and zinc diethyldithiocarbamate, chemicals used as fungicides and pesticides, and also in the manufacture of many rubber products. It contains a zinc dibutyldithiocarbamate, a zinc diethyldithiocarbamate and a 1,3-diphenylguanidine.
