The molecule is a linear amino tetrasaccharide comprising alpha-sialyl, beta-D-galactosyl, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl and N-acetyl-D-galactosamine residues in a (2->3), (1->4) and (1->3) sequence. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
