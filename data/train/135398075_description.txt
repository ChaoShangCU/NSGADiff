The molecule is a tertiary ammonium ion result from the protonation of the tertiary amino group of dehydrosecodine (the enamine form). An intermediate in the biosynthesis of aspidospermaand iboga alkaloids. It is a methyl ester, a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid, an alkaloid ester and a tertiary ammonium ion. It is a conjugate acid of a dehydrosecodine.
