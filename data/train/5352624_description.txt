The molecule is an aryl sulfide that is a metabolite of sulindac. A non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, which also has anticancer activity. It has a role as a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, an apoptosis inducer and an antineoplastic agent. It is an aryl sulfide, an organofluorine compound and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a sulindac.
