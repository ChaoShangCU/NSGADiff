The molecule is an organic anion that is the conjugate base of 3-linalylflaviolin, obtained by deprotonation of the 2-hydroxy group. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a 3-linalylflaviolin.
