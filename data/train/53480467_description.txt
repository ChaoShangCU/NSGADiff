The molecule is a lysophosphatidylcholine 20:3 in which the acyl group at position 1 is (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoyl (meadoyl) and the hydroxy group at position 2 is unsubstituted. It is a lysophosphatidylcholine 20:3 and a 1-O-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid.
