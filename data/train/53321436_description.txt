The molecule is a hydroxyisoflavanone that is (3S)-isoflavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 4' and methoxy groups at positions 7 and 2' respectively. It has been isolated from Crotalaria lachnophora. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxyisoflavanone and a methoxyisoflavanone. It derives from a (3S)-isoflavanone.
