The molecule is a member of the class of thienotriazolodiazepines that is the methyl ester of [(6R)-4-(4-chlorophenyl)-2,3,9-trimethyl-6H-thieno[3,2-f][1,2,4]triazolo[4,3-a][1,4]diazepin-6-yl]acetic acid. It is a member of monochlorobenzenes, a thienotriazolodiazepine and a methyl ester. It is an enantiomer of a MS-417.
