The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin carrying two alpha-L-rhamnosyl residues at positions O-3 and O-7. It is an alpha-L-rhamnoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol, a trihydroxyflavone and a quercetin O-glycoside. It is a conjugate acid of a quercetin 3,7-bis-O-alpha-L-rhamnoside(1-).
