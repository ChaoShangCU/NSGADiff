The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is estriol in which the phenolic hydrogen has been replaced by a beta-D-glucuronyl residue. It has a role as a human urinary metabolite. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a 3-hydroxy steroid, a member of phenols and a 17beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from an estriol. It is a conjugate acid of an estriol 16-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
