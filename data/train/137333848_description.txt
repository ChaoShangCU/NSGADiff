The molecule is a steroid acid that is 4beta,14-dimethyl-5alpha,9beta-9,19-cyclocholest-24-en-3beta-ol in which the 4alpha hydrogen has been replaced by a carboxy group. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a steroid acid, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3beta-sterol, a member of phytosterols and a member of cyclopropanes. It is a conjugate acid of a 3beta-hydroxy-9beta-9,19-cyclolanost-24-en-28-oate.
