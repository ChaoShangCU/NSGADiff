The molecule is a monounsaturated epoxy fatty acid composed of cis-9-octadecenoic acid having a 12,13-epoxy group. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an epoxy fatty acid, a monounsaturated fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and an epoxyoctadecenoic acid. It derives from an octadec-9-enoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a vernolate.
