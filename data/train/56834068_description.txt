The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone that is flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 7 and a 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-1-en-1-yl group at position 6. It has been isolated as a racemate from the bark of Cryptocarya chartacea. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavanone and a monocarboxylic acid.
