The molecule is an acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 2-hydroxy-7-methoxy-5-methyl-1-naphthoic acid. It derives from a 2-hydroxy-7-methoxy-5-methyl-1-naphthoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxy-7-methoxy-5-methyl-1-naphthoyl-CoA(4-).
