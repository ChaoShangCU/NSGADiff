The molecule is an extended flavonoid that is 5a,10a-dihydro-11H-[1]benzofuro[3,2-b]chromen-11-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3, 8 and 10a and a 3-hydroxy-2,6,10-trimethyldodeca-1,6,10-trien-12-yl group at position 5a. It has been isolated from the twigs of Morus nigra. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an extended flavonoid, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and a polyphenol.
