The molecule is a glycosphingolipid isolated from a marine sponge Oceanapia sp. that exhibits antifungal activity against fluconazole-resistant yeast Candida glabrata with an MIC value of 30 mug/ml. It has a role as a metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a glycosphingolipid, a dihydroxyquinoline and an ether. It derives from a beta-D-galactose.
