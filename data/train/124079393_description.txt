The molecule is a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid with formula C21H24N2O3, isolated from Strychnos icaja and Strychnos nux-vomica. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactam, a monoterpenoid indole alkaloid, an olefinic compound, an organic heterohexacyclic compound, a primary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound and a secondary alcohol.
