The molecule is an oligosaccharide sulfate consisting of 2-deoxy-2-(sulfoamino)-alpha-D-glucopyranose and 2-O-sulfo-alpha-L-idopyranuronic acid joined in sequence by a (1->4) glycosidic bond. It is an oligosaccharide sulfate, a member of sulfamic acids, a monocarboxylic acid and a disaccharide derivative.
