The molecule is an anthocyanin cation consisting of delphinidin with three beta-D-glucosyl residues attached to the 3-, 3'- and 5-hydroxy groups. It is an anthocyanin cation, a beta-D-glucoside and a 5-hydroxyanthocyanin O-beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a delphinidin. It is a conjugate acid of a delphinidin 3,3',5-tri-O-beta-D-glucoside betaine.
