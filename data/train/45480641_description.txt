The molecule is the tetra-anion obtained by removing protons from the phosphate and carboxylic acid groups of coenzyme gamma-F420-2. It has a role as a coenzyme. It is a tricarboxylic acid anion, a dialkyl phosphate anion, a ribitol phosphate and a member of pyrimidoquinolines. It derives from a 7,8-didemethyl-8-hydroxy-5-deazariboflavin. It is a conjugate base of a coenzyme gamma-F420-2.
