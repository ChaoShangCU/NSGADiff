The molecule is a 3-oxo-fatty acyl CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (7Z)-3-oxohexadecenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (7Z)-3-oxohexadecenoyl-CoA.
