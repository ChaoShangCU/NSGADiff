The molecule is an N-carbamoylamino acid that is aspartic acid with one of its amino hydrogens replaced by a carbamoyl group. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a human metabolite. It is a N-carbamoyl-amino acid, an aspartic acid derivative and a C4-dicarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-carbamoylaspartate(2-).
