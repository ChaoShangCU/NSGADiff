The molecule is a flavonoid oxoanion that is a dianionic form of biochanin A 7-O-(6-O-malonyl-beta-D-glucoside). It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a flavonoid oxoanion and a 3-oxo monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a biochanin A 7-O-beta-D-glucoside 6''-O-malonate.
