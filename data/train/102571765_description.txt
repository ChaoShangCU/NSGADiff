The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 5(S),15(S)-DiHETE, arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, an icosanoid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a dihydroxyicosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 5(S),15(S)-DiHETE.
