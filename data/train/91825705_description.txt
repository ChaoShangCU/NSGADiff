The molecule is an amino pentasacchride comprising a chain of 3-O-sulfo-beta-D-glucuronic acid, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose and D-glucose residues linked sequentially (1->3), (1->4), (1->3) and (1->4). It is an amino pentasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
