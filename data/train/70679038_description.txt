The molecule is a linear amino trisaccharide consisting of a chain of beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and N-acetyl-D-galactosamine residues linked sequentially (1->4) and (1->3). It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
