The molecule is a member of the class of desferrialbomycins that is desferrialbomycin delta1 in which the oxo group at position 4 of the pyrimidone moiety is replaced by an imino group. The iron(III) complex of desferrialbomycin epsilon is the antibiotic albomycin epsilon. It is a conjugate acid of a desferrialbomycin epsilon(3-).
