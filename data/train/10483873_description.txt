The molecule is a capsaicinoid that is a dimer obtained by replacement of the hydrogen at position 5' of capsaicin with 6'',7''-dihydrocapsaicin. It is isolated from the dried fruits of Capsicum annuum and exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as an antioxidant and a plant metabolite. It is a capsaicinoid, a member of biphenyls, a monocarboxylic acid amide and a member of guaiacols.
