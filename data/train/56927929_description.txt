The molecule is a carboxylic ester derivative of chloroamphenicol incorporating an L-lysino group linked via a 2-oxoethyl group to the oxygen at C-3. It is a C-nitro compound, an organochlorine compound, a carboxylic ester, a trifluoroacetamide and a secondary carboxamide. It contains a N(2)-L-lysino group. It derives from a chloramphenicol.
