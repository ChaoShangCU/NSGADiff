The molecule is a member of the class of phenothiazines that is 10H-phenothiazine having a trifluoromethyl subsitituent at the 2-position and a 3-[4-(2-hydroxyethyl)piperazin-1-yl]propyl group at the N-10 position. It has a role as a phenothiazine antipsychotic drug, a dopaminergic antagonist and an anticoronaviral agent. It is an organofluorine compound, a member of phenothiazines and a N-alkylpiperazine. It derives from a hydride of a 10H-phenothiazine.
