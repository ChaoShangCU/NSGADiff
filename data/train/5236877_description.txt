The molecule is an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound that is 1H,5H-[1,4]dithiino[2,3-c:5,6-c']dipyrrole-1,3,5,7(2H,6H)-tetrone substituted at positions 2 and 6 by methyl groups. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is an organic heterotricyclic compound, a dicarboximide fungicide, an organosulfur pesticide, an organosulfur heterocyclic compound and an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound. It derives from a maleimide.
