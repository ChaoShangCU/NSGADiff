The molecule is a catechol resulting from the hydrolysis of one of the two methylene acetal groups in (+)-sesamin. Found as a product of (+)-sesamin in rat liver homogenate and also produced from sesamin by an enzyme (SesA) found in Sinomonas species. no. 22 growing on sesamin. It is a member of benzodioxoles, a lignan, a catechol and a furofuran. It derives from a (+)-sesamin.
