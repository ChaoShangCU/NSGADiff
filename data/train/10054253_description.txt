The molecule is an organic heteroheptacyclic compound isolated from the fermentation culture broth of Actinoplanes sp. It is a broad-spectrum antifungal agent. It has a role as a metabolite and an antifungal agent. It is a member of phenols, an organochlorine compound, a secondary alcohol, a cyclic acetal, an ether, an organic heteroheptacyclic compound, a lactam and a member of hydrazines.
