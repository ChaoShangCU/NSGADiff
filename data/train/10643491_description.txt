The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthene substituted by hydroxy group at positions 1, 3 and 7, an oxo group at position 9 and prenyl groups at positions 2 and 4. It has been isolated from the stems of Cratoxylum cochinchinense It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a member of xanthones and a member of phenols.
