The molecule is a steroid saponin that is the monosaccharide derivative of the 19-norwithanolide. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Physalis longifolia. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a steroid saponin, a beta-D-glucoside, a delta-lactone, a 1-hydroxy steroid, a 6alpha-hydroxy steroid and a monosaccharide derivative.
