The molecule is a beta-carboline alkaloid found in Stellaria dichotoma var. lanceolata. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-carboline alkaloid, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a monosaccharide derivative, an aromatic ketone, an enoate ester, a methyl ester and a beta-D-glucoside.
