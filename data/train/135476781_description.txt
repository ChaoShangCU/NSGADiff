The molecule is the 5-N-formyl derivative of 2,5-diamino-6-ribosylamino-4(3H)-pyrimidinone 5'-phosphate. It is a ribose monophosphate, a N-glycosyl compound, a pyrimidone, an aminopyrimidine and a formamidopyrimidine. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-amino-5-formylamino-6-(1-D-ribosylamino)pyrimidin-4(3H)-one 5'-phosphate(2-).
