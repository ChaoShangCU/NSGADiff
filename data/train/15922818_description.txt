The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is delphinidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(trans-4 coumaryl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an anthocyanin cation, a cinnamate ester and a polyphenol. It derives from a trans-4-coumaric acid and a delphinidin.
