The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a alpha-L-rhamnopyranosyl-(1->2)-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has been isolated from Brassica nigra. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a quercetin O-glycoside, a tetrahydroxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative.
