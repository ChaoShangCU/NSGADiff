The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid anion resulting from the removal of a proton from the carboxy group of beta-D-4-deoxy-Delta(4)-GlcpA-(1->3)-beta-D-GalpNAc. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion and a carbohydrate acid derivative anion. It is a conjugate base of a beta-D-4-deoxy-Delta(4)-GlcpA-(1->3)-beta-D-GalpNAc.
