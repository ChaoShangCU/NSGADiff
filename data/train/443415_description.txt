The molecule is an indole alkaloid obtained by formal acetylation of the 17-hydroxy group of norajmaline. It is an acetate ester, a hemiaminal, a bridged compound, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound, a secondary amino compound, an organic heteropolycyclic compound and an indole alkaloid. It derives from a norajmaline. It is a conjugate base of a 17-O-acetylnorajmaline(1+).
