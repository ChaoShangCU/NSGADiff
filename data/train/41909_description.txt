The molecule is a member of the class of 1,2,4-triazines that is 1,2,4-triazine-3,5(2H,4H)-dione substituted by an amino group at position 4 and a tert-butyl group at position 6. It is a metabolite of metribuzin. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is a diketone and a member of 1,2,4-triazines.
