The molecule is a benzoate ester that is methyl (2,4-dihydroxyphenyl)acetate attached to a (4-hydroxy-3,5-dimethoxybenzoyl)oxy group. Isolated from Vaccinium ashei, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a benzoate ester, a member of resorcinols, a dimethoxybenzene and a methyl ester.
