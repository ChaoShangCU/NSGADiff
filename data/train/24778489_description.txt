The molecule is a 1-monoglyceride resulting from the condensation of the carboxy group of prostaglandin E2 with the 1-hydroxy group of glycerol. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 1-monoglyceride, an alicyclic ketone, a prostaglandins E, a tetrol and a secondary allylic alcohol. It derives from a prostaglandin E2.
