The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is apigenin with a 6-O-beta-D-arabinopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl moiety attached at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a disaccharide derivative, a glycosyloxyflavone and a dihydroxyflavone. It derives from an apigenin.
