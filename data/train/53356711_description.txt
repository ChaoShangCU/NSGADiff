The molecule is a tetrasaccharide derivative consisting of D-glucitol having an N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl-(1->3)-[N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl-(1->6)]-beta-D-galactosyl group attached at the 4-position. It is a tetrasaccharide derivative and a glucosamine oligosaccharide. It derives from a D-glucitol.
