The molecule is an organosulfate oxoanion that is 5-dehydro-4-deoxy-2-O-sulfo-D-glucuronic acid in which a proton has been removed from both the carboxy and the sulfate group. It is an organosulfate oxoanion, a carbohydrate acid derivative anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 5-dehydro-4-deoxy-2-O-sulfo-D-glucuronic acid.
