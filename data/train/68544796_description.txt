The molecule is a steroid acid that is 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-methyl-5alpha-cholesta-8,24-diene in which the hydrogen at the 4beta position has been replaced by a carboxy group. It is a steroid acid and a 3beta-hydroxy steroid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-methyl-5alpha-cholesta-8,24-diene-4beta-carboxylate.
