The molecule is a sesquiterpene consisting of a bicyclo[3.1.1]heptane skeleton substituted at position 2 by a methylidene group and at position 6 by methyl and 4-methylpent-3-en-1-yl groups (the all-S diastereoisomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound and a sesquiterpene.
