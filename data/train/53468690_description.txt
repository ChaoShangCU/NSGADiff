The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is phenol substituted by a hydroxymethyl group at position 5 and a (2E)-6-methylhept-2-en-2-yl group at position 2. An Aspergillus metabolite isolated from the sea fan derived fungus Aspergillus sydowii. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a sesquiterpenoid, a member of phenols and a member of benzyl alcohols.
