The molecule is a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoate obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group of (5Z,8Z,11Z,14S,15S)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a (5Z,8Z,11Z,14S,15S)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid. It is an enantiomer of a (5Z,8Z,11Z,14R,15R)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoate.
