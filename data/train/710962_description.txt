The molecule is a member of the class of quinazolines that is quinazoline which is substituted at positions 2 and 4 by pyridin-4-yl and (3-fluorophenyl)nitrilo groups, respectively. It is a member of quinazolines, a secondary amino compound, an aromatic amine, a member of pyridines, a member of monofluorobenzenes and a substituted aniline.
