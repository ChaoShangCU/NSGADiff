The molecule is an aspartate(1-) that is the conjugate base of L-aspartic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite and a fundamental metabolite. It is an aspartate(1-) and a polar amino acid zwitterion. It is a conjugate base of a L-aspartic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a L-aspartate(2-). It is an enantiomer of a D-aspartate(1-).
