The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of (1R)-1,4,5,10-tetrahydrophenazine-1-carboxylic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an aromatic amino-acid anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (1R)-1,4,5,10-tetrahydrophenazine-1-carboxylic acid.
