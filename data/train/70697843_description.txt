The molecule is a cyclic ketone that is cyclohept-2-en-1-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 5, a methoxy group at position 6 and a 2-phenylethenyl group at position 5 (the 5R,6R stereoisomers). Isolated from the rootwood of Aeschynomene mimosifolia, it exhibits cytotoxic towards human cancer cell lines. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an enone, an ether, a tertiary alcohol, a member of styrenes and a cyclic ketone.
