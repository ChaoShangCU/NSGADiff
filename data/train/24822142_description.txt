The molecule is a carboxamide resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxylic acid group of 3-bromo-1-(3-chloropyridin-2-yl)-1H-pyrazole-5-carboxylic acid with the primary amino group of 2-amino-3-bromo-5-chloro-N-(1-cyclopropylethyl)benzamide. It is a member of cyclopropanes, a member of pyrazoles, an organobromine compound, a chloropyridine, a secondary carboxamide, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a member of bromobenzenes.
