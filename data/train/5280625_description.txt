The molecule is a muconic semialdehyde having a 2-amino substituent. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a muconic semialdehyde and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-aminomuconate 6-semialdehyde(1-). It is a tautomer of a 2-aminomuconic 6-semialdehyde zwitterion.
