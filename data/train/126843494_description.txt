The molecule is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion that is the conjugate base of 2-O-[alpha-D-glucosyl-(1->6)-alpha-D-glucosyl]-D-glyceric acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion and a carbohydrate acid derivative anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-O-[alpha-D-glucosyl-(1->6)-alpha-D-glucosyl]-D-glyceric acid.
