The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 13S,14S-epoxy-DHA, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a docosanoid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (13S,14S)-epoxy-(4Z,7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid.
