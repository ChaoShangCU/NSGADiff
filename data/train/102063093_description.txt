The molecule is a cyclopentapyran that is (4aR,7aS)-1,4a,5,6,7,7a-hexahydrocyclopenta[c]pyran substituted at position 1 by a hydroxy group and at positions 4 and 7 by methyl groups (the 4aR,7S,7aS)-diastereomer. It has a role as a pheromone and a plant metabolite. It is a cyclopentapyran, an iridoid monoterpenoid and a lactol.
