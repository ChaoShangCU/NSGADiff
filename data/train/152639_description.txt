The molecule is an anthracycline antibiotic that is produced by Streptomyces galilaeus and also exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, an antineoplastic agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is an aminoglycoside, an anthracycline, a member of phenols, a polyketide, a disaccharide derivative, a member of tetracenequinones and a methyl ester. It derives from an aklavinone. It is a tautomer of an aclacinomycin S zwitterion.
