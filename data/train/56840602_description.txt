The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is the cinnamate ester of cycloartenol. It has been isolated from the mycelia of Cordyceps sinensis. It has a role as a central nervous system drug, an antineoplastic agent, a fungal metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a cinnamate ester and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a cycloartenol and a ferulic acid.
