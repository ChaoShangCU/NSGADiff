The molecule is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of tetracenomycin F2, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion and an oxo monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a tetracenomycin F2. It is a conjugate acid of a tetracenomycin F2(2-).
