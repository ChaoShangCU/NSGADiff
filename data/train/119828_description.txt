The molecule is an N-acylsulfonamide resulting from the formal condensation of valdecoxib with propionic acid. It is a prodrug for valdecoxib. It has a role as a cyclooxygenase 2 inhibitor, a non-narcotic analgesic, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug and a prodrug. It is a member of isoxazoles and a N-sulfonylcarboxamide. It derives from a valdecoxib.
