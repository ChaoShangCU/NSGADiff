The molecule is an organic disulfide that is didecyl disulfide in which the hydrogens of the terminal methyl groups are replaced by 3,4-dimethoxy-5-methyl-2H-pyran-2-ones groups respectively. It has been isolated from the marine sponge of the genus Plakortis. It has a role as an animal metabolite. It is a member of 2-pyranones, an ether, a polyketide and an organic disulfide.
