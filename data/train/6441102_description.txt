The molecule is an enoate ester that is the methyl ester of (2E,3Z,5E)-6-(4-chloro-3-methoxyphenyl)-2-(methoxymethylene)-3-methylhexa-3,5-dienoic acid. It has a role as a fungal metabolite, a mitochondrial cytochrome-bc1 complex inhibitor and an antifungal agent. It is an enoate ester, an enol ether, a monomethoxybenzene, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a methoxyacrylate strobilurin antifungal agent.
