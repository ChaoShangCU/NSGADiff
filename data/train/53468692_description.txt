The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is the 7-O-methyl derivative of (+)-(7S)-sydonic acid. An Aspergillus metabolite isolated from the sea fan derived fungus Aspergillus sydowii. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a monohydroxybenzoic acid, a sesquiterpenoid and an ether. It derives from a (+)-(7S)-sydonic acid.
