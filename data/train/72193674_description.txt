The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is chrysoeriol in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by a gentiobiosyl moiety. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monomethoxyflavone, a dihydroxyflavone, a disaccharide derivative, a gentiobioside and a glycosyloxyflavone. It derives from a 4',5,7-trihydroxy-3'-methoxyflavone.
