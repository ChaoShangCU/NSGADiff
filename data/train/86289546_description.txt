The molecule is a macrolide antibiotic that is an intermediate in the biosynthesis of pikromycin by Streptomyces venezuelae. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a macrolide antibiotic, an enone, a monosaccharide derivative and a polyketide. It derives from a narbonolide. It is a conjugate base of a neopikromycin(1+).
