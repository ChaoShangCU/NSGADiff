The molecule is a monoterpenoid that is hexahydro-1-benzofuran-2-one carrying two methyl substituents at positions 3 and 6 as well as two hydroxy substituents at positions 5 and 6 (the 3R,3aR,5S,6S,7aR-diastereomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a monoterpenoid, a member of benzofurans, a gamma-lactone and a diol.
