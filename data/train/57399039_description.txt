The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that is the disaccharide derivative of hederagenin. It has been isolated from the stem bark of Kalopanax pictus. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a plant metabolite. It is a disaccharide derivative, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a triterpenoid saponin, a diol and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a hederagenin.
