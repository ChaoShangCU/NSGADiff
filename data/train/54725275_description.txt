The molecule is an organic sodium salt that is the trisodium salt of 3,3'-[(3-carboxy-4-oxocyclohexa-2,5-dien-1-ylidene)methylene]bis(6-hydroxybenzoic acid). It has a role as a histological dye, a fluorochrome and an insulin-like growth factor receptor 1 antagonist. It contains an aurintricarboxylate.
