The molecule is a glucosiduronic acid that is beta-D-glucuronic acid in which the anomeric hydroxyl hydrogen has been replaced by a 2-hydroxyphenyl group. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a glucosiduronic acid and a member of phenols. It derives from a catechol. It is a conjugate acid of a catechol beta-D-glucuronide(1-).
