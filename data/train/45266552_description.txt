The molecule is tetraanion of (R)-3-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a (R)-3-hydroxyacyl-CoA(4-) and a short-chain (R)-3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (R)-3-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA.
