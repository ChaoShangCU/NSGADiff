The molecule is a class I yanuthone that is 7-deacetoxyyanuthone A in which the methyl group attached to the epoxy-cyclohexenone ring has been oxidised to the corresponding hydroxymethyl group. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a class I yanuthone, a secondary alcohol and a primary alcohol. It derives from a 7-deacetoxyyanuthone A.
