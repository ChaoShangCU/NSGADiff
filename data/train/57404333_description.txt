The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is dammar-20-ene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 24 and 25 and an oxo group at position 3. It has been isolated from the stems of Aglaia abbreviata. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diol, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and a cyclic terpene ketone. It derives from a hydride of a dammarane.
