The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (7E,9E,11Z,13E,15R,17Z)-5,6,15-trihydroxyicosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a lipoxin anion, an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a hydroxy fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (7E,9E,11Z,13E,15R,17Z)-5,6,15-trihydroxyicosapentaenoic acid.
