The molecule is a 4-O-[(E)-2-methyl-2-butenoyl]ascaroside derived from (2E,8R)-8-hydroxynon-2-enoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a 4-O-[(E)-2-methyl-2-butenoyl]ascaroside and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an ascr#3 and a (2E,8R)-8-hydroxynon-2-enoic acid.
