The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-formyl-4beta-methylsteroid that is 24-methylenecycloartanol in which the alpha-methyl substituent at position 4 has been substituted by a hydroxy group. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a member of phytosterols, a lanostane sterol and a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-hydroxymethyl-4beta-methylsteroid. It derives from a 24-methylenecycloartanol.
