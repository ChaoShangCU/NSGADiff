The molecule is a semisynthetic derivative that is sirolimus in which the hydroxy group attached to the cyclohexyl moiety has been converted to the corresponding dimethylphosphinate. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a mTOR inhibitor. It is a semisynthetic derivative, a phosphinic ester, a macrolide lactam and a cyclic acetal. It derives from a member of sirolimus.
