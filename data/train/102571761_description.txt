The molecule is a prostaglandin carboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of 19-hydroxyprostaglandin H1, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an oxylipin anion and a prostaglandin carboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 19-hydroxyprostaglandin H1.
