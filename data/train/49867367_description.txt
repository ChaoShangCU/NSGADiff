The molecule is a nicotinamide dinucleotide consisting of NADP zwitterion lacking the 2'-phsopho group and having a (1R)-1-hydroxy-2-oxo-3-phosphonooxypropyl group at position 4 of the nicotinamide fragment. It is a nicotinamide dinucleotide and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a NADP zwitterion.
