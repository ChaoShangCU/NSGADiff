The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is 5a,10a-dihydro-11H-[1]benzofuro[3,2-b]chromen-11-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3, 8 and 10a and a triprenyl group at position 5a. It has been isolated from Morus nigra. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an extended flavonoid, a polyphenol and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
