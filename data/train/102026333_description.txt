The molecule is a glycoside that is curcumin substituted by a beta-D-glucopyranosyl-(1->6)-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-(1->6)-beta-D-glucopyranoside group at position 4'. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a trisaccharide derivative, an enone, an aromatic ether, an enol and a glycoside. It derives from a curcumin.
