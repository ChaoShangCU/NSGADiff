The molecule is an alkaloid that is an enamide obtained by the formal condensation of tiglic acid with 6-(2-amino-1-methoxyethyl)-4H-pyrido[2,3,4-kl]acridin-4-one. It is isolated from the Okinawan marine tunicate Cystodytes dellechiajei and exhibits cytotoxicity against human epidermoid carcinoma KB cells It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an alkaloid, an enamide, an enone, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, an ether and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from a tiglic acid.
