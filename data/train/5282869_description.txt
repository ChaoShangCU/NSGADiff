The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (11Z,13E)-icosadienoic acid in which the pro-S hydrogen at position 15 has been replaced by a hydroperoxy group. It is a hydroperoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (15S)-hydroperoxy-(11Z,13E)-icosadienoate.
