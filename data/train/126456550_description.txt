The molecule is a linear tetrapyrrole anion obtained by deprotonation of the two carboxy groups and protonation of the imine nitrogen of biliverdin delta; It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a linear tetrapyrrole anion and a dicarboxylic acid monoanion. It is a conjugate base of a biliverdin delta.
