The molecule is a heparan sulfate having an alpha-D-glucosaminyl residue at the non-reducing end. The heparan sulfate part consists of a variably sulfated repeating disaccharide unit. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It is a member of heparan sulfates and a heparan alpha-D-glucosaminide. It is a conjugate acid of a heparan sulfate alpha-D-glucosaminide polyanion.
