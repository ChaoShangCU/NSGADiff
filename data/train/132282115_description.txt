The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside obtained by formal condensation of the alcoholic hydroxy group of 5-hydroxy-3-oxopentanoic acid with ascarylopyranose (the alpha anomer). It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside and a 3-oxo monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a bkos#9(1-).
