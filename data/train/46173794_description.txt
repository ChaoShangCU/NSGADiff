The molecule is a macrolide antibiotic that is tylonolide having 6-deoxy-beta-D-allopyranosyl and beta-D-mycaminosyl residues attached to two of its hydroxy groups.. It is a macrolide antibiotic, a monosaccharide derivative, an enone and an aldehyde. It derives from a tylactone. It is a conjugate base of a demethyllactenocin(1+).
