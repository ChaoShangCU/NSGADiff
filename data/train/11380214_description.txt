The molecule is a germacrane sesquiterpenoid that is 4,5-epoxygermacra-9Z-ene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 2 and 6. Isolated from Santolina insularis, it exhibits cytotoxicity against human colon carcinoma cell line. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a germacrane sesquiterpenoid, a triol and an epoxide.
