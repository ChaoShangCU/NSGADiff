The molecule is a member of the class of rifamycins that is rifamycin SV lacking the O-methyl group at position 27. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic ketal, a lactam, a macrocycle, a polyphenol and a member of rifamycins. It is a conjugate acid of a 27-O-demethylrifamycin SV(1-).
