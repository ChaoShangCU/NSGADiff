The molecule is a hydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone that is 3-methylflaviolin in which the hydroxyl hydrogens at positions 2 and 7 are replaced by methyl and geranyl groups respectively. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an olefinic compound, a member of phenols, an enol ether and a hydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone. It derives from a flaviolin.
