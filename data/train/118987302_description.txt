The molecule is a member of the class of azabicycloalkanes that is 1,3-diazabicyclo[2.2.0]hex-4-en-2-one substituted at position 6 by a 5,6-diamino-2-oxo-2,3,4,5-tetrahydropyrimidin-4-yl group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is an aminopyrimidine, an azabicycloalkane and a pyrimidone.
