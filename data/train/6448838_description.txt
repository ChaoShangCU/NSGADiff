The molecule is a 24-membered macrolide antibiotic isolated from the fermentation broth of Streptomyces sp.NK154183. It exhibits antifungal and antitumour activity against the human colon adenocarcinoma. It has a role as a metabolite, an antifungal agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a macrolide antibiotic, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amine, a cyclic hemiketal and a spiroketal.
