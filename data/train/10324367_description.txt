The molecule is a synthetic tripeptide consisting of N-(tert-butylcarbamoyl)-3-methyl-L-valyl, a cyclopropyl-fused prolyl and 3-amino-4-cyclobutyl-2-oxobutanamide residues joined in sequence. Used for treatment of chronic hepatitis C virus genotype 1 infection. It has a role as a hepatitis C protease inhibitor, a peptidomimetic and an antiviral drug. It is a tripeptide and a member of ureas.
