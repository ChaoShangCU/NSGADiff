The molecule is a secondary amino compound that is spermidine in which each of the primary amino groups has been mono-acylated by formal condensation with trans-sinapic acid. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a secondary amino compound, a polyphenol, an aromatic ether, an enamide and a secondary carboxamide. It derives from a spermidine and a trans-sinapic acid. It is a conjugate base of a N(1),N(8)-bis(sinapoyl)-spermidine(1+).
