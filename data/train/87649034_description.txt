The molecule is an androstane sulfate that is 5alpha-androstan-3beta,17beta-diol in which the hydroxy hydrogen at position 17 has been replaced by a sulfo group. It is an androstane sulfate and a 3beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 5alpha-androstane-3beta,17beta-diol. It is a conjugate acid of a (3beta,5alpha,17beta)-3-hydroxyandrostan-17-yl sulfate(1-).
