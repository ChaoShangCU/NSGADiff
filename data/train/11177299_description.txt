The molecule is an ergostanoid that is (22E)-ergosta-4,7,22-triene substituted by oxo groups at positions 3 and 6. It has been isolated from the fungus, Xylaria species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 3-oxo Delta(7)-steroid, a 6-oxo steroid and an ergostanoid.
