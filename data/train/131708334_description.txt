The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol 3-phosphate(2-) in which the 1- and 2-acyl substituent are specified as oleoyl and (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl respectively; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 1-oleoyl-2-[(4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl]-sn-glycero-3-phosphate.
