The molecule is a hydroxamic acid that is urea in which both the hydrogens attached to one of the nitrogens are replaced by a hydroxy and a (1E)-1-[3-(4-fluorophenoxy)phenyl]but-1-en-3-yl group. A selective inhibitor of arachidonate 5-lipoxygenase, it can be used for the treatment of asthma. It has a role as an EC 1.13.11.34 (arachidonate 5-lipoxygenase) inhibitor. It is an organofluorine compound, a member of ureas, a hydroxamic acid and an aromatic ether.
