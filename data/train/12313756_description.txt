The molecule is a carbobicyclic compound that is 1,2,3,4,4a,5,8,8a-octahydronaphthalene which is substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 4, an isoopropyl group at position 7, and by methyl groups at positions 1 and 4a (the 1S,4R,4aR,8aR isomer). A sesquiterpenoid plant metabolite. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a tertiary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a carbobicyclic compound, a sesquiterpenoid and a member of octahydronaphthalenes.
