The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is cladoniamide E in which the chlorine attached to the methoxy-bearing indole moiety is replaced by a hydrogen. It has been isolated from the culture broth of Streptomyces uncialis. It is a cladoniamide, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an organochlorine compound, a tertiary alcohol and a secondary carboxamide.
