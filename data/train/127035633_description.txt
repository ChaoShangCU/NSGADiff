The molecule is a meroterpenoid found in Guignardia and Fusarium species and shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against Candida albicans growth. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a fungal metabolite. It is a meroterpenoid, a tertiary alcohol, a primary alcohol, an organic hydroxy compound, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a triol and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
