The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid that is (12R)-12-hydroxytridecanoic acid in which the 3-pro-R hydrogen is replaced by a hydroxy group. It is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid, a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid, a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a long-chain fatty acid. It derives from a (12R)-12-hydroxytridecanoic acid.
