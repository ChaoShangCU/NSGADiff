The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin with hederagenin as the sapogenin. It has been isolated from the stem bark of Kalopanax pictus. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a plant metabolite. It is a triterpenoid saponin, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, an acetate ester and a primary alcohol. It derives from a hederagenin. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
