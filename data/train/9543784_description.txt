The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol in which the acyl groups positions 1 and 2 are specified as alpha-linolenoyl and (11Z,14Z)-icosadienoyl respectively. It has a role as a human blood serum metabolite. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycerol and a diacylglycerol 38:5. It derives from an alpha-linolenic acid and an (11Z,14Z)-icosadienoic acid.
