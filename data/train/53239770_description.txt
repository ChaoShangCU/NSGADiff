The molecule is a branched amino tetrasaccharide comprising N-acetyl-D-glucosamine at the reducing end with a N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosaminyl-(1->3)-[alpha-L-fucosyl-(1->2)]-beta-D-galactosyl moiety attached at the 4-position. It has a role as an antigen. It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a galactosamine oligosaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
