The molecule is an indole compound having a 2-(dimethylamino)ethyl group at the 3-position and a (pyrrolidin-1-ylsulfonyl)methyl group at the 5-position. It has a role as a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, a serotonergic agonist and a vasoconstrictor agent. It is a member of indoles, a sulfonamide and a tertiary amine.
