The molecule is a butenolide that is (5-oxo-2,5-dihydrofuran-3-yl)methyl phosphate carrying an additional 6-methylheptanoyl substituent at position 4. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an enone, a butenolide and a monoalkyl phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a [4-(6-methylheptanoyl)-5-oxo-2,5-dihydrofuran-3-yl]methyl phosphate(2-).
