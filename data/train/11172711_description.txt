The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound obtained by intramolecular formation of a C-C bond between positions 8 and 5' of 2'-deoxyadenosine. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a bridged compound, a N-glycosyl compound, a diol and an aromatic amine. It derives from a 2'-deoxyadenosine.
