The molecule is a 3-methyl fatty acyl-CoA(4-) oxanion arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 3-methylundecanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3 It is a 3-methyl fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 3-methylundecanoyl-CoA.
