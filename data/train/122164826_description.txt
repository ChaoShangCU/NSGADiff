The molecule is an oligosaccharide phosphate consisting of D-myo-inositol having a 6-O-(2-aminoethylphosphono)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->2)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->6)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->4)-alpha-D-glucosaminyl residue attached at the 6-position. It is a glycoside, a tetrasaccharide derivative, a myo-inositol and an oligosaccharide phosphate.
