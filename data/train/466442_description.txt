The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is laurinterol in which the bromo group has been replaced by a hydrogen. Isolated from Laurencia intermedia and Aplysia kurodai, it exhibits antimicrobial activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimicrobial agent and an EC 3.6.3.9 (Na(+)/K(+)-transporting ATPase) inhibitor. It is a member of phenols and a sesquiterpenoid. It derives from a laurinterol.
