The molecule is an S-substituted glutathione(1-) obtained by deprotonation of the the two carboxy groups and protonation of the glutamyl amino group of (E)-1-(glutathion-S-yl)-2-(indol-3-yl)acetohydroximate. Major microspecies at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of an (E)-1-(glutathion-S-yl)-2-(indol-3-yl)acetohydroximate.
