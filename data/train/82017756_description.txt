The molecule is an aromatic amide obtained by the formal condensation of the carboxy group of 1-(2,5-dimethylphenyl)benzimidazole-5-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 2-chlorobenzylamine. It is a member of benzimidazoles, an aromatic amide, a monocarboxylic acid amide and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
