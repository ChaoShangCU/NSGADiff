The molecule is an azaspiro compound that is 1-azaspiro[5.5]undecane substituted by a hydroxy group at position 8, a but-1-en-3-yn-1-yl group at position 7 and a pent-3-en-1-yn-5-yl group at position 2 (the 2S,6R,7S,8S stereoisomer). It has a role as a metabolite. It is an azaspiro compound, a secondary alcohol and a terminal acetylenic compound.
