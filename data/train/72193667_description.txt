The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isoscoparin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by an alpha-L-rhamnosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-glucosyl moiety. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a monomethoxyflavone, a dihydroxyflavone, a glycosyloxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from an isoscoparin.
