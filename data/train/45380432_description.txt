The molecule is a member of the class of coumarins that is the methyl ester of (2E)-2-(2-{[(3-butyl-4-methyl-2-oxochromen-7-yl)oxy]methyl}phenyl)-3-methoxyprop-2-enoic acid. A fungicide active against various fungal infections including downy mildew, blight, powdery mildew and rice blast. It has a role as a mitochondrial cytochrome-bc1 complex inhibitor and an antifungal agrochemical. It is an enoate ester, an enol ether, an aromatic ether, a member of coumarins, a methyl ester and a methoxyacrylate strobilurin antifungal agent.
