The molecule is an amino pentasaccharide that is D-mannopyranose in which the hydroxy group at positions 2 and 4 have been converted to the beta-D-galactopyranosyl-(1->4)-2-acetamido-alpha-D-glucopyranoside and the beta-D-galactopyranosyl-(1->4)-2-acetamido-beta-D-glucopyranoside respectively. It is an amino pentasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
