The molecule is a dicarboximide that is thalidomide substituted at position 5 by an ethenyl group, which in turn is substituted by a feruloyl group at position 2. The compound is a hybrid based on the structures of thalidomide and curcumin and has been found to exhibit cytotoxicity against human multiple myeloma cells. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent. It is a dicarboximide, a member of isoindoles, a member of piperidones, an enone, a member of phenols and an aromatic ether. It derives from a thalidomide.
