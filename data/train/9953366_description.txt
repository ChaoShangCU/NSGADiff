The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3, 6 and 8, a geranyl group at position 1 and a methoxy group at position 2. Isolated from Mesua and Garcinia dioica, it exhibits antibacterial and cytotoxic activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and an antibacterial agent. It is an aromatic ether, a polyphenol and a member of xanthones.
