The molecule is a thromboxane anion that is the conjugate base of carbocyclic thromboxane A2, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organic molecular entity, a monocarboxylic acid anion, a thromboxane anion and a hydroxy fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a carbocyclic thromboxane A2.
