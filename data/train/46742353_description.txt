The molecule is a member of the class of 1,2,4-triazines in which the triazine ring is substituted at positions 3, 5, and 6 by pyridin-2-yl, ([biphenyl]-4-ylmethyl)amin, and methyl groups, respectively. It is an activator of the hypoxia inducible factor (HIF) pathway. It has a role as a hypoxia-inducible factor pathway activator. It is a member of biphenyls, a member of 1,2,4-triazines, a secondary amino compound and a member of pyridines.
