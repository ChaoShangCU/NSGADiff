The molecule is the monosaccharide sulfate that is N-acetylated 6-O-sulfo-beta-D-glucosamine linked glycosidically to a 3-aminopropyl group. It is a glycoside and a monosaccharide sulfate. It derives from a D-glucosamine. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-aminopropyl N-acetyl-6-O-sulfonato-beta-D-glucosaminide.
