The molecule is a DiHETE that is 12-HETE carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at position 20. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a dihydroxyicosatetraenoic acid and an omega-hydroxy fatty acid. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,10E,14Z)-12-hydroxyicosatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 12,20-DiHETE(1-).
