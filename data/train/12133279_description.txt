The molecule is a 3-oxo Delta(1)-steroid with 5beta-configuration formed from 17beta-hydroxyandrosta-1,4-dien-3-one by reduction across the C4-C5 double bond. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid and a 3-oxo-Delta(1) steroid. It derives from a 5beta-androstane.
