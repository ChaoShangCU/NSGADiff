The molecule is a twelve-membered polypeptide comprising the sequence Tyr-Ile-Ile-Lys-Gly-Val-Phe-Trp-Asp-Pro-Ala-Cys. Corresponds to the P34165[22-33] fragment of mating hormone A-factor 1 from Saccharomyces cerevisiae (strain ATCC 204508/S288c). It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite.
