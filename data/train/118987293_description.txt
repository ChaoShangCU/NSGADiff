The molecule is a nucleoside monophosphate analogue that is dAMP in which one of the exocyclic amino hydrogens is replaced by a carbamoylmethyl group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a nucleoside monophosphate analogue, a monocarboxylic acid amide, a glycine derivative and an amino acid amide. It derives from a 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-monophosphate.
