The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is flavone substituted by hydroxy group at position 5, a methoxy group at position 7, a beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy group at position 4' and a beta-D-oliopyranosyl moiety at position 6. Isolated from Drymaria diandra, it exhibits natioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a monomethoxyflavone, a monohydroxyflavone and a glycosyloxyflavone.
