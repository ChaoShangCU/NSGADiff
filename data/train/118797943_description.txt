The molecule is a ceramide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of henicosanoic acid with the amino group of 14-methylhexadecasphingosine. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a ceramide and a Cer(d38:1). It derives from a 14-methylhexadecasphingosine and a henicosanoic acid.
