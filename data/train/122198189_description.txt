The molecule is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid with formula C36H45NO17 that is isolated from the root bark of Tripterygium hypoglaucum. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid, a macrolide, a pyridine alkaloid, a bridged compound and a sesquiterpene alkaloid.
