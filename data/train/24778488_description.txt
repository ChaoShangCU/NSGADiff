The molecule is a 2-monoglyceride obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of prostaglandin E2 with the 2-hydroxy group of glycerol. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 2-monoglyceride, an alicyclic ketone, a secondary allylic alcohol, a prostaglandins E and a tetrol. It derives from a prostaglandin E2.
