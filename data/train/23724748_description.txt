The molecule is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate that is ATP substituted at position N-6 by a dimethylallyl (isopentenyl) group. It is an adenosine 5'-phosphate and a purine ribonucleoside 5'-triphosphate. It derives from an ATP. It is a conjugate acid of a N(6)-(dimethylallyl)adenosine 5'-triphosphate(4-).
