The molecule is a polycyclic cage xanthone isolated from the stems of Cratoxylum cochinchinense and has been shown to exhibit cytotoxic activity against human colon cancer cell line. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a cyclic ketone, a polycyclic cage, a cyclic ether and a member of phenols.
