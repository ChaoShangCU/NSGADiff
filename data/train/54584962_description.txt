The molecule is a monomethoxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by a methoxy group at position 5, a hydroxy group at position 7 and a 3-hydroxy-3-methylbut-1-en-1-yl group at position 8. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Tephrosia candida. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a monohydroxyflavanone, a monomethoxyflavanone and a tertiary alcohol. It derives from a (2S)-flavanone.
