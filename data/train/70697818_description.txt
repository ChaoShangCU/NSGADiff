The molecule is a monomethoxyflavone that is the 5-methyl ether derivative of myricitrin. Isolated from Rhododendron yedoense var poukhanense, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, an alpha-L-rhamnoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a tetrahydroxyflavone and a monomethoxyflavone. It derives from a myricitrin.
