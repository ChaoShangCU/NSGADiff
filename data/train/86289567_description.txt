The molecule is an anthracycline that is aklavinone having a 2-deoxy-L-fucosyl-(1->4)-2-deoxy-L-fucosyl-(1->4)-L-rhodosaminyl moiety attached at position 4. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an aminoglycoside, an anthracycline, a trisaccharide derivative, a member of phenols, a polyketide, a methyl ester and a member of p-quinones. It derives from an aklavinone. It is a tautomer of a 2-deoxy-alpha-L-fucosylaclacinomycin S zwitterion.
