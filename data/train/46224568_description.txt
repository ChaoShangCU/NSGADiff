The molecule is a trisaccharide derivative and oligosaccharide phosphate comprised of one alpha-D-glucopyranosyl and two L-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptopyranosyl residues. The sequence is present in enterobacterial lipopolysaccharide from all E. coli, Salmonella and Shigella. It is an oligosaccharide phosphate and a trisaccharide derivative.
