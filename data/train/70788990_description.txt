The molecule is a polyketide antibiotic produced by Streptomyces curacoi. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is an organochlorine compound, a member of phenols, an ortho ester, an acetate ester, a methyl ketone, a polyketide, a cyclic acetal, a glycoside, a polysaccharide derivative and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
