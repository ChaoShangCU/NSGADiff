The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that is the tetrasaccharide derivative of hederagenin. Isolated from Serjania salzmanniana, it exhibits antifungal and molluscicidal activities. It has a role as an antifungal agent, a molluscicide and a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a triterpenoid saponin, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a tetrasaccharide derivative. It derives from a hederagenin.
