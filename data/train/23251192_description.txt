The molecule is a member of the class of chalcones that is trans-chalcone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2' and 4', methoxy group at positions 4 and 6' and a prenyl group at position 3'. It is a member of chalcones, an aromatic ether and a member of resorcinols. It derives from a trans-chalcone. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-O-methylxanthohumol(1-).
