The molecule is an acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoic acid. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite. It is an acyl-CoA and a naphthohydroquinone. It derives from a 1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoyl-CoA(4-).
