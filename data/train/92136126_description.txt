The molecule is a carbohydrate sulfonate that is D-glucono-1,5-lactone in which the 6-hydroxy group is replaced by a sulfo group. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a carbohydrate sulfonate and an aldono-1,5-lactone. It derives from a quinovose and a D-glucono-1,5-lactone. It is a conjugate acid of a 6-deoxy-6-sulfoglucono-1,5-lactone(1-).
