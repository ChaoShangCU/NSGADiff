The molecule is a beta-hydroxyketone that is 3-hydroxycyclopentanone in which a hydrogen at position 4 is replaced by a (1E)-4-hydroxy-4-methyloct-1-en-1-yl group and a hydrogen at position 5 is replaced by a 7-methoxy-7-oxoheptyl group. It is a tertiary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, a beta-hydroxy ketone, a cyclic ketone and a methyl ester.
