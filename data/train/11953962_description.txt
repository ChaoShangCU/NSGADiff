The molecule is a member of the class of carbapenems that is (5R,6R)-3-{[(E)-2-aminoethenyl]sulfanyl}-6-[(1S)-1-hydroxyethyl]-7-oxo-1-azabicyclo[3.2.0]hept-2-ene-2-carboxylic acid in which the free hydroxy and amino groups are carrying sulfo and acetyl substituents respectively. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a member of acetamides, an organosulfonic acid and a member of carbapenems.
