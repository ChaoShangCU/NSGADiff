The molecule is an organic anion obtained by deprotonation of the malonyl carboxy as well as the 5- and 7-hydroxy groups of delphinidin 3-O-(6''-O-malonyl)-beta-D-glucoside. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a conjugate base of a delphinidin-5-olate 3-O-(6''-O-carboxylatoacetyl)-beta-D-glucoside(1-).
