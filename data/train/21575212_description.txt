The molecule is a semisynthetic monoterpene glycoside obtained by acetylation of the 6'-position of the natural product paeoniflorin. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and an anti-allergic agent. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a bridged compound, a cyclic acetal, a lactol, a monoterpene glycoside, a benzoate ester, an acetate ester, an O-acyl carbohydrate and a semisynthetic derivative. It derives from a paeoniflorin.
