The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as oleoyl and myristoyl respectively. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a tetradecanoate ester. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1-oleoyl-2-myristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
