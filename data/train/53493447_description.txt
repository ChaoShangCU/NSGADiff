The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is oleana-11,13(18)-diene substituted by a sulfoxy group at position 3 and a carboxy group at position 28. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Fatsia polycarpa. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a sulfuric ester. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
