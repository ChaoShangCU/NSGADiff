The molecule is a triazolothiadiazole that is 1,7a-dihydro[1,2,4]triazolo[3,4-b][1,3,4]thiadiazole substituted by a 4-methyl-1,2,3-thiadiazol-5-yl group at position 3 and by a trichloromethyl group at position 6. It is a fungicide with a wide spectrum of activity and inhibits the enzymatic activity of pyruvate kinase. It has a role as a fungicide and an EC 2.7.1.40 (pyruvate kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of thiadiazoles, an organochlorine compound and a triazolothiadiazole.
