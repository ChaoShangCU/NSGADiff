The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by the formal condensation of cyclohexanecarboxylic acid with the amino group of 5-(naphthalen-1-ylmethyl)-1,3-thiazol-2-amine. It has been shown to exhibit antifungal activity. It has a role as an antifungal agent. It is a member of 1,3-thiazoles, a member of naphthalenes and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from a cyclohexanecarboxylic acid.
