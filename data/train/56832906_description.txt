The molecule is an organic triphosphate that is GS-441524 in which the 5'-hydroxy group has been replaced by a triphosphate group. It is the active metabolite of remdesivir. It has a role as a drug metabolite, an antiviral drug and an anticoronaviral agent. It is a C-nucleoside, an aromatic amine, a nitrile, a pyrrolotriazine and an organic triphosphate. It derives from a GS-441524.
