The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is a trianion arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and carboxylic acid functions of N-acetylmuramic acid 6-phosphate. It is an organophosphate oxoanion, a monocarboxylic acid anion and a carbohydrate acid derivative anion. It is a conjugate base of a N-acetylmuramic acid 6-phosphate.
