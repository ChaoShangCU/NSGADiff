The molecule is a C20 hydroxy fatty acid obtained by formal hydrogenation across the 13,-14-double bond of 15-oxolipoxin A4. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an oxo fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid, a trienoic fatty acid, an icosanoid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 13,14-dihydro-15-oxolipoxin A4(1-).
