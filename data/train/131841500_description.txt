The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is (4Z,8Z)-4,8-dimethyltrideca-4,8-diene carrying two oxo substituents at positions 1 and 12. A product from bacterial degradation of rubber. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a methyl ketone, an aliphatic aldehyde, a sesquiterpenoid and an olefinic compound.
