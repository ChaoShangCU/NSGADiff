The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is a penta-anionic form of guanosine 3',5'-bis(diphosphate). It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a conjugate base of a guanosine 3',5'-bis(diphosphate). It is a conjugate acid of a guanosine 3',5'-bis(diphosphate)(6-).
