The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy-long-chain fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (9Z,12Z)-17-hydroxyoctadeca-9,12-dienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an (omega-1)-hydroxy-long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (9Z,12Z)-17-hydroxyoctadeca-9,12-dienoic acid.
