The molecule is an indol-3-yl carboxylic acid that is 3-(1H-indol-3-yl)butanoic acid substituted at positions 2 and 3 by hydroxy and methyl groups respectively. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an indol-3-yl carboxylic acid and a (2S)-2-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an indolmycenate.
