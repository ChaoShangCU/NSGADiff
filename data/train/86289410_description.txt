The molecule is a polyether antibiotic produced by a strain of Streptomyces mutabilis NRRL 8088. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite and an ionophore. It is a polyether antibiotic, a spiroketal, a 3-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a cyclic hemiketal and a member of oxolanes. It is a conjugate acid of a mutalomycin(1-).
