The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside triphosphate oxoanion that is the tetraanion of 2'-deoxy-2-hydroxyadenosine 5'-triphosphate, arising from deprotonation of the triphosphate OH groups. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It is a conjugate base of a 2-hydroxy-dATP(3-).
