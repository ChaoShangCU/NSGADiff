The molecule is a C16 polyunsaturated fatty acid that is the (10R,11S)-epoxy derivative of trideca-2,6-dienoic acid. It is a long-chain fatty acid, a polyunsaturated fatty acid, an epoxy fatty acid and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a trideca-2,6-dienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (2E,6E,10R,11S)-10,11-epoxy-3,7,11-trimethyltrideca-2,6-dienoate.
