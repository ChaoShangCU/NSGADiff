The molecule is a prostaglandins Falpha that is prosta-5,13-dien-1-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 9, 11 and 15. It is a naturally occurring prostaglandin used to induce labor. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a prostaglandins Falpha and a monocarboxylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a prostaglandin F2alpha(1-).
