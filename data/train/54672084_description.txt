The molecule is a dihydroxyflavone that is flavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2' and 5' and methoxy groups at positions 3, 7, 8 and 4'. It has been isolated from the aerial parts of Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a dihydroxyflavone and a tetramethoxyflavone. It derives from a flavone.
