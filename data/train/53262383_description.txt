The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by removal of the protons from the carboxy and phosphate groups of 2-O-(alpha-D-glucopyranosyl)-3-O-phospho-D-glyceric acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion, a carbohydrate acid derivative anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-O-(alpha-D-glucopyranosyl)-3-O-phospho-D-glyceric acid.
