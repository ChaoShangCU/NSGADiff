The molecule is a physalin with antimalarial and antimycobacterial activities isolated from Physalis angulata. It has a role as an antimalarial, an antimycobacterial drug and an antineoplastic agent. It is a physalin, a lactone, an enone, a 5alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 6beta-hydroxy steroid, an organic heteroheptacyclic compound and a cyclic ether.
