The molecule is a member of the class of ureas that is urea substituted by a (3beta,22E)-ergosta-4,6,8(14),22-tetraen-3-yl group at position 1. Isolated from the fruit bodies of the fungus Chlorophyllum molybdites, it exhibits cytotoxicity against Kato III cells. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is an ergostanoid and a member of ureas.
