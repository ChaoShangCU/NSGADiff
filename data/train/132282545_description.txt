The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 7,8-epoxy,17-hydroxy-(9E,11E,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a hydroxy fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 7,8-epoxy,17-hydroxy-(9E,11E,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid.
