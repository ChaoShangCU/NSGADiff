The molecule is an apo carotenoid sesquiterpenoid that is 2-trans-abscisic acid in which the keto group at position 4' has been reduced to the corresponding alcohol. It has a role as an Arabidopsis thaliana metabolite. It is an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid, an apo carotenoid sesquiterpenoid, a tertiary allylic alcohol and a secondary allylic alcohol.
