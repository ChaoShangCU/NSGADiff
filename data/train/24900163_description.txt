The molecule is an alkaloid that is the 14-nor derivative of pseurotin A. Isolated from Aspergillus sydowii, it exhibits antibacterial, antileishmanial and anticancer activities. It has a role as an antibacterial agent, an antineoplastic agent, an antileishmanial agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is an azaspiro compound, a lactam, an oxaspiro compound, a secondary alcohol and an alkaloid. It derives from a pseurotin A.
