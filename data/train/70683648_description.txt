The molecule is a member of the class of quinolones that is 2-methylquinolin-4(1H)-one in which the hydrogen at position 3 is replaced by a 4-[4-(trifluoromethoxy)phenoxy]phenyl group. A potent inhibitor of Toxoplasma gondii growth. It has a role as an antiparasitic agent. It is a quinolone, an aromatic ether and an organofluorine compound.
