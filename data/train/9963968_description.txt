The molecule is a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic isolated from the fermentation broth of Streptomyces microflavus strain No. 2445 and exhibits antiviral activity against herpes simplex virus type 1 (HSV-1), varicella-zoster virus (VZV), influenza A virus and human immunodeficiency virus type 1 (HIV-1). It has a role as a metabolite, an anti-HSV-1 agent, an anti-HIV-1 agent and an EC 3.2.1.18 (exo-alpha-sialidase) inhibitor. It is a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic, a beta-D-glucoside, a fatty acid derivative and a macrodiolide.
