The molecule is a beta-diketone isolated from Ochrocarpos punctatus and has been shown to exhibit antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a cyclic ketone, a peroxol, a bridged compound, a cyclic ether, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a beta-diketone, an enone, an aromatic ketone and a beta-triketone.
