The molecule is a member of the class of chalcones that is trans-chalcone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2, 4, 2' and 4', a methoxy group at position 6' and a 5-hydroxy-5-methyl-2-(prop-1-en-2-yl)hexyl group at position 3 respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of chalcones, a member of resorcinols and a monomethoxybenzene. It derives from a trans-chalcone.
