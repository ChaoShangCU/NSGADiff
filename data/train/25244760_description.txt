The molecule is an organophosphonate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of one of the two phosphonate OH groups of (1R,2S)-epoxypropylphosphonic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent. It is a conjugate base of a fosfomycin. It is a conjugate acid of a (1R,2S)-epoxypropylphosphonate(2-).
