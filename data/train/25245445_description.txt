The molecule is a monohydroxybenzoic acid that is 4-hydroxybenzoic acid in which the hydrogens at positions 3 and 5 are substituted by a methoxy and a nonaprenyl group. It is a member of phenols, a monohydroxybenzoic acid and a methoxybenzoic acid. It derives from a 3-nonaprenyl-4,5-dihydroxybenzoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-nonaprenyl-4-hydroxy-5-methoxybenzoate.
