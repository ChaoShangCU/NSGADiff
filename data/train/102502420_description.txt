The molecule is an acyclic sesquarterpene that is 3-methylideneoctacosa-1,6,10,14,18,22,26-heptaene carrying six methyl substituents at positions 7, 11, 15, 19, 23 and 27 (the all-E geoisomer). It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a sesquarterpene and a polyene. It derives from a trans-beta-farnesene.
