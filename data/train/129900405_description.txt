The molecule is a (3E,5Z)-dienoyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of (3E,5Z)-dodecadienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a (3E,5Z)-dienoyl-CoA(4-), a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3E,5Z)-dodecadienoyl-CoA.
