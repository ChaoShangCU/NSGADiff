The molecule is the organophosphate oxoanion that is the 2R,5Z isomer of 2-carboxylato-4-methyl-5-[(2-phosphonatooxy)ethylidene]-2,5-dihydrothiazole(3-); protonated to pH 7.3. It is a 2-carboxylato-4-methyl-5-[(2-phosphonatooxy)ethylidene]-2,5-dihydrothiazole(3-) and an organophosphate oxoanion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-[(2R,5Z)-2-carboxy-4-methylthiazol-5(2H)-ylidene]ethyl phosphate.
