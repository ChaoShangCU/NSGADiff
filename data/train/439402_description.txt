The molecule is the 2-oxo-5-amino derivative of valeric acid. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 2-oxo monocarboxylic acid and a delta-amino acid. It derives from a valeric acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-amino-2-oxopentanoate. It is a tautomer of a 5-amino-2-oxopentanoic acid zwitterion.
