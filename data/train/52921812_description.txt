The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is octatriacontanoic acid having six double bonds located at positions 23, 26, 29, 32 and 35 (the 23Z,26Z,29Z,32Z,35Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and an octatriacontapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (23Z,26Z,29Z,32Z,35Z)-octatriacontapentaenoate.
