The molecule is a cembrane diterpenoid that is cembra-2E,7E,11Z-trien-20,10-olide substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 4. It has been isolated from the leaves of Croton gratissimus. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a cembrane diterpenoid, a diterpene lactone, a macrocycle and a diol.
