The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin of the class of cucurbitane glycosides isolated from the roots of Machilus yaoshansis. It has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against protein tyrosine phosphatase. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an EC 3.1.3.48 (protein-tyrosine-phosphatase) inhibitor. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a triterpenoid saponin, a tetracyclic triterpenoid, a member of 1,3-oxazoles and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
