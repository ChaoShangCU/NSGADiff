The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of alpha-D-GlcNS-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcA-(1->4)-alpha-D-GlcNS-(1->4)-beta-D-GlcA in which the hydrogen attached to the anomeric oxygen is replaced by a 4-nitrophenyl group. It is a glycoside, a carbohydrate acid derivative, an amino tetrasaccharide and a C-nitro compound.
