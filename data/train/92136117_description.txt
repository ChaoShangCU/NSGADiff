The molecule is a carbohydrate sulfonate that is D-gluconic acid in which the hydroxy group at position 6 is replaced by a sulfo group. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite. It is a carbohydrate sulfonate and a carbohydrate acid derivative. It derives from a D-gluconic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 6-deoxy-6-sulfo-D-gluconate(2-).
