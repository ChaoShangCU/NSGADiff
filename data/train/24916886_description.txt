The molecule is trianion of 5-amino-1-(5-phospho-D-ribosyl)imidazole-4-carboxylic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and an imidazolyl carboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 5-amino-1-(5-phospho-D-ribosyl)imidazole-4-carboxylic acid.
