The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C34H47NO10 that is isolated from several Aconitum species. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a benzoate ester, an acetate ester, a bridged compound, a diol, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a secondary alcohol and a tertiary amino compound. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
