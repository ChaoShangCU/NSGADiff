The molecule is an aminotetrasaccharide that is 2-acetamido-alpha-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 4 have been glycosylated by alpha-L-fucopyranosyl and 2-acetamido-4-O-alpha-D-mannopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl groups, respectively. It is an amino sugar, an amino tetrasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
