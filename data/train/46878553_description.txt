The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide and epitope consisting of L-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptopyranose having a lactosyl moiety attached at the 3-position via an alpha-linkage and an alpha-N-acetylglucosaminyl residue at the 2-position. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino tetrasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
