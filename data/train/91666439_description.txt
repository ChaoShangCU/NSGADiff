The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3E,5Z,8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosapentaenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a (3E,5Z)-dienoyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3E,5Z,8Z,11Z,14Z)-icosapentaenoyl-CoA.
