The molecule is a disaccharide derivative that is betanidin in which a beta-D-glucuronosyl-(1->2)-beta-D-glucosyl moiety is attached at position 5. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a biological pigment. It is a disaccharide derivative, an olefinic compound, a tetrahydropyridine and a member of indoles. It derives from a betanidin.
