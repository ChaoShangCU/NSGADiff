The molecule is a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 4,8-dimethylnonanoic acid. It is a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA and a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 4,8-dimethylnonanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 4,8-dimethylnonanoyl-CoA(4-).
