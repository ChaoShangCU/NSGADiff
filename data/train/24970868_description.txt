The molecule is an organic heterohexacyclic compound that is isolated from New Caledonian deep water sponge and exhibits antiplasmodial properties. It has a role as a metabolite and an antiplasmodial drug. It is an organic heterohexacyclic compound, a secondary amine, a cyclic hemiketal, a sulfone and a member of p-quinones. It derives from an alisiaquinone A.
