The molecule is a polyene antibiotic that is TMC-1A in which the 2,4-dimethyloct-2-enoyl group has been replaced by an (E)-4,6-dimethyldec-2-enoyl group. TMC-1D is an antitumour antibiotic isolated from Streptomyces sp. A-230. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ketone, an enol, a polyene antibiotic, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, an enone, an enamide and a secondary carboxamide.
