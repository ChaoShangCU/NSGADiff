The molecule is a member of the class of prostaglandins D that is prosta-5,13,17-trien-1-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 9 and 15 and an oxo group at position 11 (the 5Z,13E,15S,17Z-stereoisomer). It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a prostaglandin D3(1-).
