The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is N,1,4,4-tetramethyl-4,5-dihydro-1H-pyrazolo[4,3-h]quinazoline-3-carboxamide substituted by a [2-methyl-4-(4-methylpiperazin-1-yl)phenyl]nitrilo group at position 8. It is an organic heterotricyclic compound, a N-methylpiperazine, an aminotoluene, a secondary carboxamide, a secondary amino compound and a tertiary amino compound.
