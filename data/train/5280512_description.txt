The molecule is a macrolide antibiotic that is tylonolide having mono- and diglycosyl moieties attached to two of its hydroxy groups.. It is an aldehyde, an enone, a macrolide antibiotic, a monosaccharide derivative, a disaccharide derivative and a leucomycin. It derives from a tylactone. It is a conjugate acid of a demethylmacrocin(1+).
