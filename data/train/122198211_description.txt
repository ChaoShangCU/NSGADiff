The molecule is a 1-(alk-1-enyl)-2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion in which the alkenyl and acyl groups are specified as (1Z)-hexadecenyl and linoleoyl respectively. It is a 1-(Z)-alk-1-enyl-2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion and a 1-O-(1Z-hexadecenyl)-2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion. It is a tautomer of a 1-(1Z-hexadecenyl)-2-linoleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine.
