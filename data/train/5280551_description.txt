The molecule is a member of the class of 4'-methoxyisoflavones that is formononetin with a hydroxy group at the 2'position. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a member of 7-hydroxyisoflavones and a member of 4'-methoxyisoflavones. It derives from a formononetin. It is a conjugate acid of a 2'-hydroxyformononetin(1-).
