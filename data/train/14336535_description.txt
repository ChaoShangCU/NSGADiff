The molecule is a fourteen-membered macrocyclic diterpene consisting of 12-isopropylidene-1,5,9-trimethylcyclotetradecane having three endocyclic double bonds located at positions 1, 5 and 9. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a diterpene, a macrocycle and a cycloalkatriene. It derives from a hydride of a cembrane.
