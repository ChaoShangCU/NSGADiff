The molecule is a member of the class of cyclohexenones that is (1R,5S,6R)-4-amino-5-hydroxy-1-(hydroxymethyl)-7-oxabicyclo[4.1.0]hept-3-en-2-one in which one of the amino hydrogens is replaced by an acetyl group. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an acetamide, an epoxide, a member of cyclohexenones, an enone, an oxabicycloalkane, a primary alcohol and a tertiary alcohol.
