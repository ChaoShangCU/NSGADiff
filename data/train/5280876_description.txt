The molecule is a long-chain fatty acid consisting of leukotriene B4 having a 12-keto group in place of the 12-hydroxy group. It is an oxo fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid, a leukotriene and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It derives from an icosa-6,8,10,14-tetraenoic acid and a leukotriene B4. It is a conjugate acid of a 12-dehydro-leukotriene B4(1-).
