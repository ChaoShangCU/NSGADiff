The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of beta-D-galactopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-D-mannopyranose joined in sequence by a (1->4) glycosidic bond. It is an amino disaccharide, a member of acetamides and a glycosylmannose derivative. It derives from a N-acetyl-D-mannosamine and a beta-D-galactose.
