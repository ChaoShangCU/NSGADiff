The molecule is an N-(polyunsaturated fatty acyl)ethanolamine that is anandamide in which one of the methyl hydrogens at position 20 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a N-(long-chain-acyl)ethanolamine, a N-(polyunsaturated fatty acyl)ethanolamine and an endocannabinoid. It derives from an anandamide and a 20-HETE.
