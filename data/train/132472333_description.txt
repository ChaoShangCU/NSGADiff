The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 22-hydroxyprotectin D1, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion, a docosanoid anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 22-hydroxyprotectin D1.
