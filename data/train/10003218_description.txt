The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C24H39NO6 that is isolated from several Aconitum species. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, a polyether, a diterpene alkaloid, a secondary alcohol, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound and a triol. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
