The molecule is an S-acyl-4'-phosphopantetheine obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of S-(3alpha,7alpha,12alpha-trihydroxy-5beta-cholestan-26-oyl)-4'-phosphopantetheine; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a S-(3alpha,7alpha,12alpha-trihydroxy-5beta-cholestan-26-oyl)-4'-phosphopantetheine.
