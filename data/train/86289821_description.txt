The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside that is ascr#24 in which the pro-R hydrogen that is beta to the carboxy group is replaced by a hydroxy group. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside, a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an ascr#24 and a (3R,13R)-3,13-dihydroxymyristic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a bhas#24(1-).
