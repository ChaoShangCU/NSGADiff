The molecule is a member of the class of benzamides obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 3-{[1-(2,4-difluorophenyl)-7-methyl-6-oxo-6,7-dihydropyrazolo[3,4-b]pyridin-4-yl]amino}-4-methylbenzoic acid with the amino group of cyclopropylamine. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzamides, an organofluorine compound, a pyrazolopyridine, an aromatic amine, a secondary amino compound and a member of cyclopropanes.
