The molecule is an anthracycline obtained by hydrolysis of the methyl ester group of aclacinomycin T. It is an anthracycline, an aminoglycoside, a deoxy hexoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a member of p-quinones. It derives from an aklavinone. It is a tautomer of a 15-demethylaclacinomycin T zwitterion.
