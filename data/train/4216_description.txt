The molecule is an N-sulfonyldiazepane resullting from the formal condensation of 5-iodo-1-naphthylsulfonic acid with one of the nitrogens of 1,4-diazepane. It is a selective inhibitor of myosin light chain kinase (EC 2.7.11.18). It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.18 (myosin-light-chain kinase) inhibitor. It is an organoiodine compound and a N-sulfonyldiazepane.
