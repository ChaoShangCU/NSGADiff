The molecule is a mannose phosphate consisting of beta-D-mannose having a (4S,8S,12S,16S,20S)-4,8,12,16,20-pentamethylheptacosyl)phosphate group at position 1. It is a mannose phosphate and a beta-D-mannosyl 4,8,12,16,20-pentamethylheptacosyl phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a beta-D-mannosyl C32-phosphomycoketide(1-).
