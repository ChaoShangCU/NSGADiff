The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-22-hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-22-hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid.
