The molecule is a 3-hydroxy steroid that is estrone substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 16. It has a role as an estrogen and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a 16beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3-hydroxy steroid, a 17-oxo steroid, a member of phenols and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from an estrone. It derives from a hydride of an estrane.
