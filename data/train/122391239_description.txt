The molecule is a flavone C-glycoside that consists of chrysoeriol carrying alpha-L-arabinosyl and beta-L-arabinosyl groups at positions 6 and 8 respectively It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavone, a polyphenol, a monomethoxyflavone and a flavone C-glycoside. It derives from a 4',5,7-trihydroxy-3'-methoxyflavone.
