The molecule is an alkaloid that consists of piperidine substituted by [1-acetyl-7-methyl-1,2,3,4,6,7,8,8a-octahydroquinolin-5-yl]methyl moieties at positions 2 and 6 respectively. Isolated from Lycopodium hamiltonii, it exhibits acetylcholinesterase inhibitory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor. It is an alkaloid, a member of isoquinolines, a member of acetamides and a member of piperidines.
