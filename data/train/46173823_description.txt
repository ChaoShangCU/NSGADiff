The molecule is the parent member of the class of aplysiatoxins. It is a cyanotoxin produced by several species of freshwater and marine cyanobacteria, as well as algae and molluscs. It has a role as a carcinogenic agent, a cyanotoxin, a protein kinase C agonist, a marine metabolite and an algal metabolite. It is a member of aplysiatoxins, a cyclic hemiketal, an ether, a secondary alcohol, a bromophenol, an organic heterotricyclic compound and a spiroketal.
