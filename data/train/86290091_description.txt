The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 12-HPE(8,10,14)TrE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a hydroperoxyicosatrienoate. It is a conjugate base of a 12-HPE(8,10,14)TrE.
