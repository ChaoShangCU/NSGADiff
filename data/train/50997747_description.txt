The molecule is a pyrazolopyrimidine that is pyrazolo[1,5-a]pyrimidine bearing quinolin-4-yl and 4-isopropyloxyphenyl substituents at positions 3 and 6 respectively. It has a role as a protein kinase inhibitor, a bone morphogenetic protein receptor antagonist and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of quinolines, a pyrazolopyrimidine and an aromatic ether.
