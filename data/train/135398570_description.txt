The molecule is a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide in which the purine nucleobase is specified as guanidine. It has a role as a plant metabolite, a human metabolite, a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a guanyl ribonucleotide and a 3',5'-cyclic purine nucleotide. It is a conjugate acid of a 3',5'-cyclic GMP(1-).
