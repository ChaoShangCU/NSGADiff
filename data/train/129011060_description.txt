The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 5alpha-androstane-3beta,17beta-diol having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 17. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid, a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid and a 3beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 5alpha-androstane-3beta,17beta-diol. It is a conjugate acid of a 5alpha-androstane-3beta,17beta-diol 17-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
