The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3 and 6, a methoxy group at position 5 and a beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 1 via a C-glycosyl linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of xanthones, a C-glycosyl compound, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether.
