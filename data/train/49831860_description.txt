The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is (6E)-6-hepten-3-ol substituted by a 3,4-dihydroxyphenyl group at position 1 and a phenyl group at position 7 (the 3R- stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma kwangsiensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a secondary alcohol and a member of catechols.
