The molecule is a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic that is the semisynthetic derivative of lincomycin, a natural antibiotic. It has a role as a xenobiotic and an environmental contaminant. It is a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic, a S-glycosyl compound, a pyrrolidinecarboxamide, an organochlorine compound and a semisynthetic derivative. It derives from a lincomycin.
