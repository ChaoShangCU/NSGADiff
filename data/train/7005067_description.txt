The molecule is a D-methionine derivative that is the amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of D-methionine with the amino group of 2-naphthylamine. It is a 2-amino-4-(methylsulfanyl)-N-(2-naphthyl)butanamide and a D-methionine derivative. It is an enantiomer of a L-methionine 2-naphthylamide.
