The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminyl, beta-D-galactosyl and 6-sulfated N-acetyl-beta-D-gluosamine residues linked sequentially (2->3) and (1->4). It has a role as an epitope. It is a glucosamine oligosaccharide, an amino trisaccharide and an oligosaccharide sulfate.
