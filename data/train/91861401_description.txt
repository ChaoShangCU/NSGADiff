The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of beta-D-glucopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->4) glycosidic bond. It is a member of acetamides, an amino disaccharide and a glycosylglucose derivative. It derives from a beta-D-glucose and a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine.
