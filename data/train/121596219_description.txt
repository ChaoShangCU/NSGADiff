The molecule is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion that is the conjugate base of N-[(9-beta-D-ribofuranosylpurin-6-yl)carbamoyl]threonine, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a human metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a N-[(9-beta-D-ribofuranosylpurin-6-yl)carbamoyl]threonine.
