The molecule is a sesquiterpenoid that is 2-methylheptan-4-one substituted by a 4-methylphenyl group at position 6. It has been isolated from Peltophorum dasyrachis. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor. It is a sesquiterpenoid, a ketone and a member of toluenes.
