The molecule is a tricyclic sesquiterpenoid that is pentalenene in which the 13-methyl group is oxidsed to the carboxylic acid. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a sesquiterpenoid, a carbotricyclic compound and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a pentalenene. It is a conjugate acid of a 1-deoxypentalenate.
