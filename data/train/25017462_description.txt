The molecule is a cyclodepsipeptide isolated from Jaspis splendens. A derivative of jaspamide, it has been shown to exhibit cytotoxic and microfilament disruption activity. It has a role as an actin polymerisation inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent, an animal metabolite and a marine metabolite. It is a cyclodepsipeptide, a macrocycle and an organobromine compound.
