The molecule is an ultra-long-chain fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of dotriacontanoic acid (lacceroic acid), obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a straight-chain saturated fatty acid anion and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid anion. It derives from a 3-oxodotriacontanoyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a dotriacontanoic acid.
