The molecule is a member of the class of oxazolidinones that is oxazolidin-2-one substituted at positions 3, 4 and 5 by (naphthalen-1-yl)acetyl, methyl and 3,5-bis(trifluoromethyl)phenyl groups respectively. An inhibitor of teichoic acid biosynthesis. It has a role as a teichoic acid biosynthesis inhibitor. It is an oxazolidinone, a carbamate ester, a member of naphthalenes, a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes and a dicarboximide. It derives from an oxazolidin-2-one.
