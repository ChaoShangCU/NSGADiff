The molecule is a sesquiterpene that is cyclohexane substituted at positions 1, 1, 2, and 4 by methyl, vinyl, isopropenyl and isopropylidene groups, respectively (the S,S stereoisomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite and a volatile oil component. It is a sesquiterpene, a monocyclic hydrocarbon and an olefinic compound.
