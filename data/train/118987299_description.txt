The molecule is a member of the class of pyrimidones that is 1,2-dihydropyrimidin-2-one in which the hydrogen at position 4 is replaced by a 5,6-diamino-2-oxo-2,3,4,5-tetrahydropyrimidin-4-yl group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a pyrimidone, a ring assembly and an aminopyrimidine.
