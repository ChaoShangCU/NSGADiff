The molecule is a leukotriene that is (5S,7E,9E,11Z)-5-hydroxyicosa-7,9,11-trienoic acid in which an L-cysteinylglycinyl group is attached at position 6R via a sulfide linkage. It has a role as a mouse metabolite, a rat metabolite and a xenobiotic metabolite. It is a leukotriene, an organic sulfide and a dipeptide.
