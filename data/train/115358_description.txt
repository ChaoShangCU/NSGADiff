The molecule is an aminoquinoline that is 8-aminoquinoline which is substituted by methoxy groups at positions 2 and 6, a methyl group at position 4, and a m-(trifluoromethyl)phenoxy group at position 5, and in which the amino substituent at position 8 is itself substituted by a 5-aminopentan-2-yl group. It is a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes, an aminoquinoline, an aromatic ether, a primary amino compound and a secondary amino compound.
