The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is hexatriacontanoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 21, 24, 27, 30 and 33 (the 21Z,24Z,27Z,30Z,33Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and a hexatriacontapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (21Z,24Z,27Z,30Z,33Z)-hexatriacontapentaenoate.
