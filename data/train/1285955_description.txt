The molecule is a hydrazone obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 1,3-benzodioxolane-5-carboxylic acid with the hydrazino group of 4'-benzyloxy-3'-methoxybenzylidene hydrazide. It has a role as a formyl peptide receptor agonist. It is a member of benzodioxoles, a monomethoxybenzene, a benzyl ether and a hydrazone.
