The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the sulfanylcarbonyl and phosphate OH groups of 1-(5-O-phosphono-beta-D-ribofuranosyl)-5-(sulfanylcarbonyl)pyridinium-3-carbonyl adenylate; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 1-(5-O-phosphono-beta-D-ribofuranosyl)-5-(sulfanylcarbonyl)pyridinium-3-carbonyl adenylate.
