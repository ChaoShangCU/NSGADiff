The molecule is an alkaloid of the class of acridone derivatives isolated from Oriciopsis glaberrima and has been shown to exhibit radical scavenging and alpha-glucosidase inhibitory activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor and a radical scavenger. It is a member of acridone derivatives, an alkaloid, a cyclic ether, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a polyphenol and a ring assembly.
