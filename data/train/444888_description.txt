The molecule is a C-nitro compound that is a chloramphenicol phosphonate hapten which acts as a transition state analogue for the hydrolysis of chloramphenicol esters mediated by the catalytic antibody 7C8. It has a role as a hapten. It is an organochlorine compound, a C-nitro compound and a trifluoroacetamide. It derives from a chloramphenicol.
