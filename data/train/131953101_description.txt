The molecule is an anionic phospholipid that is the conjugate base of (R,R)-2-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a (R,R)-2-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol. It is an enantiomer of a (S,S)-2-oleoylglycero-1-phospho-1'-glycerol(1-).
