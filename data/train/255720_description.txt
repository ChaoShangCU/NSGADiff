The molecule is an aminobenzoic acid that is anthranilic acid in which the hydrogen at position 3 is substituted by a methoxy group. It is a metabolite of kynurenine. It has a role as a mammalian metabolite. It is a member of benzoic acids and an aminobenzoic acid. It derives from an anthranilic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-methoxyanthranilate.
