The molecule is a semisynthetic penicillin having a 6beta-{(2R)-2-[(2-oxoimidazolidine-1-carbonyl)amino]-2-phenylacetyl}amino side-group. It is an antibiotic used in treating infections caused by Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Escherichia coli and Haemophilus influenzae. It has a role as an antibacterial drug. It is a penicillin, a semisynthetic derivative and a penicillin allergen. It is a conjugate acid of an azlocillin(1-).
