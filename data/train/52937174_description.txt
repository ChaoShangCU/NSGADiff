The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is podocarpa-8,11,13-triene substituted by a hydroxy group at position 11, a propan-2-yl group at position 14 and a carboxy group at position 16. It has been isolated from Podocarpus latifolius. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, a member of phenols and a monocarboxylic acid.
