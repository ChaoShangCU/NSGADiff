The molecule is the anion resulting from the removal of a proton from the carboxylic acid group of 3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dienyl)propanoic acid. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion and a cyclohexadienediol. It is a conjugate base of a 3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dienyl)propanoic acid.
