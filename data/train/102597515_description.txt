The molecule is a monoterpene glycoside with formula C30H32O13, originally isolated from Paeonia suffruticosa. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a radical scavenger. It is a 4-hydroxybenzoate ester, a bridged compound, a cyclic acetal, a lactol, a monoterpene glycoside, an O-acyl carbohydrate and an alpha-D-glucoside.
