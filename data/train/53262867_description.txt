The molecule is a para-terphenyl that is the 4''-dehydro derivative of 3-hydroxyterphenyllin. It has been isolated from Aspergillus taichungensis. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a para-terphenyl, a member of catechols and a dimethoxybenzene. It derives from a 3-hydroxyterphenyllin.
