The molecule is a member of the class of pyrimidines that is pyrimidine which has been substituted at positions 2, 4, and 5 by (1-isopropyl-2-oxo-2,3-dihydro-1H-indol-5-yl)amino, [3-(methylsulfonyl)benzyl]amino, and trifluoromethyl groups, respectively. It is a sulfone, a secondary amino compound, a member of pyrimidines, a member of oxindoles and a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes.
