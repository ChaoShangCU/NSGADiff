The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone that is isolated from Acroptilon repens and displays anti-allergic properties. It has a role as an anti-allergic agent and a plant metabolite. It is a sesquiterpene lactone, an azulenofuran, an epoxide, an olefinic compound, a tertiary alcohol, a secondary alcohol, an organochlorine compound and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
