The molecule is an amino disaccharide consisting of 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose and D-galactopyranose residues joined in sequence by a (1->6) glycosidic bond. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a D-galactopyranose and a N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine.
