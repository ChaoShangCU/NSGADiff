The molecule is an anthocyanin cation consisting of delphinidin with beta-D-glucosyl groups at the 3- and 3'-positions. It is a beta-D-glucoside and an anthocyanin cation. It derives from a delphinidin. It is a conjugate acid of a delphinidin-5-olate 3-O-(6''-O-carboxylatoacetyl)-beta-D-glucoside-3'-O-beta-D-glucoside(1-).
