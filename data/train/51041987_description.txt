The molecule is a ring assembly composed of fisetinidol and gallocatechin units. Isolated from Acacia mearnsii, it exhibits inhibitory activity against alpha-amylase. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.2.1.1 (alpha-amylase) inhibitor. It is a catechin and a ring assembly. It derives from a fisetinidol and a gallocatechin.
