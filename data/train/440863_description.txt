The molecule is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate consisting of adenosine 5'-monophosphate where one of the hydroxy groups of the phosphate has been condensed with propionic acid. It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It derives from an adenosine 5'-monophosphate and a propionic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a propanoyl-AMP(1-).
