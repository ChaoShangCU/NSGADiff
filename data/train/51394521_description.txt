The molecule is a cadinane sesquiterpenoid that consists of 4-isopropyl-1,6-dimethyl-1,2,3,4,4a,7,8,8a-octahydronaphthalene having a hydroxy substituent at position 1 and (1R,4R,4aS,8aR)-configuration. It is a cadinane sesquiterpenoid and a member of octahydronaphthalenes. It is an enantiomer of a (-)-Tau-muurolol.
