The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is 3,4,5,6-tetrahydro-2H-2,6-epoxyanthra[2,3-b]oxocine-8,13-dione substituted at position 2 by a methyl group and at positions 7, 9 and 11 by hydroxy groups (the S,S-diastereomer) It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a polyketide, a cyclic ketal and a polyphenol.
