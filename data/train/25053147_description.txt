The molecule is an organic heterohexacyclic compound that is cladoniamide B in which the chlorine attached to the methoxy-bearing indole moiety is replaced by a hydrogen. It has been isolated from the culture broth of Streptomyces uncialis. It is a cladoniamide, an organic heterohexacyclic compound, an organochlorine compound, a dicarboximide, a tertiary alcohol and a diol.
