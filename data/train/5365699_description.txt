The molecule is a carbonate ester that is dimethyl carbonate in which one of the methyl groups has been replaced by a (Z)-hex-3-en-1-yl group. It has a floral scent and is used in the production of fruit or vegetable-like flavour additives. It has a role as a flavouring agent. It is a carbonate ester and an olefinic compound.
