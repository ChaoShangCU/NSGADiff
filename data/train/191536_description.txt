The molecule is an N-glycosyl compound that is cytosine in which the proton at position 1 is replaced by a beta-D-glucuronosyl residue. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, a nucleobase-containing molecular entity, a carbohydrate acid derivative and a monosaccharide derivative. It derives from a cytosine and a beta-D-glucuronic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a cytosylglucuronate.
