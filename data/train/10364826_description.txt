The molecule is a member of the class of bipyridines that is 4,4'-bipyridine substituted at positions 2 and 2' by 4-fluoroaminophenyl and 4-methoxycyclohexanecarboxamido groups respectively. It is a member of bipyridines, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an ether, an organofluorine compound, an aromatic amine and a secondary amino compound.
