The molecule is a dihydroxybenzaldehyde that is 2,4-dihydroxybenzaldehyde which is substituted by a methyl group at position e and by a at positions 3 and 4-hydroxy-2-oxopentyl group at position 6. A fungal metabolite isolated from Pseudobotrytis and Aspergillus niger (stereochemistry not been determined). It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a dihydroxybenzaldehyde, a beta-hydroxy ketone and a secondary alcohol.
