The molecule is the zwitterion obtained from N(omega)-methyl-L-arginine by transfer of a proton from the carboxylic acid group to the alpha-amino group. It is a member of guanidines and an amino acid zwitterion. It is a conjugate base of a (2S)-2-ammonio-5-{[iminio(methylamino)methyl]amino}pentanoate. It is a tautomer of a N(omega)-methyl-L-arginine.
