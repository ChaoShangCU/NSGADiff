The molecule is a derivative of vitexin having an alpha-L-rhamnosyl residue attached at the 2''-position of the glucitol moiety. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a trihydroxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a vitexin. It is a conjugate acid of a vitexin 2''-O-alpha-L-rhamnoside(1-).
