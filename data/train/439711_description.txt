The molecule is a fenchane monoterpenoid that is bicyclo[2.2.1]heptane substituted by methyl groups at positions 1, 3 and 3 and a hydroxy group at position 2 (the 1S,2S,4R stereoisomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite and a volatile oil component. It is a fenchane monoterpenoid and a carbobicyclic compound.
