The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is isovitexin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by a 6-(6-methoxycaffeoyl)glucosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a cinnamate ester, a dihydroxyflavone and a glycosyloxyflavone. It derives from an isovitexin and a trans-caffeic acid.
