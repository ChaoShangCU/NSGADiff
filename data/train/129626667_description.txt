The molecule is a member of the class of resolvins that is resolvin D2 in which the 7-hydroxy group has undergone formal oxidation to the corresponding ketone. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a diol, an enone, an oxo fatty acid, a resolvin, a secondary allylic alcohol and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 7-oxoresolvin D2(1-).
