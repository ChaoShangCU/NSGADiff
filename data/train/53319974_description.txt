The molecule is an ergostanoid that is the methyl ester of zhankuic acid A. It has been isolated from the fruiting bodies of Taiwanofungus camphoratus. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an ergostanoid, a 3-oxo steroid, a 7-oxo steroid, an 11-oxo steroid and a steroid ester. It derives from a zhankuic acid A.
