The molecule is a piperidine alkaloid that is decahydronaphtho[2,3-c]furan-1(3H)-one substituted by a methyl group at position 3 and a 2-[(2R,6S)-1,6-dimethylpiperidin-2-yl]ethenyl group at position 4. It has been isolated from the bark of Australian magnolias. It has a role as a muscarinic antagonist. It is a piperidine alkaloid, a gamma-lactone and an organic heterotricyclic compound.
