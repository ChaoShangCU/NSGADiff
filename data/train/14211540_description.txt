The molecule is a member of the class of ethanolamines that is 2-aminoethanol substituted by a [4-(2-amino-2-oxoethyl)phenoxy]methyl group at position 1. It is a metabolite of the drug atenolol. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is an aromatic ether, a member of ethanolamines, a propanolamine, a monocarboxylic acid amide and a primary amino compound.
