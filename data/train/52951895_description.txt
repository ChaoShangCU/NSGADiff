The molecule is a limonoid that is 4,4,8-trimethylandrosta-1,16-diene substituted by a furan-3-yl group at position 17, oxo groups at positions 3 and 15, a hydroxy group at position 16 and an acetoxy group at position 7. It has been isolated from Azadirachta indica. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a member of furans, a limonoid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and an enol.
