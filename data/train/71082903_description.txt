The molecule is a methyl 2-(4-isopropyl-4-methyl-5-oxo-4,5-dihydro-1H-imidazol-2-yl)-5-methylbenzoate in which the chiral centre has S configuration. It derives from a 6-[(4S)-4-isopropyl-4-methyl-5-oxo-2-imidazolin-2-yl]-m-toluic acid. It is an enantiomer of a methyl 6-[(4R)-4-isopropyl-4-methyl-5-oxo-2-imidazolin-2-yl]-m-toluate.
