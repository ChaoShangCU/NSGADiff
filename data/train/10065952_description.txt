The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 2, 5 and 8, methoxy group at position 1 and a 2-methylbut-3-en-2-yl group at position 4. Isolated from the woods of Garcinia subelliptica, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a member of xanthones, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether.
