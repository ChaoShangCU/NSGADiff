The molecule is an ultra-long-chain fatty acid that is (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-dotriacontapentaenoic acid substituted at position 2 by a hydroxy group. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid, a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid and an ultra-long-chain fatty acid. It derives from a (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-dotriacontapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-2-hydroxydotriacontapentaenoate.
