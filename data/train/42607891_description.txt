The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is (S)-naringenin substituted by a beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 8 via a C-glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a trihydroxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a (2S)-flavan-4-one. It derives from a (S)-naringenin.
