The molecule is a dithiocarbamate salt that is the zinc salt of dimethyldithiocarbamic acid. It is a broad-spectrum fungicide and bird and animal repellent that is also used to accelerate the vulcanisation of rubber. It has a role as an apoptosis inducer and an antifungal agrochemical. It is a dithiocarbamate salt and a zinc molecular entity. It contains a zinc(2+) and a dimethyldithiocarbamate. It derives from a dimethyldithiocarbamic acid.
