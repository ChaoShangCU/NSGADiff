The molecule is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion arising from selective deprotonation of the carboxy function of D-glucuronic acid 1-phosphate. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It derives from a D-glucuronate. It is a conjugate base of a D-glucuronic acid 1-phosphate.
