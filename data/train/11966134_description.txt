The molecule is an oxo-fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxylic acid group of 7-methyl-3-oxooctanoic acid. It is a methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA and a 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from an octanoyl-CoA and a 7-methyl-3-oxooctanoic acid.
