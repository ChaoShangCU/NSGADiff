The molecule is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid that is testosterone in which the 4,5 double bond has been reduced to a single bond with alpha-configuration at position 5. It has a role as an androgen, a human metabolite, a Daphnia magna metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17beta-hydroxyandrostan-3-one and a 3-oxo-5alpha-steroid. It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-androstane.
