The molecule is a 2H-azirine that is 2H-azirene-2-carboxylic acid substituted by a 13,13-dibromotrideca-1,12-dien-1-yl group at position 3 (the 2R stereoisomer). It is isolated from the marine sponge Siliquariaspongia sp. and exhibits antibacterial properties. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is a monocarboxylic acid and an organobromine compound. It derives from a 2H-azirine.
