The molecule is a trihydroxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 4', a geranyl group at position 3' and a prenyl group at position 6. Isolated from Schizolaena hystrix, it exhibits cytotoxicity against ovarian cancer cell line. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a trihydroxyflavanone and a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones.
