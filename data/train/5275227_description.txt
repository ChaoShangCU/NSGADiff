The molecule is a tetrahydroxyflavanone having the hydroxy groups at the 2'-, 4'-, 5- and 7-positions and a prenyl group at 8-position. It is a tetrahydroxyflavanone, a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones and a (2S)-flavan-4-one. It derives from a (S)-naringenin. It is a conjugate acid of a leachianone G(1-).
