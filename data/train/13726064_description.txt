The molecule is a dibenzoazepine that is 10,11-dihydro-5H-dibenzo[b,f]azepine-5-carboxamide substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 10 and 11. It is a metabolite of the drug carbamazepine. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is a member of ureas, a diol and a dibenzoazepine.
