The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is 9beta,19-cyclolanostan-28-oic acid substituted by an acetyloxy group at position 7 and hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 25. It has been isolated from the leaves of Combretum quadrangulare. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, an acetate ester and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of a cycloartane.
