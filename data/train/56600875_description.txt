The molecule is a member of the class of catechols that is benzene-1,2-diol substituted by a methoxy group at position 5 and a 3-(3,4-dimethoxyphenyl)propyl group at position 4. It has been isolated from the stems of Combretum griffithii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of catechols and a dimethoxybenzene.
