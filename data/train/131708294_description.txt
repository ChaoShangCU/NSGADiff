The molecule is a bicyclic diterpene that is 1,2,3,3a,4,5,8,8a-octahydroazulene carrying two methyl substituents at positions 6 and 8a as well as a 6-methylhepta-2,5-dien-2-yl at position 3. The relative stereochemistry has been assigned tentatively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diterpene, a carbobicyclic compound and a polycyclic olefin.
