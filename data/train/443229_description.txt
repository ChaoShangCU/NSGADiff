The molecule is a member of the class of triazoles that is 2,4-dihydro-3H-1,2,4-triazol-3-one which is substituted at positions 2, 4, and 5 by 5-(2-carboxy-2-chloroethyl)-4-chloro-2-fluorophenyl, difluoromethyl, and methyl groups, respectively. It is a member of triazoles, a member of monochlorobenzenes, a member of monofluorobenzenes and a monocarboxylic acid.
