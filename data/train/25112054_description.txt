The molecule is an angucycline that consists of tetrangomycin linked to a deoxy sugar moiety at position 9 via a C-glycosidic bond. It is isolated from Streptomyces sp.KY002 and exhibits cytotoxicity against human lung cancer and MCF-7 human breast cancer cells. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an antimicrobial agent. It is a C-glycosyl compound and an angucycline antibiotic. It derives from a tetrangomycin.
