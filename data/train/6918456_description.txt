The molecule is a member of the class of thienopyridines that is 2-acetoxy-4,5,6,7-tetrahydrothieno[3,2-c]pyridine in which the amino hydrogen is replaced by a 2-cyclopropyl-1-(2-fluorophenyl)-2-oxoethyl group. It is an acetate ester, a member of cyclopropanes, a ketone, a member of monofluorobenzenes, a tertiary amino compound and a thienopyridine.
