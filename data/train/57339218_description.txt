The molecule is a member of the class of benzamides obtained by formal condensation of the two primary amino groups of spemidine with the carboxy groups from two molecules of 2,5-dihydroxybenzoic acid. It has a role as an antibacterial agent. It is a member of benzamides and a member of hydroquinones. It derives from a spermidine and a 2,5-dihydroxybenzoic acid.
