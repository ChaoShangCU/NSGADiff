The molecule is a diastereoisomeric mixture of L-(R)- and L-(S)-valifenalate. An anti-peronosporic fungicide, it is used to control mildew in many crops including grapes, potatoes and tomatoes. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a diastereoisomeric mixture and an acylamino acid fungicide. It contains a L-(R)-valifenalate and a L-(S)-valifenalate.
