The molecule is a triterpenoid of the nor-ceanothane-type isolated from the roots of Breynia fruticosa and has been shown to exhibit cytotoxicity against human cancer cell lines. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a plant metabolite. It is an organic heteroheptacyclic compound, an epoxide, a terpene lactone, a monocarboxylic acid and a triterpenoid.
