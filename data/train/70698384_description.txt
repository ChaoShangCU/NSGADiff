The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by a 2,2-dimethyl-4-(prop-1-en-2-yl)cyclopentyl group at position 2, hydroxy groups at positions 1, 3 and 6 and a methoxy group at position 5. It is isolated from the callus of Hypericum perforatum and exhibits antioxidant and antimicrobial activities. It has a role as a radical scavenger, an antimicrobial agent and a plant metabolite. It is a member of xanthones, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether.
