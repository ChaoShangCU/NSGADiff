The molecule is a myo-inositol monophosphate derivative consisting of 1-O-(6-thiohexylphosphono)-D-myo-inositol having an alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->2)-6-O-(2-aminoethylphosphono)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->6)-alpha-D-mannosyl-(1->4)-alpha-D-glucosaminyl residue at the 6-position. It is a myo-inositol monophosphate derivative, a glycoside and a tetrasaccharide derivative. It derives from a myo-inositol.
