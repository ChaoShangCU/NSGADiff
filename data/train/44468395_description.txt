The molecule is a member of the class of thiadiazoles that is 1,2,3-thiadiazole substituted at positions 4 and 5 by 2,4-dihydroxy-4-chlorophenyl and 4-methoxyphenyl groups respectively. It has a role as a Hsp90 inhibitor. It is a member of thiadiazoles, a member of resorcinols, a member of monochlorobenzenes and a monomethoxybenzene.
