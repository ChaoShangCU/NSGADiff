The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid natural product found particularly in Azadirachta indica and Cedrela odorata. It has a role as an antimalarial, an antineoplastic agent, a Hsp90 inhibitor and a plant metabolite. It is a limonoid, an acetate ester, an epoxide, an enone, a member of furans, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound and a lactone.
