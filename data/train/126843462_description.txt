The molecule is an omega-carboxyacyl-CoA(5-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate, diphosphate and carboxy groups of any 3-carboxy-3-hydroxypropanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-carboxyacyl-CoA(5-) and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 3-carboxy-3-hydroxypropanoyl-CoA.
