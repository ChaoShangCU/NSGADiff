The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl groups at positions 1 and 2 are specified as stearoyl and linoleoyl respectively. It derives from a linoleic acid and an octadecanoic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1-stearoyl-2-linoleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
