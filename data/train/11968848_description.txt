The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a alpha-D-arabinofuranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a quercetin O-glycoside, an alpha-D-arabinoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a tetrahydroxyflavone.
