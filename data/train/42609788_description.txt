The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside triphosphate oxoanion obtained from thymidine 5'-triphosphate by deprotonation of three of the four free triphosphate OH groups; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a dTTP. It is a conjugate acid of a dTTP(4-).
