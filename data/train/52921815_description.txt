The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is triacontahexaenoic acid having six double bonds located at positions 12, 15, 18, 21, 24 and 27 (the 12Z,15Z,18Z,21Z,24Z,27Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and a triacontahexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (12Z,15Z,18Z,21Z,24Z,27Z)-triacontahexaenoate.
