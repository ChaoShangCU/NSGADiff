The molecule is an allo-bile acid that is 5alpha-cholan-24-oic acid bearing two alpha-hydroxy substituents at position 3 and 12. It has a role as a marine metabolite, a rat metabolite and a xenobiotic metabolite. It is an allo-bile acid, a 12alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid and a C24-steroid. It is a conjugate acid of an allodeoxycholate.
