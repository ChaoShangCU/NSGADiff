The molecule is a chromenol that is 2,2-dimethyl-2H-chromen-8-ol substituted by a 3-(2,4-dihydroxyphenyl)-3-oxoprop-1-en-1-yl group at position 6 (the E-isomer). It is isolated from the stems of Erythrina abyssinica and displays moderate cytotoxic effect against human colorectal cancer cell line. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of chalcones, a member of resorcinols and a chromenol.
