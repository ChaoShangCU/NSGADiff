The molecule is a secondary carboxamide resulting from the foraml condensation of the carboxy group of (2R)-2-[(1S)-1-hydroxy-2-(hydroxyamino)-2-oxoethyl]-4-methylpentanoic acid with the alpha-amino group of N,3-dimethyl-L-valinamide. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and a matrix metalloproteinase inhibitor. It is a secondary carboxamide and a hydroxamic acid.
