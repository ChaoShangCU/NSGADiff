The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is the dianion of 1D-myo-inositol 1-phosphate. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is an inositol phosphate oxoanion, a myo-inositol phosphate(2-) and a monosaccharide 1-phosphate(2-). It is a conjugate base of a 1D-myo-inositol 1-phosphate.
