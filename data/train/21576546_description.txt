The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is cyanidin attached to a 2''-O-galloyl-beta-D-galactopyranosyl residue at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an anthocyanin cation, a gallate ester, a beta-D-galactoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from a cyanidin cation.
