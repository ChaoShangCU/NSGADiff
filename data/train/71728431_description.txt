The molecule is an indolyl carbohydrate that is the beta-D-glucosaminide of indoxyl in which the indole moiety is substituted at positions 4 and 5 by chlorine and bromine, respectively. It is an indolyl carbohydrate, a D-glucosaminide, a monosaccharide derivative, a bromoindole and a chloroindole. It derives from an indoxyl.
