The molecule is a cholestanoid that is cholest-4-en-3-one which has been substituted by hydroxy groups at the 7alpha, 12alpha, and 26 positions. It is an intermediate in bile acid metabolism. It has a role as a bile acid metabolite. It is a 7alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 12alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 26-hydroxy steroid, a 3-oxo steroid, a cholestanoid and a C27-steroid.
