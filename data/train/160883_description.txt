The molecule is a carboxylic ester resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of (2S)-3-hydroxy-2-phenylpropanoic acid with the hydroxy group of (2R,4S,5S,7s)-9-butyl-7-hydroxy-9-methyl-3-oxa-9-azoniatricyclo[3.3.1.0(2,4)]nonane. It has a role as a muscarinic antagonist and an antispasmodic drug. It is a carboxylic ester, an epoxide, a quaternary ammonium ion and a tropane alkaloid.
