The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as docosanoyl and tetradecanoyl respectively. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a phosphatidylethanolamine 36:0. It derives from a docosanoic acid and a tetradecanoic acid.
