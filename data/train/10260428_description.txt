The molecule is an amino hexasaccharide consisting of alpha-L-fucose, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine, alpha-D-galactose, beta-D-galactose and beta-D-glucose residues joined in sequence with (1->2)-, (1->3)-, (1->3)-, (1->4)- and (1->4)-linkages, respectively. It has a role as an epitope. It is a galactosamine oligosaccharide and an amino hexasaccharide.
