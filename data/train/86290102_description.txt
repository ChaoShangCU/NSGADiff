The molecule is an organosulfonic acid that is 2-oxoethanesulfonic acid substituted by a (2,6-dimethylphenyl)(1H-pyrazol-1-ylmethyl)amino group at position 2. It is metabolite of the herbicide metazachlor. It has a role as a marine xenobiotic metabolite. It is an aromatic amide, a member of pyrazoles and an organosulfonic acid.
