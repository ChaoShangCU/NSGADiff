The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is 7-phenyl-5-imino-5,7-dihydrobenzo[a]phenazine-3-sulfonic acid in which the imino hydrogen is replaced by a 4-sulfophenyl group. The monosodium salt is the biological stain 'azocarmine G'. It is an arenesulfonic acid, an organic heterotetracyclic compound and an imine. It is a conjugate acid of an azocarmine G(1-).
