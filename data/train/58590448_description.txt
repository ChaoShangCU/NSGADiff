The molecule is a member of the class of indanes that is 2,5-dimethylindane in which the hydrogen at position 3 has been replaced by one of the primary amino groups of 6-(1-fluoroethyl)-1,3,5-triazine-2,4-diamine. It is a member of indanes, a diamino-1,3,5-triazine, a secondary amino compound and an organofluorine compound.
