The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of 15-hydroxy-(5Z,8Z,11Z,13E)-icosatetraenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 15-hydroxy-(5Z,8Z,11Z,13E)-icosatetraenoyl-CoA.
