The molecule is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate that is ADP substituted at position N-6 by a dimethylallyl (isopentenyl) group. It is a purine ribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate and an adenosine 5'-phosphate. It derives from an ADP. It is a conjugate acid of a N(6)-(dimethylallyl)adenosine 5'-diphosphate(3-).
