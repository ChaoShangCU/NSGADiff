The molecule is a leukotriene anion obtained by deprotonation of the three carboxy groups and protonation of the glutathionyl alpha-amino group of 11,12-dihydro-12-oxoleukotriene C4; major species at pH 7.3. It is a leukotriene anion and a tricarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of an 11,12-dihydro-12-oxoleukotriene C4.
