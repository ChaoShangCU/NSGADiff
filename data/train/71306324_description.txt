The molecule is a quassinoid isolated from Quassia indica and has been shown to exhibit antimalarial and cytotoxic activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimalarial and an antineoplastic agent. It is an acetate ester, a delta-lactone, a cyclic ether, an enone, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a quassinoid, a secondary alcohol, a triol and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
