The molecule is an azabicycloalkane that is 1,5-dimethyl-3-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,4-dione in which the amino hydrogen is replaced by a 3,5-dichlorophenyl group. A fungicide widely used in horticulture as a seed dressing, pre-harvest spray or post-harvest dip for the control of various diseases. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is an azabicycloalkane and a dichlorophenyl dicarboximide fungicide.
