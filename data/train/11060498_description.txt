The molecule is a homodetic cyclic peptide that consists of L-threonine and L-valine and L-serine as the amino acid residues cyclised via amide bonds. It is isolated from Lissoclinum bistratum and exhibits antitumour activity against the human colon tumour cell line It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a homodetic cyclic peptide and a macrocycle.
