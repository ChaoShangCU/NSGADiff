The molecule is a member of the class of naphthalenediols that is 1,2-dihydronaphthalene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 2 respectively. It has a role as a bacterial xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a member of dihydronaphthalenes and a member of naphthalenediols. It derives from a naphthalene-1,2-diol. It derives from a hydride of a 1,2-dihydronaphthalene.
