The molecule is a hydrochloride resulting from the formal reaction of equimolar amounts of neticonazole and hydrogen chloride. An inhibitor of P450-dependent C-14alpha-demethylation of lanosterol (preventing conversion to ergosterol and inhibiting cell wall synthesis in fungi), it is used in Japan as an antifungal drug for the treatment of superficial skin infections. It has a role as an antifungal drug and an EC 1.14.13.70 (sterol 14alpha-demethylase) inhibitor. It is a hydrochloride, an imidazole antifungal drug and a conazole antifungal drug. It contains a neticonazole(1+).
