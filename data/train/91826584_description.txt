The molecule is a nine-membered glucosamine oligosaccharide that consists of the disaccharide beta-D-Gal-(1->4)-beta-D-Glc, to the galactose residue of which are attached alpha-L-Fuc-(1->4)-[alpha-L-Fuc-(1->2)-beta-D-Gal-(1->3)]-beta-D-GlcNAc and beta-D-Gal-(1->4)-[alpha-L-Fuc-(1->3)]-beta-D-GlcNAc groups at O-3 and O-6 respectively. It is an amino nonasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
