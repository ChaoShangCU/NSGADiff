The molecule is an unsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (8E,10S)-10-hydroxy-8-octadecenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and an unsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an (8E,10S)-10-hydroxy-8-octadecenoic acid.
