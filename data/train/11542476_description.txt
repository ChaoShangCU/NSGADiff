The molecule is a homodectic cyclic peptide made up from N-methylglycyl, L-phenylalanyl, N-methyl-L-tyrosyl, L-isoleucyl, L-leucyl, N-methyl-D-phenylalanyl and L-prolyl residues linked in sequence. It is an antimalarial drug isolated from the insect pathogenic fungus Cordyceps. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is a homodetic cyclic peptide, a member of phenols and a macrocycle.
