The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-methylsteroid-4beta-carboxylate resulting from the deprotonation of the carboxy group of 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-methyl-5alpha-cholesta-8,24-diene-4beta-carboxylic acid. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-methyl-5alpha-cholesta-8,24-diene-4beta-carboxylic acid.
