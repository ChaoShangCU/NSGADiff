The molecule is a carbohydrate acid anion that results from the deprotonation of the carboxylic acid group of beta-D-Delta(4)-GlcpA-(1->4)-beta-D-Glcp-(1->4)-alpha-L-Rhap-(1->3)-beta-D-Glcp. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a beta-D-Delta(4)-GlcpA-(1->4)-beta-D-Glcp-(1->4)-alpha-L-Rhap-(1->3)-beta-D-Glcp.
