The molecule is a hydroxy-cannabidiol that is cannabidiol in which one of the hydrogens at position 4 of the pentyl chain has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is one of the main metabolites of cannabidiol by human liver microsomes, produced particularly by CYP3A. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a hydroxy-cannabidiol, an olefinic compound, a member of resorcinols and a secondary alcohol.
