The molecule is an ortho-fused bicyclic hydrocarbon that is 1,2-dihydronaphthalene substituted by methyl groups at positions 1, 1 and 5 respectively. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an ortho-fused bicyclic hydrocarbon and a member of dihydronaphthalenes. It derives from a hydride of a 1,2-dihydronaphthalene.
