The molecule is a pentamethoxyflavone that is flavone substituted by methoxy groups at positions 3, 7, 8, 4' and 5' and a hydroxy group at position 2'. It has been isolated from Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentamethoxyflavone and a monohydroxyflavone. It derives from a flavone.
