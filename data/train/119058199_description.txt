The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (5S)-hydroperoxy-(14R,15S)-epoxy-(6E,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3.
