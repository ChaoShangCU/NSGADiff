The molecule is an amino pentasaccharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-6-O-sulfo-D-glucitol which has been glycosylated at positions 3 and 4 by 6-deoxy-alpha-L-galactopyranosyl and 6-O-sulfo-beta-D-galactopyranosyl-(1->4)-2-acetamido-2-deoxy-6-O-sulfo-beta-D-glucopyranosyl-(1->3)-beta-D-galactopyranosyl groups, respectively. It is an oligosaccharide sulfate, an amino pentasaccharide and a member of acetamides.
