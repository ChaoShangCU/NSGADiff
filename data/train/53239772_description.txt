The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid having an ent-dammarane skeleton with a double bond at C-24 and a Z-double bond at C-17(20). It has a role as a metabolite. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and a 3beta-hydroxy-4,4-dimethylsteroid. It derives from a hydride of a protostane.
