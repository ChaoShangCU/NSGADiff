The molecule is a member of the class of biphenyls that is the amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 2'-methyl-5'-(5-methyl-1,3,4-oxadiazol-2-yl)[1,1'-biphenyl]-4-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 3-cyanoaniline. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzamides, a member of biphenyls, a member of 1,3,4-oxadiazoles and a nitrile.
