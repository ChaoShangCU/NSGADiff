The molecule is a member of the class of dibenzofurans that is candidusin A substituted by a prenyl group at position 3 of the phenyl ring substituent. It has been isolated from Aspergillus taichungensis. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an Aspergillus metabolite. It is an aromatic ether, a member of catechols and a member of dibenzofurans. It derives from a candidusin A.
