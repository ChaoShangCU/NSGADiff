The molecule is a member of the class of morpholines that is morpholine-2,5-dione substituted by a benzyl, isopropyl and a methyl group at positions 3, 6 and 4 respectively. It is isolated from the culture broth of the fungus Beauveria bassiana and acts as a platelet aggregation inhibitor. It has a role as a metabolite, an antimicrobial agent and a platelet aggregation inhibitor. It is a member of morpholines and a diketone.
