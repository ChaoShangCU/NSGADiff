The molecule is an amino cyclitol glycoside that is 2-deoxystreptamine in which the pro-R hydroxy group is substituted by a 6-dehydro-alpha-D-glucosyl residue. It is an amino cyclitol glycoside and an aldehyde. It derives from a 2-deoxystreptamine. It is a conjugate acid of a 2'-deamino-2'-hydroxy-6'-dehydroparomamine(2+).
