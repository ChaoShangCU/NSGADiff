The molecule is a 3-oxo steroid that is cholic acid in which the hydroxy group at position 3 has undergone formal oxidation to the corresponding ketone. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a bile acid, a 12alpha-hydroxy steroid, a dihydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid, a 7alpha-hydroxy steroid and a 3-oxo-5beta-steroid. It is a conjugate acid of a 7alpha,12alpha-dihydroxy-3-oxo-5beta-cholan-24-oate.
