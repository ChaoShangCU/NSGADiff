The molecule is a lignan that is 3,4-dihydronaphthalen-1(2H)-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 7, a methoxy group at position 6 and methyl groups at positions 2 and 3 (the 2R,3S-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the bark of Machilus robusta. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of tetralins, a lignan and a member of guaiacols.
