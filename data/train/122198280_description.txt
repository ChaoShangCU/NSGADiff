The molecule is a leukotriene that is (5S,7E,9E,11Z)-5-hydroxyicosa-7,9,11-trienoic acid in which a glutathionyl group is attached at position 6 via a sulfide linkage. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a rat metabolite. It is a leukotriene, a glutathione derivative and an organic sulfide. It is a conjugate acid of a leukotriene C3(2-).
