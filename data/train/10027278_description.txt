The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazoles that is N-[4-(pyrazol-1-yl)phenyl]glycinamide in which the pyrazole ring is substituted at positions 3 and 5 by trifluoromethyl and phenanthrene-2-yl groups respectively. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.1 (non-specific serine/threonine protein kinase) inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an apoptosis inducer. It is a member of pyrazoles, a member of phenanthrenes, an organofluorine compound, a glycine derivative, an aromatic amide and an antibiotic antifungal drug.
