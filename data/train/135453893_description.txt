The molecule is a member of the class of benzochromenones that is 4H-benzo[g]chromen-4-one carrying a methyl group at position 2 and a hydroxy group at positions 5, 6 and 8, respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a benzochromenone, a member of phenols, a naphtho-gamma-pyrone and a heptaketide. It is a conjugate acid of a norrubrofusarin(1-).
