The molecule is a limonoid that is 20,21,22,23-tetrahydroazadirone substituted by an additional oxo group at position 23. It has been isolated from Azadirachta indica. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a limonoid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and a butan-4-olide. It derives from an azadirone.
