The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (13R,14S)-dihydroxy-(4Z,7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (13R,14S)-dihydroxy-(4Z,7Z,9E,11E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid.
