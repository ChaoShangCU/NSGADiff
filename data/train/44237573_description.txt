The molecule is a butanoate ester of egonol isolated from the fruits of Styrax agrestis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, an aromatic ether, a member of benzodioxoles, a fatty acid ester and a butyrate ester. It derives from an egonol. It derives from a hydride of a 1-benzofuran.
