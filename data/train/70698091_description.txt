The molecule is a member of the class of pterocarpans that is (6aR,11aR)-pterocarpan substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 9, a methoxy group at position 1 and a prenyl group at position 2. It has been isolated from Glycyrrhiza uralensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a polyphenol, a member of pterocarpans and an aromatic ether. It derives from a (6aR,11aR)-pterocarpan.
