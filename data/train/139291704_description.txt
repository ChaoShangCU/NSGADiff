The molecule is an epoxy fatty acid that is (6E,8E,10Z,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosa-6,8,10,13,15,19-hexaenoic acid which is carrying an epoxy group at position 4S and a hydroxy group at position 17R. It is a metabolite of docosahexaenoic acid that can be converted to a D-resolvin. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and an epoxy fatty acid.
