The molecule is a hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 12-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 12-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 12-hydroxyoctadecanoyl-CoA(4-).
