The molecule is a phthalate ester that is the mixed diester obtained by the formal condensation of carboxy groups of phthalic acid with hydroxy groups of 2,4,6-tribromophenol and prop-2-yn-1-ol respectively. It is an organobromine compound, a phthalate ester and a terminal acetylenic compound. It derives from a 2,4,6-tribromophenol and a prop-2-yn-1-ol.
