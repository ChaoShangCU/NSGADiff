The molecule is a nucleotide-sugar oxoanion resutling from the deprotonation of both free OH groups of the diphosphate group of ADP alpha-D-glucoside. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a NDP-alpha-D-glucose(2-) and a ribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate-alpha-D-glucose(2-). It is a conjugate base of an ADP alpha-D-glucoside.
