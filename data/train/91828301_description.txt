The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3S,7Z)-3-hydroxyhexadecenoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain (3S)-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (3S,7Z)-3-hydroxyhexadecenoyl-CoA.
