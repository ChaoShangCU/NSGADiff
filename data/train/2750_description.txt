The molecule is an aromatic ether that consists of 1,3-thiazolidine-2,4-dione with position 5 substituted by a 4-[(1-methylcyclohexyl)methoxy]benzyl group. A selective PPARgamma agonist. It has a role as an insulin-sensitizing drug and an antineoplastic agent. It is a thiazolidinone and an aromatic ether.
