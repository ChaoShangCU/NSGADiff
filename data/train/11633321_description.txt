The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid that is (3beta)-stigmasta-4,6-diene-3-ol with an additional hydroxy group at position 22 (the 22R stereoisomer). Isolated from the whole plants of Haloxylon recurvum, it exhibits chymotrypsin inhibitory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.4.21.1 (chymotrypsin) inhibitor. It is a 22-hydroxy steroid and a 3beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a hydride of a stigmastane.
