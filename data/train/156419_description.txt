The molecule is a secondary amino compound that is (1R)-1-(naphthalen-1-yl)ethanamine in which one of the hydrogens attached to the nitrogen is substituted by a 3-[3-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl]propyl group. It has a role as a calcimimetic and a P450 inhibitor. It is a member of naphthalenes, a secondary amino compound and a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes.
