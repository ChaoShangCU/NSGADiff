The molecule is a diester obtained by the formal condensation of gallic acid with the hydroxy groups at positions 7 and 4' of 3',4',5',5,7-pentahydroxyflavan. Isolated from the leaves of Pithecellobium clypearia, it exhibits antiviral activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antiviral agent. It is a diester, a gallate ester and a trihydroxyflavan. It derives from a flavan.
