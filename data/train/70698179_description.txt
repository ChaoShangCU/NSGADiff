The molecule is a flavone C-glycoside that is 5,7,4'-trihydroxy-3'-methoxyflavone substituted by a 2-O-(6-deoxy-alpha-L-mannopyranosyl)-beta-D-glucopyranosyl residue at position 8. It has been isolated from the leaves and roots of Petrorhagia velutina. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a flavone C-glycoside, a trihydroxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative.
