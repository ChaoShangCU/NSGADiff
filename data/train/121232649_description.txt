The molecule is an indolyl carbohydrate that is indole-3-carboxylic acid in which the hydrogen at position 5 is replaced by a 6-O-malonyl-beta-D-glucosyloxy group. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an indolyl carbohydrate, an indolyl carboxylic acid, a dicarboxylic acid and a beta-D-glucoside. It derives from an indole-3-carboxylic acid.
