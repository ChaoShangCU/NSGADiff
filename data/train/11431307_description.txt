The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is lanost-8-en-21-oic acid substituted by a methylidene group at position 24 and an oxo group at position 3. It has been isolated from Poria cocos. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid and a cyclic terpene ketone. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
