The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid that is benzoic acid in which the hydrogen at position 3 is replaced by a (1-carboxyvinyl)oxy group. It is a member of benzoic acids, a dicarboxylic acid, an aromatic ether and an enol ether. It derives from an acrylic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-[(1-carboxylatovinyl)oxy]benzoate(2-).
