The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone that is flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 7, 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-2-en-1-yl group at positions 6 and a 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-1-en-3-yl group at position 8. It has been isolated as a racemate from the bark of Cryptocarya chartacea and exhibits inhibitory activity against dengue virus NS5 polymerase. It has a role as an antiviral agent and a plant metabolite. It is a dicarboxylic acid and a dihydroxyflavanone.
