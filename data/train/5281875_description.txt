The molecule is a member of the class of hydrazones that is used as an insecticide for control of ants and cockroaches. It has a role as a mitochondrial cytochrome-bc1 complex inhibitor and an insecticide. It is a hydrazone, a member of pyrimidines, a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes, an olefinic compound and a member of guanidines.
