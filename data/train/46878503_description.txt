The molecule is the hexaanion of coenzyme alpha-F420-3 arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid and phosphate functions as well as the 3-position on the pyrimidinoquinoline ring system; major species at pH 7.3. It is a ribitol phosphate and a tetracarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a coenzyme alpha-F420-3(5-).
