The molecule is an octadecatrienoyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of alpha-linolenic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is an alpha-linolenoyl bioconjugate and an octadecatrienoyl-CoA. It derives from an alpha-linolenic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an alpha-linolenoyl-CoA(4-).
