The molecule is a 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-monophosphate(2-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of 2'-deoxyinosine 5'-monophosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 2'-deoxynucleoside 5'-monophosphate(2-) and a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-phosphate(2-). It is a conjugate base of a 2'-deoxyinosine-5'-monophosphate.
