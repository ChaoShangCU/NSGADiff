The molecule is a sesquiterpene alkaloid that is isolated from the stem of Tripterygium regelii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a sesquiterpene alkaloid, a benzoate ester, an organic heterotricyclic compound, an oxacycle, a bridged compound, a tertiary alcohol, a pyridine alkaloid and a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid. It derives from a nicotinic acid.
