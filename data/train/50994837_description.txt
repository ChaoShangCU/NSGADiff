The molecule is a beta-D-glucoside in which the anomeric position is substituted by a 4-hydroxyphenoxy residue and the positions 2 and 6 are substituted by [(2Z)-3-(2-hydroxy-5-methoxyphenyl)prop-2-enoyl]oxy residues. Isolated from Grevillea, it exhibits antimalarial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an enoate ester, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether.
