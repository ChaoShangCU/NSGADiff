The molecule is a cembrane diterpenoid that is an epimer of sinularolide B at C-3. Isolated from Sinularia gibberosa and has been found to exhibit antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a gamma-lactone, a cembrane diterpenoid, an epoxide, a macrocycle, a primary alcohol and a secondary alcohol.
