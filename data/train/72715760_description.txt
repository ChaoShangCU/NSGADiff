The molecule is a diterpenoid that is isopimar-7-ene substituted by a carboxy group at position 19 and a hydroxy group at position 16. It has been isolated from the fungus, Xylaria species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a diterpenoid and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a hydride of an isopimarane.
