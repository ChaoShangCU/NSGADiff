The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is oleana-11,13(18)-diene substituted by an alpha-hydroxy group at position 3 and a carboxy group at position 28. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Fatsia polycarpa. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
