The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of omega-hydroxytetracosanoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acid anion and a very long-chain fatty acid anion. It derives from a tetracosanoate. It is a conjugate acid of an omega-hydroxytetracosanoic acid.
