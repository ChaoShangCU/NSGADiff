The molecule is a secondary carboxamide resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-isopropoxybenzoic acid with the primary amino group of 2-methylquinolin-8-amine. An allosteric activator of sarco/endoplasmic reticulum Ca(2+)-ATPase (SERCA). It has a role as a SERCA activator. It is a member of quinolines, a secondary carboxamide and an aromatic ether.
