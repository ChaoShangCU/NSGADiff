The molecule is a sulfonamide that is sulfanilamide acylated on the sulfonamide nitrogen. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, an antiinfective agent, an EC 2.5.1.15 (dihydropteroate synthase) inhibitor and an antibacterial drug. It is a substituted aniline and a N-sulfonylcarboxamide. It derives from a sulfanilamide. It is a conjugate acid of a sulfacetamide(1-).
