The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (3S)-hydroxyoctadecanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (S)-3-hydroxyoctadecanoyl-CoA.
