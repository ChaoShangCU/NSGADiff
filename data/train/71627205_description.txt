The molecule is an organic heterohexacyclic compound and spirooxindole-type pyranopyrimidine spiro compound in which the shared atom of the spiro system is the carbon at position 3 of 1-allyl-7-fluoro-1,3-dihydro-2H-indol-2-one. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent. It is an organic heterohexacyclic compound, an organofluorine compound, a spiro compound and a member of oxindoles.
