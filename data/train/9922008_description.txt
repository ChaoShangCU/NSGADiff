The molecule is a member of the class of benzodioxoles that is 1,3-benzodioxole in which the hydrogen at position 5 has been replaced by a dodec-1-en-1-yl group. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an EC 3.2.1.20 (alpha-glucosidase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzodioxoles and an olefinic compound.
