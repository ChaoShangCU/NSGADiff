The molecule is an oxime O-ether that is the (Z)-oxime derivative of 1-methyl-1H-tetrazol-5-yl phenyl ketone in which the hydrogen of the hydroxy group has been replaced by the N-tert-butoxycarbonyl derivative of a [6-(Boc-amino)pyridin-2-yl]methyl group. It is a novel fungicide developed for use on cereals. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a carbamate ester, a member of tetrazoles, a member of pyridines, an oxime O-ether and a carbamate fungicide.
