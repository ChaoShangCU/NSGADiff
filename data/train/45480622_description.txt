The molecule is the dianion of the fragment of coenzyme F420 remaining after formal hydrolytic removal of all of the glutamate residues. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion, a dialkyl phosphate anion, a ribitol phosphate, a rhamnonate and a member of pyrimidoquinolines. It derives from a 2-phospho-L-lactic acid. It is a conjugate base of a F420-0.
