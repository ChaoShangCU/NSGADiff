The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid that is ecdysone in which the hydroxy group at position 25 is replaced by a hydrogen. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, a 2beta-hydroxy steroid, a 14alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 22-hydroxy steroid, a 6-oxo steroid and an enone. It derives from an ecdysone. It derives from a hydride of a 5beta-cholestane.
