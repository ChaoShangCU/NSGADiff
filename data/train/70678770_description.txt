The molecule is a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic isolated from Actinomadura sp. MK73-NF4. It specifically inhibits the growth of gram-positive bacteria including multi-drug resistant strains such as Staphylococcus aureus MS9610 and menthicilin-resistant S.aureus (MRSA). It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is an organochlorine compound, a gamma-lactone, an amide, a carbohydrate-containing antibiotic, a macrocycle, a monocarboxylic acid, an oxaspiro compound, an enol, a trideoxyhexose derivative and a member of pyrroles.
