The molecule is a monohydroxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by a hydroxy group at position 5, a methoxy group at position 7 and methyl groups at positions 6 and 8. It has been isolated from the buds of Cleistocalyx operculatus. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a monohydroxyflavanone and a monomethoxyflavanone. It derives from a (2S)-flavanone.
