The molecule is an oxysterol that is cholesterol which is substituted by hydroxy groups at 4beta and 26 positions and has R-configuration at position 25. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a 3beta-sterol, a 4-hydroxy steroid, a cholestanoid, an oxysterol, a 26-hydroxy steroid and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid.
