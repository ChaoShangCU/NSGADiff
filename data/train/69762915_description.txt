The molecule is a cholestanoid that is desmosterol carrying an additional hydroxy susbstituent at position 26. It has a role as a mouse metabolite and a human metabolite. It is a cholestanoid, a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid, a 26-hydroxy steroid, a primary allylic alcohol and a C27-steroid. It derives from a desmosterol.
