The molecule is a indolocarbazole alkaloid that is 4'-N-demethyl staurosporine substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 8 and 9 and an xo group at position 7. Isolated from Cystodytes solitus, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an indolocarbazole, an indolocarbazole alkaloid, an organic heterooctacyclic compound and a member of phenols. It derives from a staurosporine.
