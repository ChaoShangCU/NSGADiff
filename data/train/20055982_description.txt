The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C24H39NO6, originally isolated from Aconitum forrestii. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound, a triol and a diterpene alkaloid. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
