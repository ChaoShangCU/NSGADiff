The molecule is a linear amino tetrasaccharide comprised of N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminyl, beta-D-galactosyl, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl and N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine residues linked sequentially (2->3), (1->3) and (1->6). It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
