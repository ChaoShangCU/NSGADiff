The molecule is a linear amino hexasaccharide comprised of beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, alpha-D-mannose, beta-D-mannose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and N-acetyl-D-glucosamine residues linked sequentially (1->4), (1->2), (1->6), (1->4) and (1->4). It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino hexasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
