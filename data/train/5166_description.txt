The molecule is a member of the class of imidazoles carrying 4-hydroxycyclohexyl, 4-fluorophenyl and 2-methoxypyrimidin-4-yl substituents at positions 1, 4 and 5 respectively. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of cyclohexanols, a member of imidazoles, a member of pyrimidines, an aromatic ether, a member of monofluorobenzenes and a secondary alcohol.
