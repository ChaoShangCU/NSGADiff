The molecule is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA having 2-hydroxyphytanoyl as the S-acyl group. It is a hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA, a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA, a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a phytanoyl-CoA and a 2-hydroxyphytanic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxyphytanoyl-CoA(4-).
