The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is 6H-furo[3,2-c]xanthen-6-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 7 and 10, 2-methoxypropan-2-yl group at position 2 and a 2-methylbut-3-en-2-yl group at position 8. Isolated from the woods of Garcinia subelliptica, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a cyclic ether, a polyphenol, a cyclic ketone and an organic heterotetracyclic compound.
