The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone isolated from Ixeris chinensis and has been shown to exhibit cytotoxic activity against human PC-3 tumor cells. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a sesquiterpene lactone, a carboxylic ester, a member of phenols and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a 4-hydroxyphenylacetic acid.
