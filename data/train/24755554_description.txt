The molecule is an N-acyl amino acid that is alanine substituted by a 2,6-dichlorobenzoyl group at the N and a 2-phenoxy-6-quinolyl group at position 3. It is a member of quinolines, a dichlorobenzene, a non-proteinogenic amino acid derivative, an aromatic ether and a N-acyl-amino acid. It contains a 2,6-dichlorobenzoyl group.
