The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is octacosanoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 13, 16, 19, 22 and 25 (the 13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and an octacosapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (13Z,16Z,19Z,22Z,25Z)-octacosapentaenoate.
