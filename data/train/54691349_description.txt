The molecule is a hydroxycoumarin that is novobiocin lacking the 3-O-carbamoyl and 4-O-methyl groups from the hexose ring. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a member of benzamides, a hexoside, a hydroxycoumarin and a monosaccharide derivative. It is a conjugate acid of a desmethyldescarbamoylnovobiocin(1-).
