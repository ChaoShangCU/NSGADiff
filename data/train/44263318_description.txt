The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is 5alpha-cholest-8-en-3beta-ol carrying an additional carboxy substituent at position 4alpha. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a 3beta-sterol, a cholestanoid and a steroid acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3beta-hydroxy-5alpha-cholest-8-ene-4alpha-carboxylate.
