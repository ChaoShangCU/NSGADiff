The molecule is a xanthone glycoside that is 9H-xanthene substituted by a hydroxy group at position 3, methyl groups at positions 6 and 7, an oxo group at position 9 and a beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy group at position 1. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a member of phenols, a xanthone glycoside and a monosaccharide derivative.
