The molecule is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion arising from deprotonation of both carboxy groups of N-acetyl-L-2-aminoadipic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a N-acetyl-L-2-aminoadipic acid.
