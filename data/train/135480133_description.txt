The molecule is a guanosine 5'-phosphate that is the N(2),N(2),N(7)-trimethyl derivative of guanosine 5'-monophosphate. It is a guanosine 5'-phosphate and an ammonium betaine. It derives from a guanosine 5'-monophosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a N(2),N(2),N(7)-trimethylguanosine 5'-phosphate(1-).
