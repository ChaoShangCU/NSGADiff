The molecule is an oxysterol that is cholesterol substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 17 and 20 (the 20R-stereoisomer). It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 20-hydroxy steroid, an oxysterol and a 3beta-hydroxy-Delta(5)-steroid. It derives from a cholesterol.
