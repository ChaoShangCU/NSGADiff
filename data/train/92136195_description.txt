The molecule is a linear amino pentasaccharide comprising a (1->4), (1->3), (1->4), (1->3) sequence of beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and (at the reducing end) N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine. It is an amino pentasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
