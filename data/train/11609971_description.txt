The molecule is an organic heteropentacyclic compound that is 2H-pyrano[3',4',5'',6'']-pterocarpene substituted by a hydroxy group at position 7, a methoxy group at position 5 and geminal methyl groups at position 2''. Isolated from the roots of Glycyrrhiza uralensis, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is an organic heteropentacyclic compound, an ether and a member of phenols.
