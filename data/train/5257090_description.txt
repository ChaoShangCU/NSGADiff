The molecule is an alkaloid that is beta-carboline substituted at position C-1 by a 2-aminopyrimidin-4-yl moiety. An antiparasitic agent found in Annona montana and Annona foteida. It has a role as a metabolite and an antiparasitic agent. It is an alkaloid, a member of beta-carbolines and an aminopyrimidine. It derives from a beta-carboline and a pyrimidin-2-amine.
