The molecule is an N-arylpiperazine that consists of piperazine carrying two 4-substituted phenyl groups at positions 1 and 4. A triazole antifungal drug. It has a role as a trypanocidal drug. It is a member of triazoles, a N-arylpiperazine, an organofluorine compound, a member of oxolanes, an aromatic ether, a conazole antifungal drug and a triazole antifungal drug.
