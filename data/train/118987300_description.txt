The molecule is a member of the class of azabicycloalkanes that is 1,3-diazabicyclo[2.2.0]hex-4-en-2-one substituted at position 6 by a 5-amino-5-methyl-2,4-dioxodihydropyrimidin-6-yl group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a pyrimidone, an aminopyrimidine and an azabicycloalkane.
