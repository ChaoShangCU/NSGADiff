The molecule is an aromatic ether that is diphenyl ether substituted by a 2-hydroxy-1-(1H-1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)propan-2-yl group at position 1, trifluoromethyl group at position 2 and a chloro group at position 4. It is a member of monochlorobenzenes, a member of triazoles, a tertiary alcohol, an aromatic ether and a member of (trifluoromethyl)benzenes.
