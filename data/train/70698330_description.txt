The molecule is a linear amino pentasaccharide comprising N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminyl, beta-D-galactosyl, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl, beta-D-galactosyl and N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine residues linked in a (2->3), (1->4), (1->3), (1->3) sequence. It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino pentasaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
