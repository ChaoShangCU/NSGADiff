The molecule is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate having adenine as the nucleobase. It has a role as a human metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a purine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside 5'-diphosphate and a 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a dADP(3-).
