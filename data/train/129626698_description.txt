The molecule is a metalloporphyrin having nickel as the central metal atom; an intermediate in the biosynthesis of coenzyme F430 in methanogenic bacteria. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a metalloporphyrin and a nickel coordination entity. It derives from a sirohydrochlorin. It is a conjugate acid of a 15,17(3)-seco-F430-17(3)-acid(6-).
