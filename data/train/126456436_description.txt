The molecule is a brassinosteroid that is brassinolide carrying an alpha-D-glucosyl residue at position O-23. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an alpha-D-glucoside, a 2alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 22-hydroxy steroid, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid, a brassinosteroid and a steroid saponin. It derives from a brassinolide.
