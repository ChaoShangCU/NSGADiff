The molecule is a C20 hydroxy fatty acid having (5S)- and (6R)- -hydroxy groups as well as a 15-oxo group and (7E)- (9E)-, (11Z)- and (13E)-double bonds. It is a lipoxin, an oxo fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 15-oxolipoxin A4(1-).
