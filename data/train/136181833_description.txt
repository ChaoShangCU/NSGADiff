The molecule is trianion of N(5)-formyl-5,6,7,8-tetrahydromethanopterin arising from deprotonation of the carboxy and phosphate groups; major species at pH 7.3. It is a dicarboxylic acid dianion and an organophosphate oxoanion. It is a conjugate base of a N(5)-formyl-5,6,7,8-tetrahydromethanopterin.
