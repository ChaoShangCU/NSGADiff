The molecule is a member of the class of benzamides that is entinostat in which the hydrogen that is para to the nitrogen of the benzamide moiety has been replaced by a fluorine. It has a role as an EC 3.5.1.98 (histone deacetylase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzamides, a carbamate ester, a primary amino compound, a member of pyridines, a substituted aniline and a member of monofluorobenzenes. It derives from an entinostat.
