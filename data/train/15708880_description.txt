The molecule is an androstanoid that is testosterone substituted by a beta-hydroxy group at position 16. A natural product found in Daphnia magna exposed to the biocide tributyltin. It has a role as an androgen and a Daphnia magna metabolite. It is a 17beta-hydroxy steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, an androstanoid and a 16beta-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a testosterone.
