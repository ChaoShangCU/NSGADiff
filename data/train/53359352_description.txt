The molecule is a monohydroxybenzoic acid that is 2-hydroxybenzoic acid substituted by a 2-(4-hydroxyphenyl)-2-oxoethyl group at position 6. It has been isolated from the roots of Scorzonera judaica. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a monohydroxybenzoic acid, a member of phenols and an aromatic ketone.
