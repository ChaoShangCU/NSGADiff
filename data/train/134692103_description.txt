The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid that is (3beta,5alpha)-4,4,14-trimethylandrost-8-en-3-ol which is substituted by a (3E,6S)-2-methoxy-2-methylhept-3-en-6-yl group at position 17. It has a role as an antifungal agent and a fungal metabolite. It is a tetracyclic triterpenoid, a 3beta-hydroxy steroid and an ether. It derives from a hydride of a lanostane.
