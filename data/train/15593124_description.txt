The molecule is a gallate ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of gallic acid with the (3'R)-hydroxy group of procyanidin B2. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a proanthocyanidin, a gallate ester, a polyphenol and a biflavonoid. It derives from a gallic acid and a procyanidin B2.
