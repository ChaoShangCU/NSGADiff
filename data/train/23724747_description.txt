The molecule is a disaccharide derivative that is trans-chalcone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 4, 2' and 6' and a 2-O-(6-deoxy-alpha-L-mannopyranosyl)-beta-D-glucopyranosyloxy group at position 4' respectively. It is a member of chalcones, a member of resorcinols and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a trans-chalcone.
