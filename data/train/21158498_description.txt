The molecule is conjugate base of (11S)-11-hydroperoxylinoleic acid arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function. It is a hydroperoxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and an octadecanoid anion. It is a conjugate base of an (11S)-11-hydroperoxylinoleic acid. It is an enantiomer of an (11R)-11-hydroperoxylinoleate.
