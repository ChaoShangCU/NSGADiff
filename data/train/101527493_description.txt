The molecule is a fatty acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of octadecanoic acid with the hydroxy group of 13-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a fatty acid ester and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a 13-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid and an octadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 13-(octadecanoyloxy)octadecanoate.
