The molecule is a methoxyisoflavone that is isoflavone substituted by a methoxy group at position 6, a methyl group at position 8 and hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 2'. It has been isolated from Pisonia aculeata. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a member of 2'-hydroxyisoflavones, a member of 7-hydroxyisoflavones and a methoxyisoflavone. It derives from an isoflavone.
