The molecule is a hydroxycoumarin antibiotic with formula C34H35ClN2O11, that is produced by Streptomyces roseochromogenes It has a role as an antimicrobial agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is a hexoside, a hydroxycoumarin, a monosaccharide derivative, a member of benzamides, an organochlorine compound, a member of pyrroles, an ether and a member of phenols.
