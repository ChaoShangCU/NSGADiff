The molecule is a member of the class of prostaglandins J that is prosta-5,9,12-trien-1-oic acid carrying oxo and hydsroxy substituents at positions 11 and 15 respectively (the 5Z,12E,15S-stereoisomer). It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an antiviral agent. It is a prostaglandins J and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a prostaglandin J2. It is a conjugate acid of a 13,14-dihydro-Delta(12)-prostaglandin J2(1-).
