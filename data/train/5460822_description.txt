The molecule is an optically active form of tyrosinate having L-configuration. It has a role as a fundamental metabolite. It is a tyrosinate(1-) and a L-alpha-amino acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a L-tyrosine and a L-tyrosine zwitterion. It is a conjugate acid of a L-tyrosinate(2-). It is an enantiomer of a D-tyrosinate(1-).
