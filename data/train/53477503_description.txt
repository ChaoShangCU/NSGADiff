The molecule is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid that is (4Z,9E,11E,13Z,15E,19Z)-docosa-4,9,11,13,15,19-hexaenoic acid carrying an epoxy group at position 7S and a hydroxy group at position 17R. It is a metabolite of docosahexaenoic acid, and serves as a precursor to D-resolvins. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a hydroxydocosahexaenoic acid and an epoxy fatty acid.
