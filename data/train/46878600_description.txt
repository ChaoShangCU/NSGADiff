The molecule is the dianion obtained by removal of a proton from both carboxyic acid groups of 4-(4-deoxy-alpha-D-gluc-4-enosyluronic acid)-D-galacturonic acid. It is a dicarboxylic acid dianion and a carbohydrate acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 4-(4-deoxy-alpha-D-gluc-4-enosyluronic acid)-D-galacturonic acid.
