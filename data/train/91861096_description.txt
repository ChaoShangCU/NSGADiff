The molecule is an oligosaccharide sulfate consisting of 2-O-sulfo-beta-L-threo-hex-4-enopyranuronose and 2-deoxy-2-(sulfoamino)-beta-D-glucopyranose joined in sequence by a (1->4) glycosidic bond. It is a disaccharide, a member of sulfamic acids, an oligosaccharide sulfate, a monocarboxylic acid and an enol.
