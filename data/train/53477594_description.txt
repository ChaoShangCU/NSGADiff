The molecule is a glycoside that consists of alpha-D-galactosyl-(1->4)-beta-D-galactosyl-(1->4)-beta-D-glucose having a 2-(2-aminocarbonylethylthio)ethyl moiety attached to the reducing end anomeric centre. It is a glycoside, an aliphatic sulfide, a trisaccharide derivative and a monocarboxylic acid amide.
