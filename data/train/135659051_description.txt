The molecule is a guanosine 5'-phosphate that consists of guanosine 5'-diphosphate bearing a 7-methyl substituent and having a negatively charged diphosphate group. It is an ammonium betaine and a guanosine 5'-phosphate. It derives from a GDP. It is a conjugate base of a 7-methylguanosine 5'-diphosphate(1+). It is a conjugate acid of a 7-methylguanosine 5'-diphosphate(2-).
