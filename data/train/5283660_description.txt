The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that is campesterol in which position 22 has been dehydrogenated to introduce a double bond. It is a phytosterol found in marine algae, mussels, crinoids, mites and plants. It has a role as an algal metabolite, a biomarker, a plant metabolite, an animal metabolite and a marine metabolite. It is a 3beta-sterol, an ergostanoid and a member of phytosterols. It derives from a campesterol.
