The molecule is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside derived from (2E,9R)-9-hydroxydec-2-enoic acid. It is a metabolite of the nematode Caenorhabditis elegans. It has a role as a Caenorhabditis elegans metabolite. It is a 4-O-(1H-indol-3-ylcarbonyl)ascaroside, an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid ascaroside. It derives from an ascr#15 and a (2E,9R)-9-hydroxydec-2-enoic acid.
