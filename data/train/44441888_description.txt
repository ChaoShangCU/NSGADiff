The molecule is a 3-hydroxy-fatty acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 3-hydroxyoctanoic acid. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA. It derives from a 3-hydroxyoctanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 3-hydroxyoctanoyl-CoA(4-).
