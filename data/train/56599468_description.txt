The molecule is a hopanoid that is hopane substituted by an acetoxy group at position 3 and hydroxy groups at positions 7, 15 and 22 respectively (the 3beta,7beta,15alpha-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from Hypocrella species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a hopanoid, an acetate ester, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a triol.
