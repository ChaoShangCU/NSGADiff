The molecule is a C-nitro compound consisting of 2-nitroethene-1,1-diamine where one of the nitrogens bears ethyl and (6-chloro-3-pyridinyl)methyl while the other nitrogen carries a methyl group. It has a role as a neonicotinoid insectide. It is a C-nitro compound and a monochloropyridine. It derives from a 2-chloropyridine.
