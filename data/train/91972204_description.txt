The molecule is a linear amino tetrasaccharide consisting of N-acetyl-alpha-neuraminyl, beta-D-galactosyl, N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl and N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine residues linked sequentially (2->3), (1->4) and (1->6). It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
