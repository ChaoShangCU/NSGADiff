The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (10R,17S)-dihydroxy-(7Z,11E,13E,15Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a docosanoid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (10R,17S)-dihydroxy-(7Z,11E,13E,15Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid.
