The molecule is a three-membered deoxy oligosaccharide which has a 2-acetamido-2,3-dideoxy-D-ribo-hexose unit at the reducing end with an alpha-D-galactosyl-(1->3)-beta-D-galactosyl group attached at the 4-position. It is an amino trisaccharide, a deoxy oligosaccharide derivative and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
