The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid anion arising from deprotonation of both carboxylic acid functions of (6R)-5,10-methenyltetrahydrofolic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite and a Saccharomyces cerevisiae metabolite. It is a dicarboxylic acid anion and a tetrahydrofolate. It is a conjugate base of a (6R)-5,10-methenyltetrahydrofolic acid.
