The molecule is a 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 2-hydroxy-6-oxocyclohexane-1-carbonyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3-oxo-fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 2-hydroxy-6-oxocyclohexane-1-carbonyl-CoA.
