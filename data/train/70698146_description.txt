The molecule is a member of the class of pterocarpans that is (6aR,11aR)-pterocarpan substituted by a hydroxy group at position 9, methoxy groups at position 1 and 3 and a prenyl group at position 2. It has been isolated from Glycyrrhiza uralensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of phenols and an aromatic ether. It derives from a (6aR,11aR)-pterocarpan.
