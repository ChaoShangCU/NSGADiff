The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (9Z,12Z)-hexadeca-9,12,15-trienoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (9Z,12Z)-hexadeca-9,12,15-trienoyl-CoA.
