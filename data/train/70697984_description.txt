The molecule is a monosaccharide derivative of platensimycin isolated from Streptomyces platensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, a polycyclic cage, a member of phenols, a monosaccharide derivative, a monocarboxylic acid, an aromatic amide and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from a platensimycin.
