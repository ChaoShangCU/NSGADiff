The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is triacontanoic acid having four double bonds located at positions 15, 18, 21 and 24 (the 15Z,18Z,21Z,24Z-isomer). It is an omega-6 fatty acid and a triacontatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (15Z,18Z,21Z,24Z)-triacontatetraenoate.
