The molecule is a polycyclic cage that is the methyl ester derivative of 7-hydroxy substituted platensimycin. It is isolated from Streptomyces platensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ether, a cyclic ketone, a polycyclic cage, a member of resorcinols, a tertiary alcohol, a benzoate ester, an aromatic amide and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from a platensimycin.
