The molecule is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid that is (9Z)-octadec-9-enoic acid (oleic acid) in which a hydrogen at position 17 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is an (omega-1)-hydroxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid and a hydroxy monounsaturated fatty acid. It derives from an oleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (9Z)-17-hydroxyoctadec-9-enoate.
