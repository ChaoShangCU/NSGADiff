The molecule is a quercetin O-glycoside that is quercetin attached to a alpha-L-fucopyranosyl moiety at position 3 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monosaccharide derivative, a tetrahydroxyflavone, an alpha-L-fucoside and a quercetin O-glycoside. It derives from an alpha-L-fucose.
