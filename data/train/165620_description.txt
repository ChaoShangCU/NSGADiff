The molecule is an acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of phenylacetic acid. It has a role as a metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It derives from a phenylacetic acid and an acetyl-CoA. It is a conjugate acid of a phenylacetyl-CoA(4-).
