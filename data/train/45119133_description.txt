The molecule is a member of the class of monohydroxy-1,4-benzoquinones that is 1,4-benzoquinone which is substituted by a chloro group and hydroxy group at positions 2 and 6, respectively. It is an organochlorine compound and a member of monohydroxy-1,4-benzoquinones. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-chloro-6-hydroxy-1,4-benzoquinone(1-).
