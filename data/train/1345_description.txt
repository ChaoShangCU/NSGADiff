The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 1-(2-chlorophenyl)isoquinoline-3-carboxylic acid with the amino group of sec-butylmethylamine It has a role as an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of isoquinolines, a monocarboxylic acid amide, an aromatic amide and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
