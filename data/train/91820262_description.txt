The molecule is a member of the class of anthracyclines that is beta-rhodomycin lacking the phenolic hydroxy group at position 11. It is an aminoglycoside, an anthracycline, a deoxy hexoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol and a member of p-quinones. It is a tautomer of an 11-deoxy-beta-rhodomycin zwitterion.
