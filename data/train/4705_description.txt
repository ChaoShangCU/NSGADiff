The molecule is a member of the class of quinazolines carrying a 3-bromophenylamino substituent at position 4 and two methoxy substituents at positions 6 and 7. It has a role as an EC 2.7.10.1 (receptor protein-tyrosine kinase) inhibitor and an epidermal growth factor receptor antagonist. It is a member of quinazolines, an aromatic amine, a secondary amino compound, a member of bromobenzenes and an aromatic ether. It is a conjugate base of a PD-153035(1+).
