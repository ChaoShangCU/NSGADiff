The molecule is a biflavonoid that is the 7,4',7''-trimethyl ether derivative of robustaflavone. Isolated from Selaginella doederleinii, it exhibits cytotoxic activity against human cancer cell lines. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a biflavonoid, a hydroxyflavone, a methoxyflavone and a ring assembly. It derives from a robustaflavone.
