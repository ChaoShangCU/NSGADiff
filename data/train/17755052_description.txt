The molecule is a sulfonamide composed of indazole, morpholine, and methylsulfonyl-substituted piperazine rings bound to a thienopyrimidine ring. It has a role as an EC 2.7.1.137 (phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase) inhibitor. It is a sulfonamide, a member of piperazines, a member of morpholines, a member of indazoles and a thienopyrimidine.
