The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid that is (5Z,8Z,10E)-icosatrienoic acid in which the hydroperoxy group is located at the 12(S)-position. It is a hydroperoxy fatty acid, a long-chain fatty acid, a polyunsaturated fatty acid and an icosanoid. It derives from a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-icosatrienoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 12(S)-HPE(5,8,10)TrE(1-).
