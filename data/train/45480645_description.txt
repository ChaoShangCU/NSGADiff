The molecule is the tri-anion obtained by removing protons from the phosphate and carboxylic acid groups of coenzyme F420-1. It has a role as a coenzyme. It is a dialkyl phosphate anion, a member of pyrimidoquinolines, a ribitol phosphate and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It derives from a 7,8-didemethyl-8-hydroxy-5-deazariboflavin. It is a conjugate base of a coenzyme F420-1. It is a conjugate acid of a coenzyme F420-1(4-).
