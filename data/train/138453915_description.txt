The molecule is a member of the class of 1,3-benzoxazoles that is 5-methyl-2-phenyl-1,3-benzoxazole which is substituted by a [(4-chlorophenyl)carbamothioyl]nitrilo group at position 5. It is a bacterial glutamate racemase inhibitor. It has a role as an EC 5.1.1.* (racemases acting on amino acids and derivatives) inhibitor. It is a member of 1,3-benzoxazoles, a member of thioureas and a member of monochlorobenzenes.
