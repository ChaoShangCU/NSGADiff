The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is lup-20(29)-ene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 3 respectively (the 1beta,3alpha-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from Breynia fruticosa. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid and a diol. It derives from a hydride of a lupane.
