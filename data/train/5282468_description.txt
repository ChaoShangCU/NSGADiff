The molecule is a racemate comprising equimolar amounts of (R)- and (S)-tosufloxacin tosylate hydrate. It has a role as an antimicrobial agent, an antiinfective agent, a DNA synthesis inhibitor, a hepatotoxic agent and a topoisomerase IV inhibitor. It contains a (S)-tosufloxacin tosylate hydrate, a (R)-tosufloxacin tosylate hydrate and a tosufloxacin tosylate.
