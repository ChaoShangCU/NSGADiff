The molecule is an anionic phospholipid obtained by deprotonation of the free carboxy group of 1-O-hexadecyl-2-(8-carboxyoctanoyl)-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine; major species at pH 7.3. It is an anionic phospholipid and a monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 1-O-hexadecyl-2-(8-carboxyoctanoyl)-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine.
