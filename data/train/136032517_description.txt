The molecule is a pterin phosphate that is 7,8-dihydroneopterin carrying a single monophosphate substituent at position 2'. It is a dihydropterin, a member of neopterins and a pterin phosphate. It derives from a 7,8-dihydroneopterin. It is a conjugate acid of a 7,8-dihydroneopterin 2'-phosphate(2-).
