The molecule is a member of the class of piperidines that is 2-methylpiperidine in which the hydrogen attached to the nitrogen is replaced by a 3-[(3,4-dichlorobenzoyl)oxy]propyl group. It is a member of piperidines, a tertiary amino compound, a benzoate ester and a dichlorobenzene. It derives from a 3,4-dichlorobenzoic acid.
