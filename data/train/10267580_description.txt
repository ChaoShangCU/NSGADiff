The molecule is a member of the class of quinazolines that is quinazoline which is substituted at positions 2 and 4 by 6-methylpyridin-2-yl and pyridin-4-ylnitrilo groups, respectively. It is an ALK5 and PKN3 kinase inhibitor. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.30 (receptor protein serine/threonine kinase) inhibitor and an EC 2.7.11.13 (protein kinase C) inhibitor. It is a member of quinazolines, a member of pyridines, a secondary amino compound, an aromatic amine and a member of methylpyridines.
