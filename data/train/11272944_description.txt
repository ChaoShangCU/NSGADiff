The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone and a monosaccharide sulfate that is the 8-O-beta-D-glucuronopyranoside-3''-O-sulfate derivative of hypolaetin. It has been isolated from the seeds of Theobroma grandiflorum and has been shown to exhibit antioxidant activity. It has a role as an antioxidant and a plant metabolite.
