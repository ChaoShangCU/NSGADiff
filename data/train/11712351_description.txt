The molecule is a terpene lactone that is a bisnortriterpenoid possessing an aromatic ring. It is isolated from the leaves and stems of Schisandra rubriflora and has been shown to exhibit anti-HIV-1 activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-HIV-1 agent. It is a hexacyclic triterpenoid, a terpene lactone, a cyclic ether and a gamma-lactone.
