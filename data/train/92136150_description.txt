The molecule is a 3-sn-phosphatidyl L-serine in which the phosphatidyl acyl groups at positions 1 and 2 are oleoyl and 6-[(7-nitro-2-1,3-benzoxadiazol-4-yl)amino]hexanoyl respectively. It has a role as a fluorescent probe. It is a 3-sn-phosphatidyl-L-serine and a benzoxadiazole. It derives from an oleic acid.
