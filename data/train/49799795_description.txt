The molecule is a germacranolide isolated from Neurolaena lobata and Austroeupatorium inulifolium and has been shown to exhibit antimalarial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is an enone, a fatty acid ester, a germacranolide, an acetate ester and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from an isovaleric acid.
