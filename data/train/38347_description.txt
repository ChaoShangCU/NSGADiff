The molecule is a phosphorodiamide that is the active metabolite of the nitrogen mustard cyclophosphamide. It has potent antineoplastic and immunosuppressive properties. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an immunosuppressive agent, an alkylating agent, a metabolite and a drug allergen. It is a phosphorodiamide, a nitrogen mustard, a peroxol and an organochlorine compound.
