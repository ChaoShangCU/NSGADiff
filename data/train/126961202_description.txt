The molecule is a flavin mononucleotide that is FMN in which the 8-methyl group has been oxidised to the corresponding carboxylic acid. It is a flavin mononucleotide, a ribitol phosphate and an aromatic carboxylic acid. It derives from a FMN. It is a conjugate acid of an 8-carboxy-8-demethylriboflavin 5'-phosphate(4-).
