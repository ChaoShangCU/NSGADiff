The molecule is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid and sulfate groups of the repeating units of heparan sulfate N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosaminide; major species at pH 7.3. It is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion, an organic sulfamate oxoanion and an ionic polymer. It is a conjugate base of a heparan sulfate N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosaminide.
