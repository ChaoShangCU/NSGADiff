The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazoles that is an immunomodulator used for treatment of rheumatoid arthritis, Crohn's disease and psoriasis. It has a role as an immunomodulator and an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of morpholines, a member of pyrazoles, a member of naphthalenes, a member of ureas and an aromatic ether.
