The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 8-oxoresolvin D1, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and an oxo fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an 8-oxoresolvin D1.
