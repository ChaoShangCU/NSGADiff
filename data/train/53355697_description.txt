The molecule is an organic heterotricyclic compound that is 10,11-dihydro-6H-oxepino[2,3-d]pyrazino[1,2-a]pyrimidine-6,9(8H)-dione substituted by a benzyl group at position 8 and a butan-2-yl group at position 11. It has been isolated from Aspergillus species. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is an organic heterotricyclic compound, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound, a cyclic ether and a lactam.
