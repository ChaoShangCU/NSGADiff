The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is hexacosanoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 11, 14, 17, 20, 23 (the 11Z,14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and a hexacosapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of an (11Z,14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z)-hexacosapentaenoate.
