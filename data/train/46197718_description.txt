The molecule is a polycyclic cage that is the 4-hydroxy derivative of platencin. It is isolated from Streptomyces platensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a cyclic ketone, a dihydroxybenzoic acid, a polycyclic cage, a secondary alcohol, an aromatic amide and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It derives from a platencin.
