The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are specified as hexadecanoyl and 7-oxoheptanoyl respectively. It has a role as a Papio hamadryas metabolite. It is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine and an aldehyde. It derives from a 7-oxoheptanoic acid and a hexadecanoic acid.
