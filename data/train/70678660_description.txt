The molecule is an anionic ceramide phosphoinositol compound having a hexacosanoyl group attached to the ceramide nitrogen, no hydroxylation at C-4 of the long-chain base, and hydroxylation at C-2 of the very-long-chain fatty acid. Major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of an Ins-1-P-Cer(d18:0/2-OH-26:0).
