The molecule is a member of the class of phenazines that is 5,10-dihydrophenazine substituted at positions 1 and 9 by carboxy and dimethylallyl groups respectively. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is an aromatic carboxylic acid, a member of phenazines and an olefinic compound. It is a conjugate acid of a 5,10-dihydro-9-dimethylallylphenazine 1-carboxylate.
