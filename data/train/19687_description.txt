The molecule is a phenothiazine derivative having a trifluoromethyl subsitituent at the 2-position and a 3-[4-(2-hydroxyethyl)diazepin-1-yl]propyl group at the N-10 position. It has a role as a phenothiazine antipsychotic drug and an anxiolytic drug. It is a member of phenothiazines, an organofluorine compound and a diazepine.
