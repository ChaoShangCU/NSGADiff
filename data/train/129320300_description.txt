The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is the trianion arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and carboxylic acid functions of N-acetyl-alpha-D-muramate 1-phosphate. Major microspecies at pH 7.3. It is a carbohydrate acid derivative anion, a monocarboxylic acid anion and an organophosphate oxoanion. It derives from a N-acetylmuramate. It is a conjugate base of a N-acetyl-alpha-D-muramic acid 1-phosphate.
