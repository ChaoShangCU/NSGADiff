The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is hexatriacontanoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 18, 21, 24, 27 and 30 (the 18Z,21Z,24Z,27Z,30Z-isomer). It is a hexatriacontapentaenoic acid and an omega-6 fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (18Z,21Z,24Z,27Z,30Z)-hexatriacontapentaenoate.
