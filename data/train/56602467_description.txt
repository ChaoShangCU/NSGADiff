The molecule is a hopanoid that is hop-22(29)-ene substituted by an acetoxy group at position 3 and a hydroxy group at position 15 (the 3beta,15alpha-stereoisomer). It has been isolated from Hypocrella species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a hopanoid, a secondary alcohol, a pentacyclic triterpenoid and an acetate ester.
