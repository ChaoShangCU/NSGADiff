The molecule is a 2-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid resulting from the hydrolysis of the delta-lactone ring of SN-38. It is a metabolite of SN-38. It has a role as a drug metabolite. It is a member of phenols, a tertiary alcohol, an organonitrogen heterocyclic compound, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, an enone, a 2-hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a primary alcohol. It derives from a SN-38. It is a conjugate acid of a SN-38 carboxylate.
