The molecule is a member of the class of xanthones that is 9H-xanthen-9-one substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 8, methoxy groups at position 2 and 4 and a prenyl group at position 1. A methyl ether derivative of celebixanthone, it is isolated from the roots of Cratoxylum cochinchinense and exhibits antimalarial activities. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is an aromatic ether, a polyphenol and a member of xanthones. It derives from a celebixanthone.
