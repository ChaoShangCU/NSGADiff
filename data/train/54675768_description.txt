The molecule is a hydroxycoumarin antibiotic that is obtained from Streptomyces rishiriensis and exhibits potent antibacterial and anticancer activity. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite, a DNA synthesis inhibitor, a topoisomerase IV inhibitor, a Hsp90 inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an antimicrobial agent. It is a member of coumarins, a glycoside, a member of pyrroles, a heteroarenecarboxylate ester and an aromatic amide.
