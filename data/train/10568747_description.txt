The molecule is an icosanoid that is (6E,8Z,10E,14Z)-icosatetraenoic acid substituted at positions 5 and 12 by oxo and hydroxy groups respectively. It has a role as a human blood serum metabolite. It is an enone, an icosanoid, a long-chain fatty acid, an oxo fatty acid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid.
