The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is luteolin with a 6-O-beta-D-arabinopyranosyl-beta-D-glucopyranosyl entity attached at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a trihydroxyflavone and a disaccharide derivative. It derives from a luteolin.
