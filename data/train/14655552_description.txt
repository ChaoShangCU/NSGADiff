The molecule is the glycosyloxyflavone which is the 7-O-glucuronide of oroxylin A. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone, a monohydroxyflavone, a monosaccharide derivative and a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid. It derives from an oroxylin A. It is a conjugate acid of an oroxylin A 7-O-beta-D-glucuronate.
