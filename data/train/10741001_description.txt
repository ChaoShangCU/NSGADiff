The molecule is a tetrahydroxyflavanone that is (2S)-flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7, 2' and 4' and a lavandulyl group at position 8. Isolated from Physena madagascariensis, it exhibits antibacterial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antibacterial agent. It is a tetrahydroxyflavanone and a member of 4'-hydroxyflavanones.
