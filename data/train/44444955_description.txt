The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of 6-azauridine 5'-monophosphate. Major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an EC 4.1.1.23 (orotidine-5'-phosphate decarboxylase) inhibitor. It is a conjugate base of a 6-azauridine 5'-monophosphate.
