The molecule is a C-nitro compound that is nitrobenzene in which the four hydrogens located ortho- and para- to the nitro group have been replaced by chlorines. A fungicide used to control dry rot, it is no longer approved for use within the European Union. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a C-nitro compound, a tetrachlorobenzene and an aromatic fungicide. It derives from a 1,2,4,5-tetrachlorobenzene.
