The molecule is a myo-inositol tetrakisphosphate that consists of myo-inositol having the four phospho groups located at positions 2, 3, 4 and 6 as well as a diphospho group at position 5. It derives from a myo-inositol. It is a conjugate acid of a 5-diphospho-1D-myo-inositol 1,3,4,6-tetrakisphosphate(11-).
