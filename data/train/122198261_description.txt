The molecule is a class I yanuthone that is 7-deacetoxyyanuthone A in which one of the hydrogens of the trans-terminal methyl groups of the sesquiterpenoid side-chain has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a class I yanuthone, a primary alcohol and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a 7-deacetoxyyanuthone A.
