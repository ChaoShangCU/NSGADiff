The molecule is a tetracyclic diterpenoid that is hexadecahydropyrene substituted by isocyano group at position 8, methyl groups at positions 1, 4, 7 and 8 and a formamido group at position 1. Isolated from the tropical marine sponge Cymbastela hooperi, it exhibits antiplasmodial activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antiplasmodial drug. It is a tetracyclic diterpenoid, an isocyanide and a member of formamides.
