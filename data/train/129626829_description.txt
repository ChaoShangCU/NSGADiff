The molecule is a thromboxane A that is thromboxane A2 carrying an additional hydroxy substituent at position 19. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a thromboxanes A, an epoxy monocarboxylic acid, a secondary allylic alcohol and a diol. It derives from a thromboxane A2. It is a conjugate acid of a 19-hydroxythromboxane A2(1-).
