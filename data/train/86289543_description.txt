The molecule is a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of (E)-2-methylpentadec-2-enoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a monounsaturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of an (E)-2-methylpentadec-2-enoyl-CoA.
