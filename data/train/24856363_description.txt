The molecule is a dicarboxylic acid diamide obtained by condensation of the 3- and 4'-carboxy groups of 6-methyl[1,1'-biphenyl]-3,4'-dicarboxylic acid with cyclpropylamine and cyclpropylmethylamine respectively. It has a role as an EC 2.7.11.24 (mitogen-activated protein kinase) inhibitor. It is a member of biphenyls, a dicarboxylic acid diamide, a member of benzamides and a member of cyclopropanes.
