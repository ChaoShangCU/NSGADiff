The molecule is an oxoaporphine alkaloid that is 7H-dibenzo[de,g]quinolin-7-one substituted by a methylenedioxy group across positions 1 and 2, a methoxy group at position 11 and a hydroxy group at position 10. Isolated from Hernandia nymphaeifolia and Lindera chunii, it exhibits activity against HIV-1 integrase. It has a role as a metabolite and a HIV-1 integrase inhibitor. It is an aromatic ether, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a cyclic ketone, a member of phenols, an oxacycle and an oxoaporphine alkaloid. It derives from an aporphine.
