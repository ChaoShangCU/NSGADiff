The molecule is a monobactam that is produced by Nocardia uniformis subsp. tsuyamanensis. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a monobactam, a member of phenols, a dicarboxylic acid and a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid. It derives from a L-homoserine. It is a tautomer of an isonocardicin C dizwitterion.
