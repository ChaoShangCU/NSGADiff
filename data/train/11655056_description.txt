The molecule is a hydroxyisoflavone that is 8,9-dihydro-4H-furo[2,3-h]chromen-4-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 5, a 3,4-dihydroxyphenyl group at position 3, a 2-hydroxypropan-2-yl group at position 8 and a prenyl group at position 6. Isolated from Millettia pachycarpa, it exhibits anti-estrogenic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-estrogen. It is a hydroxyisoflavone and a tertiary alcohol.
