The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is podocarpa-8,11,13-triene substituted by a propan-2-yl group at position 14, a hydroxy group at position 11 and a formyl group at position 16. It is isolated from Podocarpus latifolius. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an abietane diterpenoid, a member of phenols and an aldehyde.
