The molecule is a pyrrolidinone derivative that is the methyl ester of sch 210972. Isolated from fungal fermentation broth of Chaetomium globosum, it exhibits inhibitory activity against chemokine receptor CCR-5. It has a role as a chemokine receptor 5 antagonist and a Chaetomium metabolite. It is a carbobicyclic compound, an enol, a tertiary alcohol, a carboxylic ester, a member of pyrrolidin-2-ones and a member of octahydronaphthalenes. It derives from a sch 210972.
