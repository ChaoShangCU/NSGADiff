The molecule is an acetamide obtained by acetylation of one of the amino groups of any alkane-alpha,omega-diamine. AcNHCH2(CH2)nCH2NH2, where n = 0, 1, 2, etc. It is a member of acetamides and a primary amino compound. It derives from an alkane-alpha,omega-diamine. It is a conjugate base of a N-monoacetylalkane-alpha,omega-diamine(1+).
