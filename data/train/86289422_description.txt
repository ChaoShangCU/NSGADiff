The molecule is a phenolate anion obtained by deprotonation of the 5- and 7-hydroxy groups of cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-galactoside. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as a food component and a plant metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a cyanidin 3-O-beta-D-galactoside betaine.
