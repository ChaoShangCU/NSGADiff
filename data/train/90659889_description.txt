The molecule is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 2,6-dimethylheptanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 2,6-dimethylheptanoyl-CoA.
