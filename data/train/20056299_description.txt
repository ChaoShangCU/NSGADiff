The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C23H37NO6 that is isolated from several Aconitum species. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tertiary amino compound, a diether and a tetrol. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
