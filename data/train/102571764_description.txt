The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 5(S),15(R)-DiHETE arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a dihydroxyicosatetraenoate. It is a conjugate base of a 5(S),15(R)-DiHETE.
