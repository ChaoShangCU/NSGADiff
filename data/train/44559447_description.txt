The molecule is a macrocyclic lactam with oximo amide groups having a -E,Z configuration. Isolated from the marine sponge Ianthella, it has been shown to exhibit calcium channel modulatory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and a calcium channel modulator. It is a cyclic ether, a ketoxime, a lactam, a macrocycle, an organobromine compound and a polyphenol.
