The molecule is an ergostanoid that is (22E)-ergosta-7,22-diene substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 5 and a methoxy group at position 6 (the 3beta,5alpha stereoisomer). It has been isolated from the fungus, Xylaria species. It has a role as a fungal metabolite. It is a 3beta-hydroxy steroid, an ergostanoid, a 5alpha-hydroxy steroid and an ether.
