The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid anion resulting from the removal of a proton from the carboxy group of 17alpha-estradiol 3-glucosiduronic acid. It is a monocarboxylic acid anion, a steroid glucosiduronic acid anion and a beta-D-glucosiduronate. It is a conjugate base of a 17alpha-estradiol 3-glucosiduronic acid.
