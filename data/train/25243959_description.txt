The molecule is conjugate base of 3-(4-oxo-4,5-dihydro-1H-imidazol-5-yl)propanoic acid. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a (S)-3-(4-oxo-4,5-dihydro-1H-imidazol-5-yl)propanoic acid, a 3-(4-oxo-4,5-dihydro-1H-imidazol-5-yl)propanoate(1-) and a 3-(4-oxo-4,5-dihydro-1H-imidazol-5-yl)propanoic acid.
