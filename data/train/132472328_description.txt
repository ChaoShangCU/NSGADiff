The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of (16S,17S)-epoxy-(4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14E,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (16S,17S)-epoxy-(4Z,7Z,10Z,12E,14E,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid.
