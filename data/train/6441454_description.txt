The molecule is the all-cis-isomer of a C22 polyunsaturated fatty acid having five double bonds in the 4-, 7-, 10-, 13- and 16-positions. It is a member of n-6 PUFA and a product of linoleic acid metabolism. It has a role as a Daphnia tenebrosa metabolite, a human metabolite and an algal metabolite. It is a docosapentaenoic acid and an omega-6 fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z)-docosapentaenoate.
