The molecule is an amino alcohol that is dodeca-2,6,8,10-tetraen-1-amine in which all four double bonds have trans configuration and in which the pro-S hydrogen at position 5 has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is a primary allylic alcohol, a primary amino compound, an olefinic compound and an amino alcohol. It is a conjugate base of a (2E,5S,6E,8E,10E)-1-ammoniododeca-2,6,8,10-tetraen-5-ol.
