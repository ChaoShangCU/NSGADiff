The molecule is a taxane diterpenoid that is paclitaxel which is lacking the N-benzoyl group. It is a biosynthetic precursor of paclitaxel. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a primary amino compound, a taxane diterpenoid, a tetracyclic diterpenoid and an acetate ester. It derives from a baccatin III. It is a conjugate base of a 3'-N-debenzoyltaxol(1+).
