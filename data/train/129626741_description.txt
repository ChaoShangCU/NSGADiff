The molecule is a diterpenyl phosphate that is the O-diphospho derivative of (-)-kolavenol It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diterpenyl phosphate and a member of octahydronaphthalenes. It is a conjugate acid of a (-)-kolavenyl diphosphate(3-). It is an enantiomer of a (+)-kolavenyl diphosphate.
