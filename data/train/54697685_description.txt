The molecule is a member of the class of quinolones that is the amide obtained from formal condensation of the carboxy group of 1-allyl-4-hydroxy-2-oxo-1,2-dihydroquinoline-3-carboxylic acid with the amino group of 2-amino-3-ethyl-1,3,4-thiadizole. It is a monocarboxylic acid amide, a monohydroxyquinoline, a quinolone and a member of thiadiazoles.
