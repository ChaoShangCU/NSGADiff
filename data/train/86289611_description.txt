The molecule is a trihydroxyflavone that is vitexin having a beta-L-rhamnosyl residue attached at the 2''-position of the glucitol moiety. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a disaccharide derivative and a trihydroxyflavone. It derives from a vitexin. It is a conjugate acid of a vitexin 2''-O-beta-L-rhamnoside(1-).
