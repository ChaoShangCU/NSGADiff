The molecule is a member of the class of pradimicins that is isolated from the cultured broth of Actinomadura hibisca No. P157-2 (ATCC 53557). It is a disaccharide derivative, a polyphenol, an aromatic ether, a secondary alcohol, a pradimicin, a L-alanine derivative, a member of p-quinones and a carboxylic acid. It derives from a D-alanine.
