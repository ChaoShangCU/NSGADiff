The molecule is an amidobenzoic acid that is anthranilic acid in which one of the anilino hydrogens is replaced by a 4-pentylcinnamoyl group. It is a transient receptor potential (TRP) channel blocker and phospholipase A2 (PLA2) inhibitor. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.4 (phospholipase A2) inhibitor and a TRP channel blocker. It is an amidobenzoic acid, a member of cinnamamides and a secondary carboxamide.
