The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is peonidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(cis-4 coumaryl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an anthocyanin cation, a cinnamate ester, an aromatic ether and a polyphenol. It derives from a cis-4-coumaric acid and a peonidin.
