The molecule is a member of the class of octahydronaphthalenes that is 1,2,3,4,6,7,8,8a-octahydronaphthalene which is substituted by methyl groups at the 8 and 8a positions, and by a 2-hydroxypropan-2-yl group at position 2 (the 2R,8R,8aS stereoisomer). It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a germacrane sesquiterpenoid, a tertiary alcohol, a member of octahydronaphthalenes and an eremophilane sesquiterpenoid.
