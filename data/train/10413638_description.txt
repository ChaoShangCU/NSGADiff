The molecule is a disaccharide derivative that consists of 5-hydroxy-2-methylchromone substituted by a rutinosyl group at position 7 via a glycosidic linkage. Isolated from Crossosoma bigelovii, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of chromones, a disaccharide derivative, a member of phenols and a rutinoside.
