The molecule is a member of the class of prodimicins that is isolated from the cultured broth of Actinomadura hibisca No. P157-2 (ATCC 53557). It is an aromatic ether, a polyphenol, a secondary alcohol, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyketide, a pradimicin and a L-alanine derivative. It derives from a D-alanine.
