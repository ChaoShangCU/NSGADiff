The molecule is a macrolide that is the 7-oxo derivative of zeaenol (the 5Z stereoisomer). Isolated from Fungi, it exhibits cytotoxic, antibacterial and inhibitory activity against NF-kappaB. It has a role as a metabolite, an antibacterial agent, an antineoplastic agent and a NF-kappaB inhibitor. It is an aromatic ether, a macrolide, a member of phenols, a secondary alcohol and a secondary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
