The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of alpha-L-fucopyranose, 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose and 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose residues joined in sequence by (1->3) and (1->6) glycosidic bonds. It is a member of acetamides, an amino trisaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
