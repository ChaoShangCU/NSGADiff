The molecule is a withanolide that is (22R,25S)-22,26-epoxycholest-4-en-26-one substituted by an oxo group at position 3 and an alpha-acetoxy group at position 11. Isolated from Paraminabea acronocephala, it exhibits anti-inflammatory activity. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent and a coral metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a cholestanoid, an enone, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a withanolide and an acetate ester.
