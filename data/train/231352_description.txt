The molecule is a steroid ester that is pregn-4-en-21-yl acetate substituted by oxo groups at positions 3 and 20, hydroxy groups at positions 11 and 17 and a fluoro group at position 6. It is a fluorinated steroid, a steroid ester, an acetate ester, an 11beta-hydroxy steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 3-oxo-Delta(4) steroid, a 20-oxo steroid and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a hydride of a pregnane.
