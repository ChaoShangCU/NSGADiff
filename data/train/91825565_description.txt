The molecule is a limonoid with a phragmalin skeleton isolated from the leaves of Trichilia connaroides. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a delta-lactone, a bridged compound, a member of furans, a limonoid, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a methyl ester and an enoate ester. It derives from a tiglic acid and a 2-hydroxyisobutyric acid.
