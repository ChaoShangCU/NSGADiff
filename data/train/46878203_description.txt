The molecule is a hydrochloride resulting from the formal reaction of equimolar amounts of Ac-(D)Phe-Pro-boroArg-OH and hydrogen chloride. A thrombin (Factor IIa) inhibitor, thereby acting as an anticoagulant. It has a role as an EC 3.4.21.5 (thrombin) inhibitor and an anticoagulant. It contains an Ac-(D)Phe-Pro-boroArg-OH(1+).
