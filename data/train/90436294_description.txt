The molecule is a fatty acid amide resulting from the formal condensation of the carboxy group of (9Z)-hexadecenoic acid with the amino group of glycine. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a N-acylglycine 16:1 and a fatty amide. It derives from a palmitoleic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-[(9Z)-hexadecenoyl]glycinate.
