The molecule is tetraanion of (S)-3-hydroxylauroyl-CoA arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions; principal microspecies at pH 7.3. It is a fatty acyl-CoA(4-), an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (S)-3-hydroxylauroyl-CoA.
