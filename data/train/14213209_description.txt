The molecule is a member of the class of benzofurans that is 1-benzofuran substituted by a 3-hydroxypropyl group at position 5 and a 4-hydroxy-2-methoxyphenyl group at position 2. It is a lignan derivative isolated from the roots of Krameria lappacea. It has a role as a plant metabolite, a NF-kappaB inhibitor, a cyclooxygenase 1 inhibitor, a cyclooxygenase 2 inhibitor and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a member of benzofurans, a member of phenols, a monomethoxybenzene and a primary alcohol.
