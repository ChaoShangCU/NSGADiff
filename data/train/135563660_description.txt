The molecule is an imidazoquinoline that is 1,3-dihydro-2H-imidazo[4,5-c]quinolin-2-one which is substituted at positions 1, 3, and 8 by by p-(pyrrol-1-yl)phenyl, methyl, and pyridin-3-yl group, respectively. A potent and specific inhibitor of Target Of Rapamycin Complex 2 (TORC2). It has a role as a protein kinase inhibitor. It is an imidazoquinoline, a member of pyrroles, a member of pyridines and a member of phenylureas.
