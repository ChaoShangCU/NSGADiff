The molecule is the L-enantiomer of thyroxine. It has a role as a thyroid hormone, an antithyroid drug, a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a thyroxine, an iodophenol, a 2-halophenol, a L-phenylalanine derivative and a non-proteinogenic L-alpha-amino acid. It is a conjugate acid of a L-thyroxine(1-). It is an enantiomer of a D-thyroxine. It is a tautomer of a L-thyroxine zwitterion.
