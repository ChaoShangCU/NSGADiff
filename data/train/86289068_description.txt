The molecule is a dithiocarbamate salt that is the iron(III) salt of dimethyldithiocarbamic acid. Formerly used as a fungicide. It has a role as an antifungal agrochemical. It is a dithiocarbamate salt and an iron coordination entity. It contains an iron(3+) and a dimethyldithiocarbamate. It derives from a dimethyldithiocarbamic acid.
