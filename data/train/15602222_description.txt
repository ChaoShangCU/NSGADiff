The molecule is an abietane diterpenoid that is ferruginol in which a hydrogen of the bridgehead methyl group and the hydrogen ortho to the phenolic hydroxy group have both been replaced by hydroxy groups. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a carbotricyclic compound, a meroterpenoid, an abietane diterpenoid, a member of catechols and a primary alcohol. It derives from a ferruginol.
