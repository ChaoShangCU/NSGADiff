The molecule is an acyl-CoA that results from the formal condensation of the thiol group of coenzyme A with the carboxy group of 4-trimethylammoniobutanoic acid. It is an acyl-CoA and a quaternary ammonium ion. It derives from a coenzyme A and a 4-(trimethylammonio)butanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a gamma-butyrobetainyl-CoA(3-).
