The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 17-epiestriol having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 3. It is a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid. It derives from a 17-epiestriol. It is a conjugate acid of a 17-epiestriol 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
