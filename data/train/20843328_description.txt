The molecule is an octadecanoid that is (9Z,11Z,15Z)-octadecatrienoic acid having an epoxy group located across positions 12 and 13. It is an octadecanoid, a long-chain fatty acid, a polyunsaturated fatty acid and an epoxy fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (9Z,11Z,13S,15Z)-12,13-epoxyoctadecatrienoate.
