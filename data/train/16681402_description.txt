The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound isolated from the whole plants of Ardisia japonica. It is a dimeric lactone that exhibits inhibitory activity against HIV-1 RNAse H. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.1.26.13 (retroviral ribonuclease H) inhibitor. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a lactone, an aromatic ether and a gallate ester.
