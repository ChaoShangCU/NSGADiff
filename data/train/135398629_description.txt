The molecule is a guanosine bisphosphate having a diphosphate at the 3'-position and a triphosphate at the 5'-position. It has a role as an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a guanosine bisphosphate and a guanosine 5'-phosphate. It is a conjugate acid of a guanosine 3'-diphosphate 5'-triphosphate hexaanion.
