The molecule is a carbobicyclic compound that is bicyclo[3.2.1]oct-3-en-2-one which is substituted at positions 3 and 4 by 2-chloro-4-(methylsulfonyl)benzoyl and phenylthio groups, respectively. It is a carbobicyclic compound, a cyclic ketone, an organic sulfide, a sulfone, a member of monochlorobenzenes and an aromatic ketone.
