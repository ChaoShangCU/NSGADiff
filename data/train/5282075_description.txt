The molecule is a methoxyisoflavone in which the methoxy group is located at position 4' together with three additional hydroxy substituents at positions 2' 5 and 7. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an apoptosis inducer. It is a methoxyisoflavone, a member of 7-hydroxyisoflavones, a member of 2'-hydroxyisoflavones and a member of 4'-methoxyisoflavones.
