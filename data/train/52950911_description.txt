The molecule is a triterpenoid saponin that is 28-O-beta-D-glucopyranosyl derivative of 23-hydroxyursolic acid. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Juglans sinensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a carboxylic ester, a beta-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative and a triterpenoid saponin. It derives from a 23-hydroxyursolic acid. It derives from a hydride of an ursane.
