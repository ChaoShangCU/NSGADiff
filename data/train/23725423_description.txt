The molecule is a lactate salt having panobinostat(1+) as the counterion. A histone deacetylase inhibitor used in combination with bortezomib and dexamethasone for the treatment of multiple myeloma. It has a role as an EC 3.5.1.98 (histone deacetylase) inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an angiogenesis modulating agent. It is a lactate salt and an organoammonium salt. It contains a panobinostat(1+).
