The molecule is a 1-benzofuran that is 5-(piperazin-1-yl}-1-benzofuran-2-carboxamide having a (5-cyanoindol-3-yl)butyl group attached at position N-4 on the piperazine ring. Used for the treatment of major depressive disorder. It has a role as an antidepressant, a serotonergic agonist and a serotonin uptake inhibitor. It is a member of indoles, a nitrile, a N-arylpiperazine, a N-alkylpiperazine, a member of 1-benzofurans and a monocarboxylic acid amide. It is a conjugate base of a vilazodone(1+).
