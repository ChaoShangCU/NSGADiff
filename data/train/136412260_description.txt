The molecule is a benzamide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of 4-[5-(3,5-dichlorophenyl)-5-(trifluoromethyl)-4,5-dihydro-1,2-oxazol-3-yl]-2-methylbenzoic acid with the amino group of N-methoxymethanimidamide. It is a member of benzamides, an isoxazoline, a dichlorobenzene, a member of formamidines, an organofluorine compound and an ether.
