The molecule is a steroid sulfate that is 5alpha-ergostane substituted by sulfate groups at positions 2, 3 and 6 (the (2beta,3alpha,6alpha stereoisomer). It has a role as an anti-HIV-1 agent, an anti-HIV-2 agent and a metabolite. It is a conjugate acid of a halistanol sulfate G(3-). It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-ergostane.
