The molecule is a flavonoid oxoanion that is the conjugate base of vitexin 2''-O-beta-L-rhamnoside obtained by deprotonation of the OH group at position 7 on the chromene. It is the major microspecies at pH 7.3 (according to Marvin v 6.2.0.). It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a vitexin 2''-O-beta-L-rhamnoside.
