The molecule is a sesquiterpene lactone obtained by regioselective Bayer-Villiger oxidation of 1-deoxy-11-oxopentalenic acid. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a monocarboxylic acid, an organic heterotricyclic compound and a sesquiterpene lactone. It is a conjugate acid of a neopentalenolactone D(1-).
