The molecule is a member of the class of chromanes that is gamma-CEHC in which the phenolic hydrogen is replaced by a beta-glucuronosyl residue. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a member of chromanes and a dicarboxylic acid. It derives from a gamma-CEHC. It is a conjugate acid of a gamma-CEHC beta-glucuronide anion.
