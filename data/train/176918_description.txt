The molecule is a benzochromenone that is rubrofusarin in which the hydroxy group at position 6 has been converted to the corresponding methyl ether. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a benzochromenone, an aromatic ether, a member of phenols, a polyketide and a naphtho-gamma-pyrone. It derives from a rubrofusarin. It is a conjugate acid of a rubrofusarin B(1-).
