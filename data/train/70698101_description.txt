The molecule is a diterpenoid that is cyclodeca-1,6-diene substituted by a methyl group at position 8, a methylidene group at position 4 and a 2-methyl-4-oxohept-2-en-6-yl group at position 1. It has been isolated from the Hainan soft coral Lobophytum cristatum. It has a role as a coral metabolite. It is a diterpenoid and an enone.
