The molecule is an aryl sulfate that is pyrogallol in which the phenolic hydrogens at positions 1 and 2 are replaced by sulfo and methyl groups respectively. It has a role as a human blood serum metabolite. It is an aryl sulfate and a member of guaiacols. It derives from a pyrogallol. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-methoxyresorcinol sulfate(1-).
