The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is the dianion of alpha-D-glucose 1-phosphate, obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups. It has a role as a human metabolite and a fundamental metabolite. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a monosaccharide 1-phosphate(2-). It is a conjugate base of an alpha-D-glucose 1-phosphate.
