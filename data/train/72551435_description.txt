The molecule is a kaempferol O-glucoside that is kaempferol attached to a beta-L-glucopyranosyl moiety at position 5 via a glycosidic linkage. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a beta-L-glucoside, a kaempferol O-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a trihydroxyflavone and a member of flavonols. It derives from a beta-L-glucose.
