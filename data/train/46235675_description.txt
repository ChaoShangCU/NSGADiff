The molecule is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid that is (11Z)-icos-11-enoic acid which carries a hydroxy group at position 2. It is a 2-hydroxy fatty acid, a hydroxy monounsaturated fatty acid and a long-chain fatty acid. It derives from an (11Z)-icos-11-enoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxygondoate.
