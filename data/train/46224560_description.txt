The molecule is a steroidal acyl-CoA(4-) that is the tetraanion of deoxycholoyl-CoA, arising from deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate functions. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a steroidal acyl-CoA(4-) and a 3alpha-hydroxy bile acid CoA thioester(4-). It is a conjugate base of a deoxycholoyl-CoA.
