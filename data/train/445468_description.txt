The molecule is a 1,2-diacyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the 1- and 2-acyl groups are both specified as hexadecanoyl (palmitoyl). It has a role as a mouse metabolite. It derives from a hexadecanoic acid. It is a tautomer of a 1,2-dihexadecanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
