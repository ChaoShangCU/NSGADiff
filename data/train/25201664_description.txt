The molecule is trianion of 2-(alpha-D-mannosyl)-3-phosphoglyceric acid arising from deprotonation of carboxylic acid and phosphate functions; major species at pH 7.3. It is an organophosphate oxoanion and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-(alpha-D-mannosyl)-3-phosphoglyceric acid.
