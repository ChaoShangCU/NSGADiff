The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl group is specified as (5Z,8Z,11Z,14Z)-eicosatetraenoyl (arachidonoyl). It has a role as a metabolite. It is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a lysophosphatidylethanolamine 20:4. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a tautomer of a 2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine zwitterion.
