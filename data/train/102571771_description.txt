The molecule is a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 13-HPODE arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a hydroperoxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a HPODE(1-). It is a conjugate base of a 13-HPODE.
