The molecule is an isoquinoline alkaloid that is 1',2'-didehydroemetan bearing two hydroxy substituents at positions 6' and 9 as well as three methoxy substituents at positions 7', 10 and 11. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an isoquinoline alkaloid, a member of isoquinolines, a pyridoisoquinoline, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether. It derives from a hydride of an emetan.
