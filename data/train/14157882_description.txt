The molecule is a lignan that is (+)-lariciresinol substituted by additional methoxy groups at positions 5 and 5'. It has been isolated from them stems of Sinocalamus affinis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a lignan, a member of oxolanes, a member of phenols, a primary alcohol and a dimethoxybenzene. It derives from a (+)-lariciresinol.
