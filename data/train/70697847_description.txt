The molecule is an oxazinane alkaloid that is methyl (4S)-1,3-oxazinane-4-carboxylate substituted by a (1H-pyrrol-2-yl group at position 2 and a 2-methylheptyl group at position 5. Isolated from Celastrus angulatus, it exhibits cytotoxic activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of pyrroles, an alkaloid, an oxazinane and a methyl ester.
