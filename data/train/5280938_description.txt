The molecule is a leukotriene composed of (7E,9E,11Z,14Z)-icosa-7,9,11,14-tetraenoic acid having (5S)-hydroxy and (6R)-(L-gamma-glutamyl-L-cystein-S-yl) substituents. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is an organic sulfide, a dipeptide and a leukotriene. It derives from an icosa-7,9,11,14-tetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a leukotriene F4(2-).
