The molecule is a benzazepine metabolite resulting from demethylation of the antidpressant, mirtazapine. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite, an alpha-adrenergic antagonist, an anxiolytic drug, a H1-receptor antagonist, a histamine antagonist and a serotonergic antagonist. It is a tetracyclic antidepressant and a benzazepine.
