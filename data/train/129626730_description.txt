The molecule is a fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate functions of 2-hydroxyisovaleryl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and a 2-hydroxy-3-methylacyl-CoA(4-). It derives from an isovaleryl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 2-hydroxyisovaleryl-CoA.
