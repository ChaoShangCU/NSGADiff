The molecule is a C27 bile acid that is 5beta-cholest-24-en-26-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 3alpha and 7beta. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a cholestanoid, a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid, a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a bile acid, a 7beta-hydroxy steroid and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a hydride of a 5beta-cholestane.
