The molecule is a docosanoid that is (9E,11E,14E,16Z,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid carrying three hydroxy substituents at positions 7, 8 and 13. It has a role as an anti-inflammatory agent, a human xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a docosanoid, a resolvin and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a resolvin T3(1-).
