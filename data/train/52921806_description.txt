The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is octatriacontanoic acid having four double bonds located at positions 23, 26, 29 and 32 (the 23Z,26Z,29Z,32Z-isomer). It is an omega-6 fatty acid and an octatriacontatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (23Z,26Z,29Z,32Z)-octatriacontatetraenoate.
