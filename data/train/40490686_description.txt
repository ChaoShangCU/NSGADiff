The molecule is a (5Z,8Z,14Z)-11,12-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid in which the two stereocentres at positions 11 and 12 both have S-configuration. It derives from an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (5Z,8Z,11S,12S,14Z)-11,12-dihydroxyicosatrienoate. It is an enantiomer of a (5Z,8Z,11R,12R,14Z)-11,12-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid.
