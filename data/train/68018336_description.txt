The molecule is a member of the class of pyrimidines that is pyrimidine which is substituted at positions 2, 4, and 6 by 2,4-dichloroanilino, trifluoromethyl, and {2-[(1E)-1,3-dimethoxy-3-oxoprop-1-en-2-yl]benzyl}oxy groups, respectively. An acaricide used for the control of mites on citrus and cotton crops. It has a role as an acaricide. It is a member of pyrimidines, a dichlorobenzene, an enol ether, an organofluorine compound, an aromatic ether, a secondary amino compound, a methyl ester and a substituted aniline.
