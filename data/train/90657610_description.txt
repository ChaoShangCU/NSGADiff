The molecule is a trans-3-enoyl-CoA(4-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of trans-dec-3-enoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a trans-3-enoyl-CoA(4-), a 4-saturated-trans-3-enoyl-CoA(4-) and a medium-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a trans-dec-3-enoyl-CoA.
