The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is cucurbitadienol in which the methylene hydrogens at position 11 have been replaced by an oxo group. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a tetracyclic triterpenoid, a cyclic terpene ketone and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a cucurbitadienol. It derives from a hydride of a cucurbitane.
