The molecule is a 3beta-sterol that consists of 24-methylcholest-6-en-3beta-ol with a peroxy group between positions 5 and 8 and a methylene group between positions 22 and 23. An antineoplastic agent isolated from Sinularia sp. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an organic peroxide, a member of cyclopropanes and a 3beta-sterol.
