The molecule is an indole alkaloid that is jerantinine A substituted with an oxo group at position 13. Isolated from Tabernaemontana corymbosa, it exhibits cytotoxicity against human KB cells. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an alkaloid ester, an aromatic ether, an indole alkaloid, a lactam, a member of phenols, an organic heteropentacyclic compound and a methyl ester. It derives from a jerantinine A.
