The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine in which the acyl group is specified as (9S,11R)-epidioxy-(15S)-hydroperoxy-(5Z,13E)-prostadienoyl. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine, a prostanoid and a lipid hydroperoxide. It derives from a prostaglandin G2.
