The molecule is a nucleoside 3',5'-cyclic phosphorothioate having 8-bromoguanine as the nucleobase (the Sp-stereoisomer). It has a role as a protein kinase agonist. It is a nucleoside 3',5'-cyclic phosphorothioate, an organobromine compound and a member of purines. It derives from a 3',5'-cyclic GMP.
