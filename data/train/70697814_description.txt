The molecule is an epoxide that is the 8,8a-epoxy derivative of morellic acid. Isolated from Garcinia hanburyi it exhibits cytotoxic and anti-HIV activity.. It has a role as a metabolite, an anti-HIV-1 agent and an antineoplastic agent. It is a cyclic ketone, a dioxo monocarboxylic acid, an organic heterohexacyclic compound, a member of phenols, a polycyclic cage, an epoxide and an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a morellic acid.
