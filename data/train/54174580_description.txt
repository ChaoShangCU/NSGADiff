The molecule is a glycosyloxyflavone that is the 3-O-arabinofuranosyl derivative of myricetin. It has been isolated from Mimosa diplotricha. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a glycosyloxyflavone, a monosaccharide derivative, a pentahydroxyflavone and an arabinoside. It derives from a myricetin.
