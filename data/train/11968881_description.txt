The molecule is a quercetin O-glucoside that is quercetin with two beta-D-glucosyl residues attached at positions 7 and 4'. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a member of flavonols, a monosaccharide derivative, a polyphenol, a quercetin O-glucoside and a trihydroxyflavone.
