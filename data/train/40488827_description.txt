The molecule is a long-chain polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosatrienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a docosatrienoate. It is a conjugate base of a (13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosatrienoic acid.
