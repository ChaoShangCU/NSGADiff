The molecule is a 1-phosphatidyl-1D-myo-inositol 5-phosphate in which both phosphatidyl acyl groups are specified as octanoyl. It is a 1-phosphatidyl-1D-myo-inositol 5-phosphate and an octanoate ester. It is a conjugate acid of a 1,2-dioctanoyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'D-myo-inositol-5'-phosphate)(3-).
