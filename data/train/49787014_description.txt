The molecule is a member of the class of tetrahydropyridines that is 2,3-dihydroxy-4-methoxy-1-methyl-6-oxo-1,2,3,6-tetrahydropyridine-3-carbonitrile having a beta-D-glucosyl residue attached at position 3 via a glycosidic bond. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a beta-D-glucoside, a tetrahydropyridine, an enol ether, a delta-lactam and an aliphatic nitrile.
