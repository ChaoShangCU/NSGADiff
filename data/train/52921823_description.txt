The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is dotriacontapentaenoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 14, 17, 20, 23 and 26 (the 14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z-isomer). It is a dotriacontapentaenoic acid and an omega-6 fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (14Z,17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-dotriacontapentaenoate.
