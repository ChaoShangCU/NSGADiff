The molecule is a hydroxy-cannabidiol that is cannabidiol in which the 6-pro-R hydrogen of the cyclohexene ring has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It is one of the main metabolites of cannabidiol by human liver microsomes, produced by CYP3A. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a hydroxy-cannabidiol, an olefinic compound, a member of resorcinols and a secondary alcohol.
