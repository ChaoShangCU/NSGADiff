The molecule is a trihydroxyflavone that is flavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 4', a methoxy group at position 3 and a 6-hydroxy-2-methylhept-2-en-6-yl substituted tetrahydrofuran ring across positions 5' and 6'. It has been isolated from Morus nigra. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone and an extended flavonoid.
