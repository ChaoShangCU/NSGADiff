The molecule is an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid that is acrylic acid in which one of the olefinic hydrogens at position 3 has been replaced by a 3,4-dioxocyclohexa-1,5-dien-1-yl group. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is an alpha,beta-unsaturated monocarboxylic acid and a member of 1,2-benzoquinones. It derives from an acrylic acid.
