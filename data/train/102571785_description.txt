The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 8-HETE arising from deprotonation of the carboxylic acid function; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion and a hydroxy fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of an 8-HETE.
