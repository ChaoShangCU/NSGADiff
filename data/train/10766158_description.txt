The molecule is a terpene lactone that is 13,28-epoxyolean-11-en-28-one substituted by an alpha-hydroxy group at position 3. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Fatsia polycarpa. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a hexacyclic triterpenoid, a terpene lactone and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
