The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the acyl group is specified as 4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z-docosahexaenoyl. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine and a lysophosphatidylethanolamine 22:6. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid.
