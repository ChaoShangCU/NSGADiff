The molecule is a disaccharide phosphate that is N'-monoacetylchitobiose substituted at position 6' by a phospho group. It is an amino disaccharide and a disaccharide phosphate. It derives from a beta-D-glucosaminyl-(1->4)-D-glucosamine. It is a conjugate acid of a N'-monoacetylchitobiose-6'-phosphate(1-).
