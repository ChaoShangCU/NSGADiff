The molecule is an amino trisaccharide consisting of N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose and N-acetyl-D-galactosamine residues linked sequentially (1->2) and (1->3). It has a role as an epitope. It is an amino trisaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a galactosamine oligosaccharide.
