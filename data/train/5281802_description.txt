The molecule is a hydroxyisoflavone that is isoflavone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5, 7 and 3', prenyl groups at positions 2' and 6', a methoxy group at position 5' and an amino group at position 4'. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxyisoflavone, a substituted aniline and a methoxyisoflavone. It derives from an isoflavone.
