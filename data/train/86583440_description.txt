The molecule is an omega-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate OH groups of 16-hydroxyhexadecanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is an omega-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a long-chain fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 16-hydroxyhexadecanoyl-CoA.
