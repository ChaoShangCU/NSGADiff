The molecule is a cholestanoid that zymosterol in which the two methyl groups at positions 26 and 27 have been replaced by a cyclopropane ring. It has a role as a Brassica napus metabolite and a sterol methyltransferase inhibitor. It is a 3beta-sterol, a cholestanoid and a member of cyclopropanes. It derives from a zymosterol. It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-cholestane.
