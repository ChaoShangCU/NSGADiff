The molecule is a (1E)-1-(4-chlorophenyl)-4,4-dimethyl-2-(1H-1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)pent-1-en-3-ol that has R configuration at the chiral centre. It is the enantiomer of the fungicide and plant growth retardant uniconazole-P; the fungicide and plant growth retardant uniconazole is the racemic mixture comprising equimolar amounts of (R)-uniconazole and uniconazole-P. It is an enantiomer of a uniconazole P.
