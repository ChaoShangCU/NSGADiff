The molecule is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of furoic acid with the secondary amino group of 6,7-dimethoxy-2-[(4aR,8aS)-octahydroquinoxalin-1-yl]quinazolin-4-amine. It has a role as an adenosine A2A receptor antagonist. It is a member of quinazolines, a member of furans, an aromatic ether, a quinoxaline derivative, an aromatic amide and a monocarboxylic acid amide.
