The molecule is an N-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine in which the N-acyl group is specified as (4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-(4Z,7Z,10Z,13Z,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine(1-).
