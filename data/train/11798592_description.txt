The molecule is a hexacyclic triterpenoid that is a lactone of friedelin. Isolated from Celastrus hindsii, it exhibits anti-HIV and antineoplastic activities. It has a role as a metabolite, an antineoplastic agent and an anti-HIV agent. It is a cyclic terpene ketone, a terpene lactone, a hexacyclic triterpenoid, a secondary alcohol and a bridged compound. It derives from a friedelin.
