The molecule is a member of the class of 2-pyranones that is 5,6-dihydro-2H-pyran-2-one substituted by a hydroxy group at position 5 and a 10-(acetyloxy)-2,4,6,8-tetrahydroxypentacosyl group at position 6.It has been isolated from Cryptocarya species It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is a member of 2-pyranones, a pentol and an acetate ester.
