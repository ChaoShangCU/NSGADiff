The molecule is a dihydroxyflavanone that is flavanone substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 5 and 7 and 6-carboxy-1-phenylhex-2-en-1-yl groups at positions 6 and 8 respectively. It has been isolated as a racemate from the bark of Cryptocarya chartacea and exhibits inhibitory activity against dengue virus NS5 polymerase. It has a role as a plant metabolite and an antiviral agent. It is a dihydroxyflavanone and a dicarboxylic acid.
