The molecule is an inositol phosphate oxoanion that is the conjugate base of 1-amino-1-deoxy-scyllo-inositol 4-phosphate, arising from deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups and protonation of the amino group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a 1-amino-1-deoxy-scyllo-inositol 4-phosphate.
