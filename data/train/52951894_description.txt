The molecule is a limonoid that is 17-epiazadiradione substituted by a hydroxy group at position 17. Isolated from Azadirachta indica, it exhibits antineoplastic activity. It has a role as a metabolite, a plant metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an acetate ester, a cyclic terpene ketone, a member of furans, a limonoid, a tetracyclic triterpenoid, a tertiary alcohol and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a 17-epiazadiradione.
