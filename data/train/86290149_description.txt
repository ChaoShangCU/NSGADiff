The molecule is an acyl-CoA(4-) resulting from the removal of all four protons from the phosphate and diphosphate groups of (25S)-3-oxocholest-4-en-26-oyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a steroidal acyl-CoA(4-) and a (2S)-2-methylacyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a (25S)-3-oxocholest-4-en-26-oyl-CoA.
