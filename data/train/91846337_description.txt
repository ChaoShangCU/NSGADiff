The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide consisting of alpha-D-mannosyl, D-mannosyl, 2-acetamido-beta-D-glucosyl and 2-acetamido-D-glucosyl residues joined in sequence by (1->6), (1->4), and (1->4) glycosidic bonds. It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a glucosamine oligosaccharide and a mannooligosaccharide derivative.
