The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of (5Z,8Z,11Z)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a long-chain fatty acid anion, an icosanoid anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a (5Z,8Z,11Z)-14,15-dihydroxyicosatrienoic acid.
