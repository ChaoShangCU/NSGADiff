The molecule is a very long-chain omega-6 fatty acid that is tetracosanoic acid having four double bonds located at positions 17, 20, 23 and 26 (the 17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z-isomer). It is an omega-6 fatty acid and a dotriacontatetraenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (17Z,20Z,23Z,26Z)-dotriacontatetraenoate.
