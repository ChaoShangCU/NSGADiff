The molecule is a 7-deazaguanosine ribonucleoside that has 7-carbamoyl-7-deazaguanine as the nucleobase. It is present in archaeal tRNA and has also been found in bacterial DNA. It is a 7-deazaguanine ribonucleoside and a primary carboxamide. It derives from a 7-carboxy-7-deazaguanine and an archaeosine.
