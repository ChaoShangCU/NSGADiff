The molecule is an N-jasmonyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group of N-[(+)-7-isojasmonyl]-L-isoleucine; major species at pH 7.3. It is a N-jasmonyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion and a N-[(3R)-jasmonyl]-L-isoleucinate. It is a conjugate base of a N-[(+)-7-isojasmonyl]-L-isoleucine.
