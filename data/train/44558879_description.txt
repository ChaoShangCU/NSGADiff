The molecule is a cephalosporin carboxylis acid anion having 2-(2-amino-1,3-thiazol-4-yl)-2-(methoxyimino)acetylamino and [(2-methyl-5,6-dioxo-1,2,5,6-tetrahydro-1,2,4-triazin-3-yl)sulfanyl]methyl side-groups, formed by proton loss from the carboxy group of ceftriaxone. It is a conjugate base of a ceftriaxone.
