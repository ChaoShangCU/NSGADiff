The molecule is a diterpene alkaloid with formula C22H35NO6, originally isolated from Aconitum carmichaeli. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, a diterpene alkaloid, an organic heteropolycyclic compound, a polyether, a secondary alcohol, a tertiary alcohol, a tetrol and a secondary amino compound. It derives from a hydride of an aconitane.
