The molecule is a quassinoid isolated from Brucea antidysenterica and has been shown to exhibit in vitro cytotoxicity towards several human tumour cell lines. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a delta-lactone, a cyclic ether, an enoate ester, an organic heteropentacyclic compound, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a quassinoid, a triol, an enol and a methyl ester.
