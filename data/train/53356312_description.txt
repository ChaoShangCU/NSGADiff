The molecule is a lignan that consists of tetrahydro-1H,3H-furo[3,4-c]furan substituted by 3,4,5-trimethoxyphenyl and a 4-hydroxy-3,5-dimethoxyphenyl group at positions 4 and 1 respectively. It has been isolated from the stems of Sinocalamus affinis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a lignan, a member of methoxybenzenes, a member of phenols and a furofuran.
