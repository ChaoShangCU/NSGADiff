The molecule is an alicyclic ketone that is cyclohexanone in which the pro-S hydrogens at positions 2, 3, and 4 are substituted by methoxy, (2E)-6-methylhept-2-en-2-yl, and perfluoro-2-hydroxypropan-2-yl groups, respectively. It has a role as an angiogenesis inhibitor. It is an organofluorine compound and an alicyclic ketone. It derives from a fumagalone.
