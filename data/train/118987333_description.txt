The molecule is an organic heterotetracyclic compound obtained by intramolecular formation of a C-C bond between positions 8 and 5' of dAMP. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite. It is a N-glycosyl compound, a bridged compound, an aromatic amine, an organic heterotetracyclic compound, a phosphate monoester and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a 2'-deoxyadenosine 5'-monophosphate.
