The molecule is a cyclic ketone that is cyclohexa-2,5-dien-1-one substituted by a methoxy group at position 5, a 2-oxopropyl group at position 4, a hydroxy group at position 4 and a prop-2-en-1-yl group at position 2 which in turn is substituted by s a 3-hydroxy-4-methoxyphenyl group at position 1. It has been isolated from Pterocarpus santalinus. It has a role as a metabolite and a plant metabolite. It is an aromatic ether, a member of phenols and a cyclic ketone.
