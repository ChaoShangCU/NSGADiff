The molecule is a very long-chain fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of gondoic acid, formed by deprotonation of the carboxylic acid group. It has a role as a plant metabolite and a human metabolite. It is an unsaturated fatty acid anion, an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and an icosenoate. It is a conjugate base of an (11Z)-icos-11-enoic acid.
