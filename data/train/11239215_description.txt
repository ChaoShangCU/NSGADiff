The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is malvidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(E-caffeoyl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue. It is a beta-D-glucoside, an anthocyanin cation, an aromatic ether, a cinnamate ester, a monosaccharide derivative and a polyphenol. It derives from a malvidin and a trans-caffeic acid.
