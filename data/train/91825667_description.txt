The molecule is an oligosaccharide derivative that is produced by Streptomyces viridochromogenes and is an epimeric intermediate in the biosynthesis of avilamycin A. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a benzoate ester, a cyclic acetal, a dichlorobenzene, an oligosaccharide derivative, an ortho ester, an oxaspiro compound, a member of phenols and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone.
