The molecule is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid that consists of dihydroagarofuran substituted by a acetyloxy group at position 1, benzyloxy groups at positions 6, 9 and 15 and a hydroxy group at position 2. It is isolated from the roots of Microtropis fokienensis and exhibits antitubercular activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antitubercular agent. It is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid, an acetate ester, a benzoate ester, an organic heterotricyclic compound, a bridged compound and a secondary alcohol.
