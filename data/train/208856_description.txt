The molecule is a neolignan that is 2,3-dihydro-1-benzofuran carrying 3,4-dimethoxyphenyl, methyl, formyl and methoxy substituents at positions 2, 3, 5 and 7 respectively. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a member of 1-benzofurans, a dimethoxybenzene, a neolignan, a ring assembly and an arenecarbaldehyde.
