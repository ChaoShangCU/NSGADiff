The molecule is a myricetin O-glucoside that is myricetin with a alpha-D-glucosyl residue attached at position 3'. It has a role as a metabolite. It is an alpha-D-glucoside, a monosaccharide derivative, a myricetin O-glucoside, a pentahydroxyflavone and a member of flavonols. It derives from an alpha-D-glucose.
