The molecule is a diarylheptanoid that is 3-heptanone substituted by a 4-hydroxy-3-methoxyphenyl group at position 1 and a 4-hydroxyphenyl group at position 7. It has been isolated from the rhizomes of Curcuma kwangsiensis. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diarylheptanoid, a ketone and a member of guaiacols.
