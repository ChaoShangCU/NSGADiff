The molecule is a steroid acid anion that is the conjugate base of (25R)-3beta-hydroxycholest-5-en-7-one-26-oic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a (25R)-3beta-hydroxycholest-5-en-7-one-26-oic acid.
