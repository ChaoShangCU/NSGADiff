The molecule is a hydroxy fatty acid anion obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy function of 15-oxolipoxin A4; major species at pH 7.3. It is a hydroxy fatty acid anion, a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, an oxo fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and an icosanoid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 15-oxolipoxin A4.
