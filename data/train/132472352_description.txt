The molecule is a docosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 16,17(S)-dihydroxy-(7Z,10Z,13E,14E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a docosanoid anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 16,17(S)-dihydroxy-(7Z,10Z,13E,14E,19Z)-docosapentaenoic acid.
