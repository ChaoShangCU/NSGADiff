The molecule is a C20-gibberellin that is tha lactone form of gibberellin A15 carrying two additional hydroxy substituents at positions 3 and 7. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a C20-gibberellin, a gibberellin monocarboxylic acid, a lactone, a dihydroxy monocarboxylic acid and an olefinic compound. It derives from a gibberellin A15 (lactone form).
