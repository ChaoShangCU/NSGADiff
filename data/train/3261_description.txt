The molecule is a triazolo[4,3-a][1,4]benzodiazepine having a phenyl group at position 6 and a chloro substituent at position 8. A short-acting benzodiazepine with general properties similar to diazepam, it is given by mouth as a hypnotic in the short-term management of insomnia. It has a role as an anxiolytic drug, an anticonvulsant and a GABA modulator. It is a member of triazoles and a triazolobenzodiazepine.
