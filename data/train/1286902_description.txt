The molecule is a chromone that is 3-(1,3-benzothiazol-2-yl)-8-(1-piperidinylmethyl)chromen-4-one bearing additional ethyl and hydroxy substituents at positions 6 and 7 respectively. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent and an EC 6.3.2.19 (ubiquitin--protein ligase) inhibitor. It is a member of benzothiazoles, a member of chromones, a member of piperidines and a member of phenols.
