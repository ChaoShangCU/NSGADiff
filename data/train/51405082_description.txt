The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 12(S)-HETE, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion, a HETE anion and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 12(S)-HETE.
