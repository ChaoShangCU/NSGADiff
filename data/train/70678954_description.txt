The molecule is an amino hexasaccharide consisting of N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine, alpha-D-galactose, beta-D-galactose, N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose, and beta-D-glucose residues joined in sequence with (1->3)-, (1->4)-, (1->4)-, (1->3)- and (1->4)-linkages, respectively. It is an amino hexasaccharide, a galactosamine oligosaccharide and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
