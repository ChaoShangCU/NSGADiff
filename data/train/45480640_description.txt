The molecule is the penta-anion obtained by removing protons from the phosphate and carboxylic acid groups of coenzyme alpha-F420-3. It has a role as a coenzyme. It is a dialkyl phosphate anion, a ribitol phosphate, a member of pyrimidoquinolines and a tetracarboxylic acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a coenzyme alpha-F420-3. It is a conjugate acid of a coenzyme alpha-F420-3(6-).
