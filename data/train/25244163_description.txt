The molecule is dianion of dTDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose arising from deprotonation of the diphosphate OH groups; major species at pH 7.3. It is an intermediate in dTDP-rhamnose biosynthesis. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a conjugate base of a dTDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose.
