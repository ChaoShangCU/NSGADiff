The molecule is a flavonoid oxoanion resulting from the deprotonation of the hydroxy group at position 7 of myricetin 3-O-[(6-O-caffeoyl-beta-D-glucosyl)-(1->2)-alpha-L-rhamnoside]. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a conjugate base of a myricetin 3-O-[(6-O-caffeoyl-beta-D-glucosyl)-(1->2)-alpha-L-rhamnoside].
