The molecule is an anthocyanin cation that is peonidin substituted at position 3 by a 6-O-(trans-4 coumaryl)-beta-D-glucosyl residue It has a role as a metabolite. It is an anthocyanin cation, a cinnamate ester, a beta-D-glucoside, a polyphenol and an aromatic ether. It derives from a trans-4-coumaric acid and a peonidin.
