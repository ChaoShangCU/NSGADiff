The molecule is an oxodocosahexaenoic acid that is (4Z,7Z,10Z,14E,16Z,19Z)-docosahexaenoic acid in which the oxo group is located at position 13. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators. It has a role as a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is an oxodocosahexaenoic acid and an enone. It derives from an all-cis-docosa-4,7,10,13,16,19-hexaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (4Z,7Z,10Z,14E,16Z,19Z)-13-oxodocosahexaenoate.
