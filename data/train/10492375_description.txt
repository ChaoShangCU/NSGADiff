The molecule is a nucleoside analogue that is 2'-deoxyuridine in which the hydrogen at position 5 on the uracil ring is replaced by a carboxy group. It has a role as a Mycoplasma genitalium metabolite and a drug metabolite. It is a nucleoside analogue, a pyrimidine 2'-deoxyribonucleoside and an aromatic carboxylic acid. It derives from a uracil.
