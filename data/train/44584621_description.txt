The molecule is a diterpenoid isolated from Scoparia dulcis and has been shown to exhibit inhibitory activity against beta-glucuronidase from bovine liver. It has a role as a metabolite and an EC 3.2.1.31 (beta-glucuronidase) inhibitor. It is a diterpenoid, a benzoate ester, a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid, a primary alcohol and a carbobicyclic compound.
