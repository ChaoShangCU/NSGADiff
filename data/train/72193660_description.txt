The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isoscoparin in which the hydroxyl hydrogen at position 7 is replaced by a 6-sinapoylglucosyl residue. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a cinnamate ester, a dihydroxyflavone, a monomethoxyflavone and a glycosyloxyflavone. It derives from an isoscoparin and a trans-sinapic acid.
