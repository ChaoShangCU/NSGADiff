The molecule is a member of the class of pyrazoles that is cyenopyrafen in which the pivalate ester group has been hydrolysed to give the corresponding enol. It is the active acaricide of the proacaricide cyenopyrafen. It has a role as an agrochemical and an acaricide. It is a nitrile, a member of pyrazoles, an enol and a member of benzenes.
