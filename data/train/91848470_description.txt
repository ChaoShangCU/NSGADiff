The molecule is an amino trisaccharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 6 have each been glycosylated by an alpha-L-fucopyranosyl group. It is an amino trisaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from an alpha-L-Fucp-(1->3)-beta-D-GlcpNAc.
