The molecule is an eighteen-membered homodetic cyclic peptide which is isolated from Oscillatoria sp. and exhibits antimalarial activity against the W2 chloroquine-resistant strain of the malarial parasite, Plasmodium falciparum. It has a role as a metabolite and an antimalarial. It is a homodetic cyclic peptide, a macrocycle, a member of 1,3-oxazoles and a member of 1,3-thiazoles.
