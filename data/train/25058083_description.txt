The molecule is an indole alkaloid that is tabersonine substituted by a hydroxy group at potition 10, a methoxy group at position 11 and an epoxy group across positions 14 and 15. Isolated from Tabernaemontana corymbosa, it exhibits cytotoxicity against human KB cells. It has a role as a metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is an alkaloid ester, an aromatic ether, an indole alkaloid, a member of phenols, an epoxide, a methyl ester and an organic heterohexacyclic compound. It derives from a tabersonine.
