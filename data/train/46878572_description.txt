The molecule is an organophosphate oxoanion that is the dianion of N(6)-acetyl-N(2)-(5'-phosphopyridoxyl)-L-alanine having anionic carboxy, hydroxy and phosphate groups with the secondary amino group and pyridine nitrogen protonated. It has a role as an epitope and an antigen. It is a conjugate base of a N(6)-acetyl-N(2)-(5'-phosphopyridoxyl)-L-lysine.
