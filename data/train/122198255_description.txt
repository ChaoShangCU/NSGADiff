The molecule is an icosanoid anion that is the conjugate base of 5(S),6(S)-epoxy-18-hydroxy-(7E,9E,11Z,14Z,16E)-icosapentaenoic acid, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is an icosanoid anion, a hydroxy fatty acid anion and a long-chain fatty acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a 5(S),6(S)-epoxy-18-hydroxy-(7E,9E,11Z,14Z,16E)-icosapentaenoic acid.
