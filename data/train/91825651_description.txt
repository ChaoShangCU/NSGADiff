The molecule is a member of the class of phosphonic acids that is propylphosphonic acid substituted by thio and hydroxy groups at positions 1 and 2 respectively (the 1R,2S-stereoisomer) It is a member of phosphonic acids, a secondary alcohol and an alkanethiol. It is a conjugate acid of a [(1R,2S)-2-hydroxy-1-thiopropyl]phosphonate(2-).
