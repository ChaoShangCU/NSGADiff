The molecule is a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-formyl-4beta-methylsteroid that is 9beta-9,19-cyclolanost-24-ene-3beta,28-diol in which the hydroxy group at position 28 has been oxidised to the corresponding aldehyde. It is a 3beta-hydroxy-4alpha-formyl-4beta-methylsteroid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a 9beta-9,19-cyclolanost-24-ene-3beta,28-diol.
