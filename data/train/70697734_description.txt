The molecule is a flavagline with a cyclopenta[bc]benzopyran skeleton isolated from the leaves of Aglaia forbesii. It exhibits antitubercular and antiviral activity. It has a role as a metabolite, an antitubercular agent and an antiviral agent. It is a member of methoxybenzenes, a member of benzamides, a bridged compound, a flavagline, a tertiary alcohol and a secondary alcohol.
