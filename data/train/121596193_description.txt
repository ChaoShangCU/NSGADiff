The molecule is a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid that is isolated from Tripterygium wilfordii. It has a role as an antiviral agent and a plant metabolite. It is a bridged compound, a benzoate ester, an acetate ester, a dihydroagarofuran sesquiterpenoid, an organic heterotricyclic compound, an oxacycle and a tertiary alcohol.
