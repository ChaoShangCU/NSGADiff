The molecule is a carbamate ester that is phenyl methylcarbamate substituted at position 2 by a propan-2-yloxy group. It has a role as an EC 3.1.1.7 (acetylcholinesterase) inhibitor, a carbamate insecticide, an acaricide and an agrochemical. It is a carbamate ester and an aromatic ether. It derives from a methylcarbamic acid and a 2-isopropoxyphenol.
