The molecule is a very long-chain omega-3 fatty acid that is tetratriacontapentaenoic acid having five double bonds located at positions 19, 22, 25 ,28 and 31 (the 19Z,22Z,25Z,28Z,31Z-isomer). It is an omega-3 fatty acid and a tetratriacontapentaenoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a (19Z,22Z,25Z,28Z,31Z)-tetratriacontapentaenoate.
