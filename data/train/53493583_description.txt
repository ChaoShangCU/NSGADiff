The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is olean-18-ene substituted by alpha-hydroxy groups at positions 3 and 11 respectively and a carboxy group at position 28. It has been isolated from the leaves and twigs of Fatsia polycarpa. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid and a pentacyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a hydride of an oleanane.
