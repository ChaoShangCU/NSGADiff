The molecule is a tetracyclic triterpenoid that is the 7-oxo derivative of cucurbitadienol. Isolated from Trichosanthes kirilowii, it exhibits anti-inflammatory activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an anti-inflammatory agent. It is a cyclic terpene ketone and a tetracyclic triterpenoid. It derives from a cucurbitadienol. It derives from a hydride of a cucurbitane.
