The molecule is a carbohydrate acid anion arising from deprotonation of both carboxy groups of 2-(beta-D-glucopyranosyluronic acid)-D-glucuronic acid; major species at pH 7.3. It is a carbohydrate acid anion and a dicarboxylic acid dianion. It is a conjugate base of a 2-(beta-D-glucopyranosyluronic acid)-D-glucuronic acid.
