The molecule is a steroid glucosiduronic acid that is 2-hydroxyestrone having a single beta-D-glucuronic acid residue attached at position 2. It is a beta-D-glucosiduronic acid, a 17-oxo steroid, a steroid glucosiduronic acid and a 3-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a 2-hydroxyestrone. It is a conjugate acid of a 2-hydroxyestrone 2-O-(beta-D-glucuronide)(1-).
