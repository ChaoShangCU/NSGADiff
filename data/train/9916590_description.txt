The molecule is a disaccharide derivative that is the 2"-O-beta-D-glucosyl derivative of isovitexin. It has a role as a metabolite. It is a C-glycosyl compound, a disaccharide derivative and a trihydroxyflavone. It derives from an isovitexin. It is a conjugate acid of an isovitexin 2''-O-beta-D-glucoside(1-).
