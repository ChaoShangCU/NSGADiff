The molecule is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of phosphate and diphosphate functions of phytanoyl-CoA. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA(4-), a 3-methyl fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a phytanoyl-CoA.
