The molecule is a member of the class of 2-pyranones that is 2H-pyran-2-one substituted by a methoxy group at position 4, a methyl group at position 3 and a 4,6-dimethylbiphenyl-2-yl group at position 6. It has been isolated from an endophytic fungus, Aspergillus niger. It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite and an antineoplastic agent. It is a member of 2-pyranones and a ring assembly.
