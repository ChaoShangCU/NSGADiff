The molecule is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) arising from deprotonation of the phosphate and diphosphate groups of 3-hydroxyoctadecanoyl-CoA; major species at pH 7.3. It is a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl-CoA(4-) and an 11,12-saturated fatty acyl-CoA(4-). It is a conjugate base of a 3-hydroxyoctadecanoyl-CoA.
