The molecule is a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid that is (3R,5R)-3,5-dihydroxyheptanoic acid in which one of the methyl hydrogens is substituted by a (1S,2S,6S,8S,8aR)-6,8-dihydroxy-2-methyl-1,2,6,7,8,8a-hexahydronaphthalen-1-yl group. It is a 3-hydroxy carboxylic acid, a member of hexahydronaphthalenes and a secondary allylic alcohol.
