The molecule is an amino tetrasaccharide that is beta-D-Galp-beta-D-GalpNAc-D-Glcp in which the acetaminogalactosyl group has undergone formal glycosylation at position 6 by a beta-D-acetaminogalactopyranosyl group. It is an amino tetrasaccharide, a member of acetamides and a glucosamine oligosaccharide.
