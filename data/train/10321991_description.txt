The molecule is a hydrochloride resulting from the formal reaction of equimolar amounts of prinomastat and hydrogen chloride. A selective inhibitor with of matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs) 2, 3, 9, 13, and 14. It has a role as a matrix metalloproteinase inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent and an EC 3.4.24.35 (gelatinase B) inhibitor. It contains a prinomastat(1+).
