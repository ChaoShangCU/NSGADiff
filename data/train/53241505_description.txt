The molecule is an enamide resulting from the formal condensation of 3-(p-tert-butylphenyl)-3-(6-chloropyridin-2-yl)acrylic acid with the amino group of morpholine. A fungicide developed in China, it shows high antifungal activity against diseases caused by Phytophthora infestans, Phytophthora capsici, Rhizoctonia solani, Peronophythora litchi, and Pseudoperonospora cubensis. It has a role as a fungicide. It is a member of morpholines, a chloropyridine, a tertiary carboxamide and an enamide.
