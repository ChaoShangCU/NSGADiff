The molecule is a carboxamide that is cyantraniliprole in which the bromine atom has been replaced by a [5-(trifluoromethyl)-2H-tetrazol-2-yl]methyl group. It has a role as a ryanodine receptor agonist. It is a nitrile, an organochlorine compound, a pyrazole insecticide, a member of pyridines, a member of tetrazoles, an organofluorine compound and a secondary carboxamide.
