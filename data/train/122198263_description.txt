The molecule is a class I yanuthone that is 5,6-epoxy-4-hydroxy-3-hydroxymethylcyclohex-2-en-1-one which is substituted at position 6 by a (2E)-7-carboxy-3-methyloct-2-en-1-yl group (the R,R,R stereoisomer). It has a role as an Aspergillus metabolite. It is a class I yanuthone, a monocarboxylic acid, a primary alcohol and a secondary alcohol.
