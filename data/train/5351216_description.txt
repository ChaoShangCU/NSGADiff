The molecule is a pyrimidotriazine that is 6-methyl-5,6,7,8-tetrahydropyrimido[5,4-e][1,2,4]triazine with oxo groups at positions 5 and 6. It has a role as an antineoplastic agent, an antimicrobial agent and a bacterial metabolite. It is a pyrimidotriazine and a carbonyl compound. It derives from a 1,6-didemethyltoxoflavin.
