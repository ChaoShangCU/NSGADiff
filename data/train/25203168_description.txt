The molecule is a L-glutamine derivative obtained by formal condensation of the side-chain carboxy group of L-glutamine with one of the amino groups of 2-(4-{[5-(aminomethyl)furan-3-yl]methoxy}phenyl)ethan-1-amine. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It is a L-glutamine derivative, a member of furans, a primary amino compound and an aromatic ether.
