The molecule is a 2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate(2-) obtained by deprotonation of the phosphate OH groups of 2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate; major species at pH 7.3. It has a role as a mammalian metabolite. It derives from an arachidonate. It is a conjugate base of a 2-arachidonoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphate.
