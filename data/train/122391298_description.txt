The molecule is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion resulting from the deprotonation of the carboxy group of N-arachidonoyl-L-phenylalanine. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion, a N-(fatty acyl)-L-alpha-amino acid anion and a N-(fatty acyl)-L-phenylalanine(1-). It is a conjugate base of a N-arachidonoyl-L-phenylalanine.
