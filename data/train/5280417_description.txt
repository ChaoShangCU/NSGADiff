The molecule is a dimethoxyflavone that the 3,7-di-O-methyl derivative of quercetin. It has a role as an EC 1.3.1.22 [3-oxo-5alpha-steroid 4-dehydrogenase (NADP(+))] inhibitor and a metabolite. It is a trihydroxyflavone and a dimethoxyflavone. It derives from a quercetin. It is a conjugate acid of a 3',4',5-trihydroxy-3,7-dimethoxyflavone(1-).
