The molecule is an N-alkylpiperazine that consists of (2-methoxyphenyl)piperazine in which the amine hydrogen is substituted by a 4-(2-phthalimido)butyl group. It has a role as a serotonergic antagonist. It is a N-alkylpiperazine, a N-arylpiperazine and a member of phthalimides. It is a conjugate base of a NAN 190(1+).
