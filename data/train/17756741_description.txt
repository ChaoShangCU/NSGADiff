The molecule is a 3-oxo steroid that is cortisone in which the double bond between positions 4 and 5 has been reduced to a single bond. It is a 3-oxo steroid, an 11-oxo steroid, a 20-oxo steroid, a 17alpha-hydroxy steroid, a 21-hydroxy steroid, a primary alpha-hydroxy ketone and a tertiary alpha-hydroxy ketone. It derives from a cortisone.
