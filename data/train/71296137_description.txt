The molecule is an amino pentasaccharide consisting of alpha-D-galactose, beta-D-galactose N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine, beta-D-galactose, and beta-D-glucose residues joined in sequence with ((1->4)-, (1->4)-, (1->3)- and (1->4)-linkages, respectively. It is a glucosamine oligosaccharide and an amino pentasaccharide.
