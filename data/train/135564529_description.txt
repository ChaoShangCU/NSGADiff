The molecule is a cyclic purine dinucleotide that consists of AMP and GMP units cyclised via 3',5'- and 2',5'-linkages respectively. It is a cyclic purine dinucleotide, a guanyl ribonucleotide and an adenyl ribonucleotide. It derives from a Gp[2'-5']Ap[3']. It is a conjugate acid of a 2'-3'-cGAMP(2-).
