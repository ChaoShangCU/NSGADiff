The molecule is a C-terminal boronic acid peptide inhibitor which induces apoptosis in multiple myeloma, hematological and solid tumor cell lines. It has a role as a proteasome inhibitor, an apoptosis inducer and an antineoplastic agent. It is a threonine derivative, a phenylpyridine, a C-terminal boronic acid peptide and a secondary alcohol. It derives from a L-threonine.
