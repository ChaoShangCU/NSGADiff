The molecule is a pentacyclic triterpenoid that is 9beta,19-cyclolanost-24-ene substituted by an oxo group at position 3 and hydroxy groups at positions 7 and 23. It has been isolated from the leaves of Combretum quadrangulare. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a diol, a pentacyclic triterpenoid, a cyclic terpene ketone and a 3-oxo-5alpha-steroid. It derives from a hydride of a cycloartane.
