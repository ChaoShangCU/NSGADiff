The molecule is an azaspiro compound that is 1,3,8-triazaspiro[4.5]decane which is substituted at positions 1, 4, and 8 by phenyl, oxo, and 4-(p-fluorophenyl)-4-oxobutyl groups, respectively. It has a role as a dopaminergic antagonist, a serotonergic antagonist, an alpha-adrenergic antagonist, an antipsychotic agent and a psychotropic drug. It is an organofluorine compound, an azaspiro compound, a member of piperidines, a tertiary amino compound and an aromatic ketone.
