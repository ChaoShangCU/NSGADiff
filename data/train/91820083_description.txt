The molecule is a CMP-sugar having 8-amino-3,8-dideoxy-alpha-D-manno-oct-2-ulosonic acid as the sugar component. It has a role as a bacterial metabolite. It derives from an 8-amino-3,8-dideoxy-alpha-D-manno-oct-2-ulosonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a CMP-8-amino-3,8-dideoxy-alpha-D-manno-oct-2-ulosonate(1-).
