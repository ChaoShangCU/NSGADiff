The molecule is a thioxanthene derivative having a trifluoromethyl substituent at the 2-position and a 3-(4-(2-hydroxyethyl)piperazin-1-yl)propylidene group at the 10-position with undefined double bond stereochemistry. It has a role as a dopaminergic antagonist, an alpha-adrenergic antagonist, an anxiolytic drug, an antidepressant, a first generation antipsychotic and an EC 3.4.21.26 (prolyl oligopeptidase) inhibitor. It is a N-alkylpiperazine, a member of thioxanthenes, a fluorine molecular entity and a primary alcohol.
