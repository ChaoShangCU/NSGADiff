The molecule is a prostaglandins Falpha that is prost-5-en-1-oic acid substituted by hydroxy groups at positions 9, 11 and 15. It has a role as a rat metabolite. It is a prostaglandins Falpha and a hydroxy monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a prostaglandin F2alpha. It is a conjugate acid of a 13,14-dihydroprostaglandin F2alpha(1-).
