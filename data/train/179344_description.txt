The molecule is the acetate ester, with S configuration, of licarbazepine. An anticonvulsant, it is approved for use in Europe and the United States as an adjunctive therapy for epilepsy. It has a role as an anticonvulsant and a drug allergen. It is an acetate ester, a dibenzoazepine, a carboxamide and a member of ureas. It derives from a licarbazepine.
