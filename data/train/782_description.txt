The molecule is a imidazolidine-2,4-dione that is hydantoin substituted by a 2-carboxyethyl group at position 4. It has a role as a metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is an imidazolidine-2,4-dione and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from a hydantoin. It is a conjugate acid of a hydantoin-5-propionate.
