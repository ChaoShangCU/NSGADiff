The molecule is a member of the class of 1,3-benzoxazoles that is 6-chloro-1,3-benzoxazol-2-ol in which the hydrogen of the hydroxy group at position 2 is substituted by a 4-({1-[(2-fluorophenyl)(methyl)amino]-1-oxopropan-2-yl}oxy)phenyl group. It is a member of 1,3-benzoxazoles, an organochlorine compound, an aromatic ether, a member of monofluorobenzenes and a tertiary carboxamide.
