The molecule is an N-acyllysophosphatidylethanolamine in which the N-acyl group is specified as arachidonoyl while the phosphatidyl acyl group is specified as oleoyl. It derives from an oleic acid and an arachidonic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a N-arachidonoyl-1-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphoethanolamine(1-).
