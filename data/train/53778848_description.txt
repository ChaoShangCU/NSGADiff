The molecule is an aminodisaccharide that is 2-acetamido-2-deoxy-beta-D-glucopyranose in which the hydroxy group at position 6 has been glycosylated by an alpha-D-mannopyranosyl group. It is an amino disaccharide and a member of acetamides. It derives from a N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosamine and an alpha-D-mannose.
