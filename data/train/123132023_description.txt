The molecule is an N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion resulting from the deprotonation of the carboxy groups of N-oleoyl-L-glutamic acid. The major species at pH 7.3. It is a N-acyl-L-alpha-amino acid anion and a N-(fatty acyl)-L-alpha-amino acid anion. It is a conjugate base of a N-oleoyl-L-glutamic acid.
