The molecule is a fatty acid ester obtained by formal condensation of the carboxy group of oleic acid with the hydroxy group of 9-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a fatty acid ester and a monocarboxylic acid. It derives from an oleic acid and a 9-hydroxyoctadecanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 9-[(9Z)-octadecenoyloxy]octadecanoate.
