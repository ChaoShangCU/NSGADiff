The molecule is a C-glycosyl compound that is isoscutellarein attached to a beta-D-glucopyranosyl moiety at position 6 via a C-glycosidic linkage. Isolated from the rhizomes of Iris pseudopumila, it exhibits antioxidant activity. It has a role as a metabolite and an antioxidant. It is a tetrahydroxyflavone and a C-glycosyl compound. It derives from an isoscutellarein.
