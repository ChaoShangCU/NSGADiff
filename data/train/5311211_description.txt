The molecule is a prostaglandin J derivative comprising prostaglandin J2 lacking the 15-hydroxy group and having C=C double bonds at the 12- and 14-positions. It has a role as a metabolite, an electrophilic reagent and an insulin-sensitizing drug. It derives from a prostaglandin J2. It is a conjugate acid of a 15-deoxy-Delta(12,14)-prostaglandin J2(1-).
