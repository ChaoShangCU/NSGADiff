The molecule is a steroid sulfate that is the 3-sulfate of epiandrosterone. It has a role as a human metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a 17-oxo steroid, an androstanoid and a steroid sulfate. It derives from an epiandrosterone. It is a conjugate acid of an epiandrosterone sulfate(1-). It derives from a hydride of a 5alpha-androstane.
