The molecule is a member of the class of ureas that is urea in which one of the hydrogens is replaced by a hydroxy group. An antineoplastic used in the treatment of chronic myeloid leukaemia as well as for sickle-cell disease. It has a role as a DNA synthesis inhibitor, an EC 1.17.4.1 (ribonucleoside-diphosphate reductase) inhibitor, an antineoplastic agent, a genotoxin, an antimetabolite, a teratogenic agent, a radical scavenger, an immunomodulator and an antimitotic. It is a member of ureas and a one-carbon compound.
