The molecule is a bile acid that is lithocholic acid carrying an additional oxo substituent at position 7. It has a role as a human metabolite. It is a bile acid, a monohydroxy-5beta-cholanic acid, an oxo-5beta-cholanic acid and a 3alpha-hydroxy steroid. It derives from a lithocholic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 7-oxolithocholate.
