The molecule is a dithioloquinoxaline that results from the formal condensation of 6-methylquinoxaline-2,3-dithiol with phosgene. It has been used as a fungicide and acaricide for the control of mites and powdery mildew on citrus, vegetables, and walnuts, but is not approved for use in the EU. It has a role as an agrochemical. It is a quinoxaline acaricide, a quinoxaline antifungal agent and a dithioloquinoxaline.
