The molecule is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion that is the conjugate base of 11,12-EET, obtained by deprotonation of the carboxy group; major species at pH 7.3. It is a polyunsaturated fatty acid anion, a long-chain fatty acid anion and an EET(1-). It derives from an arachidonate. It is a conjugate base of an 11,12-EET.
