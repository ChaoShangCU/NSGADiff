The molecule is a docosanoid that is (14E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,12,14,19-pentaenoic acid carrying three hydroxy substituents at positions 7, 16 and 17. An intermediate of specialised proresolving mediators It has a role as a specialised pro-resolving mediator and a human xenobiotic metabolite. It is a docosanoid and a hydroxy polyunsaturated fatty acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 7,16,17-trihydroxy-(14E,19Z)-docosa-8,10,12,14,19-pentaenoate.
